🎨 艺术

Fine Arts

中文介绍

艺术专业是一门培养视觉艺术创作能力的学科。它涵盖了绘画、雕塑、版画、摄影、数字艺术、艺术史等多个方向。艺术专业的课程包括基础绘画、色彩理论、素描、雕塑、艺术史、数字媒体等。毕业生在画廊、博物馆、艺术机构、广告公司、教育机构等都有广泛的就业机会。艺术专业培养学生的创造力、审美能力和艺术表达能力。

English Introduction

Fine Arts is a discipline that develops visual art creation abilities. It covers painting, sculpture, printmaking, photography, digital art, art history, and more. Coursework includes foundational drawing, color theory, sketching, sculpture, art history, and digital media. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in galleries, museums, art institutions, advertising agencies, and educational institutions. Fine Arts develops students' creativity, aesthetic abilities, and artistic expression.
