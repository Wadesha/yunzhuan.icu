📢 传媒

Communication

中文介绍

传媒专业是一门研究信息传播和媒体影响的学科。它涵盖了新闻学、公共关系、广告学、媒体研究、数字媒体等多个方向。传媒专业的课程包括新闻写作、媒体理论、公共关系、广告策划、数字媒体制作等。毕业生在新闻媒体、广告公司、公关公司、社交媒体平台、企业传播部门等都有广泛的就业机会。根据NCES数据，传媒与新闻专业的学位授予量排名第十。

English Introduction

Communication is the study of information dissemination and media influence. It covers journalism, public relations, advertising, media studies, digital media, and more. Coursework includes news writing, media theory, public relations, advertising planning, and digital media production. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in news media, advertising agencies, PR firms, social media platforms, and corporate communications. According to NCES data, communications and journalism ranks tenth in degree awards.
