🎨 设计

Design

中文介绍

设计专业是一门培养创意设计和视觉传达能力的学科。它涵盖了平面设计、产品设计、交互设计、时装设计、室内设计等多个方向。设计专业的课程包括设计基础、色彩理论、排版设计、设计软件、设计史等。毕业生在设计公司、广告公司、科技公司、时尚行业等都有广泛的就业机会。设计专业培养学生的创意能力和审美素养。

English Introduction

Design is a discipline that develops creative design and visual communication abilities. It covers graphic design, product design, interaction design, fashion design, interior design, and more. Coursework includes design fundamentals, color theory, typography, design software, and design history. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in design firms, advertising agencies, tech companies, and fashion industry. Design develops students' creative abilities and aesthetic literacy.
