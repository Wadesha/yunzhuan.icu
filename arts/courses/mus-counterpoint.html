对位法

Counterpoint

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：和声学 | 学期：第三学期

本课程研究多声部音乐中各独立旋律线之间的关系与结合原则。以巴赫风格为基础，系统讲授二声部至四声部的严格对位与自由对位技法，包括五种对位类型：一对一、一对二、一对四、切分音和装饰音对位。通过写作练习和分析巴赫等作曲家的复调作品，学生将掌握声部进行的独立性与协调性，理解模仿、卡农、赋格等复调形式，为复调音乐创作和分析奠定基础。

This course studies the relationships and combining principles of independent melodic lines in polyphonic music. Based on Bach's style, it systematically teaches strict and free counterpoint techniques from two to four voices, including the five species of counterpoint: note-against-note, two notes against one, four notes against one, syncopation, and florid counterpoint. Through writing exercises and analysis of polyphonic works by Bach and other composers, students master the independence and coordination of voice leading and understand imitative forms such as canon and fugue, laying the foundation for polyphonic composition and analysis.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：复调对位作品创作与分析

学生需运用严格或自由对位技法创作一首复调作品，如卡农或赋格片段，并撰写技法分析。最终提交乐谱与分析说明。

Students compose a polyphonic work such as a canon or fugue segment using strict or free counterpoint, with a technique analysis. The score and analytical notes are submitted.
