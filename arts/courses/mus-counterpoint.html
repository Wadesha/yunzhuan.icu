对位法

Counterpoint

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：和声学 | 学期：第三学期

本课程研究多声部音乐中各独立旋律线之间的关系与结合原则。以巴赫风格为基础，系统讲授二声部至四声部的严格对位与自由对位技法，包括五种对位类型：一对一、一对二、一对四、切分音和装饰音对位。通过写作练习和分析巴赫等作曲家的复调作品，学生将掌握声部进行的独立性与协调性，理解模仿、卡农、赋格等复调形式，为复调音乐创作和分析奠定基础。

This course studies the relationships and combining principles of independent melodic lines in polyphonic music. Based on Bach's style, it systematically teaches strict and free counterpoint techniques from two to four voices, including the five species of counterpoint: note-against-note, two notes against one, four notes against one, syncopation, and florid counterpoint. Through writing exercises and analysis of polyphonic works by Bach and other composers, students master the independence and coordination of voice leading and understand imitative forms such as canon and fugue, laying the foundation for polyphonic composition and analysis.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：复调对位作品创作与分析

学生需运用严格或自由对位技法创作一首复调作品，如卡农或赋格片段，并撰写技法分析。最终提交乐谱与分析说明。

Students compose a polyphonic work such as a canon or fugue segment using strict or free counterpoint, with a technique analysis. The score and analytical notes are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：创作一首二声部创意曲，比如基于一个原创主题的对位练习。你需要先设计一个有发展潜力的核心动机，用倒影、逆行、扩大等手法发展主题，写出一个完整乐段的二声部对位（包含呈示、模进、小结尾），最后用Sibelius打谱并导出MIDI试听。
步骤 1
对位理论学习与范例分析
本步骤的核心任务是系统学习对位法理论并分析经典对位范例，为复调创作奠定理论基础。对位法是复调音乐的核心技法，有严格的规则和丰富的表现力。通过理论学习和范例分析，掌握对位法的基本规律和写作技巧，理解复调音乐的逻辑和美感。

• 学习对位法基础理论，掌握一音对一音、二音对一音、三音对一音、四音对一音等基本对位类型，理解对位的种类（严格对位、自由对位）、规则和写作要求
• 分析经典复调作品，选择巴赫、亨德尔、贝多芬等大师的复调作品进行深入分析，研究其对位手法、模仿技法、声部关系、结构布局，总结对位写作的规律
• 进行基础对位练习，从最简单的一对一音对音开始，逐步增加难度，练习不同类型的对位写法，熟悉对位规则，培养复调思维
产出：理论学习与分析笔记（含理论笔记、作品分析、基础练习）｜ 质量标准：理论掌握扎实，分析深入，练习规范
步骤 2
对位主题写作与创意设计
本步骤聚焦于对位主题的创作和创意设计，是复调写作的起点。好的对位主题既要具有鲜明的个性，又要具有良好的对位潜质——能够与自身或其他主题形成良好的对位关系。通过精心设计的主题，为复调创作打下坚实的基础。

• 创作Dux（先导声部）主题，创作具有鲜明性格和良好轮廓的主题旋律，考虑主题的音程关系、节奏型、走向高点和收束，使主题既有个性又适合对位发展
• 设计Comes（模仿声部）和答题，研究主题在不同高度、不同时间的模仿形式，设计答题（真实答题、守调答题），处理好主题与答题的调式调性关系
• 探索对题（Countersubject）的写作，创作与主题形成良好对位关系的对题旋律，对题要与主题在节奏、走向、性格上形成对比又协调统一，能够与主题形成良好的复调关系
产出：对位主题素材（含主题旋律、答题设计、对题写作）｜ 质量标准：主题鲜明，答题规范，对题配合好
步骤 3
模仿对位与复调发展
本步骤是复调写作的核心技术环节，学习和运用各种模仿对位技法，发展复调音乐。模仿对位是复调音乐的核心手法，包括卡农、赋格、创意曲等多种形式。通过熟练运用各种模仿技法，创造出丰富的复调织体和层次。

• 学习和练习各种模仿技法，包括严格模仿、自由模仿、定格式模仿、卡农式模仿（同度卡农、五度卡农、倒影卡农、扩大卡农等），掌握不同模仿类型的写作方法
• 进行创意曲或卡农曲的写作，运用模仿对位技法创作一首二部或三部创意曲，或一首卡农曲，练习主题的呈示、发展、再现，处理好各声部的关系
• 探索复调发展手法，运用增值、减值、逆行、倒影、密接和应（Stretto）、转调、紧接模仿等手法发展主题，增加音乐的紧张度和丰富性
产出：模仿对位习作（含卡农/创意曲、技法练习、发展变体）｜ 质量标准：对位规范，声部独立，技法熟练
步骤 4
赋格写作与结构布局
本步骤是对位法学习的综合应用阶段，进行赋格曲的结构设计和写作练习。赋格是复调音乐的最高形式，融合了各种对位技法和结构原则，是对位法学习的综合检验。通过赋格写作，系统掌握复调音乐的结构逻辑和发展手法。

• 学习赋格曲的结构和理论，掌握赋格的结构组成（呈示部、间插段、发展部、再现部），理解主题、答题、对题、间插段、固定对题等基本概念，研究不同类型赋格的特点
• 设计赋格的主题和整体结构，创作适合赋格发展的主题，规划赋格的调性布局、声部数量、结构比例，设计呈示部、中间部、再现部的内容和长度
• 写作赋格作品，按照赋格的结构原则，完整写作一首二部或三部赋格，处理好主题的进入、发展、再现，运用间插段进行过渡和转调，实现结构的完整性和逻辑的严谨性
产出：赋格作品初稿（含结构设计、主题素材、赋格乐谱）｜ 质量标准：结构完整，对位严谨，发展逻辑清晰
步骤 5
作品润色与分析报告
本步骤是对位法学习的总结阶段，对创作的复调作品进行最后的润色完善，并撰写对位技法分析报告。通过细致的打磨使作品更加精致，通过分析报告的写作深化对复调技法的理解，将实践经验上升为理论认识。

• 对复调作品进行润色和完善，检查各声部的独立性和流畅性，调整对位细节，优化声部的平衡和层次，添加适当的力度速度标记，提升音乐的表现力
• 进行听觉检验和调整，使用打谱软件播放或邀请演奏家试奏，从听觉角度评估复调效果，根据听感进行调整和修改，确保音响效果良好
• 撰写对位技法分析报告，系统分析自己创作的复调作品，说明使用的对位技法、结构布局、声部处理，总结对位写作的经验和体会
产出：完成作品与分析报告（含最终乐谱、分析论文、创作总结）｜ 质量标准：作品精致，分析深入，总结深刻

Steps

Step 1
Counterpoint Theory Study & Example Analysis
The core task of this step is to systematically learn counterpoint theory and analyze classic counterpoint examples, laying theoretical foundation for polyphonic creation. Counterpoint is the core technique of polyphonic music, with strict rules and rich expressive power. Through theoretical study and example analysis, master basic laws and writing skills of counterpoint, understand logic and beauty of polyphonic music.

• Learn basic theory of counterpoint, master basic counterpoint types such as note-against-note, two-against-one, three-against-one, four-against-one, understand types of counterpoint (strict counterpoint, free counterpoint), rules and writing requirements
• Analyze classic polyphonic works, select polyphonic works by masters such as Bach, Handel, Beethoven for in-depth analysis, study their counterpoint techniques, imitation techniques, voice relationships, structural layout, summarize laws of counterpoint writing
• Conduct basic counterpoint exercises, start from simplest one-to-one note counterpoint, gradually increase difficulty, practice different types of counterpoint writing, familiarize with counterpoint rules, cultivate polyphonic thinking
Deliverable: Theoretical study and analysis notes (including theory notes, work analysis, basic exercises) | Quality standard: Solid theoretical mastery, in-depth analysis, standardized exercises
Step 2
Counterpoint Theme Writing & Creative Design
This step focuses on creation of counterpoint themes and creative design, which is the starting point of polyphonic writing. A good counterpoint theme needs both distinct personality and good counterpoint potential—able to form good counterpoint relationships with itself or other themes. Through carefully designed themes, lay a solid foundation for polyphonic creation.

• Compose Dux (leading voice) theme, create thematic melody with distinct character and good contour, consider intervallic relationship, rhythmic pattern, direction high point and cadence of the theme, make the theme both individual and suitable for contrapuntal development
• Design Comes (imitating voice) and answer, study imitation forms of theme at different heights and times, design answer (real answer, tonal answer), handle modal/tonal relationship between theme and answer well
• Explore Countersubject writing, create countersubject melody that forms good counterpoint relationship with the theme; countersubject should form contrast yet coordination with theme in rhythm, direction, character, able to form good polyphonic relationship with the theme
Deliverable: Counterpoint theme materials (including thematic melody, answer design, countersubject writing) | Quality standard: Distinct theme, standardized answer, well-coordinated countersubject
Step 3
Imitative Counterpoint & Polyphonic Development
This step is the core technical phase of polyphonic writing, learning and applying various imitative counterpoint techniques to develop polyphonic music. Imitative counterpoint is the core technique of polyphonic music, including canon, fugue, invention and other forms. Through proficient use of various imitation techniques, create rich polyphonic textures and layers.

• Learn and practice various imitation techniques, including strict imitation, free imitation, fixed-form imitation, canonic imitation (canon at unison, canon at fifth, inversion canon, augmentation canon, etc.), master writing methods of different imitation types
• Conduct invention or canon composition, use imitative counterpoint techniques to create a two-voice or three-voice invention, or a canon piece, practice theme presentation, development, recapitulation, handle relationships of each voice part well
• Explore polyphonic development techniques, use augmentation, diminution, retrograde, inversion, Stretto, modulation, close imitation and other techniques to develop themes, increase tension and richness of the music
Deliverable: Imitative counterpoint exercises (including canon/invention, technique practice, development variants) | Quality standard: Standardized counterpoint, independent voices, proficient techniques
Step 4
Fugue Writing & Structural Layout
This step is the comprehensive application phase of counterpoint study, conducting structural design and writing practice of fugue. Fugue is the highest form of polyphonic music, integrating various counterpoint techniques and structural principles, and is the comprehensive test of counterpoint study. Through fugue writing, systematically master structural logic and development techniques of polyphonic music.

• Learn structure and theory of fugue, master structural composition of fugue (exposition, episode, development, recapitulation), understand basic concepts such as subject, answer, countersubject, episode, fixed countersubject, study characteristics of different types of fugue
• Design fugue theme and overall structure, create a theme suitable for fugue development, plan tonal layout, number of voices, structural proportion of the fugue, design content and length of exposition, middle section, recapitulation
• Write fugue work, according to fugue structural principles, completely write a two-voice or three-voice fugue, handle theme entries, development, recapitulation well, use episodes for transition and modulation, achieve structural completeness and logical rigor
Deliverable: Fugue work draft (including structural design, thematic materials, fugue score) | Quality standard: Complete structure, rigorous counterpoint, clear development logic
Step 5
Work Polishing & Analysis Report
This step is the summary phase of counterpoint study, conducting final polishing and improvement of the created polyphonic work, and writing a counterpoint technique analysis report. Through meticulous polishing make the work more refined; through writing analysis report deepen understanding of polyphonic techniques, elevate practical experience to theoretical understanding.

• Polish and improve the polyphonic work, check independence and fluency of each voice part, adjust counterpoint details, optimize balance and layering of voices, add appropriate dynamic/tempo marks, enhance expressive power of the music
• Conduct auditory inspection and adjustment, use notation software playback or invite performers for trial performance, evaluate polyphonic effect from auditory perspective, make adjustments and revisions based on auditory feeling, ensure good sound effect
• Write counterpoint technique analysis report, systematically analyze the polyphonic work you created, explain counterpoint techniques used, structural layout, voice handling, summarize experience and insights of counterpoint writing
Deliverable: Completed work and analysis report (including final score, analysis paper, creation summary) | Quality standard: Exquisite work, in-depth analysis, profound summary
