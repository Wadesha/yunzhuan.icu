品牌设计

Brand Design

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：平面设计 | 学期：第五学期

本课程是设计专业的高阶综合课程，系统讲授品牌设计的理论体系与实践方法。课程内容涵盖品牌战略与定位、品牌命名、品牌识别系统设计、品牌视觉资产管理、品牌触点设计、品牌传播策略等核心内容，从品牌策略到视觉落地形成完整的知识链条。通过真实品牌项目的模拟实践，学生将掌握品牌调研分析、品牌概念提炼、VI系统设计、品牌应用延展等专业技能。课程注重策略思维与视觉表达的结合，培养学生从品牌整体视角思考设计问题的能力，为成为品牌设计师或设计总监奠定基础。

This advanced comprehensive course systematically teaches the theoretical system and practical methods of brand design. The curriculum covers core content including brand strategy and positioning, brand naming, brand identity system design, brand visual asset management, brand touchpoint design, and brand communication strategy, forming a complete knowledge chain from brand strategy to visual implementation. Through simulated practice of real brand projects, students master professional skills such as brand research and analysis, brand concept refinement, VI system design, and brand application extension. The course emphasizes the combination of strategic thinking and visual expression, cultivating students' ability to think about design problems from a holistic brand perspective and laying the foundation for becoming brand designers or design directors.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：完整品牌视觉识别系统设计

学生需为一个新品牌完成从策略到落地的完整VI设计，包括品牌定位、标志设计、识别系统规范和应用延展。最终提交品牌手册与设计成果。

Students complete a full VI design from strategy to execution for a new brand, including positioning, logo design, identity system guidelines, and applications. A brand manual and design deliverables are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
品牌调研与策略定位
本步骤的核心任务是深入了解品牌现状与目标受众，明确品牌视觉识别系统的设计方向和策略定位。品牌视觉设计不是凭空的艺术创作，而是基于品牌战略的视觉表达。通过系统性的调研和分析，找出品牌的核心价值和差异化定位，为后续设计提供清晰的方向指引。

• 收集品牌历史资料、现有视觉资产和竞品视觉系统，进行SWOT分析和视觉审计，找出品牌识别的优势与不足
• 通过用户访谈和问卷调查了解目标受众的品牌认知和审美偏好，建立用户画像，确定设计的风格方向
• 撰写品牌视觉策略简报，明确品牌调性、核心关键词、色彩方向和设计原则，作为后续设计的指导纲领
产出：品牌视觉策略简报（含竞品分析、用户画像、风格定位、设计关键词）｜ 质量标准：策略定位清晰，方向明确可执行，与品牌调性一致
步骤 2
标志设计与概念发想
本步骤是品牌视觉识别系统设计的核心创意环节，从策略思考转向视觉表达，将抽象的品牌概念转化为具象的标志图形。标志是品牌视觉识别的核心，需要兼具独特性、识别性和记忆点。通过多方向的概念探索和筛选，找到最能传达品牌精神的视觉符号。

• 运用头脑风暴和思维导图进行创意发想，从品牌名称、行业属性、核心价值、文化内涵等多个维度探索标志设计方向，生成至少20个创意草图
• 从识别性、独特性、美观性、可延展性四个维度评估设计方案，筛选出3-5个有发展潜力的方向进行深化设计，制作精致的矢量图形稿
• 进行标志应用测试，包括最小尺寸应用、黑白反白效果、不同材质应用、动态效果测试等，确保标志在各种场景下的适用性
产出：标志设计方案（含创意草图、设计深化稿、应用测试稿、设计说明）｜ 质量标准：创意独特有内涵，视觉表现精美，应用性强
步骤 3
视觉识别系统基础设计
本步骤将标志设计扩展为完整的基础识别系统，建立品牌视觉的标准规范。基础系统是品牌视觉识别的基石，包括标志、标准字、标准色、辅助图形等核心元素及其使用规范。系统化的基础设计确保品牌视觉在任何应用场景下都能保持统一和专业。

• 设计基础元素系统，包括标志标准制图、标准字设计、标准色与辅助色系统、图形元素系统、组合规范、禁用规范等，建立完整的视觉元素库
• 制定详细的使用规范，包括标志的最小使用尺寸、安全空间、颜色规范、背景要求、错误用法示例等，确保品牌视觉的一致性
• 设计辅助图形和视觉纹理系统，开发品牌的专属图形语言，增强品牌视觉的丰富性和识别度，为应用设计提供更多视觉素材
产出：基础识别系统规范（含标志、标准字、色彩系统、辅助图形、组合规范）｜ 质量标准：系统完整规范，元素协调统一，可操作性强
步骤 4
应用识别系统设计
本步骤将基础视觉系统落地到具体的应用场景中，展示品牌视觉在实际使用中的效果。应用系统是品牌视觉与公众接触的界面，涵盖办公事务、公关礼品、广告宣传、环境导视、数字媒体等多个领域。完善的应用设计能让客户直观感受到品牌视觉系统的实际价值。

• 设计办公事务用品，包括名片、信纸、信封、工作证、文件夹、PPT模板、邮件签名等，建立品牌的专业形象
• 设计品牌推广物料，包括海报、宣传册、包装、社交媒体素材、网站Banner等，展示品牌视觉系统在不同媒介上的应用效果
• 进行空间环境调研和人流分析，梳理导视需求和信息层级，制定导视系统总体规划，包括一级导视、二级导视、三级导视的层级架构
产出：应用识别系统设计（含办公用品、宣传物料、包装设计、环境导视）｜ 质量标准：应用场景丰富，视觉效果统一，落地性强
步骤 5
品牌手册制作与交付
本步骤是品牌VI设计的收官环节，将所有设计成果整理成系统的品牌视觉手册，并进行最终的交付。品牌手册是品牌视觉管理的法典，为品牌的日常视觉应用提供标准指引。规范完整的手册能确保品牌视觉在长期使用中保持一致性和专业性。

• 整理所有设计成果，按照标准VI手册结构组织内容，包括封面、目录、品牌理念、基础系统、应用系统、版权声明等板块
• 制作品牌手册PDF版本和印刷版本，设计清晰的版式和阅读路径，确保手册内容完整、结构清晰、易于查阅
• 提供完整的设计源文件和使用指南，包括AI矢量文件、字体文件、色彩配置文件等，为客户后续的品牌维护和扩展提供支持
产出：品牌视觉识别系统手册（含完整手册PDF、源文件、字体包、使用指南）｜ 质量标准：手册结构完整规范，内容详实专业，交付物齐全

Steps

Step 1
Brand Research & Strategic Positioning
The core task of this step is to deeply understand the brand status and target audience, and clarify the design direction and strategic positioning of the brand visual identity system. Brand visual design is not凭空 artistic creation, but visual expression based on brand strategy. Through systematic research and analysis, identify the brand's core value and differentiated positioning, providing clear direction guidance for subsequent design.

• Collect brand history materials, existing visual assets and competitor visual systems, conduct SWOT analysis and visual audit to identify strengths and weaknesses of brand recognition
• Understand target audience's brand perception and aesthetic preferences through user interviews and questionnaires, build user personas, determine design style direction
• Write brand visual strategy brief, clarifying brand tone, core keywords, color direction and design principles as guiding framework for subsequent design
Deliverable: Brand visual strategy brief (including competitor analysis, user personas, style positioning, design keywords) | Quality standard: Clear strategic positioning, executable direction, consistent with brand tone
Step 2
Logo Design & Concept Development
This step is the core creative phase of brand visual identity system design, transitioning from strategic thinking to visual expression, transforming abstract brand concepts into concrete logo graphics. Logo is the core of brand visual identity, requiring uniqueness, recognizability and memorability. Through multi-directional concept exploration and screening, find the visual symbol that best conveys the brand spirit.

• Apply brainstorming and mind mapping for creative development, explore logo design directions from multiple dimensions such as brand name, industry attributes, core values, cultural connotations, generate at least 20 creative sketches
• Evaluate design proposals from four dimensions: recognizability, uniqueness, aesthetics, and scalability, screen 3-5 promising directions for deepening design, produce exquisite vector graphics
• Conduct logo application testing, including minimum size application, black and white reverse effect, different material applications, dynamic effect testing, etc., ensure logo applicability in various scenarios
Deliverable: Logo design proposal (including creative sketches, detailed design drafts, application test drafts, design explanation) | Quality standard: Unique and meaningful creativity, exquisite visual expression, strong applicability
Step 3
Visual Identity System Basic Design
This step expands logo design into a complete basic identity system, establishing standard specifications for brand vision. The basic system is the cornerstone of brand visual identity, including core elements such as logo, standard typography, standard colors, auxiliary graphics and their usage specifications. Systematic basic design ensures brand vision remains unified and professional in any application scenario.

• Design basic element system including logo standard drawing, standard typography design, standard and auxiliary color systems, graphic element system, combination specifications, prohibited specifications, etc., establish a complete visual element library
• Develop detailed usage specifications, including minimum usage size of logo, safe space, color specifications, background requirements, incorrect usage examples, etc., ensure consistency of brand vision
• Design auxiliary graphics and visual texture systems, develop exclusive visual language for the brand, enhance richness and recognition of brand vision, provide more visual materials for application design
Deliverable: Basic identity system specifications (including logo, standard typography, color system, auxiliary graphics, combination specifications) | Quality standard: Complete and standardized system, coordinated and unified elements, strong operability
Step 4
Application Identity System Design
This step implements the basic visual system into specific application scenarios, demonstrating the effect of brand vision in actual use. The application system is the interface between brand vision and the public, covering office supplies, public relations gifts, advertising, environmental wayfinding, digital media and other fields. Complete application design allows clients to intuitively feel the practical value of the brand visual system.

• Design office supplies including business cards, letterhead, envelopes, ID badges, folders, PPT templates, email signatures, etc., establish professional brand image
• Design brand promotion materials including posters, brochures, packaging, social media assets, website banners, etc., demonstrate application effects of the brand visual system on different media
• Conduct spatial environment research and pedestrian flow analysis, sort out wayfinding needs and information hierarchy, develop overall wayfinding system plan including hierarchical structure of primary, secondary, and tertiary wayfinding
Deliverable: Application identity system design (including office supplies, promotional materials, packaging design, environmental wayfinding) | Quality standard: Rich application scenarios, unified visual effect, strong implementability
Step 5
Brand Manual Production & Delivery
This step is the concluding phase of brand VI design, organizing all design results into a systematic brand visual manual and conducting final delivery. The brand manual is the code of brand visual management, providing standard guidelines for daily brand visual application. A standardized and complete manual ensures brand vision maintains consistency and professionalism in long-term use.

• Organize all design results, structure content according to standard VI manual structure, including cover, table of contents, brand philosophy, basic system, application system, copyright statement, etc.
• Produce PDF version and printed version of brand manual, design clear layout and reading path, ensure manual has complete content, clear structure, and is easy to consult
• Provide complete design source files and usage guides, including AI vector files, font files, color configuration files, etc., provide support for client's subsequent brand maintenance and expansion
Deliverable: Brand visual identity system manual (including complete manual PDF, source files, font package, usage guide) | Quality standard: Complete and standardized manual structure, detailed and professional content, complete deliverables
