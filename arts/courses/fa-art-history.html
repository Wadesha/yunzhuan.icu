艺术史

Art History

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学期

本课程是艺术专业的核心理论课程，系统梳理西方与东方艺术发展的历史脉络。课程内容涵盖史前艺术、古代艺术、中世纪、文艺复兴、巴洛克、洛可可、新古典主义、浪漫主义、印象派、现代主义及当代艺术等重要时期，同时介绍中国、日本、印度等东方艺术传统。通过对各时代代表性艺术家、作品与流派的深入分析，帮助学生建立完整的艺术史知识框架。课程强调视觉分析能力与艺术批评方法的训练，培养学生理解艺术作品所处历史语境的能力。

This core theory course systematically examines the historical development of Western and Eastern art. Covering prehistoric, ancient, medieval, Renaissance, Baroque, Rococo, Neoclassical, Romantic, Impressionist, modernist, and contemporary periods, the course also introduces Chinese, Japanese, and Indian artistic traditions. Through in-depth analysis of representative artists, works, and movements, students build a comprehensive art historical framework. The course emphasizes visual analysis and art criticism methods.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：艺术流派比较研究论文

学生需选择两个不同时期或不同文化的艺术流派进行比较研究，撰写一篇约3000字的学术论文。要求学生运用艺术史研究方法，深入分析两个流派的艺术特征、代表艺术家、历史背景与文化意义，并探讨二者之间的关联或差异。论文需引用至少8篇学术文献，包含作品图像分析。学生需提交论文提案，经教师指导后完成终稿，培养独立研究与学术写作能力。

Students select two art movements from different periods or cultures for comparative research, writing an approximately 3000-word academic paper. Using art historical research methods, students analyze the artistic characteristics, representative artists, historical context, and cultural significance of both movements, exploring connections or differences. The paper must cite at least eight scholarly sources and include visual analysis of works.
