绘画基础

Painting Fundamentals

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：素描基础 | 学期：第二学期

本课程是艺术专业的核心绘画课程，系统介绍绘画材料与技法。课程内容涵盖水彩、油画、丙烯等多种媒介的特性与运用方法，包括调色技巧、笔触表现、画面层次构建、光影塑造等核心技能。通过静物、风景、肖像等主题的练习，帮助学生掌握不同画种的基本语言。课程强调个人艺术语言的探索，引导学生在掌握传统技法的基础上发展独特的绘画表达方式，为高阶绘画创作打下坚实基础。

This core painting course introduces painting materials and techniques systematically. Covering watercolor, oil, and acrylic media, topics include color mixing, brushwork, pictorial layering, and light and shadow modeling. Through still life, landscape, and portrait exercises, students master the basic languages of different painting genres. The course emphasizes exploration of personal artistic voice, guiding students to develop unique expressive approaches while building on traditional techniques.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：主题性绘画创作与技法研究

学生需选取一种绘画媒介完成主题性创作，研究材料技法与画面语言，体现个人艺术表达。最终提交创作作品与技法研究笔记。

Students complete a themed painting in a chosen medium, studying material techniques and painterly language to express personal artistry. The artwork and technique study notes are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：创作一组主题绘画作品，比如"城市夜景"系列。你需要先画10张速写收集素材，选择3个最有代表性的场景进行深入创作，尝试不同的技法（比如厚涂、薄涂），最后展出3-5幅完成作品。
步骤 1
创作构思与材料准备
本步骤的核心任务是进行绘画创作的构思和材料准备，为创作奠定思想和物质基础。绘画创作始于观念，好的构思是作品成功的关键。通过深入的主题思考和充分的材料准备，将创作意图转化为清晰的视觉概念，同时准备好创作所需的材料和工具。

• 确定创作主题和构思，进行创意发想和主题探索，明确作品的表达意图和情感基调，撰写创作构思说明和创作计划
• 收集创作素材，通过写生、摄影、临摹等方式收集参考资料，学习大师作品，进行色彩小稿和构图小稿练习，确定创作方向
• 准备绘画材料和工具，包括画布（亚麻布、棉布）、内框、油画颜料（各种颜色）、画笔（不同型号的圆头、平头、扇形笔等）、调色板、媒介剂（松节油、亚麻仁油）等
产出：创作方案与材料准备（含构思说明、素材收集、材料清单）｜ 质量标准：主题明确有内涵，材料准备充分
步骤 2
构图起稿与底色铺设
本步骤是绘画创作的正式开始阶段，进行画布起稿和大色调铺设。构图是画面的骨架，底色是画面的基调，两者共同决定了作品的基本面貌。扎实的起稿和统一的底色能为后续的深入塑造奠定良好的基础。

• 设计构图并起稿，根据创作意图确定画面构图，用素描方式在画布上轻轻起稿，勾勒出物象的基本轮廓和位置关系，检查比例、透视和构图的准确性
• 制作画布底色（Tonning），选择统一的色调（如褐色、灰色、赭石色）做底色，可以是单色底也可以是彩色底，为画面奠定统一的色调基础，同时填充画布纹理
• 铺大色块和基本关系，用大笔刷快速铺设各大色块，建立画面的基本色彩关系和明暗关系，区分前景、中景、远景，拉开空间层次，把握整体色调
产出：构图底色稿（含构图起稿、底色制作、大关系铺设）｜ 质量标准：构图合理，色调统一，大关系明确
步骤 3
形体塑造与色彩表现
本步骤是绘画创作的核心阶段，在大关系的基础上进行形体塑造和色彩表现。绘画不仅要画出物象的外形，更要表现其体积、空间、质感和色彩。通过丰富的色彩层次和塑造手法，使画面具有真实感和艺术感染力。

• 深入塑造形体，从主要物体开始，逐步深入刻画，通过色彩的冷暖、深浅、纯灰变化塑造物体的体积感和空间感，注意形体的转折和过渡
• 表现材质质感，针对不同物体的材质特点（皮肤、丝绸、金属、玻璃、陶瓷、水果等）采用不同的表现手法，利用画笔的笔触、颜料的厚薄、色彩的变化表现质感
• 刻画细节亮点，有选择地刻画画面的精彩细节，如高光、反光、纹理、特征等，使用小笔刷进行精细刻画，注意细节要服从整体，不能破坏整体关系
产出：深入塑造作品（含深入中稿、质感表现、细节刻画）｜ 质量标准：塑造深入充分，质感表现生动，整体与局部统一
步骤 4
整体调整与完成上光
本步骤是绘画创作的收尾阶段，进行最后的整体调整和作品完成，使画面达到完整和谐的状态。最后的整体调整需要从大局出发，敢于取舍和调整，使画面更加完整和有艺术感染力。油画完成后需要上光保护，这是油画的最后一道工序。

• 整体观察和调整，将作品放远观察，检查整体的色彩关系、明暗关系、虚实关系、主次关系，进行最后的调整，包括加强对比、统一色调、突出主体、减弱细节等
• 完善画面细节，对画面进行最后的润色和完善，处理边缘线、调整色彩的微妙变化、增加必要的笔触和肌理，使画面更加生动和富有表现力
• 作品完成与上光，签署姓名和日期，待画面完全干燥后（通常需要数月）进行上光保护，使用上光油（达玛上光油或玛蒂上光油）均匀涂刷，保护画面并增加光泽度
产出：完成作品与创作总结（含油画成品、创作过程记录、创作总结）｜ 质量标准：画面完整和谐，艺术表现力强，作品保存规范

Steps

Step 1
Creative Conception & Material Preparation
The core task of this step is to conduct conception and material preparation for painting creation, laying the ideological and material foundation for creation. Painting creation begins with ideas; a good concept is the key to a successful work. Through in-depth thematic thinking and thorough material preparation, transform creative intentions into clear visual concepts, while preparing materials and tools needed for creation.

• Determine creation theme and conception, conduct creative development and theme exploration, clarify expressive intention and emotional tone of the work, write creative conception statement and creation plan
• Collect creation materials, collect reference materials through sketching, photography, copying, etc., study master works, practice color small sketches and composition small sketches, determine creation direction
• Prepare painting materials and tools, including canvas (linen, cotton), stretcher bars, oil paints (various colors), brushes (different sizes of round, flat, fan brushes), palette, mediums (turpentine, linseed oil), etc.
Deliverable: Creation plan and material preparation (including conception statement, material collection, material list) | Quality standard: Clear and meaningful theme, thorough material preparation
Step 2
Composition Block-in & Underpainting
This step is the official beginning phase of painting creation, conducting canvas block-in and overall tone laying. Composition is the skeleton of the picture, and underpainting is the keynote of the picture—together they determine the basic appearance of the work. Solid block-in and unified underpainting can lay a good foundation for subsequent deep modeling.

• Design composition and block in, determine picture composition according to creative intent, lightly block in on canvas using sketch method, outline basic contours and positional relationships of objects, check accuracy of proportion, perspective and composition
• Make canvas toning, choose a unified tone (such as brown, gray, ochre) for underpainting, can be monochrome or colored ground, lay a unified tonal foundation for the picture, while filling canvas texture
• Lay large color blocks and basic relationships, use large brushes to quickly lay out major color blocks, establish basic color relationship and light-dark relationship of the picture, distinguish foreground, middle ground, background,establish spatial hierarchy, grasp overall tone
Deliverable: Composition underpainting draft (including composition block-in, underpainting, big relationship laying) | Quality standard: Reasonable composition, unified tone, clear big relationships
Step 3
Form Modeling & Color Expression
This step is the core phase of painting creation, conducting form modeling and color expression on the basis of big relationships. Painting not only needs to draw the outer shape of objects but also express their volume, space, texture and color. Through rich color layers and modeling techniques, make the picture realistic and artistically appealing.

• Deeply model forms, starting from main objects, gradually deepen depiction, through color changes in cold/warm, light/dark, pure/gray to sculpt volume sense and spatial sense of objects, pay attention to form transitions and gradients
• Express material texture, adopt different expression techniques for different material characteristics of objects (skin, silk, metal, glass, ceramics, fruits, etc.), use brushstrokes, paint thickness, color changes to express texture
• Depict detail highlights, selectively depict wonderful details of the picture, such as highlights, reflections, textures, features, etc., use small brushes for fine depiction, note that details mustobey the whole and cannotdestroy the overall relationship
Deliverable: In-depth modeling work (including mid-stage draft, texture expression, detail depiction) | Quality standard: Sufficiently deep modeling, vivid texture expression, unified whole and parts
Step 4
Overall Adjustment & Final Varnishing
This step is the concluding phase of painting creation, conducting final overall adjustment and work completion, making the picture reach a complete and harmonious state. Final overall adjustment requires starting from the overall situation, daring to make choices and adjustments, making the picture more complete and artistically appealing. After oil painting is completed, it needs varnish protection, which is the final process of oil painting.

• Overall observation and adjustment, view the work from a distance, check overall color relationship, light-dark relationship, virtual-real relationship, primary-secondary relationship, make final adjustments including strengthening contrast, unifying tone, highlighting subject, weakening details, etc.
• Perfect picture details, perform final polishing and improvement of the picture, handle edge lines, adjust subtle color changes, add necessary brushstrokes and textures, make the picture more vivid and expressive
• Work completion and varnishing, sign name and date, after the picture is completely dry (usually takes several months), apply varnish protection, use varnish (damar varnish or mastic varnish) and apply evenly, protect the picture and increase gloss
Deliverable: Completed work and creation summary (including oil painting finished product, creation process records, creation summary) | Quality standard: Complete and harmonious picture, strong artistic expressiveness, standardized work preservation
