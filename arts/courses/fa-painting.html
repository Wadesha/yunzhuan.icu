绘画基础

Painting Fundamentals

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：素描基础 | 学期：第二学期

本课程是艺术专业的核心绘画课程，系统介绍绘画材料与技法。课程内容涵盖水彩、油画、丙烯等多种媒介的特性与运用方法，包括调色技巧、笔触表现、画面层次构建、光影塑造等核心技能。通过静物、风景、肖像等主题的练习，帮助学生掌握不同画种的基本语言。课程强调个人艺术语言的探索，引导学生在掌握传统技法的基础上发展独特的绘画表达方式，为高阶绘画创作打下坚实基础。

This core painting course introduces painting materials and techniques systematically. Covering watercolor, oil, and acrylic media, topics include color mixing, brushwork, pictorial layering, and light and shadow modeling. Through still life, landscape, and portrait exercises, students master the basic languages of different painting genres. The course emphasizes exploration of personal artistic voice, guiding students to develop unique expressive approaches while building on traditional techniques.
