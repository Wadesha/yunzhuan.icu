设计基础

Design Foundations

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学期

本课程是设计专业的入门核心课程，系统介绍设计的基本概念、原理与方法。课程内容涵盖平面构成、色彩构成、立体构成三大构成体系，以及设计思维、视觉语言、形式美法则等基础理论。通过大量的 hands-on 练习和项目实践，培养学生的观察力、想象力和造型能力，建立对设计的整体认知。课程强调理论与实践结合，引导学生从多个维度理解设计的本质，为后续专业课程学习奠定坚实基础。

This foundational core course introduces the basic concepts, principles, and methods of design. The curriculum covers the three major composition systems: planar composition, color composition, and three-dimensional composition, as well as design thinking, visual language, and formal beauty principles. Through extensive hands-on exercises and projects, students develop observation, imagination, and form-building skills while establishing a holistic understanding of design. The course emphasizes the integration of theory and practice, guiding students to understand the essence of design from multiple dimensions and laying a solid foundation for subsequent specialized courses.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：三大构成综合设计作品集

学生需完成平面、色彩、立体三大构成的综合设计作品，体现形式美法则与设计思维的运用。最终提交作品集与设计说明。

Students complete comprehensive design works in 2D, color, and 3D composition, demonstrating form aesthetics and design thinking. A portfolio with design statements is submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
平面构成基础训练
本步骤的核心任务是学习和掌握平面构成的基本原理和形式法则，训练抽象造型能力和形式美感。平面构成是设计的基础语言，研究点、线、面等基本元素的组织规律和构成方式。通过系统性的基础训练，建立设计的形式感和秩序感，培养视觉思维能力。

• 进行平面构成基础训练，从点、线、面的基本形态入手，练习重复构成、渐变构成、发射构成、对比构成、密集构成、特异构成等基本构成形式
• 学习形式美法则，包括对称与均衡、对比与统一、节奏与韵律、比例与尺度、变化与统一等，理解美的规律和原理
• 分析优秀设计案例中的构成规律，拆解经典设计作品的构图方式和形式语言，从大师作品中学习构成技巧
产出：平面构成习作集（含点线面练习、构成形式练习、形式美分析、习作心得）｜ 质量标准：构成原理理解透彻，画面秩序感强，形式美感突出
步骤 2
色彩构成基础训练
本步骤聚焦于色彩构成的学习和训练，掌握色彩的基本性质和组织规律。色彩是设计中最具表现力的视觉元素，直接影响观者的情感和心理感受。通过系统的色彩练习，建立科学的色彩认知，培养色彩感觉和配色能力。

• 进行色彩构成基础训练，练习色彩三要素（色相、明度、纯度）的变化和组合，包括色相推移、明度推移、纯度推移、色彩混合等基础练习
• 学习色彩对比与调和原理，练习色相对比、明度对比、纯度对比、冷暖对比、补色对比等不同类型的色彩对比，以及各种调和方法
• 进行色彩情感和心理训练，练习不同情感基调的配色表达，如热情、冷静、优雅、活力、科技、传统等不同风格的色彩表现
产出：色彩构成习作集（含色彩要素练习、对比调和练习、色彩情感练习、习作心得）｜ 质量标准：色彩理论掌握扎实，配色和谐美观，色彩表现力强
步骤 3
立体构成基础训练
本步骤进入三维空间的造型训练，学习立体构成的基本原理和方法。立体构成研究三维空间中形体的组合、结构、材质和空间关系，是培养空间思维和立体造型能力的重要途径。通过从二维到三维的拓展，建立完整的设计造型基础。

• 学习立体构成基础理论，包括体量、空间、材质、结构、光影等基本概念，理解三维造型的基本规律和审美原则
• 进行基础立体构成练习，包括线材构成、面材构成、块材构成，练习累积构成、切割构成、组合构成等基本造型方法
• 探索不同材料的表现力，使用卡纸、木板、金属丝、黏土等各种材料进行立体造型，体验材料特性与造型语言的关系
产出：立体构成习作集（含理论学习、立体造型习作、材料实验、创作心得）｜ 质量标准：空间想象力丰富，造型能力强，材料运用有创意
步骤 4
综合构成设计创作
本步骤将三大构成的知识进行综合运用，完成一个完整的设计项目。综合创作是检验学习成果的重要环节，需要将平面、色彩、立体三大构成的知识有机融合，运用形式美法则进行完整的设计表达。通过综合项目实践，建立系统化的设计思维。

• 选择一个具体的设计主题（如主题海报设计、书籍装帧设计、品牌视觉设计等），进行完整的设计项目实践，从调研分析、概念发想到设计执行全过程参与
• 综合运用三大构成知识，在设计中灵活运用平面构成的形式法则、色彩构成的配色技巧、立体构成的空间思维，使设计具有扎实的形式基础
• 进行多方案比较和迭代优化，从概念草图到最终成品，经过多轮筛选和改进，不断提升设计的完成度和品质
产出：综合设计作品集（含主题设计项目、过程稿、最终成品、设计说明）｜ 质量标准：设计概念清晰，构成知识运用灵活，完成度高品质好
步骤 5
作品集整理与学习反思
本步骤是基础设计学习的总结环节，系统整理学习成果，进行深度的学习反思。整理作品集不仅是对学习成果的呈现，更是对学习过程的梳理和回顾。通过反思总结，找出优势与不足，明确后续学习和提升的方向。

• 整理所有习作和设计作品，按照学习进度和类型进行分类编排，设计制作个人作品集，展示学习成果和设计能力
• 撰写学习心得和反思总结，回顾整个学习过程中的收获与体会，分析自己在三大构成各方面的优势和不足
• 制定后续学习计划，明确在设计理论、软件技能、创意能力等方面的提升目标和路径，为专业学习打下更坚实的基础
产出：设计基础作品集（含完整作品集、学习反思、后续学习计划）｜ 质量标准：作品集完整规范，反思深刻有见地，学习计划明确可行

Steps

Step 1
2D Composition Basic Training
The core task of this step is to learn and master the basic principles and formal laws of 2D composition, training abstract modeling ability and formal aesthetic sense. 2D composition is the basic language of design, studying the organizational rules and composition methods of basic elements such as points, lines, and planes. Through systematic basic training, establish a sense of form and order in design, cultivate visual thinking ability.

• Perform 2D composition basic training, starting from basic forms of points, lines, planes, practice repetition, gradation, emission, contrast, density, special composition and other basic composition forms
• Learn formal beauty laws, including symmetry and balance, contrast and unity, rhythm and rhyme, proportion and scale, change and unity, etc., understand laws and principles of beauty
• Analyze composition laws in excellent design cases, deconstruct composition methods and formal language of classic design works, learn composition techniques from master works
Deliverable: 2D composition portfolio (including point/line/plane exercises, composition form exercises, formal beauty analysis, exercise insights) | Quality standard: Thorough understanding of composition principles, strong sense of picture order, outstanding formal beauty
Step 2
Color Composition Basic Training
This step focuses on learning and training of color composition, mastering basic properties and organizational laws of color. Color is the most expressive visual element in design, directly affecting viewer's emotion and psychological feeling. Through systematic color exercises, establish scientific color cognition, cultivate color sense and color matching ability.

• Perform color composition basic training, practice changes and combinations of three elements of color (hue, lightness, purity), including hue progression, value progression, purity progression, color mixing and other basic exercises
• Learn color contrast and harmony principles, practice hue contrast, value contrast, purity contrast, cold-warm contrast, complementary color contrast and different types of color contrast, as well as various harmony methods
• Conduct color emotion and psychology training, practice color matching expression of different emotional tones, such as enthusiasm, calmness, elegance, vitality, technology, tradition and different styles of color expression
Deliverable: Color composition portfolio (including color element exercises, contrast and harmony exercises, color emotion exercises, exercise insights) | Quality standard: Solid mastery of color theory, harmonious and beautiful color matching, strong color expressiveness
Step 3
3D Composition Basic Training
This step enters 3D space modeling training, learning basic principles and methods of three-dimensional composition. 3D composition studies the combination, structure, material and spatial relationship of forms in three-dimensional space, and is an important way to cultivate spatial thinking and 3D modeling ability. Through expansion from 2D to 3D, establish complete design modeling foundation.

• Learn basic theory of 3D composition, including basic concepts such as volume, space, material, structure, light and shadow, understand basic laws and aesthetic principles of 3D modeling
• Perform basic 3D composition exercises, including line material composition, surface material composition, block material composition, practice cumulative composition, cutting composition, combined composition and other basic modeling methods
• Explore expressive power of different materials, use various materials such as cardboard, wood board, metal wire, clay for 3D modeling, experience relationship between material characteristics and modeling language
Deliverable: 3D composition portfolio (including theory study, 3D modeling exercises, material experiments, creative insights) | Quality standard: Rich spatial imagination, strong modeling ability, creative material application
Step 4
Comprehensive Composition Design Creation
This step comprehensively applies knowledge of the three major compositions to complete a complete design project. Comprehensive creation is an important part of testing learning outcomes, requiring organic integration of 2D, color, and 3D composition knowledge, using formal beauty laws for complete design expression. Through comprehensive project practice, establish systematic design thinking.

• Choose a specific design theme (such as thematic poster design, book binding design, brand visual design, etc.), conduct complete design project practice, from research analysis, concept development to design execution full process participation
• Comprehensively apply three major composition knowledge, flexibly use formal laws of 2D composition, color matching skills of color composition, spatial thinking of 3D composition in design, make design have solid formal foundation
• Conduct multi-scheme comparison and iterative optimization, from concept sketches to final product, after multiple rounds of screening and improvement, continuously提升 design completion and quality
Deliverable: Comprehensive design portfolio (including theme design project, process drafts, final product, design explanation) | Quality standard: Clear design concept, flexible application of composition knowledge, high completion and quality
Step 5
Portfolio Organization & Learning Reflection
This step is the summary phase of basic design learning, systematically organizing learning outcomes and conducting in-depth learning reflection. Organizing portfolio is not only presentation of learning outcomes but also sorting and reviewing of learning process. Through reflective summary, identify strengths and weaknesses, clarify direction for subsequent learning and improvement.

• Organize all exercises and design works, categorize and arrange according to learning progress and type, design and produce personal portfolio,demonstrate learning outcomes and design ability
• Write learning insights and reflection summary, review gains and experiences during the entire learning process, analyze own advantages and disadvantages in various aspects of three major compositions
• Develop subsequent learning plan, clarify improvement goals and paths in design theory, software skills, creativity and other aspects, lay a more solid foundation for professional study
Deliverable: Design basics portfolio (including complete portfolio, learning reflection, subsequent learning plan) | Quality standard: Complete and standardized portfolio, profound and insightful reflection, clear and feasible learning plan
