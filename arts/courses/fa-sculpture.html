雕塑基础

Sculpture Fundamentals

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：素描基础 | 学期：第二学期

本课程是艺术专业的雕塑入门课程，系统介绍三维形体塑造的基本理论与实践。课程内容涵盖泥塑、木雕、石雕、金属焊接等多种材料与技法，包括形体构建、空间关系、材质表现、表面处理等核心技能。通过头像泥塑、人体速写、抽象构成等练习，帮助学生建立立体造型思维。课程强调对空间、体量、质感的感知与表达，引导学生探索雕塑语言的可能性，为后续雕塑创作与装置艺术学习奠定基础。

This introductory sculpture course covers the fundamental theories and practices of three-dimensional form-building. Topics include clay modeling, wood carving, stone carving, and metal welding, with focus on form construction, spatial relationships, material expression, and surface treatment. Through portrait clay work, figure sketches, and abstract composition exercises, students develop three-dimensional thinking. The course emphasizes perception and expression of space, volume, and texture.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：雕塑主题创作与材质表现

学生需选择一种雕塑材料完成主题创作，研究形体空间与材质表现，完成立体造型作品。最终提交雕塑作品与创作笔记。

Students complete a themed sculpture in a chosen material, studying form, space, and material expression to create a three-dimensional work. The sculpture and creation notes are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：创作一件人物半身像雕塑，比如用粘土塑造一位老人。你需要先画3张不同角度的结构草图，用铁丝和铝箔搭建内部骨架，分层塑造肌肉和皮肤细节，翻制成石膏或树脂成品，最后拍摄多角度作品照片并撰写创作说明。
步骤 1
主题构思与小稿创作
本步骤的核心任务是确定雕塑创作主题并进行小稿创作，为正式雕塑奠定创意基础。雕塑是三维空间的造型艺术，需要从各个角度考量形体和空间。小稿创作是雕塑创作的重要环节，可以快速验证创意，探索不同的造型方案，为正式创作找到最佳方向。

• 确定创作主题和雕塑类型，选择具象雕塑、抽象雕塑、装置艺术、人物雕塑、景观雕塑等方向，明确创作的主题思想和情感表达，撰写创作构思说明
• 进行小稿创作，使用黏土、油泥、橡皮泥、纸黏土等易塑形材料，制作多个小比例的设计稿，尝试不同的造型、动态、构图、空间关系，探索各种可能性
• 评估和选择方案，从创意性、可行性、表现力、工艺难度等多个维度评估各小稿方案，选择最优秀的1-2个方案进行深化，确定最终的创作方案和尺寸
产出：创作构思与小稿（含主题说明、创意小稿、方案选定）｜ 质量标准：创意新颖，造型生动，方案有深度
步骤 2
骨架搭建与大形塑造
本步骤是雕塑创作的骨架搭建阶段，建立作品的内部支撑和基本形态。骨架是雕塑的内在结构，决定了作品的基本形态和稳定性。大形塑造则是在骨架基础上堆出基本体量，确立作品的整体轮廓和动势。

• 设计和制作骨架，根据雕塑的造型、尺寸、材料，设计合理的支撑结构，使用钢筋、铁丝、木板等材料制作骨架，确保骨架牢固、重心稳定、形态准确
• 上大泥/堆大体量，使用雕塑泥、石膏、泡沫等材料，在骨架上进行大形塑造，堆出基本的体量和轮廓，重点抓整体的动势、比例、重心、空间关系
• 调整大形和整体关系，从正面、侧面、背面各个角度观察，反复调整大的形体关系，检查动态是否自然、比例是否准确、体量是否均衡，确保大形准确和整体协调
产出：骨架大形稿（含骨架制作、大形塑造、整体调整）｜ 质量标准：骨架牢固，大形准确，动势生动
步骤 3
深入塑造与细部刻画
本步骤是雕塑创作的核心深入阶段，在大形基础上进行深入塑造和细节刻画。深入塑造是体现雕塑水平的关键环节，需要从整体到局部，再从局部回到整体，反复深入。通过精细的刻画，表现形体的结构、肌肉、纹理、质感等细节，使作品具有生命感和感染力。

• 进行分层深入塑造，从大的形体块面到小的结构起伏，逐层深入，研究解剖结构和形体关系，通过加减法塑造形体的起伏、转折、穿插，表现形体的体积感和重量感
• 刻画细节和质感，精心表现对象的细节特征，如人物的面部表情、肌肉结构、衣纹褶皱、毛发皮肤纹理等，使用不同的工具和手法表现不同的材质质感
• 处理空间和虚实关系，注意雕塑的空间感和透视关系，处理实体与虚体的关系，考虑不同光线、不同角度下的视觉效果，使作品具有丰富的空间层次
产出：深入塑造稿（含结构塑造、细节刻画、空间处理）｜ 质量标准：塑造深入，结构准确，细节精彩
步骤 4
翻制模具与材质转换
本步骤是将泥塑转化为永久材质的技术环节，通过翻模工艺将雕塑作品转换为石膏、树脂、青铜、玻璃钢等耐久材料。翻模是雕塑创作中重要的技术环节，需要严谨的工艺和细致的操作。材质的选择和处理也直接影响作品的最终呈现效果。

• 制作模具，根据雕塑的形态和材质选择合适的翻模方法（硅胶模、石膏模、蜡模等），分块制作模具，做好脱模剂和分模线处理，确保模具完整准确
• 进行材料浇筑和转换，选择最终呈现的材质（石膏、树脂、玻璃钢、青铜等），按照工艺流程进行浇筑、固化、脱模，得到材质转换后的作品毛坯
• 进行作品修整和表面处理，对翻制出来的作品进行修补、打磨、拼接，处理模具线和瑕疵，进行表面效果处理（着色、做旧、抛光、亚光等），达到理想的材质效果
产出：翻制成品（含模具制作、材质浇筑、表面处理）｜ 质量标准：翻制精准，材质效果好，表面处理精致
步骤 5
作品完成与创作总结
本步骤是雕塑创作的收尾阶段，完成作品的最后加工和底座制作，进行作品的整体呈现。底座是雕塑作品的重要组成部分，好的底座能提升作品的展示效果。同时，对整个创作过程进行总结反思，积累经验，为今后的创作打下基础。

• 制作底座和展示台，根据作品的风格、尺寸、材质，设计制作相匹配的底座，考虑底座的材质、形状、高度、颜色与作品的协调性，提升整体展示效果
• 进行最后的整体调整和完善，检查作品的各个角度和细节，进行最后的修整和打磨，确保作品的完整性和精美度，签署艺术家签名和创作年代
• 整理创作过程和总结，保存好创作过程中的小稿、照片、模具、工具等，撰写创作总结和心得体会，记录创作的思考、过程和收获
产出：雕塑成品与创作档案（含最终作品、底座、创作记录、总结报告）｜ 质量标准：作品完整精美，底座协调统一，总结深刻

Steps

Step 1
Theme Conception & Maquette Creation
The core task of this step is to determine sculpture creation theme and conduct maquette creation, laying creative foundation for formal sculpture. Sculpture is the plastic art of three-dimensional space, requiring consideration of form and space from all angles. Maquette creation is an important part of sculpture creation, which can quickly verify creativity, explore different modeling schemes, and find the best direction for formal creation.

• Determine creation theme and sculpture type, choose directions such as figurative sculpture, abstract sculpture, installation art, portrait sculpture, landscape sculpture, etc., clarify thematic ideas and emotional expression of creation, write creative conception statement
• Conduct maquette creation, use easily moldable materials such as clay, plasticine, oil-based clay, paper clay, etc., make multiple small-scale design drafts, try different forms, dynamics, compositions, spatial relationships, explore various possibilities
• Evaluate and select schemes, evaluate each maquette scheme from multiple dimensions such as creativity, feasibility, expressiveness, technical difficulty, select the best 1-2 schemes for deepening, determine final creation scheme and size
Deliverable: Creative conception and maquettes (including theme description, creative maquettes, scheme selection) | Quality standard: Novel creativity, vivid modeling, profound scheme
Step 2
Armature Building & Large Form Modeling
This step is the armature building phase of sculpture creation, establishing internal support and basic form of the work. Armature is the internal structure of sculpture, determining the basic form and stability of the work. Large form modeling is piling up basic volume on the basis of armature, establishing overall outline and movement of the work.

• Design and build armature, according to sculpture form, size, material, design reasonable support structure, use steel bars, wire, wood boards and other materials to make armature, ensure armature is firm, center of gravity is stable, form is accurate
• Apply big clay/heap up large volume, use sculpting clay, plaster, foam and other materials, conduct large form modeling on armature, pile out basic volume and outline, focus on grasping overall movement, proportion, center of gravity, spatial relationship
• Adjust large form and overall relationship, observe from front, side, back all angles, repeatedly adjust large form relationships, check if movement is natural, proportion is accurate, volume is balanced, ensure large form accuracy and overall coordination
Deliverable: Armature and large form draft (including armature making, large form modeling, overall adjustment) | Quality standard: Firm armature, accurate large form, vivid movement
Step 3
Deep Modeling & Detail Carving
This step is the core deepening phase of sculpture creation, conducting deep modeling and detail carving on the basis of large form. Deep modeling is the key link reflecting sculpture level, requiring from whole to parts, then from parts back to whole, repeatedly deepening. Through fine depiction, express details such as structure, muscle, texture, etc. of the form, making the work have sense of life and appeal.

• Conduct layered deep modeling, from large form planes to small structural undulations, deepen layer by layer, study anatomical structure and form relationships, through additive and subtractive methods sculpt form undulations, transitions, intersections, express volume sense and weight sense of form
• Carve details and texture, carefully depict detail characteristics of the subject, such as character facial expressions, muscle structure, clothing folds, hair and skin textures, etc., use different tools and techniques to express different material textures
• Handle space and virtual-real relationship, pay attention to spatial sense and perspective relationship of sculpture, handle relationship between solid and void, consider visual effects under different lighting and different angles, make work have rich spatial layers
Deliverable: Deep modeling draft (including structural modeling, detail carving, space processing) | Quality standard: In-depth modeling, accurate structure, wonderful details
Step 4
Mold Making & Material Conversion
This step is the technicalphase of transforming clay sculpture into permanent material, through mold makingcraftsmanship converting sculpture works into durable materials such as plaster, resin, bronze, fiberglass, etc. Mold making is an important technicalphase in sculpture creation, requiring rigorous craftsmanship and meticulous operation. Material selection and processing also directly affect the final presentation effect of the work.

• Make molds, choose appropriate mold making methods (silicone mold, plaster mold, wax mold, etc.) according to sculpture form and material, make molds in sections, properly handle release agent and parting lines, ensure molds are complete and accurate
• Perform material casting and conversion, choose final presentation material (plaster, resin, fiberglass, bronze, etc.), follow process flow for casting, curing, demolding, obtain work blank after material conversion
• Conduct work trimming and surface treatment, repair, polish, assemble the cast work, handle mold lines and defects, perform surface effect treatment (coloring, antiquing, polishing, matte, etc.), achieve ideal material effect
Deliverable: Cast finished product (including mold making, material casting, surface treatment) | Quality standard: Accurate casting, good material effect, exquisite surface treatment
Step 5
Work Completion & Creation Summary
This step is the concluding phase of sculpture creation, completing final processing of the work and base making, conducting overall presentation of the work. Base is an important component of sculpture works; a good base can enhance the display effect of the work. At the same time, summarize and reflect on the entire creation process, accumulate experience, lay foundation for future creations.

• Make base and display stand, according to work style, size, material, design and make matching base, consider coordination of base material, shape, height, color with the work, enhance overall display effect
• Conduct final overall adjustment and improvement, check all angles and details of the work, perform final trimming and polishing, ensure completeness and exquisiteness of the work, sign artist signature and creation year
• Organize creation process and summary, properly preserve maquettes, photos, molds, tools, etc. during creation process, write creation summary and experience reflections, record creative thinking, process and gains
Deliverable: Sculpture finished product and creation archive (including final work, base, creation records, summary report) | Quality standard: Complete and exquisite work, coordinated and unified base, profound summary
