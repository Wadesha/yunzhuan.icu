艺术批评

Art Criticism

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第三学期

本课程是视觉艺术专业的理论核心课程，培养学生的艺术鉴赏能力与批评写作能力。课程内容涵盖艺术批评的历史演变、主要批评流派与方法、形式分析、图像学解读、精神分析批评、女性主义批评、后现代批评理论等。通过对经典艺术作品与当代艺术实践的案例研读，帮助学生建立系统的艺术批评话语体系。课程强调理论与实践结合，引导学生从描述、分析、解释到判断，逐步形成独立的艺术判断能力与批判性思维，为艺术创作提供理论支撑与思想深度。

This core theory course develops students' art appreciation and critical writing abilities. Content covers the historical evolution of art criticism, major critical schools and methods, formal analysis, iconographic interpretation, psychoanalytic criticism, feminist criticism, and postmodern critical theory. Through case studies of classic artworks and contemporary art practices, students build a systematic critical discourse system. The course emphasizes combining theory with practice, guiding students from description and analysis to interpretation and judgment.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：当代艺术家个案批评研究

学生需选择一位在世的当代视觉艺术家，撰写一篇不少于3000字的艺术批评论文。要求运用至少两种课程所学的批评方法，对该艺术家的代表作品进行深入的形式分析与意义阐释。论文需包含艺术家背景介绍、作品形式分析、主题意义解读、文化语境关联、个人批评判断等部分。需引用至少5篇学术文献，并附作品图像清单。需提交论文大纲、初稿与终稿三个阶段，展示从选题到成稿的完整批评写作过程。

Students select a living contemporary visual artist and write an art criticism essay of at least 3000 words. Applying at least two critical methods studied in the course, students conduct in-depth formal analysis and meaning interpretation of the artist's representative works. The essay includes artist background, formal analysis of works, thematic meaning interpretation, cultural context connection, and personal critical judgment, citing at least five academic sources with an image list.
