平面设计

Graphic Design

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：设计基础 | 学期：第二学期

本课程是设计专业的核心实践课程，系统讲授平面设计的理论知识与专业技能。课程内容涵盖海报设计、包装设计、书籍装帧、标志设计、广告设计等主要平面设计领域，同时介绍视觉传达原理、信息层级构建、网格系统设计等方法论。通过真实项目模拟和案例分析，学生将掌握 Adobe Illustrator、Photoshop、InDesign 等专业软件的使用，培养解决视觉传播问题的能力。课程注重创意思维与技术实现的结合，为学生成为专业平面设计师奠定基础。

This core practice course systematically teaches the theoretical knowledge and professional skills of graphic design. The curriculum covers major graphic design areas including poster design, packaging design, book design, logo design, and advertising design, while introducing methodologies such as visual communication principles, information hierarchy, and grid system design. Through real project simulations and case studies, students master professional software including Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign, and develop the ability to solve visual communication problems. The course emphasizes the integration of creative thinking and technical implementation, laying the foundation for students to become professional graphic designers.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：品牌视觉识别系统设计

学生需为一个虚拟品牌设计完整的视觉识别系统，涵盖标志、色彩字体规范及海报包装等应用设计。最终提交VI手册与设计成果。

Students design a complete visual identity system for a fictional brand, covering logo, color and type guidelines, and applications such as posters and packaging. A VI manual and design deliverables are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：为一个真实品牌做视觉识别系统设计，比如一个新开的精品咖啡店。你需要调研同类品牌的视觉风格，设计Logo（至少3个方案）、确定主色调和辅助色、选择字体、设计名片和菜单的应用效果，最后做成VI手册。
步骤 1
项目调研与品牌分析
本步骤的核心任务是深入了解品牌背景和设计需求，为平面设计项目奠定策略基础。平面设计不是纯艺术创作，而是解决特定传播问题的视觉方案。通过系统性的调研和分析，准确把握设计目标、受众特征和品牌调性，确保设计方向正确有效。

• 收集品牌资料，包括品牌历史、企业文化、产品服务、目标市场、竞品情况等，进行全面的品牌分析和SWOT分析
• 明确设计需求和目标，包括设计用途、传播场景、目标受众、期望效果、时间预算等，撰写设计简报（Design Brief）
• 进行竞品视觉分析和参考案例收集，研究同类设计的表现手法和视觉风格，找出差异化的设计机会点
产出：设计调研简报（含品牌分析、需求梳理、竞品研究、参考案例）｜ 质量标准：调研全面深入，需求把握准确，方向清晰明确
步骤 2
创意概念与视觉探索
本步骤是平面设计的创意发想阶段，从策略思考转向视觉表达，探索多种设计方向和视觉可能性。好的创意是设计成功的关键，需要打破常规思维，探索独特而有效的视觉解决方案。通过多方向的概念探索和草图实验，找到最能传达设计目标的视觉语言。

• 运用头脑风暴、思维导图、联想发想等创意方法，围绕设计主题和核心信息，生成多个创意方向和概念点子
• 进行视觉风格探索，从排版、色彩、图形、字体、材质等多个维度实验不同的视觉表现，制作大量的设计草图和风格稿
• 从创意性、有效性、独特性、可执行性等维度评估设计方案，筛选出3-5个有发展潜力的方向进行深化设计
产出：创意概念稿（含创意草图、风格探索稿、设计概念说明）｜ 质量标准：创意丰富多样，视觉探索充分，概念有新意
步骤 3
设计深化与视觉精修
本步骤将选定的设计方向进行深化和精修，打造高质量的设计成品。设计深化需要对每一个细节进行精心打磨，包括版式构图、色彩搭配、字体选择、图形处理、层级关系等。精益求精的态度是专业设计的体现。

• 精细化版式设计，运用栅格系统（Grid System）建立秩序感，处理图文关系、信息层级、留白空间，确保视觉平衡和阅读流畅
• 优化色彩和字体系统，确定主色调和辅助色搭配，选择合适的字体组合（标题字体、正文字体），建立统一的视觉规范
• 精修图形和图像元素，进行图像处理、图标绘制、插画设计等，确保所有视觉元素的质量和风格统一性
产出：设计深化稿（含精细化设计稿、版式规范、色彩字体系统）｜ 质量标准：设计精致专业，细节处理到位，视觉品质高
步骤 4
应用延展与系统规范
本步骤将核心设计扩展到不同的应用场景和尺寸规格，建立完整的设计系统。平面设计往往需要适配多种媒介和尺寸，从海报、宣传册到社交媒体、网页Banner等。系统化的设计规范能确保不同应用场景下的视觉一致性和品牌统一性。

• 进行多尺寸多格式的设计延展，包括不同比例、不同尺寸、不同媒介的适配设计，确保设计在各种规格下都有良好表现
• 建立设计规范和使用指南，包括Logo使用规范、色彩规范、字体规范、版式规范、元素组合规范等，确保设计的一致性
• 设计配套物料和衍生应用，如名片、信纸、PPT模板、社交媒体模板等，形成完整的设计物料系统
产出：设计系统与应用规范（含多尺寸设计稿、设计规范手册、配套物料设计）｜ 质量标准：应用场景丰富，系统规范完整，视觉高度统一
步骤 5
输出制作与交付整理
本步骤是设计项目的收官环节，进行最终的输出制作和文件整理，确保设计能够准确落地。设计输出需要考虑不同媒介的技术要求，如印刷、屏幕显示、喷绘等。规范完整的文件交付是专业设计师的基本素养。

• 进行印前检查和输出准备，检查文件分辨率、色彩模式、出血位、角线、拼版等，确保文件符合印刷要求
• 输出不同格式的设计文件，包括源文件（AI/PSD/INDD）、预览文件（PDF/JPG/PNG）、印刷文件（CMYK PDF）、网页用图（RGB 72dpi）等
• 整理设计交付包，按照规范的文件结构组织所有设计文件，附上设计说明和使用指南，方便客户理解和使用
产出：设计交付成品（含设计源文件、各格式输出文件、设计说明文档）｜ 质量标准：文件规范完整，输出准确无误，交付专业有序

Steps

Step 1
Project Research & Brand Analysis
The core task of this step is to deeply understand brand background and design requirements, laying strategic foundation for graphic design projects. Graphic design is not pure artistic creation, but visual solution to specific communication problems. Through systematic research and analysis, accurately grasp design goals, audience characteristics and brand tone, ensuring correct and effective design direction.

• Collect brand materials including brand history, corporate culture, products and services, target market, competitor situation, etc., conduct comprehensive brand analysis and SWOT analysis
• Clarify design requirements and goals, including design purpose, communication scenarios, target audience, expected effects, timeline and budget, write Design Brief
• Conduct competitor visual analysis and reference case collection, study expression techniques and visual styles of similar designs, find differentiated design opportunities
Deliverable: Design research brief (including brand analysis, requirement sorting, competitor research, reference cases) | Quality standard: Comprehensive and in-depth research, accurate requirement grasp, clear and definite direction
Step 2
Creative Concept & Visual Exploration
This step is the creative development phase of graphic design, transitioning from strategic thinking to visual expression, exploring multiple design directions and visual possibilities. Good creativity is the key to successful design, requiring breaking conventional thinking, exploring unique and effective visual solutions. Through multi-directional concept exploration and sketch experimentation, find the visual language that best conveys design goals.

• Use creative methods such as brainstorming, mind mapping, associative thinking,around design theme and core message, generate multiple creative directions and concept ideas
• Conduct visual style exploration, experiment with different visual expressions from dimensions of layout, color, graphics, typography, materials, etc., producenumerous design sketches and style drafts
• Evaluate design proposals from dimensions of creativity, effectiveness, uniqueness, executability, etc., screen 3-5 promising directions for deepened design
Deliverable: Creative concept drafts (including creative sketches, style exploration drafts, design concept explanation) | Quality standard: Rich and diverse creativity, sufficient visual exploration, original concepts
Step 3
Design Deepening & Visual Refinement
This step deepens and refines the selected design direction, creating high-quality design final products. Design deepening requires careful polishing of every detail, including layout composition, color matching, font selection, graphic processing, hierarchical relationships, etc. The attitude of striving for excellence is the embodiment of professional design.

• Refine typography design, use Grid System to establish sense of order, handle image-text relationships, information hierarchy, white space, ensure visual balance and reading fluency
• Optimize color and font system, determine primary and auxiliary color matching, select appropriate font combinations (headline font, body font), establish unified visual specifications
• Refine graphic and image elements, perform image processing, icon drawing, illustration design, etc., ensure quality and style consistency of all visual elements
Deliverable: Detailed design drafts (including refined design drafts, layout specifications, color and font system) | Quality standard: Exquisite and professional design,precise detail processing, high visual quality
Step 4
Application Extension & System Specifications
This step extends core design to different application scenarios and size specifications, establishing a complete design system. Graphic design often needs to adapt to multiple media and sizes, from posters and brochures to social media and web banners. Systematic design specifications ensure visual consistency and brand unity across different application scenarios.

• Perform design extension for multiple sizes and formats, including adaptation designs of different proportions, different sizes, different media, ensure design performs well in various specifications
• Establish design specifications and usage guidelines, including logo usage specifications, color specifications, font specifications, layout specifications, element combination specifications, etc., ensure design consistency
• Design supporting materials and derivative applications such as business cards, letterhead, PPT templates, social media templates, etc., form complete design material system
Deliverable: Design system and application specifications (including multi-size design drafts, design specification manual, supporting material design) | Quality standard: Rich application scenarios, complete system specifications, highly unified vision
Step 5
Output Production & Delivery Organization
This step is the concluding phase of the design project, performing final output production and file organization, ensuring design can be accurately implemented. Design output needs to consider technical requirements of different media, such as printing, screen display, large format printing, etc. Standardized and complete file delivery is the basic literacy of professional designers.

• Perform pre-press inspection and output preparation, check file resolution, color mode, bleed, crop marks, imposition, etc., ensure files meet printing requirements
• Output design files in different formats, including source files (AI/PSD/INDD), preview files (PDF/JPG/PNG), printing files (CMYK PDF), web images (RGB 72dpi), etc.
• Organize design delivery package, organize all design files according to standardized file structure, attach design description and usage guide,facilitate client understanding and use
Deliverable: Design delivery final product (including design source files, various format output files, design explanation document) | Quality standard: Standardized and complete files, accurate and error-free output, professional and orderly delivery
