平面设计

Graphic Design

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：设计基础 | 学期：第二学期

本课程是设计专业的核心实践课程，系统讲授平面设计的理论知识与专业技能。课程内容涵盖海报设计、包装设计、书籍装帧、标志设计、广告设计等主要平面设计领域，同时介绍视觉传达原理、信息层级构建、网格系统设计等方法论。通过真实项目模拟和案例分析，学生将掌握 Adobe Illustrator、Photoshop、InDesign 等专业软件的使用，培养解决视觉传播问题的能力。课程注重创意思维与技术实现的结合，为学生成为专业平面设计师奠定基础。

This core practice course systematically teaches the theoretical knowledge and professional skills of graphic design. The curriculum covers major graphic design areas including poster design, packaging design, book design, logo design, and advertising design, while introducing methodologies such as visual communication principles, information hierarchy, and grid system design. Through real project simulations and case studies, students master professional software including Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign, and develop the ability to solve visual communication problems. The course emphasizes the integration of creative thinking and technical implementation, laying the foundation for students to become professional graphic designers.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：品牌视觉识别系统设计

学生需为一个虚拟品牌设计完整的视觉识别系统，涵盖标志、色彩字体规范及海报包装等应用设计。最终提交VI手册与设计成果。

Students design a complete visual identity system for a fictional brand, covering logo, color and type guidelines, and applications such as posters and packaging. A VI manual and design deliverables are submitted.
