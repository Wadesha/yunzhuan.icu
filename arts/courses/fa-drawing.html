素描基础

Drawing Fundamentals

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学期

本课程是艺术专业的核心基础课程，系统培养学生的观察能力与手绘表现技巧。课程内容涵盖线条运用、明暗关系、透视原理、构图法则、质感表现等基础要素。通过静物素描、石膏像素描、风景素描等循序渐进的练习，帮助学生建立扎实的造型能力。课程强调眼手协调训练，引导学生从客观观察走向主观表达，为后续绘画、雕塑等专业方向奠定坚实的视觉基础。

This foundational core course develops students' observational skills and hand-drawing techniques. Topics include line work, value relationships, perspective principles, composition rules, and texture rendering. Through progressive exercises in still life, plaster cast, and landscape drawing, students build solid representational abilities. The course emphasizes eye-hand coordination, guiding students from objective observation to subjective expression, establishing a strong visual foundation.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：素描主题创作与综合技法作品集

学生需完成一组主题素描作品，综合运用明暗透视与质感表现技法，体现从观察到表达的造型能力。最终提交作品集与习作心得。

Students complete a set of themed drawings integrating light, perspective, and texture techniques, demonstrating observational and expressive drawing ability. A portfolio and practice notes are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：完成一组素描创作，比如画室一角的全因素素描。你需要先画5张结构素描理解形体关系，再画3张明暗小稿确定光影基调，然后完成1张完整的长期素描作业（对开纸），最后用相机拍摄并做简单的后期调整，提交原作和电子档。
步骤 1
观察训练与素材收集
本步骤的核心任务是进行系统的观察训练并收集创作素材，为素描创作奠定造型基础。素描是造型艺术的基础，而观察是素描的灵魂。通过科学的观察方法训练，培养正确的观察习惯和敏锐的视觉感知能力，同时收集丰富的创作素材。

• 进行观察方法训练，学习整体观察法、比较观察法、几何归纳法，练习看形不看物、看负形、看关系，摆脱概念化的绘画习惯，建立科学的观察体系
• 进行素描写生练习，包括静物写生、石膏像写生、人像写生、人体写生、风景写生等，练习构图、造型、明暗、结构、空间、质感等基础造型能力
• 收集创作素材，通过速写、摄影、文字记录等方式，记录生活中有感的场景、人物、物件，建立个人素材库，为主题创作积累素材
产出：素描习作与素材库（含观察训练笔记、素描写生习作、创作素材集）｜ 质量标准：观察方法科学，造型能力扎实，素材收集丰富
步骤 2
主题确定与构图设计
本步骤聚焦于确定素描创作主题并进行构图设计，解决"画什么"和"怎么布局"的问题。好的素描创作不仅要有扎实的技法，更要有动人的主题和巧妙的构图。通过深入的主题思考和多方案的构图探索，找到最有表现力的创作方案。

• 确定创作主题，从个人经历、社会生活、文学作品、自然景观中寻找灵感，确定创作的主题、情感基调和思想内涵，撰写创作构思说明
• 进行构图探索，绘制大量缩略图（Thumbnail），尝试不同的构图方式（对称式、三角构图、S形构图、对角线构图、框架构图等），研究画面的分割、节奏、平衡、视觉引导
• 确定最终构图方案，选择2-3个有潜力的构图进行放大绘制，比较各方案的视觉效果和主题表达，确定最终的构图、视角和画面比例
产出：创作构思与构图稿（含主题说明、构图探索稿、最终构图）｜ 质量标准：主题有深度，构图有创意，视觉效果好
步骤 3
素描起稿与结构搭建
本步骤是素描创作的正式开始阶段，从起稿到建立画面的大关系。起稿和结构搭建是素描的骨架，决定了作品的整体框架和基本格局。准确的造型和严谨的结构是好素描的基础，需要用理性的分析和判断来建立画面的基本秩序。

• 轻线起稿定位，用长直线概括大的形体和位置，确定构图的基本框架，检查画面的比例、位置、动势，确保大的形体关系准确
• 进行结构分析，运用几何形体归纳法，将复杂的形体分解为基本几何体（立方体、球体、圆柱体、圆锥体等），理解形体的内部结构和穿插关系
• 建立明暗大关系，铺大体的明暗色调，区分受光和背光两大系统，建立画面的黑、白、灰三大面和五大调子的整体关系，拉开明暗层次
产出：素描结构稿（含起稿线稿、结构分析、大关系铺色）｜ 质量标准：造型准确，结构严谨，大关系明确
步骤 4
深入塑造与质感表现
本步骤是素描创作的核心深入阶段，在大关系的基础上进行细致的刻画和质感表现。深入塑造是素描水平高低的关键体现，需要精湛的技法和敏锐的观察。通过层次丰富的明暗过渡和精准的质感表现，使画面具有真实感和感染力。

• 从整体到局部深入塑造，先画主要物体和视觉中心，再逐步深入次要部分，通过层层叠加的调子，塑造形体的体积感、空间感和重量感
• 表现不同材质的质感，针对皮肤、毛发、布料、金属、玻璃、陶瓷、木材等不同材质，采用不同的排线方式、笔触处理和明暗对比，表现各自的质感特点
• 运用不同的素描技法，如排线法、涂抹法、擦笔法、刮画法、留白法等，结合使用不同硬度的铅笔、炭笔、橡皮、纸笔等工具，丰富素描的表现语言
产出：素描深入稿（含深入塑造、质感表现、技法探索）｜ 质量标准：塑造深入，质感生动，层次丰富
步骤 5
整体调整与作品完成
本步骤是素描创作的收官环节，进行最后的整体调整和画面完善。整体调整需要退远看画面，从大处着眼，检查整体关系，进行必要的加强和减弱，使画面更加完整和有艺术感染力。最后的调整往往能大幅提升作品的整体水准。

• 进行整体观察和调整，将作品放远观察，检查整体的明暗关系、虚实关系、主次关系、空间关系，加强对比、突出主体、减弱次要部分，使画面更加整体
• 处理边缘线和细节，精心处理物体的轮廓线和边缘虚实，调整关键部位的细节，使画面既整体又有精彩的细节亮点，做到"整而不空，细而不乱"
• 完成作品并整理，签署姓名和日期，对作品进行妥善的保存和装裱，撰写创作心得和反思，总结本次创作的经验教训
产出：素描完成作品（含最终作品、装裱方案、创作反思）｜ 质量标准：整体协调，细节精彩，完成度高

Steps

Step 1
Observation Training & Material Collection
The core task of this step is to conduct systematic observation training and collect creative materials, laying modeling foundation for sketch creation. Sketching is the foundation of plastic arts, and observation is the soul of sketching. Through scientific observation method training, cultivate correct observation habits and keen visual perception ability, while collecting rich creative materials.

• Conduct observation method training, learn overall observation method, comparative observation method, geometric induction method, practice seeing shapes instead of objects, seeing negative shapes, seeing relationships, break away from conceptualized painting habits, establish scientific observation system
• Perform sketching from life exercises, including still life sketching, plaster cast sketching, portrait sketching, human body sketching, landscape sketching, etc., practice basic modeling abilities such as composition, form, light and shade, structure, space, texture
• Collect creative materials, record touching scenes, characters, objects in life through sketching, photography, text recording, etc., build personal material library, accumulate materials for thematic creation
Deliverable: Sketch exercises and material library (including observation training notes, life sketch exercises, creative material collection) | Quality standard: Scientific observation methods, solid modeling ability, rich material collection
Step 2
Theme Determination & Composition Design
This step focuses on determining sketch creation theme and conducting composition design, solving the problems of "what to draw" and "how to lay out." A good sketch creation requires not only solid techniques but also touching themes and clever composition. Through in-depth thematic thinking and multi-scheme composition exploration, find the most expressive creative proposal.

• Determine creation theme, find inspiration from personal experience, social life, literary works, natural landscapes, determine theme, emotional tone and ideological connotation of creation, write creative conception statement
• Conduct composition exploration, drawnumerous Thumbnails, try different composition methods (symmetrical, triangular composition, S-shaped composition, diagonal composition, frame composition, etc.), study picture division, rhythm, balance, visual guidance
• Determine final composition scheme, choose 2-3 promising compositions for enlarged drawing, compare visual effects and theme expression of each proposal, determine final composition, perspective and picture proportion
Deliverable: Creative conception and composition drafts (including theme description, composition exploration drafts, final composition) | Quality standard: Profound theme, creative composition, good visual effect
Step 3
Sketch Block-in & Structure Building
This step is the official beginning phase of sketch creation, from block-in to establishing overall relationships of the picture. Block-in and structure building are the skeleton of sketching, determining the overall framework and basic pattern of the work. Accurate modeling and rigorous structure are the foundation of a good sketch, requiring rational analysis and judgment to establish the basic order of the picture.

• Light line block-in and positioning, use long straight lines to generalize large forms and positions, determine basic framework of composition, check proportion, position, movement of the picture, ensure large form relationships are accurate
• Conduct structural analysis, apply geometric form induction method, decompose complex forms into basic geometric bodies (cube, sphere, cylinder, cone, etc.), understand internal structure and intersection relationships of forms
• Establish light and shade big relationships, lay overall tone, distinguish light-receiving and back-light two major systems, establish overall relationship of black, white, gray three major surfaces and five major tones of the picture,establish light and shade levels
Deliverable: Sketch structure draft (including block-in line drawing, structural analysis, big relationship lay-in) | Quality standard: Accurate modeling, rigorous structure, clear big relationships
Step 4
Deep Modeling & Texture Expression
This step is the core deepening phase of sketch creation, conducting detailed depiction and texture expression on the basis of big relationships. Deep modeling is the key reflection of sketching level, requiring exquisite techniques and keen observation. Through richly layered light-dark transitions and precise texture expression, make the picture realistic and appealing.

• Deepen modeling from whole to parts, first draw main objects and visual center, then gradually deepen secondary parts, through层层叠加 tones, sculpt volume sense, spatial sense and weight sense of forms
• Express texture of different materials, for different materials such as skin, hair, fabric, metal, glass, ceramic, wood, adopt different hatching methods, brush stroke processing and light-dark contrast, express respective texture characteristics
• Use different sketching techniques, such as hatching, smudging, stump work, scraping, reserving white space, etc., combine use of pencils of different hardness, charcoal, erasers, stumps and other tools, enrich expressive language of sketching
Deliverable: Detailed sketch draft (including deep modeling, texture expression, technique exploration) | Quality standard: In-depth modeling, vivid texture, rich layers
Step 5
Overall Adjustment & Work Completion
This step is the concluding phase of sketch creation, conducting final overall adjustment and picture improvement. Overall adjustment requires stepping back to look at the picture, focusing on the big picture, checking overall relationships, making necessary strengthening and weakening, making the picture more complete and artistically appealing. Final adjustment can often greatly improve the overall level of the work.

• Conduct overall observation and adjustment, observe the work from a distance, check overall light-dark relationship, virtual-real relationship, primary-secondary relationship, spatial relationship, strengthen contrast, highlight subject, weaken secondary parts, make the picture more unified
• Handle edge lines and details, carefully handle contour lines and edge virtuality of objects, adjust details of key parts, make the picture both unified and with wonderful detail highlights, achieving "whole but not empty, detailed but not chaotic"
• Complete the work and organize, sign name and date, properly preserve and mount the work, write creative insights and reflection, summarize experience and lessons from this creation
Deliverable: Completed sketch work (including final work, mounting plan, creative reflection) | Quality standard: Overall coordination, wonderful details, high completion
