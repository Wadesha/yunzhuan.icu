广告学

Advertising

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：传播学导论 | 学期：春季

本课程全面介绍广告行业的理论与实践，涵盖广告发展历程、行业结构、运作流程和创意策略。学生将学习消费者行为分析、市场细分、品牌定位、广告策划、创意表现、媒介策略、效果评估等核心内容，了解电视、广播、平面、户外、数字等各类媒体的广告特点。课程结合经典案例和行业最新动态，培养学生的广告创意能力和策略思维，为从事广告相关工作奠定基础。

This comprehensive course introduces advertising theory and practice, covering industry history, structure, operational processes, and creative strategy. Students study consumer behavior analysis, market segmentation, brand positioning, advertising planning, creative execution, media strategy, and effectiveness measurement across TV, radio, print, outdoor, and digital media. Classic cases and industry trends develop creative abilities and strategic thinking for advertising careers.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：品牌广告全案策划与创意执行

学生需为一个真实或虚拟品牌策划完整的广告全案，涵盖市场调研、目标人群画像、创意概念发想、跨媒介广告执行和效果评估方案。最终提交完整的广告策划书与创意作品集。

Students plan a complete advertising campaign for a real or fictional brand, covering market research, target audience profiling, creative concept development, cross-media execution, and effectiveness evaluation. A full campaign proposal and creative portfolio are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：为一个真实品牌策划整合营销传播方案，比如一款新上市的国产运动饮料。你需要完成竞品分析和目标人群调研，提出核心创意概念并设计3个 campaign 方案，规划媒介投放组合（含预算分配），制作PPT提案并模拟客户提案演讲。
步骤 1
市场调研与竞品分析
本步骤的核心任务是全面了解品牌所处的市场环境和竞争格局，为后续广告策略制定提供数据支撑。通过系统性的市场调研，掌握行业发展趋势、消费者需求变化和竞品广告策略，找出品牌的市场机会点和差异化竞争优势。调研结果将直接影响广告策略的方向和创意概念的发想，是整个广告策划的基础环节。

• 运用PEST分析法和波特五力模型分析宏观市场环境与行业竞争格局，收集行业报告和市场数据，评估市场规模与增长潜力
• 选取3-5个主要竞品，系统收集其近一年的广告campaigns，从创意策略、媒介组合、传播节奏、效果表现四个维度进行对比分析
• 设计消费者调研问卷，结合深度访谈法了解目标人群的媒介接触习惯、购买决策路径和品牌认知现状，形成用户画像
产出：市场调研报告（含行业分析、竞品分析、消费者分析三个部分，附数据图表和调研问卷）｜ 质量标准：数据来源可靠，分析深入，机会点与问题诊断清晰准确
步骤 2
目标人群画像与品牌定位
本步骤聚焦于明确广告的传播对象和品牌的传播定位，解决"对谁说"和"说什么"的核心问题。通过深入的消费者洞察，建立精准的目标人群画像，确定品牌在消费者心智中应占据的独特位置。清晰的人群定位和品牌定位是创意发想的指南针，确保后续所有广告创意都围绕核心目标展开。

• 运用人口统计学、心理学和行为学三维度构建用户画像，包括年龄性别、生活方式、价值观、消费习惯、媒介偏好等细分维度，制作用户旅程图
• 运用定位理论和品牌心智模型，结合SWOT分析结果，确定品牌的核心价值主张和差异化卖点，撰写品牌定位声明
• 设计传播金字塔，明确核心目标人群、次级目标人群和影响者人群，针对不同层级制定差异化的传播策略和信息内容
产出：定位策略文档（含用户画像、品牌定位声明、传播金字塔、核心信息屋）｜ 质量标准：人群画像具体可感，定位差异化明显，核心信息精准有力
步骤 3
创意概念发想与创意简报
本步骤是广告策划的创意核心，从策略思考转向创意表达，将抽象的品牌定位转化为可感知的创意概念。通过系统性的创意发想方法，生成多个创意方向，筛选出最具传播力和执行力的核心概念。创意简报是连接策略与执行的桥梁，为后续具体广告作品的制作提供明确的指导方向。

• 运用头脑风暴、思维导图、六顶思考帽等创意发想工具，围绕核心创意主题生成至少20个创意点子，进行创意收敛和筛选
• 从创意相关性、原创性、冲击力、可执行性四个维度评估创意方案，确定1-2个核心创意概念，撰写创意概念说明
• 制作正式的创意简报（Creative Brief），包含项目背景、营销目标、目标受众、核心信息、创意调性、媒介考量、时间预算等要素
产出：创意简报与概念稿（含创意简报文档、创意概念视觉稿、创意发想过程记录）｜ 质量标准：创意概念独特新颖，与品牌定位一致，可执行性强
步骤 4
跨媒介创意执行与物料制作
本步骤将创意概念落地为具体的广告作品，根据不同媒介的特性进行创意适配和物料制作。每种媒介都有其独特的传播语言和技术要求，需要针对性地进行创意转化和执行。高质量的广告物料是确保传播效果的关键，需要在创意表现力和技术规范性上达到专业标准。

• 电视/视频广告：撰写分镜脚本，运用镜头语言设计叙事节奏，制作故事板（Storyboard），明确画面、声音、字幕的协同关系
• 平面广告：运用Adobe Photoshop和Illustrator进行视觉设计，遵循版式设计原理，确保主标题、副标题、正文、Logo的信息层级清晰
• 数字广告：针对社交媒体、信息流、开屏等不同格式设计适配版本，运用动态图形和交互设计增强用户参与感，确保各尺寸规格准确
产出：广告创意作品集（含视频广告片、平面广告稿、数字广告素材、社交媒体内容）｜ 质量标准：视觉表现力强，创意执行到位，各媒介物料规格准确、风格统一
步骤 5
媒介策划与效果评估方案
本步骤是广告策划的收官环节，制定完整的媒介投放计划和效果评估体系，确保广告信息准确触达目标人群并可衡量。科学的媒介组合能最大化传播效率，完善的评估机制能验证广告效果并为后续优化提供依据。本步骤输出完整的广告全案策划书，作为项目执行的指导文件。

• 运用GRP、CPM、CPC、CPA等媒介评估指标，结合目标人群的媒介接触习惯，设计线上线下整合的媒介组合策略和投放排期表
• 建立KPI评估体系，从品牌认知、品牌偏好、购买意愿、销售转化四个层面设定量化指标，设计前测、中测、后测的评估方案
• 整合所有内容形成完整的广告策划书，包含执行摘要、市场分析、策略规划、创意表现、媒介计划、效果评估、预算分配、时间进度等模块
产出：广告全案策划书（含完整策划书PPT、媒介排期表、预算表、效果评估方案）｜ 质量标准：策划逻辑严密，媒介策略科学，预算分配合理，评估体系可操作

Steps

Step 1
Market Research & Competitor Analysis
The core task of this step is to comprehensively understand the market environment and competitive landscape of the brand, providing data support for subsequent advertising strategy development. Through systematic market research, grasp industry development trends, consumer demand changes, and competitor advertising strategies, identifying brand market opportunities and differentiated competitive advantages. Research results directly influence advertising strategy direction and creative concept development, forming the foundation of the entire advertising plan.

• Apply PEST analysis and Porter's Five Forces model to analyze macro market environment and industry competition, collect industry reports and market data, assess market size and growth potential
• Select 3-5 main competitors, systematically collect their advertising campaigns from the past year, conduct comparative analysis from four dimensions: creative strategy, media mix, communication rhythm, and performance
• Design consumer survey questionnaires combined with in-depth interviews to understand target audience media habits, purchase decision paths, and brand perception status, forming user personas
Deliverable: Market research report (including industry analysis, competitor analysis, and consumer analysis, with data charts and survey questionnaires) | Quality standard: Reliable data sources, in-depth analysis, clear and accurate identification of opportunities and problems
Step 2
Target Audience Profiling & Brand Positioning
This step focuses on defining the communication target of advertising and the brand's communication positioning, solving the core questions of "who to say to" and "what to say." Through in-depth consumer insights, establish accurate target audience personas and determine the unique position the brand should occupy in consumers' minds. Clear audience and brand positioning serve as the compass for creative development, ensuring all subsequent advertising creativity revolves around core objectives.

• Build user personas using demographic, psychological, and behavioral dimensions, including age/gender, lifestyle, values, consumption habits, and media preferences, create user journey maps
• Apply positioning theory and brand mental model combined with SWOT analysis results, determine the brand's core value proposition and differentiated selling points, write brand positioning statement
• Design communication pyramid, clarify core target audience, secondary target audience, and influencer groups, develop differentiated communication strategies and messaging for each level
Deliverable: Positioning strategy document (including user personas, brand positioning statement, communication pyramid, core message house) | Quality standard: Specific and relatable audience personas, clearly differentiated positioning, precise and powerful core messaging
Step 3
Creative Concept Development & Creative Brief
This step is the creative core of advertising planning, transitioning from strategic thinking to creative expression, transforming abstract brand positioning into perceptible creative concepts. Through systematic creative development methods, generate multiple creative directions and select the core concept with the highest communicative power and executability. The creative brief bridges strategy and execution, providing clear guidance for subsequent specific advertising production.

• Use brainstorming, mind mapping, and Six Thinking Hats as creative development tools, generate at least 20 creative ideas around the core creative theme, conduct creative convergence and screening
• Evaluate creative proposals from four dimensions: relevance, originality, impact, and executability, determine 1-2 core creative concepts, write creative concept statements
• Produce formal Creative Brief including project background, marketing objectives, target audience, core message, creative tone, media considerations, timeline, and budget
Deliverable: Creative brief and concept boards (including creative brief document, creative concept visuals, creative development process records) | Quality standard: Unique and original creative concept, consistent with brand positioning, strong executability
Step 4
Cross-Media Creative Execution & Production
This step translates creative concepts into concrete advertising works, conducting creative adaptation and production according to the characteristics of different media. Each medium has its unique communication language and technical requirements, requiring targeted creative transformation and execution. High-quality advertising materials are key to ensuring communication effectiveness, requiring professional standards in both creative expressiveness and technical specification.

• TV/Video advertising: Write shooting scripts, use cinematic language to design narrative rhythm, produce storyboards, clarify the synergy between visuals, sound, and subtitles
• Print advertising: Use Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator for visual design, follow layout design principles, ensure clear information hierarchy of headline, subhead, body copy, and logo
• Digital advertising: Design adapted versions for social media, feed ads, splash screens and other formats, use motion graphics and interactive design to enhance user engagement, ensure accurate size specifications
Deliverable: Advertising creative portfolio (including video commercials, print ads, digital advertising materials, social media content) | Quality standard: Strong visual expressiveness, excellent creative execution, accurate specifications and unified style across media materials
Step 5
Media Planning & Effectiveness Evaluation
This step is the concluding phase of advertising planning, developing a complete media placement plan and evaluation system to ensure advertising messages accurately reach the target audience and are measurable. Scientific media mix maximizes communication efficiency, and a comprehensive evaluation mechanism verifies advertising effectiveness and provides a basis for subsequent optimization. This step outputs the complete advertising campaign proposal as the guiding document for project execution.

• Use media evaluation metrics such as GRP, CPM, CPC, and CPA, combined with target audience media habits, design integrated online-offline media mix strategy and placement schedule
• Establish KPI evaluation system, set quantitative indicators from four levels: brand awareness, brand preference, purchase intention, and sales conversion, design pre-test, mid-test, and post-test evaluation plans
• Integrate all content into a complete advertising proposal including executive summary, market analysis, strategic planning, creative execution, media plan, effectiveness evaluation, budget allocation, and timeline
Deliverable: Complete advertising campaign proposal (including full PPT proposal, media schedule, budget table, effectiveness evaluation plan) | Quality standard: Rigorous planning logic, scientific media strategy, reasonable budget allocation, actionable evaluation system
