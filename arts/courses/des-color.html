色彩设计

Color Design

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：设计基础 | 学期：第二学期

本课程专注于设计领域的色彩应用理论与实践方法。课程内容涵盖色彩科学基础、色彩心理学、色彩文化学、色彩系统与色立体、配色原理与方法、色彩调和与对比等专业知识，特别注重色彩在品牌设计、界面设计、环境设计等不同设计领域的具体应用。通过色彩实验、配色练习和项目实践，学生将掌握科学的色彩分析方法和创造性的色彩运用能力，学会运用色彩传递信息、营造氛围、塑造品牌形象，为各设计方向的专业实践提供色彩策略支撑。

This course focuses on color application theory and practical methods in the design field. The curriculum covers professional knowledge including color science fundamentals, color psychology, color culture, color systems and color solids, color matching principles and methods, and color harmony and contrast, with special emphasis on the specific application of color in different design fields such as brand design, interface design, and environmental design. Through color experiments, color matching exercises, and project practice, students master scientific color analysis methods and creative color application abilities, learning to use color to convey information, create atmosphere, and shape brand image, providing color strategy support for professional practice in various design directions.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：品牌色彩策略与配色系统设计

学生需为一个品牌或空间项目设计完整的色彩策略，包括色彩调研、主辅色体系、配色规范和应用场景示范。最终提交色彩设计手册。

Students design a complete color strategy for a brand or spatial project, including color research, primary-secondary color system, palette guidelines, and application scenarios. A color design manual is submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：为一个具体的应用场景设计配色系统，比如一款冥想类App的界面色彩。你需要分析目标用户的情感需求，确定主色、辅助色和强调色的比例关系，制作色板并测试在不同屏幕上的显示效果，最后输出一套包含使用规范的配色指南。
步骤 1
色彩理论学习与调研
本步骤的核心任务是系统学习色彩理论知识并进行广泛的调研，为后续色彩设计奠定理论和素材基础。色彩设计不是凭感觉的主观选择，而是基于色彩科学和心理学的专业决策。通过深入的理论学习和广泛的灵感收集，建立科学的色彩认知和丰富的色彩审美储备。

• 学习和梳理色彩基础理论，包括色彩三要素（色相、明度、纯度）、色彩混合原理、色彩对比与调和、色彩系统（CMYK/RGB/Pantone）等核心知识
• 进行广泛的色彩灵感收集，从自然、艺术、建筑、时尚、摄影等领域汲取灵感，建立灵感板（Mood Board），分类整理不同风格的色彩组合
• 研究色彩心理学和色彩文化学，了解不同颜色的情感联想、象征意义和文化差异，分析目标受众的色彩偏好和心理感受
产出：色彩调研与灵感库（含理论学习笔记、灵感板、色彩心理分析、竞品色彩研究）｜ 质量标准：理论基础扎实，灵感收集丰富，分析有深度
步骤 2
色彩策略与风格定位
本步骤聚焦于确定品牌/项目的色彩策略和风格定位，解决"用什么颜色"和"为什么用这些颜色"的核心问题。色彩策略是品牌视觉战略的重要组成部分，直接影响品牌的情感表达和受众认知。基于品牌调性和目标人群，制定清晰的色彩定位和策略方向。

• 分析品牌/项目的定位和目标人群，结合色彩心理学确定色彩的情感基调（如科技感、亲和力、高端感、活力感等），撰写色彩策略简报
• 研究行业色彩惯例和竞品色彩方案，找出差异化的色彩机会点，同时考虑品牌识别度和行业适用性的平衡
• 确定色彩风格方向，收集参考案例，建立色彩风格的视觉参考系，明确设计的审美标准和效果预期
产出：色彩策略定位文档（含品牌分析、目标人群色彩偏好、色彩策略、风格参考）｜ 质量标准：策略定位准确，色彩方向清晰，与品牌调性匹配度高
步骤 3
配色方案设计与优化
本步骤是色彩设计的核心创作环节，运用专业配色方法设计完整的色彩系统。好的配色不仅要美观，更要具有功能性和系统性。通过科学的配色方法和反复的调试优化，创造出既有审美价值又有实用价值的色彩方案。

• 运用专业配色方法进行配色设计，包括同类色配色、邻近色配色、对比色配色、互补色配色、分裂互补色配色、三角色配色等经典配色模式
• 设计色彩系统结构，包括主色、辅助色、点缀色的选择和比例分配，建立色彩的层级关系和使用规范，确保系统的完整性和可扩展性
• 进行色彩调和与优化，调整色彩的明度、纯度、色相，处理色彩之间的和谐关系，运用色彩对比理论确保信息传达的清晰度和可读性
产出：色彩系统设计方案（含主辅色系统、配色方案、色彩比例、设计说明）｜ 质量标准：配色和谐美观，系统结构完整，富有创意和表现力
步骤 4
色彩应用测试与验证
本步骤通过实际应用测试验证色彩方案的可行性和效果，确保色彩在不同场景下都能达到预期效果。色彩的表现受到媒介、材质、光线、尺寸等多种因素影响，理论上完美的配色在实际应用中可能出现各种问题。通过多场景测试和迭代优化，确保色彩方案的实用性和可靠性。

• 进行色彩应用测试，将配色方案应用到典型的设计场景中，如界面设计、平面设计、产品设计、空间设计等，检查色彩在不同场景下的表现效果
• 进行可访问性测试，包括色盲友好测试（红绿色盲、蓝黄色盲）、对比度测试（WCAG标准），确保信息的无障碍传达
• 测试色彩在不同媒介上的表现差异，包括屏幕显示（RGB）、印刷（CMYK）、材质应用等，进行色彩校正和跨媒介色彩管理
产出：色彩应用测试报告（含场景应用测试、可访问性测试、跨媒介测试、优化建议）｜ 质量标准：测试全面细致，色彩表现稳定，满足各种应用场景要求
步骤 5
色彩系统手册制作
本步骤是色彩设计的收官环节，将所有色彩设计成果整理成系统的色彩设计手册。色彩手册是色彩使用的标准指南，确保团队在使用色彩时有据可依，保持品牌色彩的一致性和专业性。规范完整的手册是专业色彩设计的重要交付物。

• 制作色彩系统手册，系统介绍色彩设计理念、色彩情感定位、色彩系统构成、色彩使用规范等内容，以图文并茂的形式呈现完整的设计逻辑
• 制定详细的色彩使用规范，包括色彩数值（Hex/RGB/CMYK/Pantone）、使用比例、搭配原则、禁用情况、常见问题解答等实用内容
• 提供色彩工具和资源，包括色板文件（ASE/ACO）、设计软件颜色配置、快速参考卡片等，方便日常工作使用
产出：色彩设计系统手册（含完整手册、色板文件、使用规范、工具资源）｜ 质量标准：手册结构完整清晰，规范详细实用，交付物齐全专业

Steps

Step 1
Color Theory Study & Research
The core task of this step is to systematically learn color theory knowledge and conduct extensive research, laying theoretical and material foundations for subsequent color design. Color design is not subjective choice based on feeling, but professional decision-making based on color science and psychology. Through in-depth theoretical study and extensive inspiration collection, establish scientific color cognition and rich color aesthetic reserves.

• Study and sort out basic color theory, including three elements of color (hue, value, saturation), color mixing principles, color contrast and harmony, color systems (CMYK/RGB/Pantone) and other core knowledge
• Conduct extensive color inspiration collection, draw inspiration from nature, art, architecture, fashion, photography and other fields, build mood boards, categorize and organize color combinations of different styles
• Study color psychology and color culturology, understand emotional associations, symbolic meanings and cultural differences of different colors, analyze target audience's color preferences and psychological feelings
Deliverable: Color research and inspiration library (including theory study notes, mood boards, color psychology analysis, competitor color research) | Quality standard: Solid theoretical foundation, rich inspiration collection, in-depth analysis
Step 2
Color Strategy & Style Positioning
This step focuses on determining the color strategy and style positioning of the brand/project, solving the core questions of "what colors to use" and "why use these colors." Color strategy is an important component of brand visual strategy, directly affecting the brand's emotional expression and audience cognition. Based on brand tone and target audience, develop clear color positioning and strategic direction.

• Analyze brand/project positioning and target audience,结合 color psychology determine emotional tone of colors (such as tech sense, affinity, high-end sense, vitality, etc.), write color strategy brief
• Study industry color conventions and competitor color schemes, find differentiated color opportunities, while considering balance between brand recognition and industry applicability
• Determine color style direction, collect reference cases, establish visual reference system for color style, clarify aesthetic standards and expected effects of design
Deliverable: Color strategy positioning document (including brand analysis, target audience color preferences, color strategy, style references) | Quality standard: Accurate strategic positioning, clear color direction, high matching degree with brand tone
Step 3
Color Scheme Design & Optimization
This step is the core creative phase of color design, designing a complete color system using professional color matching methods. Good color matching should not only be aesthetic but also functional and systematic. Through scientific color matching methods and repeated debugging and optimization, create color schemes with both aesthetic value and practical value.

• Apply professional color matching methods for palette design, including monochromatic, analogous, contrast, complementary, split complementary, triadic and other classic color matching modes
• Design color system structure, including selection and proportion allocation of primary colors, auxiliary colors, accent colors, establish hierarchical relationship and usage specifications of colors, ensure system integrity and scalability
• Perform color harmony and optimization, adjust lightness, purity, hue of colors, handle harmonious relationship between colors, use color contrast theory to ensure clarity and readability of information transmission
Deliverable: Color system design proposal (including primary and auxiliary color system, color scheme, color proportion, design explanation) | Quality standard: Harmonious and beautiful color matching, complete system structure, creative and expressive
Step 4
Color Application Testing & Verification
This step verifies feasibility and effect of the color scheme through practical application testing, ensuring colors achieve expected effects in different scenarios. Color performance is affected by multiple factors such as medium, material, light, size, etc. Theoretically perfect color matching may have various problems in practical application. Through multi-scenario testing and iterative optimization, ensure practicality and reliability of the color scheme.

• Conduct color application testing, apply color scheme to typical design scenarios such as interface design, graphic design, product design, spatial design, etc., check performance of colors in different scenarios
• Perform accessibility testing, including color blindness friendliness test (red-green color blindness, blue-yellow color blindness), contrast test (WCAG standard), ensure barrier-free information transmission
• Test performance differences of colors on different media, including screen display (RGB), printing (CMYK), material application, etc., perform color correction and cross-media color management
Deliverable: Color application test report (including scenario application test, accessibility test, cross-media test, optimization suggestions) | Quality standard: Comprehensive and detailed testing, stable color performance, meets requirements of various application scenarios
Step 5
Color System Manual Production
This step is the concluding phase of color design, organizing all color design results into a systematic color design manual. The color manual is the standard guide for color usage, ensuring the team has rules to follow when using colors, maintaining consistency and professionalism of brand colors. A standardized and complete manual is an important deliverable of professional color design.

• Create color system manual, systematically introducing color design concepts, color emotional positioning, color system composition, color usage guidelines, etc., presenting complete design logic in an illustrated format
• Develop detailed color usage specifications, including color values (Hex/RGB/CMYK/Pantone), usage proportion, matching principles, prohibited situations, FAQ and other practical content
• Provide color tools and resources, including swatch files (ASE/ACO), design software color configuration, quick reference cards, etc., convenient for daily work use
Deliverable: Color design system manual (including complete manual, swatch files, usage specifications, tool resources) | Quality standard: Complete and clear manual structure, detailed and practical specifications, complete and professional deliverables
