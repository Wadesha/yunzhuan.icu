数字艺术

Digital Art

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：素描基础 | 学期：第三学期

本课程是艺术专业的数字媒体艺术入门课程，探索数字技术在艺术创作中的应用。课程内容涵盖数字绘画、图像处理、矢量图形、三维建模基础等核心技能，主要使用Photoshop、Illustrator、Procreate等主流软件。通过从传统艺术向数字媒介的转换练习，帮助学生掌握数字艺术的基本创作方法。课程强调技术与艺术的结合，引导学生理解数字媒介的独特性，探索数字艺术在当代艺术语境中的表达方式与可能性，为后续数字媒体、交互艺术等方向奠定基础。

This introductory digital media art course explores the application of digital technology in artistic creation. Topics include digital painting, image processing, vector graphics, and basic 3D modeling, using software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and Procreate. Through exercises transitioning from traditional art to digital media, students master basic digital art creation methods. The course emphasizes the integration of technology and art, guiding students to understand the uniqueness of digital media.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：数字艺术主题创作作品集

学生需运用数字媒介完成一组主题数字艺术作品，探索数字绘画与图像合成的表现语言。最终提交作品集与创作说明。

Students create a set of themed digital art works using digital media, exploring the expressive language of digital painting and image compositing. A portfolio and artist statement are submitted.
