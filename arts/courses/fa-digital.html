数字艺术

Digital Art

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：素描基础 | 学期：第三学期

本课程是艺术专业的数字媒体艺术入门课程，探索数字技术在艺术创作中的应用。课程内容涵盖数字绘画、图像处理、矢量图形、三维建模基础等核心技能，主要使用Photoshop、Illustrator、Procreate等主流软件。通过从传统艺术向数字媒介的转换练习，帮助学生掌握数字艺术的基本创作方法。课程强调技术与艺术的结合，引导学生理解数字媒介的独特性，探索数字艺术在当代艺术语境中的表达方式与可能性，为后续数字媒体、交互艺术等方向奠定基础。

This introductory digital media art course explores the application of digital technology in artistic creation. Topics include digital painting, image processing, vector graphics, and basic 3D modeling, using software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and Procreate. Through exercises transitioning from traditional art to digital media, students master basic digital art creation methods. The course emphasizes the integration of technology and art, guiding students to understand the uniqueness of digital media.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：数字艺术主题创作作品集

学生需运用数字媒介完成一组主题数字艺术作品，探索数字绘画与图像合成的表现语言。最终提交作品集与创作说明。

Students create a set of themed digital art works using digital media, exploring the expressive language of digital painting and image compositing. A portfolio and artist statement are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：用数字工具创作一组概念插画，比如为科幻小说《三体》设计场景插图。你需要用Procreate或Photoshop绘制5张草图方案，选择2张进行深入刻画（包含光影、材质、氛围），最后排版成册并输出印刷级文件，提交电子作品集。
步骤 1
创作主题确定与灵感收集
本步骤的核心任务是确定数字艺术创作主题并收集创作灵感，为数字艺术作品奠定创意基础。数字艺术是技术与艺术的结合，好的主题需要兼顾艺术表达和技术可行性。通过广泛的灵感收集和主题探索，找到既有思想内涵又有视觉冲击力的创作方向。

• 确定数字艺术创作主题，从个人经历、社会议题、自然现象、未来想象、文化传统等领域寻找灵感，确定创作的核心理念和情感表达
• 收集视觉参考和灵感素材，建立灵感板（Mood Board），包括摄影作品、绘画作品、数字艺术、3D渲染等不同类型的视觉参考，分析其视觉语言和表现手法
• 研究数字艺术的表现形式和技术手段，了解数字绘画、图像合成、3D建模、生成艺术、动效设计等不同方向的特点，选择适合的创作媒介和技术
产出：主题方案与灵感库（含主题说明、灵感素材集、技术方向选择）｜ 质量标准：主题有内涵，灵感收集丰富，技术方向明确
步骤 2
概念设计与视觉探索
本步骤是数字艺术的创意发想阶段，将抽象的主题概念转化为具体的视觉方案。数字艺术的创作自由度很高，但也需要清晰的概念和方向。通过多方案的探索和比较，找到最能传达创作意图的视觉语言和表现风格。

• 进行概念草图和视觉探索，使用快速速写、缩略图、拼贴等方式，快速尝试多种构图、视角、光影、色彩方案，探索各种视觉可能性
• 确定视觉风格和艺术语言，是写实风、插画风、赛博朋克风、低多边形风、水墨风还是其他风格，明确整体的艺术调性和审美标准
• 制作概念设计稿，选择2-3个有潜力的方向进行深化，绘制较为完整的概念效果图，比较各方案的优缺点，确定最终的创作方向
产出：概念设计稿（含草图探索、风格确定、概念效果图）｜ 质量标准：创意丰富多样，视觉风格鲜明，概念表达清晰
步骤 3
数字创作与技法实现
本步骤是数字艺术创作的核心制作环节，使用数字工具和技法完成艺术作品的创作。数字艺术有其独特的技法体系，需要熟练掌握软件工具和创作技法。通过专业的技术实现，将创意概念转化为高质量的数字艺术作品。

• 选择合适的数字创作工具，如Photoshop、Procreate、Illustrator、Blender、Clip Studio Paint等，根据创作风格和需求选择最适合的软件和硬件（数位板、iPad等）
• 进行正式的数字创作，从线稿/打底开始，逐步深入塑造，运用图层、笔刷、蒙版、混合模式等数字绘画技法，处理构图、造型、色彩、光影、质感等要素
• 探索特殊数字技法，如照片合成、纹理叠加、滤镜效果、3D辅助、生成式AI辅助等，利用数字媒介的独特优势，创造传统手绘无法实现的视觉效果
产出：数字艺术创作稿（含创作过程文件、分层源文件、技法探索记录）｜ 质量标准：技法娴熟，画面完整，数字特色鲜明
步骤 4
细节深化与效果优化
本步骤聚焦于作品的细节打磨和整体效果优化，提升作品的完成度和品质感。数字艺术的优势在于可以无限次修改和调整，通过精细的后期处理和优化，使作品达到更高的艺术水准。精益求精的态度是专业数字艺术家的必备素养。

• 深入刻画细节，重点刻画视觉中心和主体物，精心处理纹理、光影、材质等细节，增强画面的真实感和感染力，同时注意整体与局部的关系
• 进行整体色彩和光影调整，使用调整图层（曲线、色阶、色相饱和度、色彩平衡等）统一画面色调，增强光影效果，营造氛围和情绪
• 添加特殊效果和后期处理，如光晕、景深、颗粒感、畸变、锐化等效果，增强画面的视觉冲击力和艺术感，同时检查整体画面的协调性
产出：优化完成稿（含细节深化稿、色彩调整、特效处理）｜ 质量标准：细节精致，整体协调，画面品质高
步骤 5
作品输出与创作反思
本步骤是数字艺术创作的收尾环节，输出最终作品并进行创作反思。数字作品的输出需要考虑不同应用场景的格式和质量要求。同时，通过创作反思总结经验，不断提升自己的数字艺术创作能力。

• 输出不同规格的作品文件，包括高分辨率印刷版（TIFF/PNG 300dpi）、网络展示版（JPG/PNG 72dpi）、社交媒体版等不同格式和尺寸，确保输出质量
• 整理创作过程文件，包括草图、过程稿、分层源文件、参考素材等，建立完整的创作档案，方便后续修改和作品集制作
• 撰写创作说明和反思，阐述作品的创作理念、表现手法、技术特点，总结创作过程中的收获和不足，反思可以改进的地方
产出：数字艺术作品与档案（含最终作品、源文件、创作说明、反思总结）｜ 质量标准：作品完成度高，文件规范，反思深刻

Steps

Step 1
Theme Determination & Inspiration Collection
The core task of this step is to determine digital art creation theme and collect creative inspiration, laying creative foundation for digital art works. Digital art is the combination of technology and art; a good theme needs to balance artistic expression and technical feasibility. Through extensive inspiration collection and theme exploration, find a creative direction with both ideological connotation and visual impact.

• Determine digital art creation theme, find inspiration from personal experience, social issues, natural phenomena, future imagination, cultural traditions and other fields, clarify core concept and emotional expression of creation
• Collect visual references and inspiration materials, build Mood Board, including different types of visual references such as photography, paintings, digital art, 3D renders, analyze their visual language and expression techniques
• Study expression forms and technical means of digital art, understand characteristics of different directions such as digital painting, image synthesis, 3D modeling, generative art, motion design, choose suitable creative media and technology
Deliverable: Theme proposal and inspiration library (including theme description, inspiration material collection, technical direction selection) | Quality standard: Meaningful theme, rich inspiration collection, clear technical direction
Step 2
Concept Design & Visual Exploration
This step is the creative development phase of digital art, transforming abstract thematic concepts into concrete visual proposals. Digital art has high creative freedom, but also requires clear concepts and directions. Through multi-scheme exploration and comparison, find the visual language and expression style that best conveys creative intent.

• Conduct concept sketches and visual exploration, use quick sketching, thumbnails, collage and other methods, quickly try various composition, perspective, lighting, color schemes, explore various visual possibilities
• Determine visual style and artistic language, whether it is realistic style, illustration style, cyberpunk style, low poly style, ink wash style or other styles, clarify overall artistic tone and aesthetic standards
• Produce concept design drafts, choose 2-3 promising directions for deepening, draw more complete concept renderings, compare advantages and disadvantages of each proposal, determine final creative direction
Deliverable: Concept design drafts (including sketch exploration, style determination, concept renderings) | Quality standard: Rich and diverse creativity, distinctive visual style, clear concept expression
Step 3
Digital Creation & Technique Implementation
This step is the core production phase of digital art creation, completing creation of art works using digital tools and techniques. Digital art has its unique technique system, requiring proficient mastery of software tools and creative techniques. Through professional technical implementation, transform creative concepts into high-quality digital art works.

• Choose appropriate digital creation tools, such as Photoshop, Procreate, Illustrator, Blender, Clip Studio Paint, etc., select the most suitable software and hardware (drawing tablet, iPad, etc.) according to creative style and needs
• Conduct formal digital creation, starting from line art/underpainting, gradually deepen shaping, use digital painting techniques such as layers, brushes, masks, blending modes, handle elements such as composition, form, color, light and shadow, texture
• Explore special digital techniques, such as photo compositing, texture overlay, filter effects, 3D assistance, generative AI assistance, etc., leverage unique advantages of digital media to create visual effects impossible with traditional hand-drawing
Deliverable: Digital art creation drafts (including creation process files, layered source files, technique exploration records) | Quality standard: Skilled techniques, complete picture, distinctive digital features
Step 4
Detail Deepening & Effect Optimization
This step focuses on detail polishing and overall effect optimization of the work, improving completion and quality sense of the work. The advantage of digital art is that it can be modified and adjusted infinitely; through meticulous post-processing and optimization, make the work reach a higher artistic level. The attitude of striving for excellence is an essential quality of professional digital artists.

• Deeply depict details, focus on portraying visual center and subject, carefully handle details such as texture, light and shadow, material, enhance realism and appeal of the picture, while paying attention to relationship between whole and parts
• Perform overall color and light adjustment, use adjustment layers (curves, levels, hue/saturation, color balance, etc.) to unify picture tone, enhance lighting effects, create atmosphere and emotion
• Add special effects and post-processing, such as glow, depth of field, grain, distortion, sharpening and other effects, enhance visual impact and artistic sense of the picture, while checking coordination of the overall picture
Deliverable: Optimized final draft (including detail deepening draft, color adjustment, special effect processing) | Quality standard: Exquisite details, overall coordination, high picture quality
Step 5
Work Output & Creative Reflection
This step is the concluding phase of digital art creation, outputting final work and conducting creative reflection. Output of digital works needs to consider format and quality requirements of different application scenarios. At the same time, summarize experience through creative reflection, continuously improve one's digital art creation ability.

• Output work files of different specifications, including high-resolution print version (TIFF/PNG 300dpi), web display version (JPG/PNG 72dpi), social media version and other formats and sizes, ensure output quality
• Organize creation process files, including sketches, process drafts, layered source files, reference materials, etc., establish complete creation archive, convenient for subsequent modification and portfolio production
• Write creation statement and reflection, explain creative concept, expression techniques, technical characteristics of the work, summarize gains and shortcomings during creation process, reflect on areas for improvement
Deliverable: Digital art work and archive (including final work, source files, creation statement, reflection summary) | Quality standard: High work completion, standardized files, profound reflection
