作曲基础

Composition Fundamentals

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：和声学、对位法、曲式分析 | 学期：第四学期

本课程是作曲专业的基础课程，系统讲授音乐创作的基本技法与原则。内容包括旋律写作、动机发展、和声配置、织体写作、曲式结构运用等。通过分析不同风格的经典作品，结合循序渐进的写作练习，学生将学习如何将音乐理论知识转化为实际创作能力。课程涵盖艺术歌曲、钢琴小品、室内乐重奏等多种体裁的写作，培养学生的创造性思维与音乐表达能力，为进一步的作曲学习打下基础。

This foundational composition course systematically teaches basic techniques and principles of musical creation. Topics include melody writing, motivic development, harmonic setting, texture writing, and formal structure application. Through analysis of classic works in various styles combined with progressive writing exercises, students learn to translate theoretical knowledge into practical compositional skills. The course covers multiple genres including art song, piano miniature, and chamber ensemble, cultivating creative thinking and musical expressiveness while building a foundation for further compositional study.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：室内乐作品创作与总谱制作

学生需为一组室内乐器创作一首完整作品，运用动机发展与曲式结构原则，并制作规范总谱与分谱。最终提交乐谱与创作说明。

Students compose a complete work for a chamber ensemble, applying motivic development and formal principles, and produce a standard full score and parts. The score and composition notes are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：写一首室内乐作品——设计主题、编排乐器、制作总谱。
步骤 1
创作构思与素材准备
本步骤的核心任务是确定室内乐创作的主题和风格，进行创作前期的构思和素材准备。室内乐创作始于灵感和构思，好的创意是作品成功的基础。通过广泛的聆听、分析和素材积累，为创作建立坚实的基础，明确创作的方向和目标。

• 确定创作的乐器编制和风格，选择弦乐四重奏、钢琴三重奏、木管五重奏等室内乐编制，确定作品的风格流派（古典、浪漫、现代、民族等）和情感基调
• 进行作曲技术和文献研究，分析同类型同编制的经典室内乐作品，研究其配器手法、织体写法、结构布局，学习大师的创作经验和技巧
• 收集创作素材和动机，从民歌、民间音乐、自然声音、个人情感中提取音乐素材，发展核心动机（Motif），探索不同的节奏、旋律、和声素材
产出：创作构思与素材集（含创作说明、作品分析、动机素材）｜ 质量标准：构思清晰有创意，素材准备充分
步骤 2
动机发展与主题写作
本步骤聚焦于核心动机的发展和主题旋律的写作，是作曲的创意核心。动机是音乐作品的种子，通过各种发展手法可以生长出完整的音乐。好的主题旋律具有鲜明的个性和发展潜力，能够深深打动听众。

• 创作核心动机和主题，根据作品的情感基调和风格特点，创作具有鲜明性格的核心动机和主要主题，包括主部主题、副部主题、歌唱性主题、戏剧性主题等
• 进行动机发展技术练习，运用各种动机发展手法，如模进、逆行、扩大、缩小、倒影、变奏、分裂、综合等，发展动机素材，练习主题的展开和延伸
• 探索主题之间的对比和联系，设计不同主题之间的对比关系（速度、力度、调性、性格）和统一关系（核心动机、音调材料），为结构布局做准备
产出：主题动机素材（含核心动机、主题旋律、发展变体）｜ 质量标准：动机鲜明，主题优美，发展手法丰富
步骤 3
曲式结构与整体布局
本步骤是作品的架构设计阶段，确定作品的整体曲式结构和各部分的布局安排。曲式是音乐作品的组织结构，合理的结构能让音乐的发展有逻辑、有层次、有张力。通过精心的结构设计，使作品既有对比变化又有统一协调。

• 确定作品的整体曲式结构，选择奏鸣曲式、回旋曲式、三部曲式、变奏曲式、自由曲式等合适的结构形式，规划各部分的比例、调性布局、速度布局
• 设计各段落的音乐内容，规划呈示部、发展部、再现部（或序奏、主体、尾声）各部分的功能和内容，明确每个段落的情绪、调性、织体、力度对比
• 绘制作品结构示意图，标出各段落的小节数、调性、主题材料、速度、力度、情绪等，建立作品的整体蓝图，为后续写作提供指引
产出：曲式结构设计（含结构图示、调性布局、段落规划）｜ 质量标准：结构清晰合理，布局均衡有张力
步骤 4
织体写作与配器处理
本步骤是作曲的核心写作阶段，将主题材料按照结构规划发展成完整的音乐织体。室内乐的魅力在于各声部之间的对话和互动，织体写作需要兼顾各声部的独立性和整体的协调性。精湛的配器技法能充分发挥每件乐器的特性，创造丰富的音色层次。

• 进行横向的旋律线条写作和纵向的和声配置，为各声部写作流畅的旋律线条，配置恰当的和声进行，处理好声部之间的音程关系和声部走向
• 设计丰富的织体形态，运用主调织体、复调织体、混合织体等不同织体类型，设计齐奏、对答、模仿、对位、伴奏加旋律等不同的乐器组合方式
• 进行室内乐配器处理，充分考虑每件乐器的音域、音色、演奏技术特点，合理分配声部，运用各种室内乐写作技巧，如对话式写作、角色互换、音色混合等
产出：音乐总谱初稿（含完整乐谱、和声配置、织体设计）｜ 质量标准：织体丰富，声部均衡，配器合理
步骤 5
总谱打磨与作品完成
本步骤是作曲的收官阶段，对总谱进行细致的打磨和完善，完成最终的作品。音乐创作需要反复推敲和精心打磨，通过多轮修改和调整，使作品达到更高的艺术水准。同时制作规范的乐谱，方便演奏家阅读和演奏。

• 对总谱进行整体调整和细节打磨，检查和声、对位、声部进行的合理性，调整力度、速度、表情记号，优化音乐的表现力和感染力
• 进行实际音响试听和修改，使用打谱软件播放或邀请演奏家试奏，聆听实际效果，根据听觉反馈进行修改调整，解决纸上难以发现的问题
• 制作规范的总谱和分谱，使用Sibelius、Finale等打谱软件制作规范的乐谱，添加准确的演奏法标记、力度速度标记、表情术语，进行排版和校对
产出：完成作品与乐谱（含总谱、分谱、创作说明）｜ 质量标准：作品完整，音乐表现力强，乐谱规范准确

Steps

Step 1
Creative Conception & Material Preparation
The core task of this step is to determine the theme and style of chamber music composition, conduct preliminary conception and material preparation for creation. Chamber music composition begins with inspiration and conception; good creativity is the foundation of a successful work. Through extensive listening, analysis and material accumulation, establish a solid foundation for creation, clarify the direction and goals of creation.

• Determine instrumentation and style of creation, choose chamber music formations such as string quartet, piano trio, woodwind quintet, etc., determine the style genre (classical, romantic, modern, folk, etc.) and emotional tone of the work
• Conduct composition technique and literature research, analyze classic chamber music works of the same type and instrumentation, study their orchestration techniques, texture writing, structural layout, learn creative experience and skills from masters
• Collect creative materials and motives, extract musical materials from folk songs, folk music, natural sounds, personal emotions, develop core Motif, explore different rhythmic, melodic, harmonic materials
Deliverable: Creative conception and material collection (including creation statement, work analysis, motif materials) | Quality standard: Clear and creative conception, sufficient material preparation
Step 2
Motivic Development & Theme Writing
This step focuses on development of core motives and writing of thematic melodies, which is the creative core of composition. Motif is the seed of a musical work; through various development techniques, complete music can grow. Good thematic melodies have distinct personality and development potential, able to deeply move the audience.

• Compose core motives and themes, according to emotional tone and style characteristics of the work, create core motives and main themes with distinct character, including primary theme, secondary theme, lyrical theme, dramatic theme, etc.
• Conduct motivic development technique practice, apply various motivic development methods such as sequence, retrograde, augmentation, diminution, inversion, variation, fragmentation, synthesis, etc., develop motif materials, practice expansion and extension of themes
• Explore contrast and connection between themes, design contrast relationships (tempo, dynamics, tonality, character) and unified relationships (core motif, pitch material) between different themes, prepare for structural layout
Deliverable: Theme and motif materials (including core motif, thematic melody, development variants) | Quality standard: Distinct motif, beautiful theme, rich development techniques
Step 3
Musical Form & Overall Layout
This step is the architectural design phase of the work, determining the overall musical form structure and layout arrangement of each part. Musical form is the organizational structure of a musical work; reasonable structure enables musical development to be logical, layered, and tense. Through careful structural design, make the work both contrast/varied and unified/coordinated.

• Determine overall musical form structure of the work, choose appropriate structural forms such as sonata form, rondo form, ternary form, variation form, free form, etc., plan proportion, tonal layout, tempo layout of each part
• Design musical content of each section, plan functions and content of each part such as exposition, development, recapitulation (or introduction, main body, coda), clarify emotion, tonality, texture, dynamics contrast of each paragraph
• Draw work structure diagram, mark measure numbers, tonality, thematic materials, tempo, dynamics, mood, etc. of each section, establish overall blueprint of the work, provide guidance for subsequent writing
Deliverable: Musical form structure design (including structure diagram, tonal layout, section planning) | Quality standard: Clear and reasonable structure, balanced and tense layout
Step 4
Texture Writing & Orchestration
This step is the core writing phase of composition, developing thematic materials into complete musical texture according to structural planning. The charm of chamber music lies in dialogue and interaction between voices; texture writing needs to balance independence of each voice part and overall coordination. Exquisite orchestration techniques can fullyleverage characteristics of each instrument, creating rich timbral layers.

• Conduct horizontal melodic line writing and vertical harmonic configuration, write fluent melodic lines for each voice part, configure appropriate harmonic progressions, handle intervallic relationships and voice leading between voice parts well
• Design rich texture forms, apply different texture types such as homophonic texture, polyphonic texture, mixed texture, design different instrument combinations such as unison, antiphonal, imitation, counterpoint, accompaniment plus melody
• Perform chamber music orchestration processing, fully consider range, timbre, playing technical characteristics of each instrument, reasonablydistribute voice parts, apply various chamber music writing techniques such as dialogic writing, role exchange, timbre mixing
Deliverable: Music score draft (including complete score, harmonic configuration, texture design) | Quality standard: Rich texture, balanced voices, reasonable orchestration
Step 5
Score Polishing & Work Completion
This step is the concluding phase of composition, conducting detailed polishing and improvement of the score, completing the final work. Music composition requires repeated deliberation and careful polishing; through multiple rounds of revision and adjustment, bring the work to a higher artistic level. At the same time, produce standardized scores, convenient for performers to read and play.

• Conduct overall adjustment and detail polishing of the score, check rationality of harmony, counterpoint, voice leading, adjust dynamics, tempo, expression marks, optimize expressive power and appeal of the music
• Conduct actual sound audition and revision, use notation software playback or invite performers for trial performance, listen to actual effect, make revisions and adjustments based on auditory feedback, solve problems difficult to find on paper
• Produce standardized full score and parts, use notation software such as Sibelius, Finale to make standardized scores, add accurate performance technique marks, dynamic/tempo marks, expression terms, conduct typesetting and proofreading
Deliverable: Completed work and scores (including full score, parts, creation statement) | Quality standard: Complete work, strong musical expressiveness, standardized and accurate scores
