版画艺术

Printmaking

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：素描基础 | 学期：第三学期

本课程是艺术专业的版画入门课程，系统介绍版画艺术的主要种类与制作技法。课程内容涵盖木刻、铜版画、石版画、丝网版画四大版种的材料、工具与制作流程，包括制版、印刷、套色等核心技术。通过对经典版画作品的分析与实践练习，帮助学生掌握版画的基本语言特点与艺术表现力。课程强调版画的间接性与复数性特质，引导学生探索版画独特的艺术表达方式，了解版画在当代艺术中的多元发展。

This introductory printmaking course systematically covers the major categories and production techniques of print art. Topics include materials, tools, and processes for woodcut, intaglio, lithography, and screen printing, including plate-making, printing, and color registration. Through analysis of classic print works and practical exercises, students master the basic linguistic characteristics and expressive power of printmaking. The course emphasizes the indirect and reproductive qualities of prints.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：版画主题创作与印制系列

学生需选择一种版种完成主题创作系列，掌握制版、印刷与套色全流程。最终提交版画作品与创作过程记录。

Students complete a themed print series in a chosen printmaking type, mastering platemaking, printing, and color registration. The prints and process documentation are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
主题构思与画稿设计
本步骤的核心任务是确定版画创作主题并进行画稿设计，为版画制作奠定创意基础。版画是间接性的艺术，画稿是制版的蓝图。好的版画创作不仅要有好的主题，还要考虑版画的艺术特点和技术特性，使创作内容与表现形式相得益彰。

• 确定创作主题和版画类型，选择木版画、铜版画、石版画、丝网版画等具体版种，了解不同版种的艺术特点和表现语言，确定创作方向
• 进行画稿设计，根据主题和版种特点进行创作构思，绘制多版草图，探索不同的构图、造型、黑白关系和表现手法，确定最终的画稿方案
• 制作正稿和分版稿，将画稿放大到正式尺寸，制作精确的正稿，根据版种和印刷色数设计分版方案，明确每一版的图形和颜色，为制版做准备
产出：画稿设计方案（含创意说明、草图、正稿、分版稿）｜ 质量标准：创意有想法，画稿完整，分版方案清晰可行
步骤 2
制版工艺与技法
本步骤是版画创作的核心技术环节，将画稿转移到版材上，通过各种制版技法制作印版。制版是版画的关键工序，不同的版种有不同的制版方法和工艺要求。通过精湛的制版技艺，将艺术家的创意转化为可印刷的印版。

• 准备版材和工具，根据版种选择合适的版材（木板、铜板、石板、丝网版等），准备相应的制版工具和材料，对版材进行必要的前期处理（打磨、涂底、脱脂等）
• 进行转稿和制版，使用相应的转稿方法（拷贝、复写、直接绘制、感光制版等）将画稿转移到版材上，运用各种制版技法进行刻制、腐蚀、绘制、感光等制版工序
• 进行打样和修版，印刷试印样（Test Print），检查制版效果，对照原画稿进行修版和调整，反复打样修改，直到印版达到满意的效果
产出：印版与打样稿（含版材处理、制版过程、试印样、修版记录）｜ 质量标准：制版精细，套印准确，版痕效果好
步骤 3
印刷工艺与试验
本步骤是版画创作的印刷实验阶段，探索各种印刷技法和效果。印刷是版画艺术的重要组成部分，不同的油墨、纸张、压力、印刷方式会产生截然不同的艺术效果。通过大量的印刷实验，找到最佳的印刷方案和艺术表现语言。

• 准备印刷材料和设备，选择合适的油墨（油性油墨、水性油墨）、纸张（版画纸、宣纸、铜版纸）、印刷设备（印刷机、木蘑菇、马连）等，调试油墨的稠度和颜色
• 进行印刷技法实验，尝试不同的印刷方法和技巧，如手工印刷、机器印刷、凹印、凸印、平印、孔印，以及各种特殊效果（肌理、渐变、套色、拼贴等）
• 进行分色套印试验，对于多色版画，进行分版印刷实验，调整各版的颜色、透明度、叠印顺序，研究颜色混合和叠印效果，确定最终的印刷方案
产出：印刷实验稿（含材料测试、技法实验、套色试验）｜ 质量标准：技法掌握熟练，实验充分，效果丰富
步骤 4
正式印制与编号签名
本步骤是版画创作的正式生产阶段，按照确定的方案进行正式印刷，制作限量版画。版画的魅力在于其复数性，但每一幅都是原作。专业的印制和规范的签名编号是版画作品价值的重要体现，也是版画艺术家的基本职业素养。

• 进行正式印刷，按照标准的印刷流程进行打样、印刷、晾画，确保每一张作品的质量稳定，颜色、套位、压力都保持一致，制作规定数量的版画作品
• 进行作品编号和签名，按照国际惯例在作品底部用铅笔编号（如12/50）、标注作品名称、签上艺术家姓名和创作年份，确保每幅作品的唯一性和可追溯性
• 进行品质检验和筛选，检查每幅作品的印刷质量，筛选出合格的作品，标记有瑕疵的作品（A.P. 艺术家自留版、B.A.T. 标准样），建立作品档案
产出：限量版画作品（含正式印品、编号签名、品质检验）｜ 质量标准：印刷质量优良，编号规范，作品完好
步骤 5
作品装裱与创作总结
本步骤是版画创作的收官环节，进行作品的装裱呈现和创作总结。好的作品需要好的呈现方式，专业的装裱能更好地展示作品的艺术价值。同时，通过创作总结和反思，梳理经验，为今后的创作积累经验。

• 进行作品装裱和展示，选择合适的装裱方式（卡纸装裱、画框装裱、无酸装裱等），使用无酸材料进行专业装裱，保护作品并提升展示效果
• 整理创作档案，保存好印版、试印样、正式作品、创作草图、参考资料等所有创作材料，建立完整的创作档案，方便日后研究和回顾
• 撰写创作总结和体会，总结本次版画创作的经验和教训，分析作品的优点和不足，反思创作过程中的收获和问题，制定后续的学习计划
产出：装裱成品与创作档案（含装裱作品、创作档案、总结报告）｜ 质量标准：装裱专业精美，档案完整规范，总结深刻

Steps

Step 1
Theme Conception & Drawing Design
The core task of this step is to determine printmaking creation theme and conduct drawing design, laying creative foundation for printmaking production. Printmaking is an indirect art; the drawing is the blueprint for plate making. A good print creation requires not only a good theme but also consideration of the artistic characteristics and technical features of printmaking, so that creative content and expression form complement each other.

• Determine creation theme and print type, choose specific print type such as woodblock, intaglio, lithography, silkscreen, etc., understand artistic characteristics and expression language of different print types, determine creation direction
• Conduct drawing design, carry out creative conception according to theme and print characteristics, draw multiple sketches, explore different compositions, forms, black-and-white relationships and expression techniques, determine final drawing scheme
• Make final drawings and color separation drafts, enlarge the drawing to formal size, make precise final drawing, design color separation scheme according to print type and number of colors, clarify graphics and colors of each plate, prepare for plate making
Deliverable: Drawing design proposal (including creative statement, sketches, final drawing, color separation drafts) | Quality standard: Creative ideas, complete drawing, clear and feasible color separation plan
Step 2
Plate Making Process & Techniques
This step is the core technical phase of printmaking creation, transferring the drawing onto the plate material and producing printing plates through various plate making techniques. Plate making is the key process of printmaking; different print types have different plate making methods and technical requirements. Through exquisite plate making skills, transform the artist's creativity into printable plates.

• Prepare plate materials and tools, select appropriate plate materials (wood, copper, stone, screen, etc.) according to print type, prepare corresponding plate making tools and materials, perform necessary pre-treatment on plate materials (polishing, grounding, degreasing, etc.)
• Conduct image transfer and plate making, use corresponding transfer methods (copying, carbon paper, direct drawing, photopolymer, etc.) to transfer drawing onto plate material, apply various plate making techniques for carving, etching, drawing, exposure and other plate making processes
• Perform proofing and plate revision, print test prints, check plate making effect, revise and adjust plate against original drawing, repeatedly proof and modify until the plate achieves satisfactory effect
Deliverable: Printing plates and proofs (including plate material processing, plate making process, trial prints, revision records) | Quality standard: Exquisite plate making, accurate registration, good plate mark effect
Step 3
Printing Process & Experimentation
This step is the printing experimentation phase of printmaking creation, exploring various printing techniques and effects. Printing is an important part of printmaking art; different inks, papers, pressures, printing methods produce completely different artistic effects. Through extensive printing experiments, find the best printing scheme and artistic expression language.

• Prepare printing materials and equipment, select appropriate inks (oil-based, water-based), papers (printmaking paper, rice paper, copperplate paper), printing equipment (press, wooden baren, squeegee), etc., adjust ink consistency and color
• Conduct printing technique experiments, try different printing methods and techniques, such as hand printing, press printing, intaglio, relief, planographic, screen, as well as various special effects (texture, gradient, overprinting, collage, etc.)
• Perform color separation overprinting tests, for multi-color prints, conduct multi-plate printing experiments, adjust color, transparency, overprinting order of each plate, study color mixing and overprinting effects, determine final printing scheme
Deliverable: Printing experimental drafts (including material testing, technique experiments, color registration tests) | Quality standard: Proficient technique mastery, sufficient experimentation, rich effects
Step 4
Edition Printing & Numbering/Signing
This step is the formal production phase of printmaking creation, conducting formal printing according to the determined scheme to produce limited edition prints. The charm of printmaking lies in its multiplicity, but each piece is an original work. Professional printing and standardized numbering/signing are important manifestations of the value of print works, and also the basic professional quality of print artists.

• Conduct formal printing, follow standard printing workflow for proofing, printing, drying, ensure stable quality of each work, consistent color, registration, and pressure, produce a specified number of print works
• Conduct work numbering and signing, according to international convention number the work with pencil at the bottom (e.g., 12/50), mark work title, sign artist name and creation year, ensure uniqueness and traceability of each work
• Conduct quality inspection and screening, check printing quality of each work, screen out qualified works, mark defective works (A.P. artist proof, B.A.T. bon à tirer), establish work archive
Deliverable: Limited edition prints (including formal prints, numbered/signed, quality inspection) | Quality standard: Excellent printing quality, standardized numbering, intact works
Step 5
Work Mounting & Creation Summary
This step is the concluding phase of printmaking creation, conducting work mounting/presentation and creation summary. Good works need good presentation methods; professional mounting can better showcase the artistic value of the work. At the same time, through creation summary and reflection, sort out experience, accumulate experience for future creations.

• Conduct work mounting and exhibition, choose appropriate mounting methods (mat board mounting, frame mounting, acid-free mounting, etc.), use acid-free materials for professional mounting, protect the work and enhance display effect
• Organize creation archive, properlypreserve all creation materials including plates, trial proofs, formal works, creative sketches, reference materials, etc., establish complete creation archive, convenient for future research and review
• Write creation summary and reflections, summarize experience and lessons from this printmaking creation, analyze advantages and disadvantages of the work, reflect on gains and problems during creation process, formulate subsequent learning plan
Deliverable: Mounted finished product and creation archive (including mounted work, creation archive, summary report) | Quality standard: Professional and exquisite mounting, complete and standardized archive, profound summary
