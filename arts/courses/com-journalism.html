新闻写作与报道

News Writing and Reporting

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：传播学导论 | 学期：春季

本课程培养学生的新闻采写能力，涵盖新闻价值判断、信息搜集、采访技巧、消息写作、特稿写作等核心内容。学生将学习倒金字塔结构、导语写作、引语使用等专业技能，练习硬新闻、特写、调查报道等不同体裁的写作。课程强调新闻伦理和客观性原则，通过大量实战练习提升学生的新闻敏感度和文字表达能力，为从事新闻工作打下坚实基础。

This course develops students' journalistic writing and reporting skills, covering news value judgment, information gathering, interviewing techniques, news writing, and feature writing. Students learn inverted pyramid structure, lead writing, quote usage, and practice various genres including hard news, features, and investigative reporting. The course emphasizes journalistic ethics and objectivity, building students' news sensitivity and writing abilities through extensive practical exercises.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：深度新闻报道采写与多媒体呈现

学生需围绕一个社会议题完成一篇深度报道，涵盖选题立项、多方信源采访、消息与特稿写作及多媒体补充呈现。最终提交完整报道稿件与采访素材。

Students complete an in-depth report on a social issue, covering topic development, multi-source interviewing, news and feature writing, and multimedia supplements. The complete report and interview materials are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：选一个新闻话题，做深度报道——采访、写作、多媒体呈现。
步骤 1
选题立项与信源梳理
本步骤的核心任务是确定深度报道的选题并梳理潜在信源，为后续采访报道奠定基础。好的新闻选题需要兼顾新闻价值、社会意义和可操作性，同时要评估报道的风险和可行性。通过系统性的信源梳理，确保报道有足够的信息来源支撑，能够从多个角度呈现事实真相。

• 运用新闻价值判断标准（时效性、重要性、显著性、接近性、趣味性）评估选题，确定报道角度和切入点，撰写选题立项书
• 梳理多方信源，包括官方信源、专家信源、当事人信源、目击者信源等，建立信源清单和联系方式，评估信源的可靠性和可及性
• 进行背景资料收集和案头调研，查阅相关政策文件、统计数据、历史报道和学术研究，形成初步的报道框架和问题清单
产出：选题立项书与信源清单（含选题说明、报道框架、信源列表、背景资料汇编）｜ 质量标准：选题有新闻价值和社会意义，信源多元可靠，报道框架清晰完整
步骤 2
采访准备与实地采访
本步骤是深度报道的核心采集环节，通过专业的采访获取第一手信息和素材。高质量的采访是深度报道的基石，需要充分的准备和专业的技巧。不仅要获取事实信息，还要捕捉生动的细节和真挚的情感，让报道具有感染力和说服力。

• 针对不同采访对象设计个性化的采访提纲，包括开放式问题和封闭式问题，按照由浅入深、由表及里的逻辑顺序排列问题
• 运用专业采访技巧，包括倾听技巧、追问技巧、引导技巧和应对技巧，使用录音笔和笔记本双重记录，确保采访内容准确完整
• 进行至少5人次的深度采访，覆盖不同立场和观点的信源，收集充足的引语、故事和细节素材，做好采访笔录和录音整理
产出：采访记录与素材库（含采访提纲、采访录音、采访笔录、素材分类整理）｜ 质量标准：采访深入充分，信息丰富详实，引语生动准确，信源多样平衡
步骤 3
资料核实与事实核查
本步骤是新闻报道的质量把关环节，确保报道的真实性和准确性。真实是新闻的生命，事实核查是新闻从业者的基本职责。通过严谨的核实程序，杜绝虚假信息和失实报道，维护新闻媒体的公信力和职业声誉。

• 对采访中获取的关键事实进行交叉验证，至少通过两个以上独立信源确认重要信息，对存疑信息进行补充核实或标注存疑
• 核查相关数据、时间、地点、人物身份等基本事实要素，查阅官方文件、权威数据库和原始资料，确保信息准确无误
• 建立事实核查清单和核查记录，明确每条信息的来源和验证状态，对无法核实的信息坚决舍弃或明确说明限制
产出：事实核查记录（含核查清单、信息来源对照表、存疑事项说明、核查结论）｜ 质量标准：事实核查严谨细致，关键信息全部经过交叉验证，无虚假信息和重大事实错误
步骤 4
报道写作与编辑
本步骤是将采访素材转化为新闻作品的创作环节，通过专业的写作和编辑呈现完整的报道。深度报道需要清晰的结构、流畅的叙事和精准的语言，既要客观公正地呈现事实，又要具有可读性和感染力。精心的编辑打磨能让报道更加精炼和专业。

• 设计报道结构，可采用倒金字塔式、华尔街日报体、人物故事式等结构，合理安排信息层次和叙事节奏，撰写报道大纲
• 撰写完整的深度报道，包含标题、导语、主体、结尾，运用新闻写作技巧，如直接引语、场景描写、背景穿插，增强报道的可读性
• 进行多轮编辑修改，从事实准确性、逻辑清晰度、文字流畅性、结构合理性等方面打磨稿件，确保符合专业新闻标准
产出：深度报道稿件（含完整报道文本、标题方案、配图说明、编辑修改记录）｜ 质量标准：报道结构清晰，叙事流畅，语言精准，符合新闻专业规范和伦理要求
步骤 5
多媒体呈现与发布准备
本步骤是深度报道的融合呈现环节，通过多媒体手段增强报道的表现力和传播力。在全媒体时代，深度报道不仅要有优质的文字内容，还要配合图片、视频、图表等多种媒介形式，提供更丰富的信息体验。同时要做好发布前的最后审核工作。

• 拍摄或收集配套的图片、视频、图表等多媒体素材，进行必要的后期处理，确保素材质量和版权合规，撰写图片说明和视频脚本
• 设计信息图表和数据可视化，将复杂的数据和信息转化为直观的视觉呈现，运用信息设计原理提升信息传达效率
• 进行发布前的终审，包括事实终审、法律合规审核、伦理风险评估，准备不同平台的发布版本，制定发布推广计划
产出：多媒体报道成品（含报道终稿、图片视频素材、信息图表、多平台发布版本）｜ 质量标准：多媒体素材质量优良，信息呈现直观清晰，终审严格规范，符合发布要求

Steps

Step 1
Topic Development & Source Mapping
The core task of this step is to determine the in-depth report topic and map potential sources, laying the foundation for subsequent interviewing and reporting. A good news topic requires balancing news value, social significance, and operability, while assessing risks and feasibility of the report. Through systematic source mapping, ensure the report has sufficient information sources to present factual truth from multiple perspectives.

• Apply news value judgment criteria (timeliness, importance, prominence, proximity, human interest) to evaluate topics, determine reporting angle and entry point, write topic proposal document
• Map multiple sources including official sources, expert sources, party sources, eyewitness sources, etc., establish source list and contact information, assess reliability and accessibility of sources
• Conduct background information collection and desk research, review relevant policy documents, statistical data, historical reports, and academic research, form preliminary reporting framework and question list
Deliverable: Topic proposal and source list (including topic statement, reporting framework, source list, background materials compilation) | Quality standard: Topic has news value and social significance, diverse and reliable sources, clear and complete reporting framework
Step 2
Interview Preparation & Field Reporting
This step is the core collection phase of in-depth reporting, acquiring first-hand information and materials through professional interviewing. High-quality interviewing is the cornerstone of in-depth reporting, requiring thorough preparation and professional skills. It is necessary not only to obtain factual information but also to capture vivid details and sincere emotions, making the report compelling and persuasive.

• Design personalized interview outlines for different interviewees, including open-ended and closed-ended questions, arrange questions in logical order from superficial to deep
• Apply professional interview techniques including listening skills, follow-up questioning, guiding techniques, and coping strategies, use both recorder and notebook for dual recording, ensure accurate and complete interview content
• Conduct at least 5 in-depth interviews covering sources with different positions and viewpoints, collect ample quotes, stories, and detailed materials, complete interview transcripts and audio organization
Deliverable: Interview records and material library (including interview outlines, interview recordings, interview transcripts, categorized materials) | Quality standard: In-depth and thorough interviews, rich and detailed information, vivid and accurate quotes, diverse and balanced sources
Step 3
Verification & Fact-Checking
This step is the quality assurance phase of news reporting, ensuring the truthfulness and accuracy of the report. Truth is the life of journalism, and fact-checking is the fundamental duty of journalists. Through rigorous verification procedures, eliminate false information and inaccurate reports, maintaining the credibility and professional reputation of news media.

• Cross-verify key facts obtained during interviews, confirm important information through at least two independent sources, conduct supplementary verification or mark doubtful information
• Verify relevant data, time, location, person identity and other basic factual elements, review official documents, authoritative databases and original materials, ensure information is accurate and error-free
• Establish fact-checking checklist and verification records, clarify source and verification status of each piece of information, firmly discard or clearly state limitations for information that cannot be verified
Deliverable: Fact-checking records (including verification checklist, information source comparison table, doubtful items explanation, verification conclusion) | Quality standard: Rigorous and detailed fact-checking, all key information cross-verified, no false information or major factual errors
Step 4
Report Writing & Editing
This step is the creative phase of transforming interview materials into journalistic works, presenting the complete report through professional writing and editing. In-depth reporting requires clear structure, fluent narrative, and precise language, presenting facts objectively and fairly while being readable and compelling. Careful editing and polishing makes the report more refined and professional.

• Design report structure, can adopt inverted pyramid, Wall Street Journal formula, character story style, etc., reasonably arrange information hierarchy and narrative rhythm, write report outline
• Write complete in-depth report including headline, lead, body, conclusion, apply journalistic writing techniques such as direct quotes, scene descriptions, background insertion, enhance readability of the report
• Conduct multiple rounds of editing and revision, polish the manuscript from aspects of factual accuracy, logical clarity, text fluency, structural rationality, ensure it meets professional journalism standards
Deliverable: In-depth report manuscript (including complete report text, headline options, photo captions, editing revision records) | Quality standard: Clear report structure, fluent narrative, precise language, meets professional journalistic standards and ethical requirements
Step 5
Multimedia Presentation & Publication Preparation
This step is the convergent presentation phase of in-depth reporting, enhancing the expressiveness and communicative power of the report through multimedia means. In the all-media era, in-depth reporting requires not only quality text content but also various media forms such as images, videos, and charts to provide a richer information experience. Also conduct final review before publication.

• Shoot or collect supporting multimedia materials such as images, videos, and charts, conduct necessary post-processing, ensure material quality and copyright compliance, write photo captions and video scripts
• Design infographics and data visualization, transform complex data and information into intuitive visual presentation, apply information design principles to improve information transmission efficiency
• Conduct final review before publication, including final fact check, legal compliance review, ethical risk assessment, prepare release versions for different platforms, develop publication promotion plan
Deliverable: Multimedia report final product (including final report draft, image and video materials, infographics, multi-platform release versions) | Quality standard: Excellent multimedia material quality, clear and intuitive information presentation, strict and standardized final review, meets publication requirements
