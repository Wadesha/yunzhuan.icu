色彩理论与实践

Color Theory and Practice

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学期

本课程是艺术专业的理论与实践结合课程，系统研究色彩的科学原理与艺术应用。课程内容涵盖色彩学基础理论，包括色环、色相、明度、饱和度、色彩对比与调和、色彩心理学等。通过大量调色练习、色彩构成实验、色彩搭配设计等实践环节，帮助学生建立科学的色彩认知体系。课程强调色彩在绘画、设计、数字艺术中的实际运用，培养学生敏锐的色彩感知能力和熟练的色彩运用技巧，为各专业方向的视觉表达提供色彩支撑。

This combined theory and practice course systematically studies the scientific principles and artistic applications of color. Topics include basic color theory, color wheel, hue, value, saturation, color contrast and harmony, and color psychology. Through extensive mixing exercises, color composition experiments, and color coordination design projects, students build a scientific understanding of color systems. The course emphasizes practical application in painting, design, and digital art.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：色彩情感系列表达

学生需创作一组6幅色彩构成作品，每幅尺寸30x30厘米，分别表达六种不同的情感或情绪主题。作品需运用色彩理论中的对比、调和、冷暖、明暗等原理，通过抽象或半具象的形式传达情感内涵。学生需提交色彩研究报告，说明每幅作品的色彩方案设计依据、理论运用和情感传达策略。作品需展现对色彩语言的深入理解和创造性运用能力。

Students create a series of six color composition works, each 30x30cm, expressing six different emotional or mood themes. Using contrast, harmony, warm-cool relationships, and value principles from color theory, the works convey emotional content through abstract or semi-representational forms. Students submit a color research report explaining each work's color scheme design rationale, theoretical application, and emotional communication strategy.
