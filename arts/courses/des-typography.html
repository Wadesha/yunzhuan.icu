排版设计

Typography

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：设计基础 | 学期：第三学期

本课程深入研究文字作为视觉元素的排版原理与应用技巧。课程内容涵盖字体发展史、字体分类与特征、字间距与行间距、段落排版、网格系统、信息层级构建等核心知识，同时涉及中英文混排、响应式排版等前沿议题。通过大量排版练习和实际项目，学生将掌握字体选择的逻辑、排版的节奏与韵律、以及文字与图形的整合设计。课程强调阅读体验与视觉美感的平衡，培养学生运用文字进行有效视觉传达的专业能力。

This course provides in-depth study of typographic principles and application techniques of text as a visual element. The curriculum covers core knowledge including the history of type, typeface classification and characteristics, kerning and leading, paragraph typesetting, grid systems, and information hierarchy construction, while addressing cutting-edge topics such as Chinese-English mixed typesetting and responsive typography. Through extensive typography exercises and practical projects, students master the logic of font selection, the rhythm and cadence of typesetting, and the integrated design of text and graphics. The course emphasizes the balance between reading experience and visual aesthetics, cultivating students' professional ability to use text for effective visual communication.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：中文字体设计与排版应用

学生需完成一套中文字体设计或主题排版作品，涵盖字体设计、网格系统与多场景排版应用。最终提交字体设计稿与排版作品。

Students complete a Chinese typeface design or themed typographic work, covering typeface design, grid system, and multi-scenario layout applications. Typeface designs and typographic works are submitted.
