和声学

Harmony

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：乐理基础 | 学期：第二、三学期

本课程是音乐理论的核心课程，系统讲授调性音乐的和声体系。内容包括三和弦与七和弦的构成、和弦连接的声部进行规则、正副三和弦的应用、离调与转调、模进、减七和弦与增六和弦等变化和弦、以及和声终止式等。通过写作四部和声习题和分析经典作品的和声进行，学生将掌握古典与浪漫时期的和声语言，理解和弦的功能与色彩，为音乐分析、创作和表演提供理论支撑。

This core music theory course systematically teaches the harmonic system of tonal music. Topics include triad and seventh chord construction, voice leading rules for chord progressions, primary and secondary triads, tonicization and modulation, sequences, altered chords such as diminished seventh and augmented sixth chords, and harmonic cadences. Through four-part harmony writing exercises and harmonic analysis of classic works, students master the harmonic language of the Classical and Romantic periods and understand chord function and color, providing theoretical support for analysis, composition, and performance.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：和声写作练习与作品和声分析

学生需完成一组四部和声写作练习，并对一首古典作品进行和声进行分析。最终提交和声习题与和声分析报告。

Students complete a set of four-part harmony writing exercises and analyze the harmonic progression of a Classical work. The harmony exercises and analysis report are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
和声基础复习与作品选择
本步骤的核心任务是复习四部和声基础知识并选择进行和声分析的作品。和声分析需要扎实的和声理论基础，先系统回顾和声学的核心概念和规则，为后续的分析和写作做好准备。选择合适的作品进行分析，能在实践中深化对和声理论的理解。

• 系统复习和声学基础知识，包括三和弦、七和弦、和弦转位、声部进行、终止式、和声功能、离调、转调、变和弦等核心概念和规则
• 选择和声分析作品，选择一首古典时期或浪漫时期的键盘乐作品或室内乐作品，如巴赫、莫扎特、贝多芬、肖邦、舒伯特等作曲家的作品，确保作品和声语言丰富且具有代表性
• 收集作品乐谱和不同版本的录音，获取高质量的乐谱，聆听不同演奏家的演绎版本，建立对作品整体的听觉印象和感性认识
产出：基础复习与作品资料（含复习笔记、作品乐谱、录音）｜ 质量标准：基础扎实，资料准备充分
步骤 2
和弦标记与功能分析
本步骤是和声分析的基础环节，对作品中的每个和弦进行识别和标记，分析其功能属性。准确识别和弦的性质、位置、功能是和声分析的基本功。通过逐小节的和弦标记，建立作品和声进行的全貌，为深入分析奠定基础。

• 进行逐小节的和弦识别和标记，在乐谱上标注每个和弦的性质（三和弦/七和弦、大小增减）、位置（原位/转位）、功能（T/S/D）、级数（罗马数字标记），使用规范的和声标记体系
• 分析终止式的类型和位置，识别作品中的各种终止类型（正格终止、变格终止、完全终止、不完全终止、半终止、阻碍终止等），分析终止式在结构划分中的作用
• 梳理和声的功能进行逻辑，分析主-属-下属的功能关系网络，研究功能圈的运动规律，理解和声的张力和解决逻辑
产出：和弦标记与功能分析（含标记乐谱、功能网络分析）｜ 质量标准：和弦识别准确，功能分析清晰
步骤 3
声部进行与外音分析
本步骤深入分析声部进行规律和装饰性外音，理解和声的线性运动特征。四部和声不仅有纵向的和弦结构，更有横向的声部进行。外音（经过音、辅助音、先现音、延留音等）是丰富和声色彩、增加音乐流畅性的重要手段。

• 分析各声部的声部进行，研究四个声部（高音、中音、次中音、低音）的横向线条和走向，分析声部进行的方式（平稳进行、跳进进行），检查是否有不良进行（平行五八度、反向五八度等）
• 识别和分析外音类型，在乐谱上标记各种装饰性外音，包括经过音、辅助音、先现音、延留音、倚音、逃音、持续音等，分析外音的解决方式和表情作用
• 研究和声的线条化处理，分析分解和弦、琶音、华彩等织体现象背后的和声骨架，理解和声如何通过织体的变化获得丰富的表现形式
产出：声部进行与外音分析（含声部分析谱例、外音标记、织体分析）｜ 质量标准：声部分析细致，外音识别准确
步骤 4
离调转调与和声色彩
本步骤分析作品中的离调、转调和变和弦，研究和声的色彩变化和调性关系。离调和转调扩展了音乐的调性空间，变和弦丰富了和声的色彩表现，是和声从功能性向色彩性发展的重要途径。通过分析这些高级和声手法，深化对和声表现力的理解。

• 分析离调和副属和弦，识别作品中的副属和弦、副下属和弦、副导七和弦等离调和弦，分析离调的方向、方法和表情意义，理解临时调性的转换效果
• 分析转调的类型和方法，判断作品中的转调（如关系大小调转调、近关系转调、远关系转调），分析转调的过程和手法（共同和弦转调、等和弦转调、模进转调等），研究调性布局的结构意义
• 分析变和弦和色彩和声，识别那不勒斯六和弦、增六和弦、等和弦、全音阶等变和弦或特殊和声材料，分析其色彩效果和表现意义，理解和声的表现力
产出：离调转调分析报告（含离调分析、转调分析、色彩和声分析）｜ 质量标准：转调判断准确，色彩分析深入
步骤 5
和声写作与分析总结
本步骤是和声学习的实践与总结阶段，进行和声写作练习并撰写完整的和声分析报告。和声分析最终要落实到实践中，通过写作练习加深对和声规则的理解和运用。通过系统的总结，将分析和写作的经验理论化，全面提升和声素养。

• 进行四部和声写作练习，根据给定的旋律或低音写作四部和声，正确运用各种和弦、终止、离调等和声手法，注意声部进行的流畅性和正确性，完成规定数量的和声习题
• 撰写完整的和声分析报告，将前面各步骤的分析整合起来，系统阐述作品的和声语言特点、和声功能逻辑、调性布局、和声与结构的关系等，配合作品谱例说明
• 总结和声学学习体会，总结自己在和声理论、和声分析、和声写作各方面的收获和不足，制定后续的进阶学习计划，如浪漫主义和声、爵士和声、20世纪和声等
产出：和声分析报告与写作练习（含分析报告、和声习题、学习总结）｜ 质量标准：分析系统深入，写作规范正确，总结深刻

Steps

Step 1
Harmony Basics Review & Work Selection
The core task of this step is to review four-part harmony basic knowledge and select a work for harmonic analysis. Harmonic analysis requires solid harmonic theory foundation; first systematically review core concepts and rules of harmony, prepare for subsequent analysis and writing. Selecting appropriate works for analysis can deepen understanding of harmony theory in practice.

• Systematically review basic knowledge of harmony, including triads, seventh chords, chord inversions, voice leading, cadences, harmonic functions, tonicization, modulation, altered chords and other core concepts and rules
• Select work for harmonic analysis, choose a keyboard work or chamber work from classical or romantic period, such as works by Bach, Mozart, Beethoven, Chopin, Schubert and other composers, ensure the work has rich harmonic language and is representative
• Collect work scores and different versions of recordings, obtain high-quality scores, listen to interpretations by different performers, establish auditory impression and perceptual understanding of the work as a whole
Deliverable: Basics review and work materials (including review notes, work score, recordings) | Quality standard: Solid foundation, sufficient material preparation
Step 2
Chord Labeling & Functional Analysis
This step is the basic phase of harmonic analysis, identifying and labeling each chord in the work, analyzing its functional properties. Accurately identifying quality, position, function of chords is the basic skill of harmonic analysis. Through bar-by-bar chord labeling, establish complete picture of harmonic progression in the work, lay foundation for in-depth analysis.

• Conduct bar-by-bar chord identification and labeling, mark quality (triad/seventh chord, major/minor/augmented/diminished), position (root position/inversion), function (T/S/D), degree (Roman numeral labeling) of each chord on the score, use standardized harmonic labeling system
• Analyze types and positions of cadences, identify various cadence types in the work (authentic cadence, plagal cadence, perfect cadence, imperfect cadence, half cadence, deceptive cadence, etc.), analyze role of cadences in structural division
• Sort out functional progression logic of harmony, analyze functional relationship network of tonic-dominant-subdominant, study movement law of the function circle, understand tension and resolution logic of harmony
Deliverable: Chord labeling and functional analysis (including labeled score, functional network analysis) | Quality standard: Accurate chord identification, clear functional analysis
Step 3
Voice Leading & Non-Chord Tone Analysis
This step deeply analyzes voice leading rules and decorative non-chord tones, understanding linear movement characteristics of harmony. Four-part harmony not only has vertical chord structure but also horizontal voice leading. Non-chord tones (passing tones, auxiliary tones, anticipation tones, suspensions, etc.) are important means of enriching harmonic color and increasing musical fluency.

• Analyze voice leading of each voice part, study horizontal lines and direction of four voices (soprano, alto, tenor, bass), analyze ways of voice leading (stepwise motion, skip motion), check for improper progressions (parallel fifths/octaves, contrary fifths/octaves, etc.)
• Identify and analyze non-chord tone types, mark various decorative non-chord tones on the score, including passing tones, auxiliary tones, anticipation tones, suspensions, appoggiaturas, escape tones, pedal tones, etc., analyze resolution methods and expressive effects of non-chord tones
• Study linear treatment of harmony, analyze harmonic skeleton behind texture phenomena such as broken chords, arpeggios, cadenzas, etc., understand how harmony obtains rich expressive forms through textural changes
Deliverable: Voice leading and non-chord tone analysis (including voice part analysis examples, non-chord tone labeling, texture analysis) | Quality standard: Detailed voice part analysis, accurate non-chord tone identification
Step 4
Tonicization/Modulation & Harmonic Color
This step analyzes tonicization, modulation and altered chords in the work, studying harmonic color changes and tonal relationships. Tonicization and modulation expand the tonal space of music; altered chords enrich harmonic color expression—they are important pathways for harmony to develop from functional to coloristic. Through analyzing these advanced harmonic techniques, deepen understanding of harmonic expressiveness.

• Analyze tonicization and secondary dominants, identify secondary dominant chords, secondary subdominant chords, secondary leading-tone seventh chords and other tonicizing chords in the work, analyze direction, method and expressive significance of tonicization, understand temporary tonal shift effect
• Analyze types and methods of modulation, determine modulations in the work (such as relative major/minor modulation, close key modulation, distant key modulation), analyze process and techniques of modulation (common chord modulation, enharmonic modulation, sequential modulation, etc.), study structural significance of tonal layout
• Analyze altered chords and color harmony, identify Neapolitan sixth chords, augmented sixth chords, enharmonic chords, whole tone scale and other altered chords or special harmonic materials, analyze their color effects and expressive significance, understand expressive power of harmony
Deliverable: Tonicization/modulation analysis report (including tonicization analysis, modulation analysis, color harmony analysis) | Quality standard: Accurate modulation judgment, in-depth color analysis
Step 5
Harmony Writing & Analysis Summary
This step is the practice and summary phase of harmony learning, conducting harmony writing exercises and writing a complete harmonic analysis report. Harmonic analysis ultimately needs to be implemented in practice; through writing exercises deepen understanding and application of harmony rules. Through systematic summary, theorize experience of analysis and writing, comprehensively improve harmonic literacy.

• Conduct four-part harmony writing exercises, write four-part harmony according to given melody or bass, correctly use various chords, cadences, tonicization and other harmonic techniques, pay attention to fluency and correctness of voice leading, complete specified number of harmony exercises
• Write complete harmonic analysis report, integrate analysis from previous steps, systematically expound harmonic language characteristics, harmonic functional logic, tonal layout, relationship between harmony and structure, etc. of the work,coordination with work score examples for explanation
• Summarize harmony learning experience, summarize one's gains and shortcomings in aspects of harmonic theory, harmonic analysis, harmony writing, formulate subsequent advanced learning plan, such as romantic harmony, jazz harmony, 20th century harmony, etc.
Deliverable: Harmonic analysis report and writing exercises (including analysis report, harmony exercises, learning summary) | Quality standard: Systematic and in-depth analysis, standardized and correct writing, profound summary
