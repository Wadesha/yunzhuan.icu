版画艺术

Printmaking

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：素描基础、绘画基础 | 学期：第四学期

本课程是视觉艺术专业的传统媒介实践课程，系统教授版画艺术的多种技法与创作方法。课程涵盖木刻、铜版、石版、丝网等主要版画种类，内容包括制版原理、印刷工艺、材料特性、色彩套印等核心技术。通过不同版种的基础练习与主题创作，帮助学生掌握间接绘画的思维方式与技术语言。课程重视版画的复数性与实验性特质，引导学生探索版画语言的当代可能性，培养在有限版数中追求艺术表达极致的创作态度，为后续的专业创作与展览实践打下基础。

This traditional media studio course systematically teaches various printmaking techniques and creative methods. The course covers woodcut, intaglio, lithography, and silkscreen, including plate-making principles, printing processes, material properties, and color registration. Through foundational exercises and thematic projects in different print media, students develop the indirect thinking and technical language of printmaking. The course emphasizes printmaking's multiplicative and experimental qualities, guiding students to explore contemporary possibilities of the medium.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：主题系列版画创作——"城市印象"

学生需以"城市印象"为主题，选择至少两种不同版画媒材，创作一组6-8幅的系列版画作品。要求每幅作品尺寸统一（约30cm×40cm），形成视觉上的系列感，同时每幅具有独立的表现力。作品需体现对所选版种技法的熟练掌握，可运用套色、拼贴、综合材料等手法进行实验。需提交完整的创作方案、制版过程记录、试印样张与最终成品，并附创作说明阐述对城市主题的个人解读及版画语言的运用策略。

On the theme of "Urban Impressions," students create a series of 6-8 prints using at least two different printmaking media. Each piece must be uniform in size (approximately 30cm × 40cm) to form a visual series while maintaining independent expressive power. The work should demonstrate mastery of chosen print techniques, with experimentation through color registration, collage, and mixed media acceptable. Students submit a complete creative proposal, plate-making process documentation, trial proofs, and final edition prints.
