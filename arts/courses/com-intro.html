传播学导论

Introduction to Communication

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：秋季

本课程是传播学专业的入门课程，系统介绍传播学的基本概念、理论框架和研究领域。课程涵盖人际传播、组织传播、大众传播、跨文化传播等主要分支，探讨传播过程中的信息编码、解码、媒介选择和效果评估等核心问题。学生将通过案例分析和小组讨论，理解传播在社会、政治、经济和文化中的重要作用，为后续专业课程学习奠定基础。

This introductory course provides a comprehensive overview of communication studies, covering fundamental concepts, theoretical frameworks, and major subfields including interpersonal, organizational, mass, and intercultural communication. Students examine core processes such as encoding, decoding, media selection, and effect evaluation. Through case studies and group discussions, students develop an understanding of communication's role in society, politics, economics, and culture.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：当代传播现象的理论分析报告

学生需选取一个当代传播现象，运用至少两种传播学理论进行深入分析，探讨其传播机制、社会影响与文化意义。最终提交结构完整的学术分析报告。

Students select a contemporary communication phenomenon and analyze it using at least two communication theories, exploring its mechanisms, social impact, and cultural significance. A structured academic analysis report is submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：用传播学理论分析一个当代传播现象，比如某部爆款短剧的病毒传播路径。你需要运用拉斯韦尔5W模型和两级传播理论构建分析框架，收集该案例的传播数据和受众反馈，对比不同平台的传播效果差异，最后撰写一篇2500字的案例分析报告。
步骤 1
现象选题与文献综述
本步骤的核心任务是选定研究的传播现象并进行系统性的文献梳理，为后续分析奠定理论基础。选择一个有研究价值的当代传播现象至关重要，需要兼顾典型性、时效性和可分析性。通过广泛阅读相关学术文献，了解该领域的研究现状和理论资源，找出研究的切入点和创新点。

• 从当下传播热点中选取具有典型性的研究现象，如算法推荐、短视频传播、社交媒体政治参与、虚假信息传播等，界定研究范围和核心问题
• 运用中国知网、Web of Science、Google Scholar等学术数据库检索相关文献，按理论流派和研究视角进行分类整理，撰写文献综述
• 梳理至少两种传播学理论作为分析工具，如议程设置、使用与满足、框架理论、涵化理论、传播政治经济学等，明确各理论的核心观点和适用范围
产出：文献综述与研究问题（含现象描述、文献综述、研究问题、理论选择说明）｜ 质量标准：选题有研究价值，文献综述全面系统，研究问题明确具体
步骤 2
理论框架构建与研究设计
本步骤聚焦于建立分析的理论框架和研究路径，解决"用什么理论分析"和"如何分析"的问题。理论框架是研究的骨架，决定了分析的深度和广度。将不同理论有机结合，构建适合研究对象的分析视角，能使研究更具洞见和说服力。

• 深入研究所选理论的核心概念、基本假设和分析维度，绘制理论框架图，明确各理论要素之间的逻辑关系
• 设计研究路径和分析方法，确定采用个案研究、比较分析、文本分析还是话语分析等具体方法，明确分析单位和分析维度
• 提出具体的研究问题和初步研究假设，说明理论与现象之间的关联方式，预判研究可能的发现和贡献
产出：理论分析框架（含理论框架图、研究设计、分析维度说明、研究假设）｜ 质量标准：理论框架清晰，分析路径合理，理论与现象的结合点准确
步骤 3
案例分析与理论应用
本步骤是研究的核心论证环节，运用构建的理论框架对传播现象进行深入分析。通过具体案例的细致剖析，验证理论的解释力，发现理论的局限性，同时揭示现象背后的深层机制。分析过程要做到理论与材料紧密结合，既有理论高度又有经验厚度。

• 选取2-3个典型案例进行深入分析，运用文本分析法或内容分析法对案例材料进行系统编码和解读，提取关键分析范畴
• 从多个理论视角对同一现象进行交叉分析，比较不同理论的解释力和适用边界，发现理论之间的互补性和张力
• 结合社会、政治、经济、技术语境进行语境化分析，探讨传播现象背后的深层社会文化动因，避免简单化的技术决定论
产出：分析论证初稿（含案例分析、理论应用、交叉讨论、初步发现）｜ 质量标准：分析深入有洞见，理论与材料结合紧密，论证逻辑严密
步骤 4
报告撰写与学术规范
本步骤将分析成果转化为规范的学术文本，是研究的成果呈现阶段。一篇好的学术报告不仅要有好的研究内容，还要有清晰的结构和规范的表达。严格遵循学术规范，合理引用文献，是学术研究的基本素养要求。

• 按照学术论文规范结构撰写报告，包括摘要、关键词、引言、文献综述、理论框架、案例分析、结论与讨论、参考文献等部分
• 运用学术写作规范，确保论点明确、论据充分、论证严谨，使用学术化的语言表达，避免口语化和情绪化表述
• 严格遵循引文规范，使用GB/T 7714或APA格式标注引用文献，区分直接引用和间接引用，杜绝学术不端行为
产出：学术分析报告（含完整论文正文、参考文献、附录材料）｜ 质量标准：结构完整规范，论证逻辑清晰，引用格式准确，符合学术写作要求
步骤 5
成果汇报与研究反思
本步骤是研究的总结与升华环节，通过口头汇报和书面反思，梳理研究的主要发现，检讨研究的不足，展望未来的研究方向。汇报能锻炼学术表达能力，反思则能深化对研究过程的理解，两者都是学术能力提升的重要途径。

• 制作学术汇报PPT，提炼研究的核心观点和主要发现，设计清晰的视觉呈现，准备15-20分钟的口头汇报
• 撰写研究反思报告，总结研究过程中的收获与不足，分析研究的局限性，提出未来可以进一步深入的研究方向
• 整理所有研究材料，包括原始资料、分析笔记、草稿版本、最终报告，建立完整的研究档案
产出：汇报演示与研究反思（含汇报PPT、研究反思报告、完整研究档案）｜ 质量标准：汇报清晰有条理，反思深刻有见地，研究档案完整规范

Steps

Step 1
Phenomenon Selection & Literature Review
The core task of this step is to select a communication phenomenon for study and conduct systematic literature review, laying the theoretical foundation for subsequent analysis. Choosing a contemporary communication phenomenon with research value is crucial, requiring consideration of typicality, timeliness, and analyzability. Through extensive reading of relevant academic literature, understand the research status and theoretical resources in the field, identifying research entry points and innovations.

• Select typical research phenomena from current communication hotspots such as algorithmic recommendation, short video dissemination, social media political participation, or misinformation spread, define research scope and core questions
• Use academic databases such as CNKI, Web of Science, and Google Scholar to retrieve relevant literature, categorize by theoretical schools and research perspectives, write literature review
• Review at least two communication theories as analytical tools, such as agenda-setting, uses and gratifications, framing theory, cultivation theory, or political economy of communication, clarify core viewpoints and scope of application of each theory
Deliverable: Literature review and research questions (including phenomenon description, literature review, research questions, theoretical selection rationale) | Quality standard: Research topic has academic value, comprehensive and systematic literature review, clear and specific research questions
Step 2
Theoretical Framework Construction & Research Design
This step focuses on establishing the analytical theoretical framework and research path, addressing the questions of "which theories to use for analysis" and "how to analyze." The theoretical framework is the skeleton of the study, determining the depth and breadth of analysis. Organically combining different theories to construct analytical perspectives suitable for the research object can make the study more insightful and persuasive.

• Deeply study core concepts, basic assumptions, and analytical dimensions of selected theories, draw theoretical framework diagram, clarify logical relationships between various theoretical elements
• Design research path and analytical methods, determine whether to use case study, comparative analysis, text analysis, or discourse analysis, clarify unit of analysis and analytical dimensions
• Propose specific research questions and preliminary research hypotheses, explain the connection between theory and phenomenon, anticipate potential findings and contributions of the study
Deliverable: Theoretical analytical framework (including theoretical framework diagram, research design, analytical dimension explanation, research hypotheses) | Quality standard: Clear theoretical framework, reasonable analytical path, accurate connection point between theory and phenomenon
Step 3
Case Analysis & Theory Application
This step is the core argumentation phase of the study, applying the constructed theoretical framework to conduct in-depth analysis of the communication phenomenon. Through detailed analysis of specific cases, verify the explanatory power of the theory, discover its limitations, and reveal the deep mechanisms behind the phenomenon. The analysis process should achieve close integration of theory and materials, with both theoretical height and empirical depth.

• Select 2-3 typical cases for in-depth analysis, use text analysis or content analysis to systematically code and interpret case materials, extract key analytical categories
• Conduct cross-analysis of the same phenomenon from multiple theoretical perspectives, compare explanatory power and applicable boundaries of different theories, discover complementarity and tension between theories
• Conduct contextualized analysis combined with social, political, economic, and technological contexts, explore deep socio-cultural motivations behind the communication phenomenon, avoid simplistic technological determinism
Deliverable: Analysis and argumentation draft (including case analysis, theory application, cross-discussion, preliminary findings) | Quality standard: In-depth and insightful analysis, tight integration of theory and materials, rigorous argumentation logic
Step 4
Report Writing & Academic Standards
This step transforms analytical results into standardized academic texts, serving as the results presentation phase of the study. A good academic report requires not only good research content but also clear structure and standardized expression. Strictly following academic norms and properly citing literature is a fundamental requirement for academic research.

• Write the report according to standard academic paper structure, including abstract, keywords, introduction, literature review, theoretical framework, case analysis, conclusion and discussion, references, and other sections
• Apply academic writing standards, ensure clear arguments, sufficient evidence, rigorous reasoning, use academic language expression, avoid colloquial and emotional expressions
• Strictly follow citation standards, use GB/T 7714 or APA format for citing references, distinguish between direct and indirect citations,杜绝 academic misconduct
Deliverable: Academic analysis report (including complete paper text, references, appendix materials) | Quality standard: Complete and standardized structure, clear argumentation logic, accurate citation format, meets academic writing requirements
Step 5
Presentation & Research Reflection
This step is the summary and sublimation phase of the study, through oral presentation and written reflection, sorting out main findings of the study, reviewing shortcomings, and envisioning future research directions. Presentations develop academic communication skills, while reflection deepens understanding of the research process—both are important pathways for improving academic ability.

• Create academic presentation PPT, extract core viewpoints and main findings of the study, design clear visual presentation, prepare 15-20 minute oral presentation
• Write research reflection report, summarize gains and shortcomings during the research process, analyze limitations of the study, propose future research directions for further exploration
• Organize all research materials including raw data, analysis notes, draft versions, final report, establish complete research archive
Deliverable: Presentation and research reflection (including presentation PPT, research reflection report, complete research archive) | Quality standard: Clear and organized presentation, profound and insightful reflection, complete and standardized research archive
