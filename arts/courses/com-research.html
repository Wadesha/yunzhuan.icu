传播研究方法

Communication Research Methods

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：传播学导论 | 学期：秋季

本课程系统介绍传播学研究的方法论体系，培养学生从事传播研究的基本能力。课程涵盖研究设计、抽样方法、数据收集和统计分析等内容，重点讲授问卷调查、内容分析、深度访谈、焦点小组、实验法等传播研究常用方法。学生将学习研究问题提出、文献综述、研究方案设计、数据分析和研究报告撰写的完整流程，培养科学研究的思维方式和实践能力，为毕业论文和学术研究奠定方法论基础。

This course systematically introduces communication research methodology, building students' foundational research capabilities. Coverage includes research design, sampling methods, data collection, and statistical analysis, with emphasis on survey research, content analysis, in-depth interviews, focus groups, and experimental methods. Students learn the complete research process from question formulation and literature review to research design, data analysis, and report writing, developing scientific thinking and practical skills for theses and academic research.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：传播学实证研究设计与报告

学生需围绕一个传播学研究问题，设计并实施一项实证研究，运用问卷、访谈或内容分析等方法收集与分析数据。最终提交完整的研究报告。

Students design and conduct an empirical study on a communication research question, using surveys, interviews, or content analysis to collect and analyze data. A complete research report is submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：针对一个具体的传播现象做实证研究，比如大学生社交媒体使用与焦虑情绪的关系。你需要设计研究问题和假设，选择问卷调查法或内容分析法，收集至少100份有效样本数据，用SPSS做描述性统计和相关性分析，最后撰写符合学术规范的研究报告。
步骤 1
研究问题与研究设计
本步骤的核心任务是确定研究问题并进行系统化的研究设计，为整个研究项目奠定方法论基础。好的研究始于好的问题，研究问题的清晰度和价值直接决定了研究的质量和意义。严谨的研究设计则确保研究过程科学规范，研究结果可靠有效。

• 结合课程内容和个人兴趣，初步确定研究方向，通过探索性阅读和初步调研，逐步聚焦形成具体的研究问题，撰写选题说明
• 明确研究范式和方法论基础，根据研究问题选择合适的研究方法，如问卷调查法、内容分析法、深度访谈法、焦点小组法、实验法、个案研究等
• 设计研究方案，明确研究对象、抽样方法、样本量、研究变量、测量工具、数据收集程序和分析方法，撰写完整的研究计划书
产出：研究设计方案（含研究问题、文献综述、研究方法、研究计划）｜ 质量标准：研究问题明确有价值，研究方法选择恰当，设计方案科学可行
步骤 2
研究工具开发与预测试
本步骤聚焦于开发具体的研究工具并进行预测试，确保研究工具的信度和效度。研究工具是数据收集的仪器，工具的质量直接决定了数据质量和研究结论的可靠性。通过严谨的工具开发和预测试，能发现并修正工具中的问题，为正式研究奠定坚实基础。

• 根据研究设计开发研究工具，如设计调查问卷（包括人口统计学变量、核心变量测量、量表选择）、访谈提纲、内容分析编码表等
• 进行文献检索和量表选择，选择经过验证的成熟量表或自行开发测量工具，确保测量工具具有良好的内容效度和构念效度
• 进行预测试（Pilot Study），选取小样本进行试测，检验研究工具的可行性和质量，根据预测试结果进行修改和完善
产出：研究工具与预测试报告（含调查问卷/访谈提纲/编码表、预测试结果、工具修改说明）｜ 质量标准：研究工具设计科学规范，预测试充分，信效度有保障
步骤 3
数据收集与质量控制
本步骤是研究的核心数据采集环节，按照研究设计执行正式的数据收集工作。高质量的数据是研究结论可靠的前提，需要严格遵循研究方案，确保数据收集过程的规范性和数据质量。同时要注意研究伦理，保护研究参与者的权益。

• 执行正式的数据收集，如发放问卷（线上平台如问卷星、SurveyMonkey，线下调查）、进行深度访谈、收集媒介内容样本等，确保样本量和抽样方法符合研究设计
• 实施质量控制措施，包括调查员培训、现场督导、数据录入双重录入、逻辑校验等，确保数据收集过程规范，减少系统误差和随机误差
• 遵守研究伦理规范，获得研究参与者知情同意，保护参与者隐私和数据安全，对敏感信息进行匿名化处理，确保研究符合伦理要求
产出：原始数据集与质量控制记录（含原始数据、数据收集记录、质量控制报告、伦理审查说明）｜ 质量标准：数据收集规范，样本量达标，质量控制措施到位，伦理合规
步骤 4
数据分析与结果呈现
本步骤是研究的核心分析环节，运用统计方法或质性分析方法对收集的数据进行系统分析，回答研究问题。数据分析需要根据研究设计和数据类型选择合适的分析方法，确保分析过程科学规范，结果解释合理可靠。好的分析不仅要呈现统计结果，更要解释其理论和实践意义。

• 进行数据分析，定量研究运用SPSS、R、Python等统计软件进行描述性统计、相关分析、回归分析、假设检验等；定性研究进行编码分析、主题分析、话语分析等
• 进行数据可视化，制作统计图表（柱状图、折线图、饼图、散点图、热力图等）直观呈现分析结果，图表规范清晰
• 解释分析结果，结合研究问题和理论框架，对统计结果或质性发现进行深入解读，回答研究问题，讨论结果的理论意义和实践启示
产出：数据分析报告（含数据分析结果、统计图表、结果解释、初步结论）｜ 质量标准：分析方法正确，结果呈现清晰，解释深入合理
步骤 5
研究报告撰写与成果展示
本步骤是研究的收官环节，将研究过程和结果整理成规范的研究报告，并进行成果展示。研究报告是研究成果的集中体现，需要结构完整、逻辑清晰、表达规范。成果展示则是学术交流的重要环节，能锻炼学术表达能力和获得反馈改进研究。

• 撰写完整的研究报告，按照学术规范包括摘要、关键词、引言、文献综述、研究方法、研究结果、讨论、结论、参考文献、附录等部分
• 制作研究展示PPT，提炼研究的核心发现和主要结论，设计清晰的视觉呈现，准备口头汇报和答辩
• 整理研究档案，包括研究原始数据、分析代码、研究工具、各版本草稿等所有研究材料，建立完整的研究档案
产出：研究报告与展示材料（含完整研究报告、展示PPT、研究档案）｜ 质量标准：报告结构完整规范，论证逻辑严密，展示清晰专业

Steps

Step 1
Research Questions & Research Design
The core task of this step is to determine research questions and conduct systematic research design, laying the methodological foundation for the entire research project. Good research begins with good questions; the clarity and value of research questions directly determine the quality and significance of the research. Rigorous research design ensures the research process is scientific and standardized, and research results are reliable and valid.

• Combine course content and personal interests to initially determine research direction, through exploratory reading and preliminary investigation, gradually focus to form specific research questions, write topic statement
• Clarify research paradigm and methodological foundation, select appropriate research methods according to research questions, such as questionnaire survey, content analysis, in-depth interview, focus group, experiment, case study, etc.
• Design research plan, clarify research subjects, sampling method, sample size, research variables, measurement tools, data collection procedures and analysis methods, write complete research proposal
Deliverable: Research design plan (including research questions, literature review, research methods, research plan) | Quality standard: Clear and valuable research questions, appropriate research method selection, scientific and feasible design plan
Step 2
Research Instrument Development & Pilot Testing
This step focuses on developing specific research instruments and conducting pilot testing, ensuring the reliability and validity of research instruments. Research instruments are the tools for data collection; the quality of instruments directly determines data quality and reliability of research conclusions. Through rigorous instrument development and pilot testing, problems in instruments can be identified and corrected, laying a solid foundation for formal research.

• Develop research tools based on research design, such as designing survey questionnaires (including demographic variables, core variable measurement, scale selection), interview guides, content analysis coding schemes, etc.
• Conduct literature search and scale selection, select validated mature scales or self-develop measurement tools, ensure measurement tools have good content validity and construct validity
• Conduct Pilot Study, select small samples for trial testing,检验 feasibility and quality of research instruments, revise and improve based on pilot test results
Deliverable: Research instruments and pilot test report (including questionnaire/interview guide/coding scheme, pilot test results, instrument revision explanation) | Quality standard: Scientifically and normatively designed research instruments, thorough pilot testing, guaranteed reliability and validity
Step 3
Data Collection & Quality Control
This step is the core data collection phase of the study, executing formal data collection according to the research design. High-quality data is the prerequisite for reliable research conclusions; it is necessary to strictly follow the research plan, ensuring standardization of the data collection process and data quality. Also pay attention to research ethics and protect the rights and interests of research participants.

• Execute formal data collection, such as distributing questionnaires (online platforms like Wenjuanxing, SurveyMonkey, offline surveys), conducting in-depth interviews, collecting media content samples, etc., ensure sample size and sampling methods meet research design
• Implement quality control measures including investigator training, on-site supervision, double data entry, logic verification, etc., ensure standardized data collection process, reduce systematic error and random error
• Comply with research ethics规范, obtain informed consent from research participants, protect participant privacy and data security, anonymize sensitive information, ensure research meets ethical requirements
Deliverable: Raw dataset and quality control records (including raw data, data collection records, quality control report, ethics review explanation) | Quality standard: Standardized data collection,达标 sample size,precise quality control measures, ethical compliance
Step 4
Data Analysis & Results Presentation
This step is the core analysis phase of the study, systematically analyzing collected data using statistical methods or qualitative analysis methods to answer research questions. Data analysis needs to select appropriate analytical methods according to research design and data type, ensuring scientific and standardized analysis process and reasonable and reliable result interpretation. Good analysis should not only present statistical results but also explain their theoretical and practical significance.

• Conduct data analysis, quantitative research uses statistical software such as SPSS, R, Python for descriptive statistics, correlation analysis, regression analysis, hypothesis testing, etc.; qualitative research conducts coding analysis, thematic analysis, discourse analysis, etc.
• Perform data visualization, create statistical charts (bar charts, line charts, pie charts, scatter plots, heatmaps, etc.) to intuitively present analysis results, with standardized and clear charts
• Interpret analysis results,结合 research questions and theoretical framework, conduct in-depth interpretation of statistical results or qualitative findings, answer research questions, discuss theoretical significance and practical implications of results
Deliverable: Data analysis report (including data analysis results, statistical charts, result interpretation, preliminary conclusions) | Quality standard: Correct analytical methods, clear result presentation, in-depth and reasonable interpretation
Step 5
Research Report Writing &成果demonstrate
This step is the concluding phase of the study, organizing the research process and results into a standardized research report and presenting the results. The research report is the concentrated embodiment of research results, requiring complete structure, clear logic, and standardized expression. Result presentation is an important part of academic communication, which can exercise academic expression ability and obtain feedback to improve research.

• Write complete research report, according to academic standards including abstract, keywords, introduction, literature review, research methods, research results, discussion, conclusion, references, appendix, etc.
• Create research presentation PPT, extract core findings and main conclusions of the study, design clear visual presentation, prepare oral presentation and defense
• Organize research archive, including all research materials such as raw data, analysis code, research tools, various draft versions, establish complete research archive
Deliverable: Research report and presentation materials (including complete research report, presentation PPT, research archive) | Quality standard: Complete and standardized report structure, rigorous argumentation logic, clear and professional presentation
