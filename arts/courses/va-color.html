色彩理论

Color Theory

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：素描基础 | 学期：第二学期

本课程是视觉艺术专业的理论与实践结合课程，系统研究色彩的科学原理与艺术应用。课程内容涵盖色彩物理学、色彩生理学、色彩心理学、色彩体系与色立体、色彩对比与调和规律等核心理论。通过大量的色彩构成练习、色调练习、色彩情感表达练习，帮助学生建立科学的色彩认知体系。课程强调理论与实践并重，引导学生理解色彩的视觉规律与情感力量，为绘画、设计、数字媒体等各专业方向提供色彩表现的理论支撑与实践方法。

This combined theory and practice course systematically studies the scientific principles and artistic applications of color. Topics include color physics, color physiology, color psychology, color systems and color solids, and principles of color contrast and harmony. Through extensive color composition exercises, tonal studies, and color emotion expression exercises, students build a scientific understanding of color. The course emphasizes equal parts theory and practice, guiding students to understand color's visual laws and emotional power, providing theoretical support and practical methods for painting, design, and digital media.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：色彩情感系列构成设计

学生需选择四种不同的情感主题（如喜悦、忧伤、宁静、躁动），为每种情感创作一幅25cm×25cm的色彩构成作品。要求不使用任何具象图形，仅通过色彩的色相、明度、纯度、面积、位置等要素的组织来传达特定情感。需提交完整的色彩分析报告，说明每幅作品的色彩选择依据、配色方案、视觉心理效果，并结合课程中的色彩对比与调和理论进行自我评述，展示对色彩语言的深度理解与创造性运用能力。

Students select four distinct emotional themes—such as joy, sorrow, tranquility, and restlessness—and create a 25cm × 25cm color composition for each. Using no representational imagery, students convey specific emotions solely through the organization of hue, value, chroma, area, and position. A complete color analysis report explains the rationale behind color choices, color schemes, and visual-psychological effects for each piece, with self-critique grounded in course concepts of color contrast and harmony, demonstrating deep understanding and creative application of color language.
