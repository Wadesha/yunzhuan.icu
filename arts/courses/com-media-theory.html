媒介理论

Media Theory

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：传播学导论 | 学期：秋季

本课程深入探讨媒介与社会的关系，系统介绍主要的媒介理论流派和经典研究。课程涵盖媒介技术决定论、媒介生态学、议程设置理论、涵化理论、使用与满足理论、框架理论、政治经济学派、文化研究学派等核心理论，探讨媒介对认知、态度、行为和社会结构的影响。学生将通过阅读经典文献和当代案例，批判性地分析媒介在现代社会中的角色和作用，提升理论思辨能力。

This course explores the relationship between media and society, systematically introducing major media theory schools and classic studies. Coverage includes technological determinism, media ecology, agenda-setting, cultivation theory, uses and gratifications, framing theory, political economy, and cultural studies. Students examine media's impact on cognition, attitudes, behavior, and social structure through classic readings and contemporary cases, developing critical theoretical thinking abilities.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：媒介理论与当代议题批评论文

学生需选取一种媒介理论，结合当代媒介现象撰写一篇批判性学术论文，论证理论解释力与局限性。最终提交符合学术规范的批评论文。

Students select a media theory and write a critical academic paper applying it to a contemporary media phenomenon, arguing its explanatory power and limitations. A scholarly critical essay is submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
理论选择与文献梳理
本步骤的核心任务是选择核心媒介理论并进行系统性的文献梳理，为后续的批评分析奠定理论基础。媒介理论是理解当代媒介现象的透镜，不同的理论视角会揭示不同的面向。通过广泛阅读理论原典和相关研究文献，深入把握理论的核心观点、发展脉络和批评取向。

• 从课程涵盖的媒介理论中选择一种作为核心分析框架，如议程设置理论、涵化理论、使用与满足、框架理论、媒介生态学、政治经济学派等
• 系统检索和阅读相关研究文献，包括理论应用研究、批评性研究和当代案例研究，按照研究视角和核心观点进行分类整理，撰写文献综述
• 选取1-2个具有典型性的当代媒介案例，如某平台算法推荐机制、某网络事件的传播过程、某新媒介技术的社会影响等，确保案例具有分析深度
产出：文献综述与研究设计（含理论选择说明、文献综述、案例选择、研究问题）｜ 质量标准：理论选择有针对性，文献梳理全面系统，研究问题明确具体
步骤 2
理论框架构建与案例选取
本步骤聚焦于构建分析的理论框架并选取合适的案例，解决"用什么理论分析什么案例"的问题。理论框架的清晰度直接决定了分析的深度和批判性。需要将理论的核心概念转化为可操作的分析维度，同时选取具有典型性和丰富性的案例进行深度剖析。

• 深入研读理论原典，梳理理论的发展脉络、核心假设、关键概念和方法论特征，绘制理论谱系图，明确理论的解释边界和适用范围
• 构建分析框架，将抽象的理论概念转化为具体的分析维度和观察指标，设计分析路径和论证逻辑，形成清晰的研究设计
• 选取2-3个具有典型性的当代媒介案例，确保案例具有足够的信息丰富度和分析深度，能够有效检验理论的解释力和局限性
产出：理论分析框架与案例说明（含理论框架图、分析维度、案例背景介绍、研究设计）｜ 质量标准：理论框架清晰，分析维度明确，案例选择恰当且具有分析价值
步骤 3
案例深描与理论应用
本步骤是研究的核心分析环节，运用理论工具对案例进行深入的批判性分析。分析不能停留在理论套用层面，而要在理论与经验的对话中产生新的洞见。既要验证理论的解释力，也要发现理论在新媒介环境下面临的挑战和修正空间。

• 对案例进行深度描述，还原案例的发生背景、发展过程、关键节点和社会影响，运用厚描（Thick Description）方法呈现案例的复杂性和丰富性
• 运用核心理论的分析维度对案例进行系统剖析，从多个角度揭示案例所折射出的媒介逻辑、权力关系、意识形态等深层机制
• 进行理论与经验的双向对话，既用理论解释案例，也用案例反思理论，探讨理论在数字媒介时代的适用性和局限性
产出：案例分析与理论对话（含案例深描、理论应用分析、理论反思讨论）｜ 质量标准：分析深入有洞见，理论与案例结合紧密，批判性反思有深度
步骤 4
批评论证与论文撰写
本步骤将分析成果转化为规范的学术论文，是研究的成果呈现阶段。批评性论文不仅要有独到的见解，还要有严密的论证和规范的表达。严格遵循学术写作规范，合理引用文献，是学术批评的基本素养要求。

• 按照学术论文规范撰写完整论文，包括摘要、关键词、引言、文献综述、理论框架、案例分析、批评讨论、结论等部分，字数符合要求
• 构建清晰的论证逻辑，从问题意识出发，通过理论分析和案例论证，逐步推进论点，确保论证链条完整有力
• 严格遵循学术规范，使用规范的引文格式，区分直接引用和间接引用，杜绝抄袭和学术不端行为
产出：学术批评论文（含完整论文正文、参考文献、注释、附录）｜ 质量标准：论证逻辑严密，批评有深度，结构完整规范，符合学术写作要求
步骤 5
同行评议与修改完善
本步骤是研究的质量提升和反思完善环节，通过同行评议和自我反思，进一步打磨论文质量。学术研究是一个共同体的事业，同行的反馈能帮助研究者发现盲点、深化认识。通过多轮修改完善，能让论文的论证更加严密、观点更加成熟。

• 组织同行互评或邀请同学阅读论文，收集反馈意见，包括论点清晰度、论证充分性、案例恰当性、写作规范性等方面的评价和建议
• 根据反馈意见进行修改完善，对论文进行多轮修改打磨，包括调整论证结构、补充分析深度、完善文献引用规范语言表达等方面的优化
• 撰写研究反思，总结研究过程中的收获与不足，分析研究的局限性，提出未来可以进一步深入的研究方向
产出：修改完善的终稿与研究反思（含论文终稿、修改说明、研究反思报告）｜ 质量标准：论文质量显著提升，修改认真细致，反思深刻有见地

Steps

Step 1
Theory Selection & Literature Review
The core task of this step is to select core media theory and conduct systematic literature review, laying the theoretical foundation for subsequent critical analysis. Media theory is the lens for understanding contemporary media phenomena; different theoretical perspectives reveal different dimensions. Through extensive reading of theoretical classics and related research literature, deeply grasp the core viewpoints, development脉络, and critical orientation of the theory.

• Choose one theory from the course as the core analytical framework, such as agenda-setting theory, cultivation theory, uses and gratifications, framing theory, media ecology, political economy school, etc.
• Systematically retrieve and read relevant research literature, including theoretical application studies, critical studies, and contemporary case studies, categorize by research perspective and core viewpoints, write literature review
• Select 1-2 typical contemporary media cases, such as a platform's algorithm recommendation mechanism, the spread of a certain online event, the social impact of a new media technology, etc., ensure cases have analytical depth
Deliverable: Literature review and research design (including theory selection explanation, literature review, case selection, research questions) | Quality standard: Targeted theory selection, comprehensive and systematic literature review, clear and specific research questions
Step 2
Theoretical Framework Construction & Case Selection
This step focuses on constructing the analytical theoretical framework and selecting appropriate cases, addressing the question of "which theory to use to analyze which cases." The clarity of the theoretical framework directly determines the depth and criticality of the analysis. It is necessary to transform core theoretical concepts into operational analytical dimensions, while selecting typical and rich cases for in-depth analysis.

• Deeply study theoretical classics, sort out the development context, core assumptions, key concepts, and methodological characteristics of the theory, draw theoretical genealogy diagram, clarify the explanatory boundaries and scope of application of the theory
• Construct analytical framework, transform abstract theoretical concepts into specific analytical dimensions and observation indicators, design analytical paths and argumentation logic, form clear research design
• Select 2-3 typical contemporary media cases, ensure cases have sufficient information richness and analytical depth, can effectively test the explanatory power and limitations of the theory
Deliverable: Theoretical analytical framework and case description (including theoretical framework diagram, analytical dimensions, case background introduction, research design) | Quality standard: Clear theoretical framework, clear analytical dimensions, appropriate and analytically valuable case selection
Step 3
Case Thick Description & Theory Application
This step is the core analytical phase of the study, using theoretical tools to conduct in-depth critical analysis of cases. Analysis cannot stop at theoretical application, but must generate new insights in the dialogue between theory and experience. It is necessary to both verify the explanatory power of the theory and discover the challenges and revision spaces the theory faces in the new media environment.

• Conduct thick description of cases,还原 the background, development process, key nodes and social impact of cases, use Thick Description method to present the complexity and richness of cases
• Use analytical dimensions of core theory to systematically analyze cases, reveal the deep mechanisms such as media logic, power relations, ideology, etc. reflected by cases from multiple angles
• Conduct two-way dialogue between theory and experience, both explain cases with theory, and reflect on theory with cases, explore the applicability and limitations of theory in the digital media era
Deliverable: Case analysis and theoretical dialogue (including thick case description, theoretical application analysis, theoretical reflection discussion) | Quality standard: In-depth and insightful analysis, tight integration of theory and cases, profound critical reflection
Step 4
Critical Argumentation & Paper Writing
This step transforms analytical results into standardized academic papers, serving as the result presentation phase of the study. Critical papers require not only original insights but also rigorous argumentation and standardized expression. Strictly following academic writing norms and properly citing literature is a fundamental literacy requirement for academic criticism.

• Write complete paper according to academic paper standards, including abstract, keywords, introduction, literature review, theoretical framework, case analysis, critical discussion, conclusion, etc., meet word count requirements
• Construct clear argumentation logic, starting from problem consciousness, through theoretical analysis and case argumentation, gradually advance arguments, ensure complete and powerful argumentation chain
• Strictly follow academic norms, use standardized citation format, distinguish between direct and indirect citations,杜绝 plagiarism and academic misconduct
Deliverable: Academic critical paper (including complete paper text, references, annotations, appendix) | Quality standard: Rigorous argumentation logic, profound criticism, complete and standardized structure, meets academic writing requirements
Step 5
Peer Review & Revision Improvement
This step is the quality improvement and reflection perfection phase of the study, through peer review and self-reflection, further polishing paper quality. Academic research is a communal enterprise; peer feedback helps researchers discover blind spots and deepen understanding. Through multiple rounds of revision and improvement, the paper's argumentation becomes more rigorous and viewpoints more mature.

• Organize peer review or invite classmates to read the paper, collect feedback including evaluation and suggestions on argument clarity, argument sufficiency, case appropriateness, writing standardization, etc.
• Revise and improve based on feedback, conduct multiple rounds of revision and polishing of the paper, including adjusting argumentation structure, supplementing analytical depth, improving citation规范 language expression, etc.
• Write research reflection, summarize gains and shortcomings during the research process, analyze limitations of the study, propose future research directions for further exploration
Deliverable: Revised final draft and research reflection (including final paper, revision explanation, research reflection report) | Quality standard: Significantly improved paper quality, careful and detailed revision, profound and insightful reflection
