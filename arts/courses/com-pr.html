公共关系

Public Relations

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：传播学导论 | 学期：秋季

本课程系统介绍公共关系的理论与实践，涵盖公关历史发展、核心概念、职业道德和实务操作。学生将学习媒体关系、危机公关、活动策划、品牌传播、内部沟通、政府关系等主要职能模块，掌握新闻稿撰写、新闻发布会组织、社交媒体运营、利益相关者管理等专业技能。课程结合真实案例分析，培养学生的战略思维和公关策划能力，以及应对复杂传播环境的实践能力。

This course provides a systematic introduction to public relations theory and practice, covering historical development, core concepts, professional ethics, and practical operations. Students learn media relations, crisis PR, event planning, brand communication, internal communication, and government relations, mastering skills like press release writing, press conference organization, social media management, and stakeholder engagement. Real case studies develop strategic thinking and planning abilities.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：品牌公关活动策划与危机应对方案

学生需为一个组织策划整合公关方案，包括品牌公关活动策划与一份危机公关应对预案。最终提交公关策划书与危机应对手册。

Students plan an integrated PR program for an organization, including a brand PR campaign and a crisis response plan. A PR proposal and crisis management manual are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：选一个组织或事件，策划公关活动——目标、策略、执行、评估，做出完整方案。
步骤 1
品牌诊断与形象评估
本步骤的核心任务是对品牌的公关现状进行全面诊断和形象评估，为后续公关策划奠定基础。通过系统性的品牌审计和舆情分析，了解品牌的公众形象、声誉状况和利益相关者关系，找出品牌的优势、劣势、机会和威胁。清晰的现状诊断是制定有效公关策略的前提。

• 运用品牌资产模型和声誉管理框架，收集品牌舆情数据、媒体报道、消费者评价，进行SWOT分析和声誉审计，评估品牌公众形象现状
• 识别关键利益相关者，包括媒体、消费者、员工、投资者、政府、社区等，分析各利益相关者的诉求、影响力和关系质量，绘制利益相关者地图
• 进行竞品公关对比分析，研究主要竞争对手的公关策略、传播内容和媒体关系，找出品牌的差异化机会和竞争优势
产出：品牌诊断报告（含品牌形象评估、利益相关者分析、竞品分析、SWOT分析）｜ 质量标准：诊断全面深入，数据支撑充分，问题识别准确
步骤 2
公关策略与传播规划
本步骤聚焦于制定整体公关策略和传播规划，明确公关目标、核心信息和传播路径。公关策略是连接品牌现状与目标之间的桥梁，需要基于品牌诊断的结果，制定清晰的战略方向和实施路径。好的策略能确保公关活动有的放矢、事半功倍。

• 运用公关传播模型（如ROPE模型、RACE模型）制定整体传播策略，明确传播主题、核心信息、目标受众、传播渠道和时间节奏
• 设定SMART原则的公关目标，从认知度、美誉度、和谐度三个维度设定量化目标，包括媒体曝光量、品牌认知度、美誉度提升、 stakeholder关系改善等具体指标
• 设计核心信息屋（Message House），提炼品牌的核心信息和支撑论据，确保不同传播渠道的信息一致性和协同性
产出：公关策略方案（含传播策略、目标体系、核心信息、传播路径图）｜ 质量标准：策略定位准确，目标清晰可衡量，信息架构完整
步骤 3
媒体关系与传播执行
本步骤是公关传播的核心执行环节，通过专业的媒体关系管理和传播内容制作，确保公关信息有效触达目标受众。媒体关系是公关的核心能力之一，良好的媒体关系能为品牌提供有力的舆论支持。高质量的传播内容则是确保传播效果的关键。

• 建立媒体清单和记者数据库，按媒体类型、领域、影响力进行分类，制定媒体沟通计划，主动进行媒体关系维护和定期沟通
• 撰写各类传播物料，包括新闻稿、专访提纲、背景资料、Q&A等，遵循新闻写作规范，确保内容具有新闻价值和传播价值
• 组织媒体活动，如新闻发布会、媒体专访、媒体参观、媒体沙龙等，设计活动流程和应急预案，确保活动顺利进行和传播效果
产出：媒体关系与传播执行（含媒体清单、传播物料、媒体活动方案、传播记录）｜ 质量标准：媒体关系良好，传播内容专业，活动执行到位
步骤 4
活动策划与执行管理
本步骤聚焦于品牌公关活动的策划与执行，通过创意性的公关活动提升品牌形象和公众参与度。公关活动是品牌与公众直接互动的重要平台，好的活动能创造话题、增强体验、深化关系。从策划到执行的全流程管理能力是公关专业素养的重要体现。

• 制定详细的执行时间表和分工表，明确各岗位职责，进行活动场地布置、物料准备、嘉宾邀请、流程彩排等前期准备工作
• 设计创意活动形式和互动体验环节，运用事件营销、公益活动、品牌发布会、庆典活动等多种形式，增强参与感和传播性，创造社交媒体传播话题
• 进行现场执行管理和应急处理，建立应急团队，确保活动顺利进行，及时处理突发情况，保障活动安全和效果
产出：公关活动策划与执行方案（含活动策划书、执行时间表、物料清单、应急预案）｜ 质量标准：活动创意新颖，执行细节到位，应急预案完善
步骤 5
效果评估与危机预案
本步骤是公关工作的收官环节，评估公关传播效果并制定危机公关应对预案。效果评估能检验公关工作的成效，为后续优化提供依据。危机预案则是品牌风险管理的重要组成部分，帮助品牌在突发危机时能够快速、有效地应对，维护品牌声誉。

• 运用公关效果评估模型，从媒体曝光、受众认知、态度改变、行为影响等维度评估传播效果，对比目标KPI进行成效分析，撰写效果评估报告
• 制定危机公关预案，识别潜在危机风险点，制定分级响应机制和处理原则，设计危机应对话术和发言人制度，建立危机管理团队和流程
• 建立长期声誉管理机制，建立舆情监测体系，定期进行声誉审计，制定持续的媒体关系维护和品牌传播计划，实现公关工作的常态化和体系化
产出：效果评估报告与危机预案（含效果评估报告、危机公关手册、声誉管理方案）｜ 质量标准：评估体系科学，危机预案完善，长效机制健全

Steps

Step 1
Brand Diagnosis & Image Assessment
The core task of this step is to conduct comprehensive diagnosis and image assessment of the brand's PR status, laying the foundation for subsequent PR planning. Through systematic brand audit and public opinion analysis, understand the brand's public image, reputation status, and stakeholder relationships, identify brand strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. Clear current situation diagnosis is the prerequisite for formulating effective PR strategies.

• Apply brand equity model and reputation management framework, collect brand public opinion data, media reports, consumer reviews, conduct SWOT analysis and reputation audit, assess current brand public image status
• Identify key stakeholders including media, consumers, employees, investors, government, community, etc., analyze demands, influence, and relationship quality of each stakeholder, draw stakeholder map
• Conduct competitor PR comparative analysis, study PR strategies, communication content, and media relations of main competitors, find brand differentiation opportunities and competitive advantages
Deliverable: Brand diagnosis report (including brand image assessment, stakeholder analysis, competitor analysis, SWOT analysis) | Quality standard: Comprehensive and in-depth diagnosis, sufficient data support, accurate problem identification
Step 2
PR Strategy & Communication Planning
This step focuses on formulating overall PR strategy and communication plan, clarifying PR goals, core messages, and communication paths. PR strategy is the bridge connecting brand status and goals, requiring clear strategic direction and implementation path based on brand diagnosis results. A good strategy ensures PR activities are targeted and achieve twice the result with half the effort.

• Apply PR communication models (such as ROPE model, RACE model) to develop overall communication strategy, clarify communication theme, core messages, target audiences, communication channels, and timeline
• Set SMART PR objectives, set quantitative indicators from three dimensions: awareness, reputation, harmony degree, including media exposure volume, brand awareness, reputation improvement, stakeholder relationship improvement and other specific indicators
• Design core Message House, extract brand core messages and supporting arguments, ensure information consistency and synergy across different communication channels
Deliverable: PR strategy plan (including communication strategy, goal system, core messages, communication path map) | Quality standard: Accurate strategic positioning, clear and measurable goals, complete message architecture
Step 3
Media Relations & Communication Execution
This step is the core execution phase of PR communication, ensuring PR information effectively reaches target audiences through professional media relationship management and communication content production. Media relations is one of the core competencies of PR; good media relations can provide strong public opinion support for the brand. High-quality communication content is the key to ensuring communication effectiveness.

• Build media list and journalist database, categorize by media type, field, and influence, develop media communication plan, proactively maintain media relations and conduct regular communication
• Write various communication materials including press releases, interview outlines, background materials, Q&A, etc., follow journalistic writing standards, ensure content has news value and communication value
• Organize media events such as press conferences, media interviews, media tours, media salons, etc., design event flow and emergency plans, ensure smooth event progress and communication effectiveness
Deliverable: Media relations and communication execution (including media list, communication materials, media event plan, communication records) | Quality standard: Good media relations, professional communication content, well-executed activities
Step 4
Event Planning & Execution Management
This step focuses on brand PR event planning and execution, enhancing brand image and public participation through creative PR activities. PR events are important platforms for direct interaction between the brand and the public; good events can create topics, enhance experience, and deepen relationships. Full-process management capability from planning to execution is an important manifestation of PR professional literacy.

• Develop detailed execution timeline and responsibility chart, clarify duties of each position, conduct venue setup, material preparation, guest invitation, process rehearsal and other preparatory work
• Design creative event formats and interactive experiencephase, apply event marketing, public welfare activities, press conferences, celebration events and other forms, enhance sense of participation and communicability, create social media传播话题
• Conduct on-site execution management and emergency handling, establish emergency team, ensure smooth event progress, timely handle unexpected situations, guarantee event safety and effectiveness
Deliverable: PR event planning and execution plan (including event proposal, execution timeline, materials list, emergency plan) | Quality standard: Novel event creativity, detailed execution details, complete emergency plan
Step 5
Effectiveness Evaluation & Crisis Preparedness
This step is the concluding phase of PR work, evaluating PR communication effectiveness and formulating crisis PR response plans. Effectiveness evaluation can test the effectiveness of PR work, providing basis for subsequent optimization. Crisis preparedness is an important component of brand risk management, helping brands respond quickly and effectively during unexpected crises, maintaining brand reputation.

• Apply PR effectiveness evaluation models, assess communication effects from dimensions of media exposure, audience awareness, attitude change, behavioral impact, compare with target KPIs for effectiveness analysis, write effectiveness evaluation report
• Formulate crisis PR plan, identify potential crisis risk points, develop分级 response mechanism and handling principles, design crisis response talking points and spokesperson system, establish crisis management team and process
• Establish long-term reputation management mechanism, establish public opinion monitoring system, conduct regular reputation audits, develop ongoing media relations maintenance and brand communication plan, realize normalization and systematization of PR work
Deliverable: Effectiveness evaluation report and crisis plan (including effectiveness evaluation report, crisis PR manual, reputation management plan) | Quality standard: Scientific evaluation system, complete crisis plan, sound long-term mechanism
