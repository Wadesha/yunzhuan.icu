曲式分析

Musical Form and Analysis

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：和声学 | 学期：第三、四学期

本课程深入研究音乐作品的组织结构原则与分析方法。内容涵盖一段曲式、二段曲式、三段曲式、复三部曲式、回旋曲式、变奏曲式、奏鸣曲式等古典曲式类型，以及主题动机发展、调性布局、和声结构等结构要素。通过分析巴赫、海顿、莫扎特、贝多芬等作曲家的经典作品，学生将掌握曲式分析的基本方法，理解音乐材料如何在时间中展开与组织，培养对音乐结构的感知能力与分析能力。

This course provides in-depth study of organizational principles and analytical methods in musical works. Topics include binary, ternary, compound ternary, rondo, variation, and sonata forms, as well as structural elements such as motivic development, tonal planning, and harmonic structure. Through analysis of classic works by Bach, Haydn, Mozart, and Beethoven, students master fundamental methods of formal analysis, understand how musical materials unfold and organize over time, and develop perceptual and analytical abilities for musical structure.
