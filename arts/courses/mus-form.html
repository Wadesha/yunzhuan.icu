曲式分析

Musical Form and Analysis

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：和声学 | 学期：第三、四学期

本课程深入研究音乐作品的组织结构原则与分析方法。内容涵盖一段曲式、二段曲式、三段曲式、复三部曲式、回旋曲式、变奏曲式、奏鸣曲式等古典曲式类型，以及主题动机发展、调性布局、和声结构等结构要素。通过分析巴赫、海顿、莫扎特、贝多芬等作曲家的经典作品，学生将掌握曲式分析的基本方法，理解音乐材料如何在时间中展开与组织，培养对音乐结构的感知能力与分析能力。

This course provides in-depth study of organizational principles and analytical methods in musical works. Topics include binary, ternary, compound ternary, rondo, variation, and sonata forms, as well as structural elements such as motivic development, tonal planning, and harmonic structure. Through analysis of classic works by Bach, Haydn, Mozart, and Beethoven, students master fundamental methods of formal analysis, understand how musical materials unfold and organize over time, and develop perceptual and analytical abilities for musical structure.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：经典作品曲式结构分析报告

学生需选取一首古典时期经典作品进行完整的曲式分析，涵盖曲式类型、主题发展、调性布局与和声结构。最终提交结构分析报告与图示。

Students select a Classical-era work for complete formal analysis, covering form type, thematic development, tonal plan, and harmonic structure. An analysis report with diagrams is submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：分析一首作品的曲式——结构、段落、比例，理解音乐的组织逻辑。
步骤 1
作品选择与背景研究
本步骤的核心任务是选择进行曲式分析的经典作品并进行背景研究。曲式分析是音乐分析的基础，需要先了解作品的创作背景、作曲家风格、时代特征等信息。充分的背景研究能帮助我们更深入地理解作品的结构逻辑和艺术表达。

• 选择分析作品，从古典时期经典作品中选择一首进行深入分析，如莫扎特、海顿、贝多芬的钢琴奏鸣曲或交响曲乐章，确保作品结构清晰、具有代表性
• 进行作曲家和作品背景研究，了解作曲家的生平、创作风格、该作品的创作年代、创作背景、首演情况、历史地位，以及该作品所属的体裁特点
• 收集作品相关资料，获取作品的乐谱、录音、相关分析文献、演奏版本等，建立研究档案，为后续的深入分析做好准备
产出：作品背景研究报告（含作品简介、作曲家研究、背景资料）｜ 质量标准：资料收集全面，背景研究深入
步骤 2
主题动机与材料分析
本步骤聚焦于作品的主题动机和音乐材料分析，理解音乐的基本构成元素。主题和动机是音乐发展的种子，曲式的结构逻辑往往根植于主题材料的性质和发展方式。通过细致的主题分析，把握音乐的核心材料及其性格特征。

• 识别和标记作品中的各个主题，包括主部主题、副部主题、结束主题、引子主题等，分析每个主题的调性、乐句结构、旋律轮廓、节奏特点
• 提炼核心动机和音乐细胞，分析主题中的核心动机材料，研究动机的音程、节奏、轮廓等特征，标记动机在主题中的出现和变化
• 分析主题材料的性格对比，比较不同主题之间在情绪、速度、力度、音区、织体等方面的对比关系，理解主题材料的戏剧性和抒情性
产出：主题动机分析（含主题标记、动机提炼、性格分析）｜ 质量标准：主题识别准确，动机分析细致
步骤 3
曲式结构与段落划分
本步骤是曲式分析的核心环节，确定作品的整体曲式类型并进行段落划分。曲式是音乐的组织结构，准确判断曲式类型、划分段落边界，是理解音乐逻辑的基础。通过系统的结构分析，建立对作品宏观架构的清晰认识。

• 确定作品的整体曲式类型，判断是奏鸣曲式、回旋曲式、三部曲式、变奏曲式、奏鸣曲快板乐章等哪种曲式，并说明判断依据
• 进行段落和乐句划分，标记引子、呈示部、发展部、再现部、尾声等大的段落，再细分到乐句、乐汇，建立完整的结构层级
• 绘制曲式结构图，用图示方式展示作品的结构布局，标注各段落的小节数、调性、主题材料、终止式等，直观呈现作品的结构逻辑
产出：曲式结构分析图（含段落划分、结构图示、调性布局）｜ 质量标准：结构划分准确，图示清晰规范
步骤 4
调性布局与和声分析
本步骤深入分析作品的调性布局和和声进行，理解音乐结构的和声基础。调性和和声是曲式结构的重要支撑要素，调性的转换和和声的张力/解决推动着音乐的发展。通过调性和声分析，从和声层面理解曲式结构的逻辑和动力。

• 分析作品的调性布局，梳理各段落的调性及其转换关系，研究主调、属调、下属调、远关系调等不同调性在结构中的功能和作用，分析转调的手法和意义
• 进行关键段落的和声分析，选择重要的结构部位（如呈示部结束、发展部高潮、再现部回归等）进行详细的和声分析，标注和弦、终止式、和声进行
• 分析和声与曲式的关系，研究和声张力的起伏如何与结构的起承转合相呼应，理解终止式的类型及其在结构划分中的标志性作用
产出：调性和声分析（含调性布局图、和声分析谱例、和声与结构关系）｜ 质量标准：调性分析清晰，和声分析准确
步骤 5
综合分析与报告撰写
本步骤是曲式分析的总结阶段，将各方面的分析整合起来，撰写完整的曲式分析报告。好的分析报告不仅要有准确的结构划分，更要对音乐的发展逻辑、材料关系、艺术表现有深入的理解和阐述。通过报告写作，将分析成果系统化、理论化。

• 进行综合分析和讨论，将主题材料、曲式结构、调性布局、和声进行、织体配器等各方面的分析整合起来，讨论音乐发展的逻辑和艺术表现，深入理解作品的思想内涵
• 撰写完整的曲式分析报告，按照学术规范组织报告结构，包括作品概述、背景介绍、主题分析、曲式结构、调性和声、综合讨论等部分，配合谱例和图表说明
• 进行报告校对和完善，检查分析的准确性、逻辑的严密性、语言的规范性，确保报告质量，制作清晰规范的谱例和图示
产出：曲式分析报告（含完整报告、谱例集、结构图示）｜ 质量标准：分析深入系统，报告规范完整

Steps

Step 1
Work Selection & Background Research
The core task of this step is to select a classic work for musical form analysis and conduct background research. Musical form analysis is the foundation of music analysis; first need to understand the work's creative background, composer style, era characteristics and other information. Sufficient background research can help us more deeply understand the structural logic and artistic expression of the work.

• Select work for analysis, choose a work from classical period classics for in-depth analysis, such as piano sonata or symphony movement by Mozart, Haydn, Beethoven, ensure the work has clear structure and is representative
• Conduct composer and work background research, understand composer's life, creative style, creation era of this work, creative background, premiere situation, historical position, as well as genre characteristics of this work
• Collect work-related materials, obtain scores, recordings, related analytical literature, performance versions, etc. of the work, establish research archive, prepare for subsequent in-depth analysis
Deliverable: Work background research report (including work introduction, composer study, background materials) | Quality standard: Comprehensive material collection, in-depth background research
Step 2
Thematic Motive & Material Analysis
This step focuses on analysis of thematic motives and musical materials of the work, understanding basic constituent elements of music. Themes and motives are the seeds of musical development; structural logic of musical form is often rooted in the nature and development methods of thematic materials. Through detailed thematic analysis, grasp core musical materials and their character characteristics.

• Identify and mark each theme in the work, including primary theme, secondary theme, closing theme, introduction theme, etc., analyze tonality, phrase structure, melodic contour, rhythmic characteristics of each theme
• Extract core motives and musical cells, analyze core motive materials in themes, study interval, rhythm, contour and other features of motives, mark appearance and variation of motives in themes
• Analyze character contrast of thematic materials, compare contrast relationships in emotion, tempo, dynamics, register, texture, etc. between different themes, understand dramatic and lyrical qualities of thematic materials
Deliverable: Thematic motive analysis (including theme marking, motive extraction, character analysis) | Quality standard: Accurate theme identification, detailed motive analysis
Step 3
Musical Form Structure & Section Division
This step is the core phase of musical form analysis, determining overall musical form type of the work and conducting section division. Musical form is the organizational structure of music; accurately judging form type and dividing section boundaries is the foundation for understanding musical logic. Through systematic structural analysis, establish clear understanding of the macro architecture of the work.

• Determine overall musical form type of the work, judge whether it is sonata form, rondo form, ternary form, variation form, sonata-allegro movement, etc., and explain basis for judgment
• Conduct section and phrase division, mark large sections such as introduction, exposition, development, recapitulation, coda, then subdivide into phrases, motives, establish complete structural hierarchy
• Draw musical form structure diagram, use graphical method to show structural layout of the work, mark measure numbers, tonality, thematic materials, cadences, etc. of each section, intuitively present structural logic of the work
Deliverable: Musical form structure analysis diagram (including section division, structure diagram, tonal layout) | Quality standard: Accurate structure division, clear and standardized diagram
Step 4
Tonal Layout & Harmonic Analysis
This step deeply analyzes tonal layout and harmonic progression of the work, understanding harmonic foundation of musical structure. Tonality and harmony are important supporting elements of musical form structure; tonal shifts and harmonic tension/resolution propel musical development. Through tonal and harmonic analysis, understand logic and dynamics of musical form structure from the harmonic level.

• Analyze tonal layout of the work, sort out tonality and transition relationship of each section, study functions and roles of different tonalities such as tonic, dominant, subdominant, distant keys in the structure, analyze modulation techniques and significance
• Conduct harmonic analysis of key sections, select important structural positions (such as end of exposition, development climax, recapitulation return, etc.) for detailed harmonic analysis, label chords, cadences, harmonic progressions
• Analyze relationship between harmony and musical form, study how harmonic tension起伏 corresponds with structural beginning-development-climax-conclusion, understand types of cadences and their landmark role in structural division
Deliverable: Tonality and harmony analysis (including tonal layout diagram, harmonic analysis examples, harmony-structure relationship) | Quality standard: Clear tonal analysis, accurate harmonic analysis
Step 5
Comprehensive Analysis & Report Writing
This step is the summary phase of musical form analysis, integrating all aspects of analysis, writing a complete musical form analysis report. A good analytical report requires not only accurate structural division, but also in-depth understanding and elaboration of musical development logic, material relationships, and artistic expression. Through report writing, systematize and theorize analytical results.

• Conduct comprehensive analysis and discussion, integrate analysis of thematic materials, musical form structure, tonal layout, harmonic progression, texture/orchestration and other aspects, discuss logic of musical development and artistic expression, deeply understand ideological connotation of the work
• Write complete musical form analysis report, organize report structure according to academic standards, including work overview, background introduction, thematic analysis, musical form structure, tonality and harmony, comprehensive discussion, etc.,coordination with score examples and chart illustrations
• Conduct report proofreading and improvement, check accuracy of analysis, logical rigor, language standardization, ensure report quality, produce clear and standardized score examples and diagrams
Deliverable: Musical form analysis report (including complete report, score example collection, structure diagrams) | Quality standard: In-depth and systematic analysis, standardized and complete report
