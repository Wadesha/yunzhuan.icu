数字媒体艺术

Digital Media Art

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：绘画基础、色彩理论 | 学期：第四学期

本课程是视觉艺术专业的跨媒介实践课程，探索数字技术与艺术创作的融合。课程内容涵盖数字图像处理、动态影像、交互设计、三维建模、生成艺术等多个方向。通过Photoshop、After Effects、Processing、Blender等软件工具的学习与实践，帮助学生掌握数字媒体的技术语言。课程强调技术与艺术的结合，引导学生运用数字手段表达艺术观念，探索虚拟现实、互动装置、数据可视化等新兴艺术形态，培养适应数字时代的跨媒介艺术创作能力与创新思维。

This interdisciplinary studio course explores the integration of digital technology and artistic creation. Topics include digital image processing, motion graphics, interaction design, 3D modeling, and generative art. Through hands-on practice with tools such as Photoshop, After Effects, Processing, and Blender, students master the technical language of digital media. The course emphasizes the integration of technology and art, guiding students to express artistic concepts through digital means and explore emerging forms such as virtual reality, interactive installation, and data visualization.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：互动装置艺术方案设计与原型实现

学生需设计一个互动装置艺术作品方案，并完成可演示的数字原型。主题自定，但需体现对"人与技术关系"的思考。方案需包含完整的概念阐述、交互逻辑设计、视觉风格设定、技术实现路径与空间展示方案。原型可使用TouchDesigner、Processing或Unity等工具制作，要求至少包含一种传感器或输入方式的交互功能。需提交项目提案书、视觉分镜、技术流程图、原型演示视频与创作说明，展示对数字媒体艺术语言的综合运用能力。

Students design an interactive installation art proposal and complete a demonstrable digital prototype. The theme is self-selected but must reflect on the "relationship between humans and technology." The proposal includes complete conceptual elaboration, interaction logic design, visual style definition, technical implementation path, and spatial exhibition plan. Prototypes can be built using TouchDesigner, Processing, Unity, or similar tools, requiring at least one sensor or input method for interaction.
