乐理基础

Music Theory Fundamentals

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学期

本课程是音乐专业的入门核心课程，系统讲授音乐理论的基础知识。内容包括五线谱记谱法、音程、音阶、调式、和弦基础、节奏与节拍等基本概念。通过理论学习与习题训练，学生将掌握阅读和书写乐谱的能力，理解音乐的基本构成要素，为后续和声、曲式、作曲等课程打下坚实基础。课程注重理论与实践结合，培养学生的音乐分析能力。

This introductory core course systematically teaches the fundamentals of music theory. Topics include staff notation, intervals, scales, modes, basic chord structure, rhythm, and meter. Through theoretical study and exercises, students develop skills in reading and writing music notation and understand the basic building blocks of music. The course emphasizes the integration of theory and practice, building a solid foundation for advanced studies in harmony, form, and composition.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：乐理综合应用与分析作品集

学生需完成一套乐理综合应用作品，涵盖记谱、音阶调式、和弦与节奏的综合练习与分析。最终提交作品集与分析说明。

Students complete a set of comprehensive music theory applications, covering notation, scales and modes, chords, and rhythm exercises and analysis. A portfolio with analytical notes is submitted.
