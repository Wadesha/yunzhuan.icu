乐理基础

Music Theory Fundamentals

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学期

本课程是音乐专业的入门核心课程，系统讲授音乐理论的基础知识。内容包括五线谱记谱法、音程、音阶、调式、和弦基础、节奏与节拍等基本概念。通过理论学习与习题训练，学生将掌握阅读和书写乐谱的能力，理解音乐的基本构成要素，为后续和声、曲式、作曲等课程打下坚实基础。课程注重理论与实践结合，培养学生的音乐分析能力。

This introductory core course systematically teaches the fundamentals of music theory. Topics include staff notation, intervals, scales, modes, basic chord structure, rhythm, and meter. Through theoretical study and exercises, students develop skills in reading and writing music notation and understand the basic building blocks of music. The course emphasizes the integration of theory and practice, building a solid foundation for advanced studies in harmony, form, and composition.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：乐理综合应用与分析作品集

学生需完成一套乐理综合应用作品，涵盖记谱、音阶调式、和弦与节奏的综合练习与分析。最终提交作品集与分析说明。

Students complete a set of comprehensive music theory applications, covering notation, scales and modes, chords, and rhythm exercises and analysis. A portfolio with analytical notes is submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
基础概念梳理与知识框架
本步骤的核心任务是系统梳理乐理基础概念，建立完整的乐理知识框架。乐理是音乐学习的基石，概念众多且相互关联。通过系统化的梳理，将零散的知识点组织成有机的知识网络，理清概念之间的逻辑关系，为后续的综合应用打下坚实基础。

• 梳理乐理核心概念体系，包括音的性质（音高、音值、音量、音色）、记谱法、音程、和弦、调式、节奏、节拍、旋律、速度、力度、表情术语等核心内容
• 建立知识结构图谱，理清各知识点之间的逻辑关系和先后顺序，如：音→音程→和弦→调式，节奏→节拍→节奏型，绘制乐理知识体系结构图
• 进行基础概念的系统复习，重温每个知识点的定义、分类、标记、规则，确保基本概念清晰准确，没有模糊和混淆之处
产出：乐理知识体系图（含概念梳理、结构图谱、基础复习）｜ 质量标准：概念准确清晰，体系完整系统
步骤 2
音程和弦综合练习
本步骤聚焦于音程与和弦的综合应用练习，深化对多声音乐基本元素的理解。音程和和弦是和声学的基础，也是乐理学习的重点和难点。通过各种形式的综合练习，熟练掌握音程和和弦的识别、构成、转位、听辨等技能，达到灵活运用的程度。

• 进行音程综合练习，包括音程的识别（度数和性质）、音程的构成、音程的转位、音程的听辨、等音程的判断、自然音程与变化音程的分类等各种练习
• 进行和弦综合练习，练习三和弦、七和弦的原位和转位的识别与构成，和弦的级数和功能标记，和弦的听辨，不同调式中的和弦应用，各种常见和弦的连接规则
• 进行综合应用练习，将音程和弦放在具体的调式调性中练习，练习和弦的功能进行、终止式的判断、简单的四部和声写作，提升知识的综合运用能力
产出：音程和弦综合练习（含习题集、错题集、练习总结）｜ 质量标准：音程和弦掌握熟练，综合应用能力强
步骤 3
调式调性系统掌握
本步骤是乐理学习的核心环节，系统掌握各种调式调性及其应用。调式是音乐的组织基础，不同的调式有不同的色彩和表现力。通过系统学习和练习，熟练掌握大小调体系、民族调式、教会调式等各种调式的结构特点和应用规律。

• 系统掌握大小调体系，深入理解自然大调、和声大调、旋律大调、自然小调、和声小调、旋律小调的结构特点、音级关系、调式色彩，练习调式的识别和转换
• 学习民族调式和其他调式，掌握五声音阶、七声音阶等中国民族调式的特点、分类、识别方法，了解教会调式、全音阶、半音阶等其他调式
• 进行调性判断和转调分析，练习通过乐谱或听觉判断乐曲的调式调性，分析调式中的音级和和弦，识别简单的转调、离调现象，理解调性音乐的组织逻辑
产出：调式调性练习与总结（含调式练习、调性判断、知识总结）｜ 质量标准：调式概念清晰，调性判断准确，系统掌握全面
步骤 4
节奏节拍与记谱实践
本步骤聚焦于节奏节拍和记谱法的实践应用，提升音乐的基本读写能力。节奏是音乐的时间组织，记谱法是记录音乐的书面语言。通过系统的节奏训练和记谱练习，建立稳定的内在节拍感，掌握规范的记谱方法，提升音乐的读谱和写谱能力。

• 进行节奏节拍系统练习，学习各种节拍（单拍子、复拍子、混合拍子、变换拍子）的特点和强弱规律，练习各种节奏型（切分、附点、三连音、连音符、休止符等）的听辨和书写
• 进行记谱法系统复习，掌握五线谱记谱的各种规则，包括音符休止符的规范写法、谱号调号拍号的标记、临时变音记号的使用、连线延音线的区别、各种术语符号的含义
• 进行综合记谱/读谱练习，练习将听到的旋律准确记录下来（听写），练习快速准确地读谱演唱或演奏，提升音乐的读写能力和效率
产出：节奏记谱训练集（含节奏练习、记谱练习、听写练习）｜ 质量标准：节拍稳定准确，记谱规范正确，读写能力强
步骤 5
综合应用与作品分析
本步骤是乐理学习的总结和应用阶段，通过综合练习和简单作品分析，检验和巩固所学知识，将乐理知识应用到实际音乐中。乐理知识最终要服务于音乐实践，通过分析真实的音乐作品，理解乐理知识在音乐中的具体运用，提升音乐理解能力。

• 进行乐理综合测试，完成一套涵盖各知识点的综合练习题，检验对乐理知识的整体掌握程度，找出薄弱环节，进行有针对性的巩固和强化
• 进行简单的音乐作品分析，选择一首简单的音乐作品，运用乐理知识分析其调式调性、旋律特点、节奏节拍、和声进行、曲式结构等，将理论知识与实践结合
• 总结乐理学习体系，系统梳理所有知识点，建立个人的乐理知识体系，制定后续的进阶学习计划（如和声学、对位法、曲式学、音乐理论进阶等），为进一步学习指明方向
产出：乐理综合作业（含综合测试、作品分析、学习总结）｜ 质量标准：术语掌握全面，综合应用能力强，基础知识扎实

Steps

Step 1
Basic Concept Review & Knowledge Framework
The core task of this step is to systematically sort out basic music theory concepts and establish a complete music theory knowledge framework. Music theory is the cornerstone of music learning, with numerous interconnected concepts. Through systematic organization, organize scattered knowledge points into an organic knowledge network, clarify logical relationships between concepts, lay solid foundation for subsequent comprehensive application.

• Sort out core concept system of music theory, including properties of sound (pitch, duration, volume, timbre), notation, intervals, chords, modes, rhythm, meter, melody, tempo, dynamics, expression terms and other core content
• Establish knowledge structure map, clarify logical relationships and sequential order between various knowledge points, e.g.: sound → interval → chord → mode, rhythm → meter → rhythmic pattern, draw structure diagram of music theory knowledge system
• Conduct systematic review of basic concepts, revisit definition, classification, marking, rules of each knowledge point, ensure basic concepts are clear and accurate, without ambiguity and confusion
Deliverable: Music theory knowledge system diagram (including concept sorting, structure map, basic review) | Quality standard: Accurate and clear concepts, complete and systematic system
Step 2
Interval & Chord Comprehensive Exercises
This step focuses on comprehensive application exercises of intervals and chords, deepening understanding of basic elements of polyphonic music. Intervals and chords are the foundation of harmony, and also key and difficult points in music theory learning. Through various forms of comprehensive exercises, proficiently master skills of interval and chord recognition, construction, inversion, aural discrimination, etc., achieving flexible application.

• Conduct comprehensive interval exercises, including interval recognition (degree and quality), interval construction, interval inversion, interval aural discrimination, enharmonic interval judgment, classification of diatonic and chromatic intervals and other exercises
• Conduct comprehensive chord exercises, practice recognition and construction of root position and inverted triads and seventh chords, chord degree and function labeling, chord aural discrimination, chord application in different modes, connection rules of various common chords
• Conduct comprehensive application exercises, practice intervals and chords in specific modal/tonal context, practice harmonic functional progressions, cadence judgment, simple four-part harmony writing, improve comprehensive application ability of knowledge
Deliverable: Interval and chord comprehensive exercises (including exercise collection, wrong question collection, exercise summary) | Quality standard: Proficient mastery of intervals and chords, strong comprehensive application ability
Step 3
Systematic Mastery of Modes & Tonality
This step is the core phase of music theory learning, systematically mastering various modes/tonalities and their applications. Mode is the organizational foundation of music; different modes have different colors and expressive power. Through systematic study and practice, proficiently master structural characteristics and application rules of various modes such as major/minor system, folk modes, church modes, etc.

• Systematically master major/minor system, deeply understand structural characteristics, scale degree relationships, modal color of natural major, harmonic major, melodic major, natural minor, harmonic minor, melodic minor, practice mode identification and transition
• Learn folk modes and other modes, master characteristics, classification, identification methods of Chinese folk modes such as pentatonic scale, heptatonic scale, understand church modes, whole tone scale, chromatic scale and other modes
• Conduct tonality judgment and modulation analysis, practice judging mode/tonality of music through score or hearing, analyze scale degrees and chords in modes, identify simple modulation, tonicization phenomena, understand organizational logic of tonal music
Deliverable: Mode and tonality exercises and summary (including mode exercises, tonality judgment, knowledge summary) | Quality standard: Clear mode concepts, accurate tonality judgment, comprehensive systematic mastery
Step 4
Rhythm/Meter & Notation Practice
This step focuses on practical application of rhythm/meter and notation, improving basic music reading and writing abilities. Rhythm is the temporal organization of music; notation is the written language for recording music. Through systematic rhythm training and notation practice, establish stable inner sense of beat, master standardized notation methods, improve music score reading and writing abilities.

• Conduct systematic rhythm and meter exercises, learn characteristics and strong/weak patterns of various meters (simple meter, compound meter, mixed meter, changing meter), practice aural discrimination and writing of various rhythmic patterns (syncopation, dotted notes, triplets, tuplets, rests, etc.)
• Conduct systematic review of notation, master various rules of staff notation, including standard writing of notes/rests, marking of clefs/key signatures/time signatures, use of accidental marks, difference between ties and slurs, meaning of various terminology symbols
• Conduct comprehensive notation/reading practice, practice accurately recording heard melodies (dictation), practice quickly and accurately reading scores for singing or playing, improve music reading/writing ability and efficiency
Deliverable: Rhythm and notation training set (including rhythm exercises, notation exercises, dictation exercises) | Quality standard: Stable and accurate beat, standardized and correct notation, strong reading/writing ability
Step 5
Comprehensive Application & Work Analysis
This step is the summary and application phase of music theory learning, through comprehensive exercises and simple work analysis, test and consolidate learned knowledge, apply music theory knowledge to actual music. Music theory knowledge ultimately serves musical practice; through analyzing real musical works, understand specific application of music theory knowledge in music, improve music understanding ability.

• Conduct music theory comprehensive test, complete a set of comprehensive exercises covering all knowledge points, test overall mastery level of music theory knowledge, find weak links, conduct targeted consolidation and strengthening
• Conduct simple music work analysis, choose a simple music work, use music theory knowledge to analyze its mode/tonality, melodic characteristics, rhythm/meter, harmonic progression, musical form structure, etc., combine theoretical knowledge with practice
• Summarize music theory learning system, systematically sort out all knowledge points, establish personal music theory knowledge system, formulate subsequent advanced learning plan (such as harmony, counterpoint, musical form, advanced music theory, etc.), point out direction for further study
Deliverable: Music theory comprehensive assignments (including comprehensive test, work analysis, learning summary) | Quality standard: Comprehensive term mastery, strong comprehensive application ability, solid basic knowledge
