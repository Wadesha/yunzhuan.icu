设计史

Design History

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第二学期

本课程系统梳理设计从工业革命至今的发展历程，探讨设计风格演变背后的社会、经济、技术与文化动因。课程内容涵盖工艺美术运动、新艺术运动、装饰艺术、包豪斯、现代主义、国际主义、后现代主义、数字时代设计等重要历史时期与流派，深入分析各时期的代表人物、经典作品和设计思想。通过案例研读和讨论，学生将建立完整的设计史知识框架，理解设计与时代的互动关系，培养历史视野和批判性思维，为当代设计实践提供理论参照和思想资源。

This course systematically traces the development of design from the Industrial Revolution to the present, exploring the social, economic, technological, and cultural drivers behind the evolution of design styles. The curriculum covers important historical periods and movements including the Arts and Crafts Movement, Art Nouveau, Art Deco, Bauhaus, Modernism, International Style, Postmodernism, and digital-era design, with in-depth analysis of representative figures, classic works, and design philosophies of each period. Through case studies and discussions, students build a complete design history knowledge framework, understand the interactive relationship between design and its era, and develop historical perspective and critical thinking, providing theoretical reference and intellectual resources for contemporary design practice.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：设计史专题研究论文

学生需选取一个设计史流派或运动进行专题研究，分析其历史背景、代表作品与设计思想影响。最终提交符合学术规范的研究论文。

Students conduct thematic research on a design history movement, analyzing its historical context, representative works, and ideological influence. A scholarly research essay is submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
选题确定与资料收集
本步骤的核心任务是确定研究选题并进行广泛的资料收集，为专题研究奠定文献基础。设计史研究需要扎实的史料支撑，选择一个有研究价值的切入点至关重要。通过系统性的文献检索和资料收集，全面了解研究对象的历史背景、发展脉络和研究现状。

• 从课程涵盖的设计史流派或运动中选取研究对象，如工艺美术运动、新艺术运动、包豪斯、现代主义、后现代主义、波普设计等，界定研究范围和核心问题
• 进行广泛的文献检索，使用学术数据库、图书馆资源、网络资源收集相关书籍、论文、展览图录、原始文献等资料，建立研究文献库
• 收集代表作品的图像资料和背景信息，整理设计师传记、设计作品、历史照片、时代背景等材料，为分析研究提供丰富的素材
产出：研究选题与文献综述（含选题说明、研究问题、文献清单、资料汇编）｜ 质量标准：选题有研究价值，资料收集全面，文献基础扎实
步骤 2
历史语境与背景研究
本步骤聚焦于将设计现象置于具体的历史语境中进行考察，理解设计发展的社会文化动因。设计史不是孤立的风格演变史，而是与社会、经济、科技、文化等因素紧密相关的。通过深入的背景研究，理解设计流派产生的历史必然性和社会文化意义。

• 研究设计流派/运动的历史背景，分析当时的社会状况、经济发展、技术进步、文化思潮、艺术运动等因素对设计的影响
• 梳理设计发展的脉络和线索，研究设计风格的继承与演变关系，分析不同流派之间的传承、影响和反叛，建立设计史的整体认知框架
• 研究关键人物的生平和思想，深入理解设计师的设计理念、创作方法和历史贡献，从人的角度理解设计发展的内在动力
产出：历史背景研究报告（含社会背景分析、发展脉络梳理、关键人物研究、历史语境分析）｜ 质量标准：历史视野开阔，背景分析深入，脉络梳理清晰
步骤 3
作品分析与风格研究
本步骤是设计史研究的核心环节，通过对代表作品的深入分析，总结设计风格的形式特征和美学特点。作品是设计思想的物化体现，通过细致的形式分析和图像解读，揭示设计作品背后的设计理念、美学追求和技术特征。

• 选取5-10件具有代表性的设计作品进行深入分析，从功能、形式、材料、技术、美学等多个维度进行解读，分析作品的创新点和历史意义
• 总结设计风格的形式特征，包括造型语言、色彩特点、材质运用、装饰手法、构图规律等，提炼该流派/运动的风格识别要素
• 进行比较分析，将研究对象与其他流派、其他时期、其他地区的设计进行横向和纵向比较，找出其独特性和普遍性
产出：作品分析与风格研究（含作品分析、风格特征总结、比较研究、风格图谱）｜ 质量标准：作品分析深入细致，风格总结准确到位，比较研究有洞见
步骤 4
理论框架与批评分析
本步骤引入设计理论和批评视角，对设计史现象进行更深层次的解读和反思。设计史研究不仅是描述历史事实，更要进行理论阐释和价值判断。运用设计理论、文化理论、社会学理论等工具，对设计现象进行批判性分析，深化研究的思想深度。

• 学习设计史论研究的基本理论和方法，如形式分析法、图像学方法、社会史方法、女性主义批评、后殖民理论等，选择合适的理论视角
• 运用选定的理论框架对研究对象进行批评性分析，探讨设计背后的意识形态、权力关系、文化身份、消费社会等深层问题
• 反思设计的社会影响和历史作用，既肯定其历史贡献，也分析其局限性和问题，形成辩证的历史认识
产出：设计批评与理论分析（含理论框架、批评分析、辩证讨论、研究发现）｜ 质量标准：理论运用恰当，批评分析深刻，辩证思考有深度
步骤 5
论文撰写与成果展示
本步骤是研究的收官环节，将研究成果整理成规范的学术论文，并进行成果展示。学术论文是设计史研究的正式成果形式，需要遵循学术规范，结构完整，论证严密。通过论文写作和成果展示，系统呈现研究成果，锻炼学术表达能力。

• 按照学术论文规范撰写研究论文，包括摘要、关键词、引言、历史背景、风格分析、理论讨论、结论、参考文献、图片索引等部分
• 进行多轮修改和润色，从论点、论据、论证、结构、语言等多个方面打磨论文，确保学术质量和写作水平
• 制作研究展示PPT，将研究成果转化为可视化的演示文稿，准备口头汇报，锻炼学术表达和沟通能力
产出：设计史研究论文（含完整论文、参考文献、图示资料、汇报PPT）｜ 质量标准：论文结构完整规范，论证逻辑严密，学术水平高

Steps

Step 1
Topic Determination & Data Collection
The core task of this step is to determine the research topic and conduct extensive data collection, laying literature foundation for thematic research. Design history research requires solid historical materials support, and choosing a valuable research entry point is crucial. Through systematic literature search and data collection, comprehensively understand historical background, development context and research status of the research object.

• Select research object from design history genres or movements covered in the course, such as Arts and Crafts Movement, Art Nouveau, Bauhaus, Modernism, Postmodernism, Pop Design, etc., define research scope and core questions
• Conduct extensive literature search, use academic databases, library resources, internet resources to collect relevant books, papers, exhibition catalogs, original documents and other materials, establish research literature library
• Collect image materials and background information of representative works, organize designer biographies, design works, historical photos, era background and other materials, provide rich materials for analytical research
Deliverable: Research topic and literature review (including topic statement, research questions, literature list, data compilation) | Quality standard: Research topic has academic value, comprehensive data collection, solid literature foundation
Step 2
Historical Context & Background Research
This step focuses on examining design phenomena in specific historical context, understanding socio-cultural drivers of design development. Design history is not isolated style evolution history, but closely related to social, economic, technological, cultural and other factors. Through in-depth background research, understand historical inevitability and socio-cultural significance of design schools' emergence.

• Study historical background of design school/movement, analyze influence of social conditions, economic development, technological progress, cultural trends, art movements and other factors on design at that time
• Sort out context and clues of design development, study inheritance and evolution relationship of design styles, analyze inheritance, influence and rebellion between different schools, establish overall cognitive framework of design history
• Study lives and thoughts of key figures, deeply understand designers' design concepts, creative methods and historical contributions, understand internal driving force of design development from human perspective
Deliverable: Historical background research report (including social background analysis, development context sorting, key figure research, historical context analysis) | Quality standard: Broad historical vision, in-depth background analysis, clear context sorting
Step 3
Work Analysis & Style Research
This step is the core phase of design history research, summarizing formal characteristics and aesthetic features of design style through in-depth analysis of representative works. Works are the materialized embodiment of design ideas; through detailed formal analysis and image interpretation, reveal design concepts, aesthetic pursuits and technical characteristics behind design works.

• Select 5-10 representative design works for in-depth analysis, interpret from multiple dimensions such as function, form, material, technology, aesthetics, analyze innovation points and historical significance of works
• Summarize formal characteristics of design style, including modeling language, color characteristics, material application, decoration techniques, composition rules, etc., extract style recognition elements of this school/movement
• Conduct comparative analysis, compare research object horizontally and vertically with designs of other schools, other periods, other regions, find its uniqueness and universality
Deliverable: Work analysis and style research (including work analysis, style characteristic summary, comparative study, style atlas) | Quality standard: In-depth and detailed work analysis, accurate and precise style summary, insightful comparative research
Step 4
Theoretical Framework & Critical Analysis
This step introduces design theory and critical perspective, conducting deeper interpretation and reflection on design history phenomena. Design history research is not only describing historical facts, but also conducting theoretical interpretation and value judgment. Using tools such as design theory, cultural theory, sociological theory, conduct critical analysis on design phenomena, deepen ideological depth of research.

• Learn basic theories and methods of design history research, such as formal analysis method, iconology method, social history method, feminist criticism, postcolonial theory, etc., choose appropriate theoretical perspective
• Apply selected theoretical framework to conduct critical analysis on research object, explore deep issues such as ideology, power relations, cultural identity, consumer society behind design
• Reflect on social impact and historical role of design, both affirm its historical contribution and analyze its limitations and problems, form dialectical historical understanding
Deliverable: Design criticism and theoretical analysis (including theoretical framework, critical analysis, dialectical discussion, research findings) | Quality standard: Appropriate theory application, profound critical analysis, deep dialectical thinking
Step 5
Thesis Writing & Result Presentation
This step is the concluding phase of research, organizing research results into standardized academic papers and presenting results. Academic papers are the formal outcome form of design history research, requiring compliance with academic norms, complete structure, and rigorous argumentation. Through thesis writing and result presentation, systematically present research results, exercise academic expression ability.

• Write research paper according to academic paper standards, including abstract, keywords, introduction, historical background, style analysis, theoretical discussion, conclusion, references, image index, etc.
• Conduct multiple rounds of revision and polishing, polish the paper from multiple aspects such as thesis statements, supporting arguments, argumentation, structure, language, ensure academic quality and writing level
• Create research presentation PPT, transform research results into visual presentation, prepare oral report, exercise academic expression and communication skills
Deliverable: Design history research paper (including complete paper, references, illustration materials, presentation PPT) | Quality standard: Complete and standardized paper structure, rigorous argumentation logic, high academic level
