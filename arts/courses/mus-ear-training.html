视唱练耳

Ear Training and Sight Singing

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：2 | 先修课：乐理基础 | 学期：第一、二学期

本课程是音乐专业的重要技能训练课，分为视唱与练耳两部分。视唱训练学生看谱即唱的能力，从单声部旋律逐步过渡到多声部，涵盖不同调式、节奏和音程。练耳则培养音乐听觉能力，包括音程、和弦、节奏、旋律的听辨与听写。通过系统的循序渐进训练，学生将建立良好的内心听觉，提高音乐记忆和读谱能力，为音乐表演、创作和理论学习提供重要的听觉基础。

This essential skills training course is divided into sight singing and ear training components. Sight singing develops the ability to sing from notation without preparation, progressing from single-line melodies to polyphonic textures, covering various modes, rhythms, and intervals. Ear training cultivates musical listening skills, including identification and dictation of intervals, chords, rhythms, and melodies. Through systematic progressive training, students develop strong inner hearing and improve musical memory and reading skills, providing a critical aural foundation for performance, composition, and theoretical study.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：视唱练耳训练体系设计与听写测试

学生需设计一套循序渐进的视唱练耳训练方案，并完成包含音程和弦节奏旋律的听写测试。最终提交训练方案与听写记录。

Students design a progressive ear training program and complete a dictation assessment covering intervals, chords, rhythm, and melody. The training program and dictation records are submitted.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：完成一套系统的听辨能力训练与测评，比如为期两周的调内和声听写。你需要每天进行15分钟单音、音程、和弦性质听写，累计完成10条旋律听记和5条和声连接听写，最后参加期末听辨测试并提交训练记录本。
步骤 1
能力评估与训练方案设计
本步骤的核心任务是评估当前的视唱练耳水平并设计系统化的训练方案。视唱练耳是音乐学习者的基础能力，需要有针对性的、循序渐进的训练才能有效提升。通过科学的评估了解自己的优势和不足，制定合理的训练计划，是高效提升视唱练耳能力的前提。

• 进行视唱练耳能力评估，测试自己在音高感、节奏感、和声听辨、旋律记忆、视谱演唱等方面的现有水平，找出薄弱环节和优势项目
• 学习视唱练耳的训练理论和方法，了解音程、和弦、节奏、旋律、调式等训练内容的科学训练方法，掌握正确的练习要领和进阶路径
• 制定个性化的训练方案，根据评估结果和学习目标，设计循序渐进的训练计划，安排各模块的训练内容、难度进度、练习频率和时间分配
产出：评估报告与训练方案（含能力评估、训练计划、方法指南）｜ 质量标准：评估准确客观，方案科学可行
步骤 2
音程与和弦听辨训练
本步骤聚焦于音程与和弦的听觉训练，建立和声听觉感知能力。音程和和弦是多声部音乐的基本构成元素，准确听辨音程和弦的性质、功能和色彩，是理解音乐的基础。通过系统的训练，培养内心听觉和和声感知能力。

• 进行音程听辨训练，练习各种自然音程和变化音程的听辨，包括音程性质识别（大小、增减、纯）、音程距离判断、音程连接听记，建立音程的听觉印象和概念
• 进行和弦听辨训练，练习三和弦、七和弦的原位和转位听辨，识别和弦的性质、功能、色彩，练习和弦连接的听记，理解和声的功能进行逻辑
• 运用首调思维和固定调思维两种方式训练，建立调式音级感和绝对音高感，结合键盘乐器辅助练习，强化和声听觉和内心音高感
产出：音程和弦训练记录（含练习记录、听辨测试、进步分析）｜ 质量标准：音程和弦听辨准确率高，听觉记忆牢固
步骤 3
节奏与旋律听写训练
本步骤是视唱练耳的核心训练环节，进行节奏听辨和旋律听写训练。节奏是音乐的骨架，旋律是音乐的灵魂，准确捕捉并记录节奏和旋律是音乐人的基本功。通过由浅入深、循序渐进的训练，全面提升音乐听觉记忆和记录能力。

• 进行节奏听辨和听写训练，从单声部节奏到多声部节奏，从简单节拍到复杂节拍，练习各种节奏型（切分、附点、三连音、休止符等）的听辨和听写，建立稳定的内在节拍感
• 进行旋律听写训练，从短小的动机到完整的乐句，从单声部到二声部，练习不同调式、不同风格的旋律听写，训练旋律记忆和音高感知能力
• 进行调式调性听辨训练，练习识别大调和小调、民族调式、教会调式等不同的调式色彩，能够通过听觉判断乐曲的调式调性和转调
产出：节奏旋律训练集（含节奏练习、旋律听写、调式听辨训练）｜ 质量标准：听写准确率高，节拍稳定，旋律记忆好
步骤 4
视唱与音乐表现训练
本步骤将听觉能力转化为演唱实践，进行视谱演唱和音乐表现训练。视唱是视唱练耳的输出环节，不仅要准确地唱出音高和节奏，更要有音乐表现力。通过系统的视唱训练，提升读谱能力、音准控制和音乐表达能力。

• 进行单声部视唱训练，练习不同调式、不同节拍、不同难度的视唱曲目，训练快速识谱能力和准确的音准节奏控制，做到看谱即唱、准确流畅
• 进行二声部及多声部视唱训练，练习卡农式视唱、二声部对位视唱，培养多声听觉和合作能力，体验复调音乐的声部关系和和声效果
• 进行音乐表现训练，在音准节奏准确的基础上，学习处理速度、力度、音色、分句等音乐表现要素，赋予视唱以音乐性和艺术感染力
产出：视唱训练与录音（含视唱曲目、录音作业、表现评价）｜ 质量标准：音准节奏准确，视唱流畅，有音乐表现力
步骤 5
综合测试与训练体系总结
本步骤是视唱练耳学习的综合检验和总结阶段，进行综合测试和体系化的训练总结。通过全面的测试检验学习成果，通过总结梳理将训练方法系统化、体系化，形成可以长期使用的训练方法和持续提升的路径。

• 进行综合听写测试，完成一套完整的视唱练耳听写测试卷，包括音程、和弦、节奏、旋律、二声部听写等内容，检验综合听觉能力
• 进行视唱考试，现场抽取视唱曲目进行视唱，评估识谱速度、音准节奏、音乐表现等方面的水平，检验视唱能力的提升情况
• 总结训练体系和方法，整理和总结各模块的训练方法和经验，制定后续的进阶训练计划，建立可持续的视唱练耳自我训练体系
产出：综合测试与训练总结（含测试成绩、训练总结、后续计划）｜ 质量标准：综合能力提升明显，训练体系完整，后续计划可行

Steps

Step 1
Ability Assessment & Training Program Design
The core task of this step is to assess current solfeggio and ear training level and design a systematic training program. Solfeggio and ear training are foundational abilities for music learners, requiring targeted and gradual training for effective improvement. Through scientific assessment to understand one's advantages and disadvantages, and formulating a reasonable training plan, is the prerequisite for efficiently improving solfeggio and ear training abilities.

• Conduct solfeggio and ear training ability assessment, test current level in pitch sense, rhythm sense, harmonic aural discrimination, melodic memory, sight-singing, etc., find weak links and advantageous items
• Learn training theories and methods of solfeggio and ear training, understand scientific training methods for intervals, chords, rhythm, melody, modes and other training content, master correct practice essentials and progression paths
• Develop personalized training program, according to assessment results and learning goals, design progressive training plan, arrange training content, difficulty progression, practice frequency and time allocation of each module
Deliverable: Assessment report and training program (including ability assessment, training plan, method guide) | Quality standard: Accurate and objective assessment, scientific and feasible plan
Step 2
Interval & Chord Aural Training
This step focuses on auditory training of intervals and chords, building harmonic auditory perception ability. Intervals and chords are the basic constituent elements of polyphonic music; accurately distinguishing the quality, function and color of intervals and chords is the foundation of understanding music. Through systematic training, cultivate inner hearing and harmonic perception ability.

• Conduct interval aural training, practice aural discrimination of various diatonic and chromatic intervals, including interval quality identification (major/minor, augmented/diminished, perfect), interval distance judgment, interval progression dictation, build auditory impression and concept of intervals
• Conduct chord aural training, practice root position and inversion aural discrimination of triads and seventh chords, identify quality, function, color of chords, practice dictation of chord progressions, understand functional progression logic of harmony
• Use both movable-do thinking and fixed-do thinking methods for training, establish modal scale degree sense and absolute pitch sense, combine with keyboard instrument auxiliary practice, strengthen harmonic hearing and inner pitch sense
Deliverable: Interval and chord training records (including practice records, aural discrimination tests, progress analysis) | Quality standard: High accuracy in interval/chord aural discrimination, solid auditory memory
Step 3
Rhythm & Melodic Dictation Training
This step is the core training phase of solfeggio and ear training, conducting rhythm aural discrimination and melodic dictation training. Rhythm is the skeleton of music, and melody is the soul of music; accurately capturing and recording rhythm and melody is the basic skill of musicians. through progressive training from shallow to deep, comprehensively improve musical auditory memory and recording ability.

• Conduct rhythm aural discrimination and dictation training, from monophonic rhythm to polyphonic rhythm, from simple meter to complex meter, practice aural discrimination and dictation of various rhythmic patterns (syncopation, dotted notes, triplets, rests, etc.), establish stable inner sense of beat
• Conduct melodic dictation training, from short motives to complete phrases, from monophonic to two-voice, practice melodic dictation of different modes, different styles, train melodic memory and pitch perception ability
• Conduct mode/tonality aural training, practice identifying major and minor modes, folk modes, church modes and different modal colors, able to judge mode/tonality and modulation of music through hearing
Deliverable: Rhythm and melody training set (including rhythm exercises, melodic dictation, mode aural training) | Quality standard: High dictation accuracy, stable beat, good melodic memory
Step 4
Sight-Singing & Musical Expression Training
This step transforms auditory ability into singing practice, conducting sight-singing and musical expression training. Sight-singing is the outputphase of solfeggio and ear training; not only must pitch and rhythm be sung accurately, but also musical expressiveness is required. Through systematic sight-singing training, improve score-reading ability, pitch control and musical expression ability.

• Conduct monophonic sight-singing training, practice sight-singing repertoire of different modes, different meters, different difficulty levels, train rapid score-reading ability and accurate pitch/rhythm control, achieve sing-at-sight accurately and fluently
• Conduct two-voice and multi-voice sight-singing training, practice canonic sight-singing, two-voice contrapuntal sight-singing, cultivate polyphonic hearing and cooperative ability, experience voice relationships and harmonic effects of polyphonic music
• Conduct musical expression training, on the basis of accurate pitch and rhythm, learn to handle musical expression elements such as tempo, dynamics, timbre, phrasing, endow sight-singing with musicality and artistic appeal
Deliverable: Sight-singing training and recordings (including sight-singing repertoire, recording assignments, expression evaluation) | Quality standard: Accurate pitch and rhythm, fluent sight-singing, musical expressiveness
Step 5
Comprehensive Test & Training System Summary
This step is the comprehensive test and summary phase of solfeggio and ear training learning, conducting comprehensive testing and systematic training summary. Through comprehensive testing to verify learning outcomes; through summarizing and sorting to systematize training methods, forming training methods that can be used long-term and paths for continuous improvement.

• Conduct comprehensive dictation test, complete a complete solfeggio dictation test paper, including intervals, chords, rhythm, melody, two-voice dictation and other content, test comprehensive auditory ability
• Conduct sight-singing examination, randomly draw sight-singing repertoire for sight-singing on site, evaluate sight-reading speed, pitch rhythm, musical expression and other aspects of level, test improvement of sight-singing ability
• Summarize training system and methods, organize and summarize training methods and experiences of each module, formulate subsequent advanced training plan, establish sustainable self-training system for solfeggio and ear training
Deliverable: Comprehensive test and training summary (including test scores, training summary, subsequent plan) | Quality standard: Obvious comprehensive ability improvement, complete training system, feasible follow-up plan
