雕塑基础

Sculpture Fundamentals

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：素描基础 | 学期：第三学期

本课程是视觉艺术专业的核心三维造型课程，系统培养学生的立体空间思维与雕塑创作能力。课程以泥塑为主要媒介，内容涵盖雕塑材料特性、人体解剖结构、形体塑造、空间构成、表面处理等核心技能。通过浮雕练习、头像写生、胸像塑造、人体雕塑等循序渐进的训练，帮助学生掌握三维形体的观察方法与表现技巧。课程兼顾传统写实基础与当代雕塑观念的引入，引导学生理解物质材料与空间形式的表达可能性，为进阶的装置艺术与公共艺术课程奠定基础。

This core three-dimensional form-building course develops students' spatial thinking and sculptural creation abilities. Using clay as the primary medium, topics include sculptural material properties, human anatomy, form modeling, spatial composition, and surface treatment. Through progressive training in relief, portrait study, bust modeling, and figure sculpture, students master three-dimensional form observation and expression techniques. The course balances traditional representational foundations with introduction to contemporary sculpture concepts, guiding students to understand expressive possibilities of material and spatial form.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：等大人体躯干雕塑创作

学生需以真人模特为参照，完成一件等大人体躯干泥塑作品，高度约80-90厘米。要求准确表现人体骨骼肌肉结构、体块关系与动态韵律，同时注重表面质感的处理与个人雕塑语言的探索。作品需包含完整的内骨架搭建、大形塑造、细节刻画与表面处理全过程。需提交3份不同角度的小稿模型与创作日志，记录从构思到完成的整个创作过程，体现对人体结构的理解与三维造型能力的掌握。

Working from a live model, students create a life-size clay sculpture of the human torso, approximately 80-90 centimeters in height. The work must accurately depict skeletal and muscular structure, volumetric relationships, and rhythmic movement, with attention to surface texture and personal sculptural language exploration. The project includes the complete process of armature building, massing, detailing, and surface finishing. Students submit three maquettes from different angles and a creation journal documenting the entire process.
