绘画基础

Painting Fundamentals

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：素描基础 | 学期：第二学期

本课程是视觉艺术专业的核心实践课程，系统教授绘画的基本技法与表现语言。课程以油画和丙烯为主要媒介，内容包括颜料特性、调色方法、笔触运用、画面构成、光影塑造等核心要素。通过静物写生、风景写生、人物肖像等不同题材的训练，帮助学生掌握色彩表现与画面组织能力。课程兼顾传统技法学习与个人风格探索，引导学生在掌握基础的同时发展独特的绘画语言，为进阶的创作课程做好准备。

This core studio course teaches fundamental painting techniques and expressive language. Focusing on oil and acrylic media, topics include paint properties, color mixing, brushwork, composition, and light and shadow modeling. Through still life, landscape, and portrait painting exercises, students develop color expression and pictorial organization skills. The course balances traditional technique study with personal style exploration, guiding students to develop unique painterly language while mastering fundamentals, preparing them for advanced creative coursework.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：主题性绘画创作——"光与空间"

学生需以"光与空间"为主题创作一幅尺寸不小于120cm×150cm的架上绘画作品。要求运用本学期所学的色彩理论与构图知识，通过特定光源下的空间场景表现光的氛围与情感。作品需包含至少3个不同质感的物体与明确的空间层次，可选择室内或室外场景。需提交至少5幅色彩小稿与2幅素描稿，展示创作构思过程，并附创作说明阐述对光与空间关系的个人理解。

Students create an easel painting no smaller than 120cm × 150cm on the theme of "Light and Space." Applying color theory and composition knowledge from the semester, students depict the atmosphere and emotion of light in a spatial scene under specific lighting conditions. The work must include at least three objects of different textures and clear spatial layers, with indoor or outdoor settings acceptable. Students submit at least five color studies and two sketch drawings showing the creative process, plus an artist statement explaining their personal understanding of light and space relationships.
