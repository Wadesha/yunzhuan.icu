教育史

Education History

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：2 | 建议学期：第1学期

教育史介绍教育思想和制度的发展历程。学习古代教育、近代教育改革、现代教育理论等内容，理解教育发展的历史脉络。

Education history introduces development of educational ideas and systems. Learn ancient education, modern reforms, and contemporary theories to understand historical context.

学习示例 Learning Example

📋 示例：比较中西方教育传统：中国古代强调儒家经典和科举制度，注重道德修养；西方近代强调科学知识和个性发展，注重培养批判性思维。
📋 Example: Compare Chinese and Western education traditions: Ancient China emphasized Confucian classics and imperial exams, focusing on moral cultivation; modern West emphasizes science and individuality, fostering critical thinking.
