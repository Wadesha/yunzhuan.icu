色彩理论

Color Theory

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：2 | 建议学期：第1学期

色彩理论学习色彩的基本属性和搭配原则。掌握色相、明度、饱和度、色彩和谐、对比等概念，培养色彩感知和运用能力。

Color theory teaches color properties and combination principles. Master hue, value, saturation, color harmony, and contrast to develop color perception and application skills.

学习示例 Learning Example

📋 示例：选择一套品牌配色：主色使用蓝色传达信任和专业，辅助色用橙色增加活力，中性色用灰色和白色保持平衡。确保在不同背景下都有良好的可读性。
📋 Example: Choose brand colors: Use blue as primary color for trust and professionalism, orange as accent for energy, gray and white neutrals for balance. Ensure good readability across backgrounds.
