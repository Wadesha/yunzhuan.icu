计算机科学

Computer Science

中文介绍

计算机科学是研究计算机系统、算法和计算理论的学科。它涵盖了软件开发、人工智能、计算机网络、数据库系统、操作系统等多个方向。计算机科学专业培养学生的编程能力、逻辑思维和问题解决能力，毕业生在科技、金融、医疗、教育等多个行业都有广泛的就业机会。根据NCES数据，计算机与信息科学专业的学位授予量从2014年到2024年增长了一倍以上，是目前最热门的STEM专业之一。

English Introduction

Computer Science is the study of computer systems, algorithms, and computational theory. It encompasses software development, artificial intelligence, computer networks, database systems, operating systems, and more. The Computer Science major equips students with programming skills, logical thinking, and problem-solving abilities. Graduates have extensive career opportunities in technology, finance, healthcare, education, and many other industries. According to NCES data, degrees awarded in computer and information sciences more than doubled from 2014 to 2024, making it one of the most popular STEM majors.

核心课程 Core Courses

离散数学 数据结构 算法设计与分析 计算机组成原理 操作系统 计算机网络 数据库系统 软件工程
