🔒 网络安全

Cybersecurity

中文介绍

网络安全是计算机科学的一个重要分支，研究如何保护计算机系统和网络免受未经授权的访问、攻击和数据泄露。网络安全专业的课程包括网络协议、加密技术、安全审计、入侵检测等。随着网络攻击事件的不断增加，对网络安全专业人才的需求持续上升。网络空间安全、信息安全等相关专业的火爆程度直线上升，毕业生在政府、金融、科技等领域有广泛的就业机会。

English Introduction

Cybersecurity is an important branch of computer science that studies how to protect computer systems and networks from unauthorized access, attacks, and data breaches. Coursework includes network protocols, encryption techniques, security auditing, and intrusion detection. As cyber attacks continue to increase, demand for cybersecurity professionals keeps rising. Cybersecurity and information security-related majors have become increasingly popular, with graduates having extensive employment opportunities in government, finance, technology, and other sectors.
