🤖 人工智能

Artificial Intelligence

中文介绍

人工智能是计算机科学的一个分支，研究如何使计算机系统模拟人类智能的能力。它涵盖机器学习、深度学习、自然语言处理、计算机视觉、机器人学等多个方向。人工智能专业近年来发展迅速，Common App数据显示AI方向的申请量同比增长35%。毕业生在科技巨头、金融科技、医疗健康、自动驾驶等领域有极高的就业需求，平均起薪可达135,000美元。

English Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is a branch of computer science that studies how to enable computer systems to simulate human intelligence. It covers machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, and more. The AI major has grown rapidly in recent years, with Common App data showing a 35% year-over-year increase in AI-related applications. Graduates have high employment demand in tech giants, fintech, healthcare, autonomous driving, and other fields, with average starting salaries reaching $135,000.
