人机交互

Human-Computer Interaction

中文介绍

人机交互是设计、心理学与计算机科学的交叉领域，研究人与计算机之间的交互方式。人机交互专业的课程包括用户体验设计、交互设计、可用性测试、人机界面开发等。作为艺术与科技的跨界融合，人机交互正成为艺术生跨界的"跳板"。纽约大学的数字媒体学习设计硕士项目提供教育技术、游戏设计、交互媒体三大培养路径，对跨专业申请者友好。毕业生可从事UX/UI设计师、交互设计师、产品设计师等职业。

Human-Computer Interaction is an interdisciplinary field of design, psychology, and computer science that studies the interaction between humans and computers. Coursework includes user experience design, interaction design, usability testing, and human-computer interface development. As a crossover of art and technology, HCI has become a "springboard" for art students. NYU's Digital Media Learning Design program offers three tracks: educational technology, game design, and interactive media, friendly to cross-disciplinary applicants. Graduates work as UX/UI designers, interaction designers, and product designers.
