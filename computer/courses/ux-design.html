用户体验设计

User Experience Design

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：交互设计、界面设计 | 学期：第5学期

用户体验设计是一门综合性实践课程，从全生命周期视角系统讲解UX设计方法论。课程涵盖用户体验战略、用户旅程地图、服务蓝图、设计思维、敏捷协作与设计交付，强调以用户为中心整合研究、交互、视觉与内容设计。学生将通过真实项目训练跨职能协作能力，掌握从业务目标到设计落地的完整链路，输出可工程化的设计资产与体验度量方案。

User Experience Design is a comprehensive practice course covering UX methodology across the full product lifecycle. Topics include UX strategy, user journey maps, service blueprints, design thinking, agile collaboration, and design delivery. Students develop cross-functional collaboration skills through real projects, mastering the end-to-end flow from business goals to design implementation, with measurable experience outcomes.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：全链路用户体验设计实践与体验度量

选择真实业务场景，完成从用户研究、体验战略、交互设计到设计交付的全链路UX设计。提交用户旅程地图、设计原型与体验度量方案。

Select a real business scenario, complete an end-to-end UX design from user research, experience strategy, and interaction design to design delivery. Deliver a user journey map, design prototype, and experience metrics plan.
