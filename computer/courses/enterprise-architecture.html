企业架构

Enterprise Architecture

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：系统分析与设计 | 学期：第5学期

企业架构课程讲授以TOGAF、Zachman等主流框架对企业进行整体建模与规划的方法。主要内容包括业务架构、应用架构、数据架构与技术架构四个维度，架构开发方法（ADM）各阶段实践，IT与业务战略对齐，治理与标准制定，转型路线图设计。课程结合真实案例，培养学生从战略视角统筹企业信息化建设的能力。

Enterprise Architecture covers methods for holistic enterprise modeling and planning using frameworks such as TOGAF and Zachman. Topics span business, application, data, and technology architectures; the Architecture Development Method (ADM); aligning IT with business strategy; governance and standards; and transformation roadmap design. Real-world cases build strategic capability for enterprise digital initiatives.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：企业数字化转型TOGAF方案

学生小组为选定企业（如制造、零售）制定数字化转型架构方案，按TOGAF ADM流程输出：架构愿景、当前架构（基线）、目标架构（业务/应用/数据/技术）、差距分析、机会与解决方案、迁移路线图与治理建议。提交完整架构文档与架构图，进行课堂答辩。

Teams design a digital transformation architecture plan for a chosen enterprise (such as manufacturing or retail) following TOGAF ADM: architecture vision, baseline architecture, target architecture (business/application/data/technology), gap analysis, opportunities and solutions, migration roadmap, and governance recommendations. Submit the full architecture document and diagrams with a class defense.
