用户研究方法

User Research Methods

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：心理学基础 | 学期：第3学期

用户研究方法系统讲授如何科学地理解用户需求、行为与体验。课程涵盖定性研究（深度访谈、田野观察、焦点小组）与定量研究（问卷调查、A/B测试、日志分析）两大类方法，重点训练研究方案设计、抽样、数据收集与分析的完整流程。学生将学习用户画像构建、用户旅程地图等工具，将研究结果转化为设计洞察，支撑产品决策。

User Research Methods systematically teaches how to scientifically understand user needs, behaviors, and experiences. The course covers qualitative methods such as in-depth interviews, field observation, and focus groups, as well as quantitative methods including surveys, A/B testing, and log analysis. Students learn research design, sampling, data collection, and analysis, transforming findings into design insights.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：产品用户研究全流程实践与洞察转化

针对一款产品，设计并执行完整的用户研究方案，综合运用定性访谈与定量问卷，输出用户画像与用户旅程地图。提交研究方案、原始数据与研究洞察报告。

For a product, design and execute a complete user research plan, combining qualitative interviews and quantitative surveys, and produce user personas and journey maps. Deliver a research plan, raw data, and an insight report.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
研究方案设计
明确研究问题，设计访谈提纲与问卷
产出：研究方案文档
步骤 2
定性研究
深度访谈与田野观察，记录用户行为
产出：访谈记录与转录
步骤 3
定量研究
问卷发放与A/B测试，收集定量数据
产出：问卷数据集
步骤 4
数据分析
编码分析定性数据，统计定量数据
产出：数据分析报告
步骤 5
洞察转化
构建用户画像与旅程地图，提出设计洞察
产出：最终提交材料

Steps

Step 1
Research Plan Design
Define research questions, design interview guide and survey
Deliverable: Research Plan Doc
Step 2
Qualitative Research
Conduct in-depth interviews and field observations
Deliverable: Interview Records
Step 3
Quantitative Research
Distribute surveys and A/B tests, collect data
Deliverable: Survey Dataset
Step 4
Data Analysis
Code qualitative data, analyze quantitative data
Deliverable: Data Analysis Report
Step 5
Insight Translation
Build personas and journey maps, propose insights
Deliverable: Final Submission
