统计学

Statistics

中文介绍

统计学是一门通过收集、整理、分析和解释数据来提取信息和做出决策的科学。它涵盖了描述统计、推断统计、概率论、回归分析、贝叶斯统计、统计计算等多个方向。统计学专业的课程包括概率论、数理统计、回归分析、多元统计分析、时间序列分析等。毕业生在科技公司、金融机构、政府部门、科研机构、医药行业等都有广泛的就业机会。统计学专业培养学生的数据思维和定量分析能力，是数据科学和人工智能领域的重要基础。

English Introduction

Statistics is the science of collecting, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting data to extract information and make decisions. It covers descriptive statistics, inferential statistics, probability theory, regression analysis, Bayesian statistics, statistical computing, and more. Coursework includes probability theory, mathematical statistics, regression analysis, multivariate statistical analysis, and time series analysis. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in tech companies, financial institutions, government agencies, research institutions, and the pharmaceutical industry. Statistics develops students' data thinking and quantitative analysis abilities, serving as an important foundation for data science and artificial intelligence.
