中文介绍

数学是一门研究数量、结构、变化和空间的科学。它涵盖了代数、几何、微积分、概率论、统计学、数论等多个方向。数学专业的课程包括微积分、线性代数、抽象代数、实分析、复分析等。毕业生在科技公司、金融机构、科研机构、教育机构等都有广泛的就业机会。数学专业培养学生的逻辑推理能力和抽象思维能力，是许多STEM领域的基础。

Mathematics is the study of quantity, structure, change, and space. It covers algebra, geometry, calculus, probability, statistics, number theory, and more. Coursework includes calculus, linear algebra, abstract algebra, real analysis, and complex analysis. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in tech companies, financial institutions, research institutions, and educational institutions. Mathematics develops students' logical reasoning and abstract thinking abilities, serving as the foundation for many STEM fields.
