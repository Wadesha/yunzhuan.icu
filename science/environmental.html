环境科学

Environmental Science

中文介绍

环境科学是一门研究人类与自然环境相互作用的学科。它涵盖了环境化学、环境生物学、环境工程、环境政策、可持续发展等多个方向。环境科学专业的课程包括生物学、化学、生态学、环境政策、环境工程基础等。毕业生在政府环保部门、环保组织、科研机构、企业环境部门等都有广泛的就业机会。随着环保意识的增强，环境科学专业的需求持续增长。

English Introduction

Environmental Science is the study of the interaction between humans and the natural environment. It covers environmental chemistry, environmental biology, environmental engineering, environmental policy, sustainable development, and more. Coursework includes biology, chemistry, ecology, environmental policy, and environmental engineering fundamentals. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in government environmental agencies, environmental organizations, research institutions, and corporate environmental departments. With increased environmental awareness, demand for environmental science continues to grow.
