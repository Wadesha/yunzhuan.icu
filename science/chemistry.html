中文介绍

化学是一门研究物质的组成、结构、性质和变化的科学。它涵盖了有机化学、无机化学、分析化学、物理化学、生物化学等多个方向。化学专业的课程包括普通化学、有机化学、无机化学、物理化学、分析化学等。毕业生在制药行业、化工行业、科研机构、教育机构等都有广泛的就业机会。化学专业培养学生的实验能力和科学思维。

Chemistry is the study of the composition, structure, properties, and changes of matter. It covers organic chemistry, inorganic chemistry, analytical chemistry, physical chemistry, biochemistry, and more. Coursework includes general chemistry, organic chemistry, inorganic chemistry, physical chemistry, and analytical chemistry. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in pharmaceutical industry, chemical industry, research institutions, and educational institutions. Chemistry develops students' experimental skills and scientific thinking.
