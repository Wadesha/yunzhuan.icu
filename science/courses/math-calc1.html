微积分I

Calculus I

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：高中数学 | 学期：秋季

微积分I是数学专业的基础核心课程，主要内容包括函数与极限、导数与微分、中值定理与导数应用、不定积分与定积分。通过本课程的学习，学生将掌握极限的严格定义、求导法则、积分基本定理，理解微积分的基本思想方法，为后续数学课程和物理、工程等应用学科奠定坚实基础。课程注重理论与应用的结合，培养学生的逻辑思维和计算能力。

Calculus I is a foundational core course for mathematics majors, covering functions and limits, derivatives and differentials, mean value theorems and derivative applications, indefinite and definite integrals. Students will master the rigorous definition of limits, differentiation rules, and the fundamental theorem of calculus. The course emphasizes both theory and applications, developing students' logical thinking and computational abilities.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：极限与导数的应用问题建模（Modeling Applied Problems with Limits and Derivatives）

选取一个实际应用问题（如最优化问题、相关变化率或经济学边际分析），运用极限、导数与中值定理建立数学模型，求解并讨论结果的物理或实际意义，体现微积分基本思想。

Choose a real-world problem (optimization, related rates, or marginal analysis in economics), build a mathematical model using limits, derivatives, and mean value theorems, solve it, and discuss the physical or practical meaning of the results.
