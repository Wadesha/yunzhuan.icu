概率论

Probability Theory

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：微积分、统计学导论 | 学期：大一下学期

本课程系统讲授概率论的基本理论和方法，是数理统计和随机过程的先修课程。内容包括概率空间与概率测度、条件概率与独立性、随机变量及其分布、多维随机变量、随机变量的数字特征、大数定律与中心极限定理、常用离散和连续分布等。课程注重理论推导与实际应用相结合，培养学生运用概率模型分析和解决实际问题的能力。

This course provides a rigorous introduction to probability theory, serving as a prerequisite for mathematical statistics and stochastic processes. Topics include probability spaces, conditional probability, independence, random variables and distributions, multivariate random variables, expectation, laws of large numbers, central limit theorem, and common discrete and continuous distributions.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：概率模型在实际问题中的建模与模拟（Modeling and Simulation of Probability Models in Real Problems）

选取一个实际问题（如排队系统、保险风险或随机游走），建立概率模型，推导相关数字特征与极限定理，并用蒙特卡洛模拟验证理论结果。

Choose a real problem (queuing, insurance risk, or random walk), build a probability model, derive numerical characteristics and limit theorems, and verify theory with Monte Carlo simulation.
