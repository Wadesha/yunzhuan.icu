基础物理实验

Basic Physics Laboratory

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：2 | 先修课：力学（同步） | 学期：全年

本课程是物理学专业的实验入门课程，涵盖力学、热学、电磁学和光学等基础物理实验。学生将学习常用物理仪器的使用方法、实验数据的测量与处理、误差分析理论以及实验报告的规范撰写。课程强调动手能力和科学素养的培养，通过约20个经典实验项目，使学生掌握基本的实验技能和科学研究方法，为后续专业实验课程奠定基础。

This course is the introductory experimental course for physics majors, covering basic physics experiments in mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and optics. Students will learn how to use common physics instruments, measure and process experimental data, error analysis theory, and standard experimental report writing. The course emphasizes the development of hands-on skills and scientific literacy. Through approximately 20 classic experimental projects, students master basic experimental techniques and scientific research methods, laying the foundation for subsequent specialized experimental courses.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：物理实验设计与误差分析研究（Physics Experiment Design and Error Analysis Study）

围绕力学、热学、电磁学或光学某一领域，自主设计一个物理测量实验，规范使用仪器，进行系统误差与随机误差分析，并撰写符合规范的研究性实验报告。

In mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, or optics, independently design a physics measurement experiment, use instruments properly, perform systematic and random error analysis, and write a standard research-style experimental report.
