无机化学

Inorganic Chemistry

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：普通化学 | 学期：大二上

无机化学研究除碳氢化合物以外的所有元素及其化合物。课程内容包括化学键理论的深化、配位化学、晶体结构、主族元素化学、过渡金属化学、镧系与锕系元素、无机反应机理、金属有机化学初步、生物无机化学以及无机材料化学等。通过理论学习和实验训练，学生将系统掌握无机化学的基本原理和各类元素的性质规律。

Inorganic Chemistry studies all elements and their compounds except hydrocarbons. Course content includes advanced bonding theory, coordination chemistry, crystal structure, main group chemistry, transition metal chemistry, lanthanides and actinides, inorganic reaction mechanisms, organometallic chemistry introduction, bioinorganic chemistry, and inorganic materials chemistry. Students master fundamental principles and periodic trends through theory and experiments.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：过渡金属配合物的合成与表征（Synthesis and Characterization of Transition Metal Complexes）

选定一种过渡金属配合物，完成其合成路线设计、实验制备与结构表征，运用配位化学理论解释其颜色、磁性等性质，并讨论其在催化或材料中的应用前景。

Select a transition metal complex, design its synthesis route, prepare it experimentally, characterize its structure, explain its color and magnetic properties using coordination theory, and discuss applications in catalysis or materials.
