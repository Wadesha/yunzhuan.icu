统计学习

Statistical Learning

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：回归分析、统计计算、线性代数 | 学期：大三下学期

本课程系统讲授统计学习的理论和方法，是统计学与机器学习交叉的核心课程。内容包括监督学习与无监督学习、模型评估与选择、线性模型的正则化（岭回归、Lasso）、分类方法（Logistic回归、线性判别分析、K近邻）、决策树与集成学习（随机森林、Boosting）、支持向量机、主成分分析、聚类分析（K-means、层次聚类）、神经网络基础等。课程强调理论理解与实践应用并重，培养学生解决实际数据分析问题的能力。

This course covers the theory and methods of statistical learning, a core course at the intersection of statistics and machine learning. Topics include supervised and unsupervised learning, model evaluation and selection, regularized linear models (ridge, lasso), classification methods (logistic regression, LDA, KNN), decision trees and ensemble methods (random forests, boosting), support vector machines, PCA, clustering, and neural network basics.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：统计学习方法的实际数据建模与比较（Real Data Modeling and Comparison of Statistical Learning Methods）

选取一个真实数据集，运用至少三种统计学习方法（如正则化回归、随机森林、SVM）建模，进行模型评估、超参数调优与模型解释，撰写对比分析报告。

Choose a real dataset, model with at least three statistical learning methods (regularized regression, random forests, SVM), perform evaluation, hyperparameter tuning, and model interpretation, and write a comparison report.
