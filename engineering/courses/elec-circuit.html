电路分析

Circuit Analysis

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：高等数学、大学物理 | 学期：第3学期

电路分析是电气工程专业的入门核心课程，系统讲授集总参数电路的基本理论与分析方法。课程内容包括电路元件特性、基尔霍夫定律、节点电压法与网孔电流法、戴维南与诺顿定理、一阶与二阶动态电路的时域分析、正弦稳态交流电路的相量分析及频率响应。通过本课程学习，学生能够建立电路模型并熟练分析直流与交流电路，为后续电子技术、信号与系统等课程奠定理论基础。

Circuit Analysis is the foundational core course for Electrical Engineering majors, systematically teaching the basic theory and methods of lumped-parameter circuits. Topics include circuit element characteristics, Kirchhoff's laws, nodal and mesh analysis, Thevenin and Norton theorems, transient analysis of first- and second-order dynamic circuits, phasor analysis of sinusoidal steady-state AC circuits, and frequency response. Students learn to model and analyze DC and AC circuits, laying the theoretical foundation for subsequent courses in electronics and signals.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：含运算放大器有源滤波电路设计与仿真 / Active Filter Circuit Design with Op-Amp and Simulation

设计指定通带特性的有源滤波电路（如二阶低通/带通），完成电路拓扑选择、参数计算与Multisim/SPICE仿真验证。

Design an active filter circuit (e.g. second-order low-pass/band-pass) with specified passband characteristics, completing circuit topology selection, parameter calculation and Multisim/SPICE simulation verification.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
设计指标分析
确定截止频率、品质因数与增益指标
产出：设计需求文档
步骤 2
电路拓扑选择
选择Sallen-Key或多路反馈等拓扑
产出：电路拓扑方案
步骤 3
参数计算
计算电阻电容元件参数
产出：元件参数表
步骤 4
仿真验证
在Multisim/SPICE中仿真幅频与相频特性
产出：仿真波形与曲线
步骤 5
误差分析与报告
分析灵敏度与元件误差影响并撰写设计报告
产出：电路设计报告

Steps

Step 1
Design Specification Analysis
Define cutoff frequency, Q factor and gain specs
Deliverable: Design Spec Document
Step 2
Circuit Topology Selection
Select Sallen-Key or multiple feedback topology
Deliverable: Circuit Topology Scheme
Step 3
Parameter Calculation
Calculate resistor and capacitor component values
Deliverable: Component Parameter Table
Step 4
Simulation Verification
Simulate magnitude and phase response in Multisim/SPICE
Deliverable: Simulation Waveforms
Step 5
Error Analysis and Report
Analyze sensitivity and component tolerance and write report
Deliverable: Circuit Design Report
