化工设计

Chemical Engineering Design

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：反应工程、分离工程、化工热力学 | 学期：大四上

化工设计是化学工程专业的综合性核心课程，培养学生运用所学专业知识进行化工过程与设备设计的能力。内容包括化工设计程序与内容、工艺流程设计、物料衡算与热量衡算、设备选型与设计、车间布置与管道布置、安全与环境保护、技术经济分析与评价等。课程以典型化工产品的全流程设计为主线，训练学生的工程设计能力与创新思维，为毕业设计和未来工作奠定基础。

Chemical Engineering Design is a comprehensive core course for chemical engineering majors,培养 students' ability to apply professional knowledge for chemical process and equipment design. Topics include design procedures and content, process flow design, material and energy balances, equipment selection and design, plant layout and piping design, safety and environmental protection, and techno-economic analysis and evaluation. The course uses full-process design of typical chemical products as the main thread, training engineering design ability and innovative thinking, laying the foundation for graduation design and future work.
