飞行器结构设计

Aircraft Structures Design

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：结构力学、材料力学 | 学期：大四上

本课程是航空航天工程专业的核心专业课，讲授飞行器结构设计的基本原理与方法。内容包括飞行器结构设计概述与设计准则、机翼结构设计、机身结构设计、尾翼与操纵面结构设计、起落架结构设计、连接设计、结构疲劳与断裂、复合材料结构设计基础。课程结合工程实例，使学生掌握飞行器主要承力结构的设计原理与方法，培养结构方案设计与强度分析能力。

This core aerospace engineering course teaches fundamental principles and methods of aircraft structural design. Topics include aircraft structural design overview and criteria, wing structural design, fuselage structural design, empennage and control surface design, landing gear structural design, joint design, structural fatigue and fracture, and composite structure design fundamentals. Through engineering examples, students master design principles and methods for primary load-bearing aircraft structures, developing structural configuration design and strength analysis capabilities.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：民用飞机机翼结构方案设计 / Civil Aircraft Wing Structural Configuration Design

完成某型民用飞机机翼结构方案设计，包括翼盒、蒙皮、桁条、翼梁的选材与布置。结合强度要求进行结构布置与传力路径设计。

Complete the structural configuration design of a civil aircraft wing, including material selection and arrangement of wing box, skin, stringers, and spars. Design structural layout and load paths considering strength requirements.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
设计要求分析
确定机翼气动载荷、油箱容积与强度准则
产出：设计需求文件
步骤 2
结构布局设计
设计翼盒、翼梁与桁条布置方案
产出：结构布局方案图
步骤 3
关键件选材与设计
选择蒙皮、长桁材料并设计尺寸
产出：关键件设计图
步骤 4
传力路径分析
分析气动载荷传力路径并校核主要承力件
产出：传力分析报告
步骤 5
疲劳评估与报告
进行疲劳寿命初步评估并撰写设计报告
产出：结构设计报告

Steps

Step 1
Design Requirements Analysis
Define wing aerodynamic loads, fuel volume and strength criteria
Deliverable: Design Requirements Doc
Step 2
Structural Layout Design
Design wing box, spar and stringer layout scheme
Deliverable: Layout Scheme Drawing
Step 3
Key Component Design
Select skin and stringer materials and design dimensions
Deliverable: Key Component Drawings
Step 4
Load Path Analysis
Analyze load paths and check primary load-bearing members
Deliverable: Load Path Analysis Report
Step 5
Fatigue Evaluation and Report
Conduct preliminary fatigue evaluation and write design report
Deliverable: Structural Design Report
