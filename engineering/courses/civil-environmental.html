环境工程

Environmental Engineering

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：流体力学、普通化学 | 学期：第5学期

环境工程是土木工程专业的重要方向课，研究水、大气、固体废物等污染的控制与治理技术。内容包括水质指标与水污染控制、给排水工程、活性污泥法与生物膜法、大气污染与除尘脱硫技术、固体废物处理与处置、噪声控制及环境影响评价等。课程注重原理与工程设计结合，培养学生在环境保护与可持续发展领域的工程实践能力。

Environmental Engineering is an important course in civil engineering, addressing the control and treatment of water, air, and solid waste pollution. Topics include water quality indicators, water and wastewater engineering, activated sludge and biofilm processes, air pollution control with dust removal and desulfurization, solid waste management, noise control, and environmental impact assessment. The course integrates principles with engineering design for environmental protection and sustainability.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：城市污水处理厂工艺设计与计算

给定某城镇日均污水量2万立方米及进出水水质指标，学生需选择合适的处理工艺流程（如A²/O或氧化沟），完成主要构筑物如格栅、沉砂池、初沉池、曝气池及二沉池的尺寸计算与工艺参数确定，并绘制工艺高程布置图。最终提交设计计算书、工艺流程图及主要构筑物平面布置图。

Given a daily wastewater flow of 20,000 m³ and influent/effluent quality targets for a town, students select an appropriate process (such as A²/O or oxidation ditch), calculate dimensions and parameters for major units including screens, grit chambers, primary clarifiers, aeration tanks, and secondary clarifiers, and prepare a process layout drawing. A design calculation report, flow diagram, and unit layout plan are submitted.
