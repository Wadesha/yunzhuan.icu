工业工程

Industrial Engineering

中文介绍

工业工程是一门研究如何优化生产系统和流程的工程学科。它融合了工程、管理和数学知识，旨在提高效率、降低成本和改善质量。工业工程专业的课程包括运筹学、质量管理、供应链管理、人机工程、生产系统设计等。工业工程师在制造业、物流、服务业、 healthcare等领域都有广泛的就业机会。随着智能制造和工业4.0的发展，工业工程专业的需求日益增加。

English Introduction

Industrial Engineering is an engineering discipline that studies how to optimize production systems and processes. It combines engineering, management, and mathematics to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance quality. Coursework includes operations research, quality management, supply chain management, human factors engineering, and production system design. Industrial engineers have extensive employment opportunities in manufacturing, logistics, services, and healthcare. With the development of smart manufacturing and Industry 4.0, demand for industrial engineering continues to increase.
