️ 土木工程

Civil Engineering

中文介绍

土木工程是一门研究基础设施设计、建造和维护的工程学科。它涵盖了建筑工程、道路桥梁、水利工程、环境工程等多个方向。土木工程专业的课程包括结构力学、材料力学、土力学、测量学、施工管理等。土木工程师在政府部门、建筑公司、咨询公司等都有广泛的就业机会。随着基础设施建设和环保需求的增加，土木工程专业保持稳定的就业前景。

English Introduction

Civil Engineering is an engineering discipline that studies the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure. It covers building engineering, roads and bridges, hydraulic engineering, environmental engineering, and more. Coursework includes structural mechanics, materials mechanics, soil mechanics, surveying, and construction management. Civil engineers have extensive employment opportunities in government, construction companies, and consulting firms. With increasing infrastructure development and environmental protection needs, civil engineering maintains stable employment prospects.
