⚡ 电气工程

Electrical Engineering

中文介绍

电气工程是一门研究电能的产生、传输、分配、控制和应用的工程学科。它涵盖了电路理论、电子技术、电力系统、通信系统、控制系统等多个方向。电气工程专业的课程包括高等数学、物理、电路分析、电子器件、信号处理等。毕业生在电力公司、电子设备制造、通信行业、新能源等领域有广泛的就业机会。电气工程是最受欢迎的工程专业之一，学位授予量在工程类中排名靠前。

English Introduction

Electrical Engineering is an engineering discipline that studies the generation, transmission, distribution, control, and application of electrical energy. It covers circuit theory, electronics, power systems, communication systems, control systems, and more. Coursework includes advanced mathematics, physics, circuit analysis, electronic devices, and signal processing. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in power companies, electronics manufacturing, telecommunications, and renewable energy. Electrical Engineering is one of the most popular engineering majors, with high degree award rates among engineering fields.
