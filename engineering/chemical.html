🧪 化学工程

Chemical Engineering

中文介绍

化学工程是一门研究化学过程和物理过程在工业规模上的应用的工程学科。它涵盖了化学反应工程、分离工程、传热传质、过程控制等多个方向。化学工程专业的课程包括化学原理、物理化学、热力学、流体力学、过程设计等。化学工程师在化工、制药、材料、能源等行业都有广泛的就业机会。随着新能源和新材料领域的发展，化学工程专业的需求持续增长。

English Introduction

Chemical Engineering is an engineering discipline that studies the application of chemical and physical processes on an industrial scale. It covers reaction engineering, separation engineering, heat and mass transfer, process control, and more. Coursework includes chemistry principles, physical chemistry, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and process design. Chemical engineers have extensive employment opportunities in chemical industry, pharmaceuticals, materials, and energy. With the development of new energy and new materials, demand for chemical engineering continues to grow.
