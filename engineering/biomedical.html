生物医学工程

Biomedical Engineering

中文介绍

生物医学工程是工程学、医学和生物学的交叉学科，研究如何将工程技术应用于医疗健康领域。它涵盖了医疗设备设计、生物信号处理、组织工程、医学成像等多个方向。生物医学工程专业的课程包括生物学、生理学、生物力学、电子学、材料科学等。毕业生在医疗器械公司、医院、制药行业、科研机构等都有广泛的就业机会。医疗赛道需求稳定，AI替代率低于20%。

English Introduction

Biomedical Engineering is an interdisciplinary field combining engineering, medicine, and biology, studying how to apply engineering technology to healthcare. It covers medical device design, biological signal processing, tissue engineering, medical imaging, and more. Coursework includes biology, physiology, biomechanics, electronics, and materials science. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in medical device companies, hospitals, pharmaceutical industry, and research institutions. Healthcare demand remains stable, with AI replacement rate below 20%.

核心课程 Core Courses

生物力学 生物材料 生物仪器 医学影像 生理学 生物机电 法规与伦理
