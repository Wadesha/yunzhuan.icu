️ 机械工程

Mechanical Engineering

中文介绍

机械工程是一门涉及设计、制造、分析和维护机械系统的工程学科。它涵盖了从微小的精密零件到大型工业设备的广泛领域。机械工程专业的课程包括力学、材料科学、热力学、流体力学、设计制图等。机械工程师在航空航天、汽车、能源、医疗设备等行业都有广泛的就业机会。作为传统工科的代表，机械工程始终保持稳定的需求，2024年新增岗位同比增长18%。

Mechanical Engineering is an engineering discipline that involves the design, manufacturing, analysis, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It covers a wide range from tiny precision components to large industrial equipment. Coursework includes mechanics, materials science, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and design drafting. Mechanical engineers have extensive career opportunities in aerospace, automotive, energy, medical devices, and other industries. As a representative of traditional engineering, mechanical engineering maintains stable demand, with 18% year-over-year growth in new positions in 2024.
