🚀 航空航天工程

Aerospace Engineering

中文介绍

航空航天工程是一门研究航空器和航天器设计、开发和运营的工程学科。它涵盖了空气动力学、结构力学、推进系统、导航控制等多个方向。航空航天工程专业的课程包括流体力学、固体力学、航空电子、空间系统等。毕业生在航空航天公司、国防部门、科研机构等都有广泛的就业机会。随着航天产业的快速发展和太空探索的热潮，航空航天工程专业的需求持续上升。

English Introduction

Aerospace Engineering is an engineering discipline that studies the design, development, and operation of aircraft and spacecraft. It covers aerodynamics, structural mechanics, propulsion systems, navigation and control, and more. Coursework includes fluid mechanics, solid mechanics, avionics, and space systems. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in aerospace companies, defense sectors, and research institutions. With the rapid development of the space industry and the space exploration boom, demand for aerospace engineering continues to rise.
