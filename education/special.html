🤝 特殊教育

Special Education

中文介绍

特殊教育是一门研究如何为有特殊需求的学生提供教育服务的学科。它涵盖了学习障碍、智力障碍、自闭症、情感障碍等多个方向。特殊教育专业的课程包括特殊教育导论、学习障碍、行为管理、教学策略、个别化教育计划等。毕业生在特殊教育学校、普通学校特殊教育部门、康复机构、非营利组织等都有广泛的就业机会。特殊教育专业培养学生的耐心和专业能力。

English Introduction

Special Education is the study of providing educational services to students with special needs. It covers learning disabilities, intellectual disabilities, autism, emotional disorders, and more. Coursework includes introduction to special education, learning disabilities, behavior management, teaching strategies, and individualized education plans. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in special education schools, special education departments in regular schools, rehabilitation centers, and non-profit organizations. Special Education develops students' patience and professional abilities.
