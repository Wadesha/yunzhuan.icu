📚 教育学

Education

中文介绍

教育学是一门研究教育理论、教学方法和学习过程的学科。它涵盖了教育心理学、课程设计、教育管理、教育技术、特殊教育等多个方向。教育学专业的课程包括教育心理学、教学方法、课程理论、教育评估、教育技术等。毕业生在学校、教育机构、教育出版社、教育科技公司等都有广泛的就业机会。根据NCES数据，教育专业的学位授予量排名第九。

English Introduction

Education is the study of educational theory, teaching methods, and learning processes. It covers educational psychology, curriculum design, educational administration, educational technology, special education, and more. Coursework includes educational psychology, teaching methods, curriculum theory, educational assessment, and educational technology. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in schools, educational institutions, educational publishers, and edtech companies. According to NCES data, education ranks ninth in degree awards.
