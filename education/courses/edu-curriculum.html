课程与教学论

Curriculum and Instruction

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：教育学导论、教育心理学 | 学期：春季

本课程探讨课程开发与教学设计的理论与实践。课程论部分包括课程的基本概念、课程理论流派、课程目标、课程内容选择与组织、课程实施与评价。教学论部分包括教学过程、教学原则、教学模式与方法、教学设计、教学评价、课堂管理。课程强调理论与实践结合，通过微格教学、教案设计等实践活动提升教学能力。

This course explores theories and practices of curriculum development and instructional design. Curriculum topics include basic concepts, theoretical perspectives, curriculum goals, content selection and organization, implementation, and evaluation. Instructional topics include teaching processes, principles, models and methods, lesson design, assessment, and classroom management. Practical activities such as micro-teaching and lesson planning build teaching competence.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：单元教学设计与课堂实施

选择某学科的一个教学单元，完成单元目标设计、课时安排、教学活动设计与评价方案，并进行一节课的模拟教学。

Select a teaching unit in a subject, complete unit objectives, lesson scheduling, instructional activities, and evaluation design, and conduct a simulated lesson.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：设计一个单元的教学——从课标到教案，完整规划一个单元怎么教、学生怎么学。
步骤 1
课程问题诊断与需求分析
本步骤的核心任务是对现有课程体系进行全面诊断，识别存在的问题并分析改进需求。课程诊断是课程开发的起点，只有找准问题，才能设计出有针对性的改进方案。泰勒的课程编制原理（目标-内容-方法-评价）为课程分析提供了经典框架。

• 运用文献研究法和比较研究法，梳理国内外同类课程的发展趋势和最佳实践，建立课程分析的参照系和理想模型
• 采用问卷调查、访谈、课堂观察、学生作业分析等多种方法，从课程目标、课程内容、教学方法、评价方式四个维度诊断现有课程的优势与不足
• 运用需求评估模型，分析学生发展需求、社会发展需求、学科发展需求三者之间的关系，确定课程改进的核心方向和优先级
产出：课程诊断与需求分析报告（含现状分析、问题诊断、需求评估、改进方向）｜ 质量标准：诊断全面深入，问题定位精准，需求分析有数据支撑
步骤 2
课程理念重构与目标体系设计
本步骤的核心任务是确立课程新理念，构建体现核心素养导向的课程目标体系。课程理念是课程的灵魂，决定着课程的价值取向和发展方向。核心素养导向的课程改革要求从知识本位转向素养本位，从学科中心转向学生中心。

• 深入研究核心素养理论、建构主义学习理论、深度学习理论等现代教育理念，结合学科特点和学校实际，确立课程的核心理念和价值取向
• 按照"核心素养-课程目标-单元目标-课时目标"的层级结构，设计四级课程目标体系，确保目标的一致性和可操作性
• 运用布卢姆教育目标分类学（修订版）和SOLO分类理论，对目标进行分类和层次划分，为教学实施和评价提供明确依据
产出：课程理念与目标体系设计方案（含课程理念阐述、四级目标体系图、目标分类表）｜ 质量标准：理念先进有特色，目标体系完整有层次，表述具体可测
步骤 3
课程内容重构与单元教学设计
本步骤的核心任务是按照核心素养导向重构课程内容，设计大单元教学方案。传统的以知识点为中心的课程内容组织方式难以支持核心素养的发展，需要以大概念、大任务、大项目为核心进行内容重构。逆向设计（UbD）为单元教学设计提供了有效框架。

• 运用大概念课程设计方法，筛选和提炼学科核心概念，构建以大概念为统领的课程内容结构，实现内容的整合化和结构化
• 采用逆向设计（Understanding by Design）的三阶段模式（确定预期结果-确定合适的评估证据-设计学习体验和教学）进行单元教学设计
• 设计真实情境下的驱动性问题和挑战性任务，将知识学习融入问题解决和任务完成的过程中，实现做中学、用中学、创中学
产出：课程内容重构方案与单元教学设计集（含内容结构图、3-5个单元设计详案、逆向设计模板）｜ 质量标准：内容结构清晰，大概念统领明确，单元设计符合UbD理念
步骤 4
教学模式创新与学习活动设计
本步骤的核心任务是创新教学模式，设计促进深度学习的学习活动。核心素养导向的课程需要与之相匹配的教学方式变革，从教师的"教"为中心转向学生的"学"为中心，从被动接受转向主动建构。项目式学习、探究式学习、问题式学习等是值得借鉴的教学模式。

• 借鉴PBL（项目式学习）、IBL（探究式学习）、翻转课堂、混合式学习等教学模式的核心要素，结合学科特点构建具有校本特色的教学模式
• 按照"情境导入-问题驱动-探究实践-成果展示-反思评价"的流程设计学习活动，确保学生经历完整的知识建构过程
• 设计多样化的学习支架，包括认知支架、元认知支架、社会支架、材料支架等，支持不同层次学生的深度学习
产出：教学模式创新方案与学习活动设计库（含教学模式图、典型课例设计、学习支架策略库）｜ 质量标准：教学模式有创新性，活动设计可操作，支架策略有针对性
步骤 5
课程评价体系构建与持续改进
本步骤的核心任务是构建发展性、多元化的课程评价体系，建立课程持续改进机制。评价不是课程的终点，而是促进课程不断完善的重要手段。核心素养导向的评价要求从单一纸笔测试转向多元化评价，从关注结果转向关注过程。

• 构建"教-学-评"一体化的评价体系，实现课程目标、教学活动与评价任务的一致性，确保评价贯穿教学全过程
• 设计多元化评价方式，包括过程性评价（课堂观察、学习档案、表现性任务）和终结性评价（纸笔测试、项目成果、答辩展示）相结合
• 建立课程质量监控与持续改进机制，定期收集师生反馈、进行效果评估、修订完善课程，形成PDCA循环的质量保障体系
产出：课程评价体系与持续改进方案（含评价指标体系、评价工具包、质量保障机制）｜ 质量标准：评价体系科学多元，工具具体可操作，改进机制健全有效

Steps

Step 1
Curriculum Problem Diagnosis and Needs Analysis
The core task of this step is to comprehensively diagnose the existing curriculum system, identify existing problems, and analyze improvement needs. Curriculum diagnosis is the starting point of curriculum development; only by accurately identifying problems can targeted improvement plans be designed. Tyler's curriculum development principles (objectives-content-methods-evaluation) provide a classic framework for curriculum analysis.

• Use literature research and comparative research methods to sort out development trends and best practices of similar curricula at home and abroad, establishing a reference system and ideal model for curriculum analysis
• Adopt multiple methods such as questionnaire surveys, interviews, classroom observation, and student work analysis to diagnose strengths and weaknesses of the existing curriculum from four dimensions: curriculum objectives, curriculum content, teaching methods, and evaluation methods
• Apply needs assessment models to analyze the relationship among student development needs, social development needs, and discipline development needs, determining the core direction and priorities of curriculum improvement
Deliverable: Curriculum Diagnosis and Needs Analysis Report (including status analysis, problem diagnosis, needs assessment, improvement direction) | Quality standard: Comprehensive and in-depth diagnosis, accurate problem identification, data-supported needs analysis
Step 2
Curriculum Philosophy Reconstruction and Goal System Design
The core task of this step is to establish new curriculum philosophy and construct a core competency-oriented curriculum goal system. Curriculum philosophy is the soul of curriculum, determining the value orientation and development direction of the curriculum. Core competency-oriented curriculum reform requires shifting from knowledge-based to competency-based, from subject-centered to student-centered.

• Deeply study modern educational concepts such as core competency theory, constructivist learning theory, and deep learning theory, and combine disciplinary characteristics and school reality to establish the core philosophy and value orientation of the curriculum
• Design a four-level curriculum goal system according to the hierarchical structure of "core competencies - curriculum objectives - unit objectives - lesson objectives", ensuring goal consistency and operability
• Apply Bloom's Taxonomy (revised version) and SOLO taxonomy to classify and hierarchize objectives, providing clear basis for teaching implementation and evaluation
Deliverable: Curriculum Philosophy and Goal System Design Plan (including curriculum philosophy elaboration, four-level goal system diagram, goal classification table) | Quality standard: Advanced and distinctive philosophy, complete and hierarchical goal system, specific and measurable expressions
Step 3
Curriculum Content Reconstruction and Unit Teaching Design
The core task of this step is to reconstruct curriculum content according to core competency orientation and design large-unit teaching plans. Traditional curriculum content organization centered on knowledge points is difficult to support core competency development; content reconstruction needs to be centered around big ideas, big tasks, and big projects. Understanding by Design (UbD) provides an effective framework for unit teaching design.

• Apply big idea curriculum design methods to screen and extract core disciplinary concepts, construct a curriculum content structure led by big ideas, and achieve content integration and structuring
• Adopt the three-stage model of Understanding by Design (identify desired results - determine acceptable evidence - plan learning experiences and instruction) for unit teaching design
• Design driving questions and challenging tasks in authentic contexts, integrating knowledge learning into problem-solving and task completion processes, achieving learning by doing, learning by using, and learning by creating
Deliverable: Curriculum Content Reconstruction Plan and Unit Teaching Design Collection (including content structure diagram, 3-5 detailed unit designs, UbD template) | Quality standard: Clear content structure, clear big idea leadership, unit design consistent with UbD philosophy
Step 4
Teaching Model Innovation and Learning Activity Design
The core task of this step is to innovate teaching models and design learning activities that promote deep learning. Core competency-oriented curriculum requires matching teaching method reform, shifting from teacher-centered "teaching" to student-centered "learning", from passive acceptance to active construction. Project-based learning, inquiry-based learning, and problem-based learning are worthy teaching models to learn from.

• Draw on core elements of teaching models such as PBL (Project-Based Learning), IBL (Inquiry-Based Learning), flipped classroom, and blended learning, and construct a school-based teaching model with disciplinary characteristics
• Design learning activities according to the process of "situation introduction - problem driving - inquiry practice -成果展示 - reflective evaluation", ensuring students go through the complete knowledge construction process
• Design diverse learning scaffolds, including cognitive scaffolds, metacognitive scaffolds, social scaffolds, and material scaffolds, to support deep learning for students at different levels
Deliverable: Teaching Model Innovation Plan and Learning Activity Design Library (including teaching model diagram, typical lesson designs, learning scaffold strategy library) | Quality standard: Innovative teaching model, operable activity design, targeted scaffold strategies
Step 5
Curriculum Evaluation System Construction and Continuous Improvement
The core task of this step is to construct a developmental and diversified curriculum evaluation system and establish a continuous curriculum improvement mechanism. Evaluation is not the endpoint of curriculum but an important means to promote continuous curriculum improvement. Core competency-oriented evaluation requires shifting from single paper-pencil tests to diversified evaluation, from focusing on results to focusing on process.

• Construct a "teaching-learning-assessment" integrated evaluation system, achieving alignment among curriculum objectives, teaching activities, and assessment tasks, ensuring assessment runs through the entire teaching process
• Design diversified evaluation methods, combining process-oriented assessment (classroom observation, learning portfolios, performance tasks) with summative assessment (paper-pencil tests, project成果, defense presentations)
• Establish a curriculum quality monitoring and continuous improvement mechanism, regularly collecting teacher and student feedback, conducting effect evaluations, revising and improving the curriculum, forming a PDCA cycle quality assurance system
Deliverable: Curriculum Evaluation System and Continuous Improvement Plan (including evaluation indicator system, evaluation toolkit, quality assurance mechanism) | Quality standard: Scientific and diversified evaluation system, specific and operable tools, sound and effective improvement mechanism
