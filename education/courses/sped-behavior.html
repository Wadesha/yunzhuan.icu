行为矫正原理

Principles of Behavior Modification

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：特殊教育导论、普通心理学 | 学期：秋季

本课程系统介绍应用行为分析（ABA）的基本原理与行为矫正技术。课程内容包括行为的实验分析基础、经典条件作用与操作条件作用理论、强化与惩罚原理、行为的功能评估方法、以及各类行为改变技术（正强化、负强化、消退、塑造、渐隐、代币制、反应代价等）。学生将学习如何运用ABC行为分析模式识别问题行为的功能，设计和实施行为干预方案，并对干预效果进行评估。课程强调伦理原则和积极行为支持的理念。

This course systematically introduces the principles of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) and behavior modification techniques. Topics include the experimental analysis of behavior, classical and operant conditioning, reinforcement and punishment principles, functional behavioral assessment, and behavior change techniques (positive reinforcement, negative reinforcement, extinction, shaping, fading, token economy, response cost, etc.). Students learn to use the ABC model for functional analysis, design and implement behavior intervention plans, and evaluate intervention effectiveness, with emphasis on ethical principles and positive behavior support.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：问题行为功能评估与干预方案

针对一名儿童的问题行为案例，运用ABC模式进行功能评估，设计基于积极行为支持的干预方案，并设定效果评估指标。

For a child's problem behavior case, conduct functional assessment using the ABC model, design an intervention plan based on positive behavior support, and set outcome evaluation indicators.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
行为问题评估与功能分析
本步骤的核心任务是系统评估特殊儿童的行为问题，进行功能性行为分析（FBA）。行为管理的前提是理解行为的功能和原因，而不是简单地"管"行为。行为功能分析是积极行为支持（PBS）的核心技术，强调每一个行为都有其功能，干预必须基于行为的功能而不是形式。

• 运用ABC行为记录法（前因-行为-后果）系统收集行为数据，确定问题行为的发生率、强度、持续时间和发生情境，建立行为基线
• 进行功能性行为分析（FBA），通过直接观察、间接评估（访谈/问卷）和功能分析实验，确定问题行为的四大功能：正强化、负强化、感觉调整、获得实物
• 运用功能分析判断矩阵，综合多来源数据，做出关于行为功能的假设，并验证假设的正确性，为行为干预方案设计提供依据
产出：功能性行为评估报告（含ABC记录、行为基线数据、功能分析结论）｜ 质量标准：数据收集系统全面，功能分析准确，假设可验证
步骤 2
积极行为支持方案设计
本步骤的核心任务是基于行为功能分析结果，设计积极的、整体的行为支持方案。积极行为支持（PBS）强调用积极的、建设性的方式替代问题行为，而不是简单惩罚。三级预防模式（全员普遍预防、 targeted群体干预、个别化密集干预）为行为支持提供了系统框架。

• 基于行为功能设计功能等效的替代行为，即教给儿童一种具有相同功能但更恰当的行为方式，从根源上解决问题行为
• 设计前因调控策略，通过调整环境、调整任务、预告预告、提供选择等方式，预防问题行为的发生，体现"预防胜于干预"的理念
• 制定后果处理策略，包括对适当行为的强化策略（差别强化、代币系统、行为契约）和对问题行为的应对策略（消退、区别性惩罚），确保策略的一致性和公正性
产出：积极行为支持方案（含功能等效行为、前因调控、后果策略）｜ 质量标准：基于功能分析，策略积极正向，方案系统完整
步骤 3
行为干预实施与数据监测
本步骤的核心任务是实施行为干预方案，并系统监测行为变化数据。行为干预的效果需要用数据说话，而不是凭感觉判断。持续的数据监测是确保干预有效的关键，也是及时调整方案的依据。

• 培训所有相关人员（教师、家长、康复师）统一实施干预方案，确保干预的一致性和忠实度，制作干预实施手册明确操作步骤
• 建立数据收集系统，选择合适的数据记录方法（频率记录、持续时间记录、时间取样、事件取样），定期收集行为数据并绘制图表
• 运用单被试研究设计（如AB设计、多基线设计、交替处理设计），通过可视化分析和统计分析判断干预效果，建立数据驱动的决策机制
产出：行为干预实施与监测记录（含实施手册、数据图表、效果分析）｜ 质量标准：实施规范一致，数据系统连续，效果可量化
步骤 4
泛化与维持策略设计
本步骤的核心任务是设计行为的泛化和维持策略，确保干预效果能够迁移到不同情境并长期保持。很多行为干预的失败不是因为干预没有效果，而是因为效果不能泛化和维持。泛化训练是行为干预不可或缺的重要环节。

• 设计泛化训练策略，包括刺激泛化（不同环境、不同人物、不同材料）和反应泛化（不同行为表现），采用多范例训练、宽松强化、自然强化等方法促进泛化
• 制定维持策略，逐步撤去人工强化，转向自然强化，培养行为的内在动机，建立自我管理和自我监控能力，使行为能够长期保持
• 培训家长和其他环境中的成人在自然情境中维持干预效果，实现家校一致、多场景一致，构建支持性行为生态系统
产出：泛化与维持方案（含泛化训练计划、维持策略、环境支持系统）｜ 质量标准：泛化策略有针对性，维持方案可持续，生态支持系统完善
步骤 5
危机行为应对与方案优化
本步骤的核心任务是制定危机行为应急预案，并根据干预效果持续优化行为支持方案。严重的问题行为（如自伤、攻击、破坏）可能造成安全风险，需要有预案。同时，行为干预是一个动态的过程，需要根据儿童的变化不断调整优化。

• 制定危机行为应急预案，识别危机行为的预警信号，明确应对步骤、人员分工、安全保障措施和事后处理程序，确保人身安全
• 定期（如每2-4周）召开行为支持团队会议， review行为数据，评估干预效果，分析存在的问题，讨论方案调整
• 建立持续改进机制，根据儿童的进步和新出现的挑战，动态调整行为支持方案，形成"评估-干预-监测-调整"的良性循环
产出：危机预案与方案优化报告（含应急预案、阶段评估、调整方案）｜ 质量标准：危机预案实用有效，方案调整有据可循，机制持续运行

Steps

Step 1
Behavior Problem Assessment and Functional Analysis
The core task of this step is to systematically assess behavior problems of children with special needs and conduct Functional Behavior Assessment (FBA). The premise of behavior management is to understand the function and cause of behavior, not simply to "control" behavior. Functional behavior analysis is the core technology of Positive Behavior Support (PBS), emphasizing that every behavior has its function and intervention must be based on the function rather than the form of behavior.

• Use ABC behavior recording method (Antecedent-Behavior-Consequence) to systematically collect behavior data, determine the frequency, intensity, duration, and occurrence context of problem behaviors, and establish behavior baseline
• Conduct Functional Behavior Assessment (FBA) to determine the four major functions of problem behaviors through direct observation, indirect assessment (interviews/questionnaires), and functional analysis experiments: positive reinforcement, negative reinforcement, sensory adjustment, and access to tangibles
• Use functional analysis decision matrix to synthesize multi-source data, make hypotheses about behavior function, and verify the correctness of hypotheses, providing basis for behavior intervention plan design
Deliverable: Functional Behavior Assessment Report (including ABC records, behavior baseline data, functional analysis conclusions) | Quality standard: Systematic and comprehensive data collection, accurate functional analysis, verifiable hypotheses
Step 2
Positive Behavior Support Plan Design
The core task of this step is to design positive and comprehensive behavior support plans based on functional behavior analysis results. Positive Behavior Support (PBS) emphasizes replacing problem behaviors with positive and constructive ways rather than simple punishment. The three-tier prevention model (universal prevention for all, targeted group intervention, individualized intensive intervention) provides a systematic framework for behavior support.

• Design functionally equivalent replacement behaviors based on behavior function, i.e., teach children a more appropriate behavior方式 with the same function, fundamentally solving problem behaviors
• Design antecedent control strategies, such as adjusting environment, adjusting tasks, advance notice, providing choices, etc., to prevent problem behaviors from occurring, embodying the philosophy of "prevention is better than intervention"
• Develop consequence management strategies, including reinforcement strategies for appropriate behaviors (differential reinforcement, token economy, behavior contracts) and response strategies for problem behaviors (extinction, differential punishment), ensuring consistency and fairness of strategies
Deliverable: Positive Behavior Support Plan (including functionally equivalent behaviors, antecedent control, consequence strategies) | Quality standard: Based on functional analysis, positive and proactive strategies, systematic and complete plan
Step 3
Behavior Intervention Implementation and Data Monitoring
The core task of this step is to implement the behavior intervention plan and systematically monitor behavior change data. The effectiveness of behavior intervention needs to be supported by data rather than subjective judgment. Continuous data monitoring is the key to ensuring intervention effectiveness and the basis for timely plan adjustment.

• Train all relevant personnel (teachers, parents, therapists) to consistently implement the intervention plan, ensuring intervention consistency and fidelity, and produce an intervention implementation manual clarifying operational steps
• Establish a data collection system, select appropriate data recording methods (frequency recording, duration recording, time sampling, event sampling), regularly collect behavior data and create graphs
• Use single-subject research designs (such as AB design, multiple baseline design, alternating treatments design) to judge intervention effectiveness through visual analysis and statistical analysis, establishing a data-driven decision-making mechanism
Deliverable: Behavior Intervention Implementation and Monitoring Records (including implementation manual, data charts, effectiveness analysis) | Quality standard: Standardized and consistent implementation, systematic and continuous data, quantifiable effects
Step 4
Generalization and Maintenance Strategy Design
The core task of this step is to design behavior generalization and maintenance strategies to ensure intervention effects can transfer to different contexts and be maintained long-term. Many behavior interventions fail not because they are ineffective but because effects cannot generalize and maintain. Generalization training is an indispensable and important part of behavior intervention.

• Design generalization training strategies, including stimulus generalization (different environments, different people, different materials) and response generalization (different behavior manifestations), using multiple exemplar training, loose training, natural reinforcement methods to promote generalization
• Develop maintenance strategies, gradually fading artificial reinforcement, shifting to natural reinforcement, cultivating intrinsic motivation for behavior, establishing self-management and self-monitoring abilities, enabling behaviors to be maintained long-term
• Train parents and adults in other environments to maintain intervention effects in natural contexts, achieving home-school consistency and multi-scenario consistency, building a supportive behavior ecosystem
Deliverable: Generalization and Maintenance Plan (including generalization training plan, maintenance strategies, environmental support system) | Quality standard: Targeted generalization strategies, sustainable maintenance plan, complete ecological support system
Step 5
Crisis Behavior Response and Plan Optimization
The core task of this step is to develop crisis behavior emergency plans and continuously optimize behavior support plans based on intervention effects. Severe problem behaviors (such as self-injury, aggression, destruction) may pose safety risks and require contingency plans. Meanwhile, behavior intervention is a dynamic process that requires continuous adjustment and optimization based on children's changes.

• Develop crisis behavior emergency plans, identify warning signs of crisis behaviors, clarify response steps, personnel division of labor, safety assurance measures, and post-incident handling procedures, ensuring personal safety
• Hold behavior support team meetings regularly (e.g., every 2-4 weeks) to review behavior data, assess intervention effectiveness, analyze existing problems, and discuss plan adjustments
• Establish a continuous improvement mechanism to dynamically adjust behavior support plans based on children's progress and emerging challenges, forming a virtuous cycle of "assessment-intervention-monitoring-adjustment"
Deliverable: Crisis Plan and Plan Optimization Report (including emergency plan, periodic assessment, adjustment plan) | Quality standard: Practical and effective crisis plan, evidence-based plan adjustments, continuously operating mechanism
