幼儿卫生与保健

Child Health and Nutrition

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：2 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学年春季

本课程讲授幼儿生长发育规律与卫生保健知识，内容涵盖幼儿生理特点与生长发育规律、营养需求与膳食搭配、日常卫生与生活护理、常见疾病预防与处理、意外事故急救与安全教育、心理卫生与健康等。学生将学习幼儿园一日生活中的卫生保健工作规范，掌握晨检、消毒、膳食管理、健康观察等基本技能，树立"健康第一"的教育理念，为在幼儿园开展卫生保健工作奠定专业基础。

This course covers child growth patterns and health care knowledge, including physiological characteristics, nutritional needs and dietary planning, daily hygiene and care, common disease prevention and management, first aid for accidents and safety education, and mental health. Students will learn kindergarten daily health care protocols, mastering morning checkups, disinfection, dietary management, and health observation skills, establishing a "health first" educational philosophy.
