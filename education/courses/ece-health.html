幼儿卫生与保健

Child Health and Nutrition

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：2 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学年春季

本课程讲授幼儿生长发育规律与卫生保健知识，内容涵盖幼儿生理特点与生长发育规律、营养需求与膳食搭配、日常卫生与生活护理、常见疾病预防与处理、意外事故急救与安全教育、心理卫生与健康等。学生将学习幼儿园一日生活中的卫生保健工作规范，掌握晨检、消毒、膳食管理、健康观察等基本技能，树立"健康第一"的教育理念，为在幼儿园开展卫生保健工作奠定专业基础。

This course covers child growth patterns and health care knowledge, including physiological characteristics, nutritional needs and dietary planning, daily hygiene and care, common disease prevention and management, first aid for accidents and safety education, and mental health. Students will learn kindergarten daily health care protocols, mastering morning checkups, disinfection, dietary management, and health observation skills, establishing a "health first" educational philosophy.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：幼儿园一周营养膳食方案设计

为一所幼儿园中班（4-5岁）设计一套完整的一周营养膳食方案。方案需依据《中国居民膳食指南》中幼儿膳食要求，结合幼儿营养需求和饮食特点，制定每日三餐两点的食谱。要求做到食物多样、营养均衡、粗细搭配、色香味形俱全，并考虑食物过敏和特殊饮食需求。方案需包含每日食谱、营养成分分析表、采购清单、成本核算以及膳食管理制度说明，总字数不少于3000字。

Design a complete one-week nutritional meal plan for a kindergarten middle class (ages 4-5). The plan must follow the Chinese Dietary Guidelines for young children, considering nutritional needs and dietary preferences, with three meals and two snacks daily. Requirements include food variety, balanced nutrition, whole grain integration, and attention to color, aroma, taste, and appearance, while addressing food allergies and special dietary needs. Minimum 3000 words with daily menus, nutritional analysis, shopping lists, cost calculation, and dietary management guidelines.
