幼儿教育学

Early Childhood Education

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学年春季

本课程是幼儿教育专业的入门核心课，系统介绍幼儿教育的基本概念、历史发展、理论基础和实践原则。课程内容涵盖幼儿教育的性质与意义、国内外幼儿教育制度与政策、幼儿园的任务与目标、幼儿教师的专业素养与角色、游戏在幼儿教育中的地位与作用、幼儿园与家庭社区的合作等。通过本课程的学习，学生将建立对幼儿教育事业的整体认识，树立科学的儿童观和教育观，为专业学习奠定思想基础。

This introductory core course systematically presents basic concepts, historical development, theoretical foundations, and practical principles of early childhood education. Topics include the nature and significance of early childhood education, domestic and international educational systems and policies, kindergarten missions and goals, teacher professionalism and roles, the role of play in education, and kindergarten-family-community partnerships. Students will develop a holistic understanding of early childhood education and establish scientific views of children and education.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：幼儿教育理念对比研究论文

选取两位中外著名幼儿教育家（如陈鹤琴与蒙台梭利、陶行知与杜威、瑞吉欧与高瞻等）的教育思想进行对比研究，分析其教育理念的异同点、历史背景、实践应用及对当代幼儿教育的启示。论文需包含引言、两位教育家的主要思想概述、对比分析、对我国幼儿教育实践的借鉴意义、结论等部分。字数不少于3500字，要求引用至少10篇相关文献，格式规范。

Conduct a comparative study of two influential early childhood educators (e.g., Chen Heqin vs. Montessori, Tao Xingzhi vs. Dewey, Reggio Emilia vs. HighScope). Analyze similarities and differences in their educational philosophies, historical contexts, practical applications, and implications for contemporary early childhood education. The paper must include introduction, overview of both educators' ideas, comparative analysis, practical implications for Chinese early childhood education, and conclusion. Minimum 3500 words with at least 10 cited references.
