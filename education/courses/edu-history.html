教育史

History of Education

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：教育学导论 | 学期：春季

本课程系统梳理中外教育发展的历史脉络与演变规律。内容包括：中国教育史（从先秦诸子到近现代教育改革）；西方教育史（古希腊罗马教育、中世纪教育、文艺复兴与宗教改革、近代教育制度建立、现代教育发展）；重要教育思想家及其理论；教育制度、课程、教学方法的历史变迁；教育与社会、政治、经济、文化的互动关系。

This course provides a systematic historical overview of educational development in China and the West. Topics include Chinese educational history (from ancient philosophers to modern reforms), Western educational history (Greco-Roman education, medieval education, Renaissance and Reformation, modern educational systems, contemporary developments), key educational thinkers, and the evolution of educational institutions and methods.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：教育思想家专题研究

选择一位中外教育思想家（如孔子、苏格拉底、杜威等），研究其教育思想产生的时代背景、核心观点及对当代教育的启示，撰写研究论文。

Select an educational thinker (e.g., Confucius, Socrates, Dewey), research the historical context, core ideas, and contemporary implications of their educational thought, and write a research paper.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：梳理教育思想演变——看看历史上的人们怎么思考教育问题。
步骤 1
研究主题选定与史料搜集
本步骤的核心任务是选定教育史研究主题，系统搜集相关史料和文献资料。教育史研究的第一步是确定研究问题，好的问题既要具有学术价值，又要具有现实意义。史料是教育史研究的基础，史料的丰富性和可靠性直接决定研究的质量。

• 运用问题聚焦法，从教育制度史、教育思想史、教育人物史、教育实践史等领域中筛选研究主题，评估其研究价值、可行性和创新性
• 系统搜集史料，包括官方档案、地方志、教育法令、学校章程、教育家文集、报刊杂志、口述史料等多种类型，注重一手史料的挖掘和利用
• 运用史料考证方法，对搜集到的史料进行真伪鉴别、版本校勘和源流考证，建立史料分类目录和资料库，为后续研究奠定基础
产出：研究选题与史料准备报告（含选题论证、史料清单、史料考证笔记）｜ 质量标准：选题有学术价值，史料丰富可靠，考证严谨规范
步骤 2
史学理论选择与研究框架构建
本步骤的核心任务是选择适切的史学理论视角，构建清晰的研究框架。教育史研究不是简单的史料堆砌，而是需要理论观照和问题意识。不同的史学理论（如年鉴学派、新文化史、教育史学社会史转向等）提供了不同的研究视角和分析工具。

• 深入学习和比较多种史学理论流派，包括传统史学、年鉴学派、新文化史、微观史、全球史等，理解其理论主张、研究方法和适用范围
• 结合研究主题特点，选择1-2种核心理论视角作为分析框架，明确理论运用的具体方式和切入点，避免理论与史实"两张皮"
• 构建研究的整体框架，按照"问题提出-背景分析-发展脉络-特征总结-影响评价"的逻辑结构，设计研究的章节安排和论证思路
产出：研究设计方案（含理论视角说明、研究框架图、章节目录）｜ 质量标准：理论选择适切，框架逻辑清晰，章节安排合理
步骤 3
史料深度解读与历史叙事
本步骤的核心任务是对史料进行深度解读，撰写既有史实依据又有理论深度的历史叙事。教育史研究的核心是"论从史出、史论结合"，既要尊重历史事实，又要进行合理的历史解释。陈寅恪先生提出的"同情之理解"是对待历史应有的态度。

• 运用文本分析法、语境分析法对核心史料进行深度解读，将史料置于具体的历史语境中考察，理解其产生的背景、意图和影响
• 按照时间线索和逻辑关系，将分散的史料串联成完整的历史叙事，注意历史事件的因果关系、发展脉络和内在逻辑
• 采用"叙事+分析"的写作方式，在史实叙述的基础上进行理论分析和历史评价，做到有史有论、史论结合、夹叙夹议
产出：研究初稿（含完整的历史叙事、史料引证、初步分析）｜ 质量标准：史实准确，叙事流畅，史论结合，引证规范
步骤 4
多维度比较分析与历史解释
本步骤的核心任务是从多个维度进行比较分析，提出有深度的历史解释。比较研究是教育史研究的重要方法，通过横向比较（不同地区、不同国家）和纵向比较（不同时期），可以更清晰地揭示历史发展的特点和规律。历史解释需要建立在充分的史实基础上，体现历史学家的洞察力。

• 运用比较史学方法，进行纵向的历史分期比较和横向的中外/区域比较，分析异同点及其背后的原因，揭示教育发展的普遍规律和特殊道路
• 从政治、经济、文化、社会等多个维度分析教育发展的影响因素，运用"动因-过程-结果"的分析框架，解释教育变革的内在机制
• 提出核心论点和历史解释，运用充分的史料证据支撑论点，做到观点鲜明、论据充分、论证严密、结论可靠
产出：研究分析篇（含比较分析表、多维因素分析、核心论点论证）｜ 质量标准：比较视角新颖，分析有深度，论点明确，论据充分
步骤 5
研究结论凝练与史学反思
本步骤的核心任务是凝练研究结论，进行史学方法论反思，并探讨历史研究的当代价值。教育史研究的最终目的不仅是还原历史真相，更是以史为鉴，为当代教育改革提供历史智慧。史学反思是提升研究质量和研究者学术自觉的重要途径。

• 系统总结研究的主要发现和核心结论，回应对研究问题，说明研究的创新点、贡献和局限性，指明未来研究的方向
• 进行史学方法论反思，讨论史料运用、理论视角、研究方法的得失，总结经验教训，体现史学研究的批判性和自觉性
• 探讨研究的当代启示，从历史经验中提炼对当代教育改革有借鉴意义的智慧和启示，实现"以史为鉴、古为今用"的研究价值
产出：最终研究报告/论文（含结论、反思、当代启示、参考文献）｜ 质量标准：结论严谨有新意，反思深刻，当代启示有现实意义

Steps

Step 1
Research Topic Selection and Historical Data Collection
The core task of this step is to select an educational history research topic and systematically collect relevant historical materials and literature. The first step in educational history research is to determine the research question; a good question should have both academic value and practical significance. Historical materials are the foundation of educational history research, and their richness and reliability directly determine research quality.

• Use problem-focusing method to screen research topics from fields such as educational institutional history, educational thought history, educational figures history, and educational practice history, evaluating their research value, feasibility, and innovation
• Systematically collect historical materials, including official archives, local chronicles, educational decrees, school charters, educators' collected works, newspapers and magazines, and oral history materials, focusing on excavation and utilization of primary sources
• Apply historical material textual criticism methods to authenticate collected materials, collate editions, and verify origins, establishing a classified catalog and database of historical materials to lay the foundation for subsequent research
Deliverable: Research Topic Selection and Historical Materials Preparation Report (including topic justification, materials list, textual criticism notes) | Quality standard: Academically valuable topic, rich and reliable materials, rigorous and standardized criticism
Step 2
Historical Theory Selection and Research Framework Construction
The core task of this step is to select appropriate historiographical theoretical perspectives and construct a clear research framework. Educational history research is not simple accumulation of historical materials but requires theoretical insight and problem consciousness. Different historiographical theories (such as the Annales School, new cultural history, social history turn in educational historiography) provide different research perspectives and analytical tools.

• Deeply study and compare various historiographical theoretical schools, including traditional historiography, Annales School, new cultural history, microhistory, and global history, understanding their theoretical propositions, research methods, and applicable scopes
• Based on research topic characteristics, select 1-2 core theoretical perspectives as analytical framework, clarifying specific ways and entry points of theory application, avoiding the "two skins" problem of theory and historical facts
• Construct the overall research framework, designing chapter arrangement and argumentation思路 according to the logical structure of "problem posing - background analysis - development trajectory - feature summary - impact evaluation"
Deliverable: Research Design Plan (including theoretical perspective explanation, research framework diagram, chapter outline) | Quality standard: Appropriate theory selection, clear logical framework, reasonable chapter arrangement
Step 3
In-depth Historical Material Interpretation and Historical Narrative
The core task of this step is to conduct in-depth interpretation of historical materials and write historical narratives with both factual basis and theoretical depth. The core of educational history research is "arguments emerge from history, history and theory combine",既要尊重历史事实，又要进行合理的历史解释。The "sympathetic understanding" proposed by Chen Yinque is the proper attitude toward history.

• Apply text analysis and contextual analysis methods for in-depth interpretation of core historical materials, examining materials in specific historical contexts, understanding their background, intentions, and influences
• According to chronological clues and logical relationships, string together scattered historical materials into a complete historical narrative, paying attention to causal relationships, development threads, and internal logic of historical events
• Adopt the writing style of "narrative + analysis", conducting theoretical analysis and historical evaluation on the basis of factual narration, achieving both history and argumentation, combining history with theory, and interleaving narrative with discussion
Deliverable: Research Draft (including complete historical narrative, historical citations, preliminary analysis) | Quality standard: Accurate historical facts, fluent narrative, history-theory integration, standardized citations
Step 4
Multi-dimensional Comparative Analysis and Historical Interpretation
The core task of this step is to conduct comparative analysis from multiple dimensions and propose in-depth historical interpretations. Comparative research is an important method in educational history research; through horizontal comparison (different regions, different countries) and vertical comparison (different periods), the characteristics and laws of historical development can be revealed more clearly. Historical interpretation needs to be based on sufficient historical facts and reflect the historian's insight.

• Apply comparative historiography methods to conduct vertical historical period comparison and horizontal Sino-foreign/regional comparison, analyze similarities and differences and their underlying causes, revealing universal laws and special paths of educational development
• Analyze influencing factors of educational development from multiple dimensions such as politics, economy, culture, and society, using the "cause-process-result" analytical framework to explain the internal mechanism of educational变革
• Propose core arguments and historical interpretations, supporting arguments with sufficient historical evidence, achieving clear viewpoints, sufficient论据, rigorous论证, and reliable conclusions
Deliverable: Research Analysis Section (including comparative analysis tables, multi-dimensional factor analysis, core argument demonstration) | Quality standard: Innovative comparative perspective, in-depth analysis, clear arguments, sufficient evidence
Step 5
Research Conclusion Consolidation and Historiographical Reflection
The core task of this step is to consolidate research conclusions, conduct historiographical methodological reflection, and explore the contemporary value of historical research. The ultimate purpose of educational history research is not only to restore historical truth but also to take history as a mirror, providing historical wisdom for contemporary educational reform. Historiographical reflection is an important way to improve research quality and researchers' academic self-awareness.

• Systematically summarize main findings and core conclusions of the research, respond to research questions, explain innovations, contributions, and limitations of the study, and point out future research directions
• Conduct historiographical methodological reflection, discussing gains and losses in material utilization, theoretical perspectives, and research methods, summarizing experiences and lessons, embodying the criticality and self-awareness of historical research
• Explore contemporary implications of the research, extracting wisdom and insights from historical experience that are referential for contemporary educational reform, realizing the research value of "taking history as a mirror, making the past serve the present"
Deliverable: Final Research Report/Paper (including conclusions, reflections, contemporary implications, references) | Quality standard: Rigorous and innovative conclusions, deep reflection, practically significant contemporary implications
