特殊儿童心理学

Psychology of Exceptional Children

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：特殊教育导论、普通心理学 | 学期：春季

本课程从发展心理学角度探讨各类特殊儿童的心理发展规律与特点。课程内容包括特殊儿童的认知发展、语言发展、情绪与社会性发展、人格发展等方面。重点分析智力障碍、学习障碍、自闭症谱系障碍、注意缺陷多动障碍等儿童的心理特征，以及影响其发展的生物、心理和社会因素。学生将学习如何观察和评估特殊儿童的心理发展水平，为制定教育干预方案提供心理学依据。

This course examines the psychological development of exceptional children from a developmental psychology perspective. Topics include cognitive development, language acquisition, emotional and social development, and personality development in children with various disabilities. The course focuses on psychological characteristics of children with intellectual disabilities, learning disabilities, autism spectrum disorder, and ADHD, as well as biological, psychological, and social factors influencing their development. Students learn to observe and assess psychological development to inform educational interventions.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：特殊儿童心理发展案例分析

选择一名特殊需要儿童（如自闭症、ADHD、学习障碍等）案例，分析其认知、语言、社会性发展特征，并提出心理支持与干预建议。

Select a case of a child with special needs (e.g., autism, ADHD, learning disabilities), analyze their cognitive, language, and social development characteristics, and propose psychological support and intervention recommendations.
