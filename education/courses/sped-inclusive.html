全纳教育

Inclusive Education

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：特殊教育导论 | 学期：春季

本课程探讨全纳教育的理论基础、国际发展趋势与实践策略。课程内容涵盖全纳教育的概念与价值观、从隔离到融合的历史演变、全纳教育的法律与政策支持、普通学校的全纳教育实践模式、合作教学与团队协作、通用学习设计（UDL）、差异化教学、同伴支持策略、以及学校文化与氛围的营造。学生将了解不同国家和地区的全纳教育经验，分析全纳教育实施中的挑战与对策，树立尊重差异、全员参与的教育理念。

This course explores the theoretical foundations, international development, and practical strategies of inclusive education. Topics include concepts and values of inclusion, historical evolution from segregation to integration, legal and policy support, inclusive school practice models, co-teaching and team collaboration, Universal Design for Learning (UDL), differentiated instruction, peer support strategies, and school culture development. Students examine international experiences, analyze implementation challenges, and develop values centered on respect for diversity and full participation.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：全纳教育学校改进方案设计

学生需选择一所普通学校（真实或模拟），设计一份全纳教育推进方案。方案内容包括：学校现状分析（学生多样性、现有支持资源、教师专业能力等）、全纳教育的愿景与目标、具体改进措施（课程调整策略、教学方法改进、支持体系建设、教师专业发展计划、家校合作机制、校园文化建设等）、资源需求与时间规划、以及成效评估指标。要求结合全纳教育理论，提出切实可行的改进路径，体现对全体学生教育需求的关注。

Students will select a regular school (real or simulated) and design a school improvement plan for inclusive education. The plan should include current state analysis (student diversity, existing support resources, teacher capacity), vision and goals, specific improvement measures (curriculum adaptation, instructional strategies, support systems, professional development, home-school collaboration, school culture), resource requirements and timeline, and evaluation indicators. Evidence-based strategies and practical implementation pathways that address all students' needs are required.
