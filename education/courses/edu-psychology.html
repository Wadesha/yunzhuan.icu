教育心理学

Educational Psychology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：教育学导论 | 学期：春季

本课程运用心理学理论与方法研究教育过程中的心理现象及其规律。主要内容包括认知发展理论、学习理论（行为主义、认知主义、建构主义）、学习动机、智力与创造力、人格发展、课堂管理心理、学习评估等。课程注重理论联系实际，通过案例分析和小组讨论，培养学生运用教育心理学原理分析和解决教育实际问题的能力。

This course applies psychological theories and methods to study mental phenomena in educational contexts. Topics include cognitive development, learning theories (behaviorism, cognitivism, constructivism), motivation, intelligence and creativity, personality development, classroom management psychology, and learning assessment. Emphasis is placed on connecting theory with practice through case analysis and group discussion.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：教育心理学案例分析报告

选择一个真实学习问题案例（如学习动机不足、课堂管理困难等），运用学习理论、动机理论等分析成因并提出干预方案，撰写分析报告。

Select a real learning problem case (e.g., low motivation, classroom management difficulties), analyze causes using learning and motivation theories, propose an intervention plan, and write an analysis report.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：分析一个真实的学习困难案例，比如一个学生总是数学考不好。你需要用学习理论分析原因（是知识基础薄弱、还是学习策略不当、还是动机问题），设计针对性的辅导方案并跟踪效果。
步骤 1
研究问题聚焦与变量界定
本步骤的核心任务是聚焦教育心理学研究问题，明确研究变量并进行操作化定义。教育心理学研究的是教育过程中的心理现象和规律，好的研究问题应当具体明确，具有理论和实践价值。变量的操作化定义是实证研究的关键环节，直接关系到研究的可重复性和结论的可靠性。

• 从教育实践中的真实问题出发，结合教育心理学理论，提炼出具有研究价值的科学问题，区分研究问题与实践问题，确保问题的可研究性
• 明确研究的自变量、因变量和控制变量，对每个核心概念进行操作化定义，说明变量的测量方法和指标体系
• 提出研究假设，包括备择假设和虚无假设，说明假设的理论依据和逻辑推理过程，确保假设具有可检验性
产出：研究设计方案（含问题提出、变量界定、研究假设）｜ 质量标准：问题具体明确，变量定义清晰，假设可检验
步骤 2
研究设计与工具选择开发
本步骤的核心任务是选择合适的研究设计类型，开发或选择标准化的研究工具。教育心理学研究方法丰富多样，包括实验法、准实验法、相关研究法、调查法、个案研究法等，每种方法都有其适用条件和局限性。研究工具的质量直接决定研究数据的可靠性。

• 根据研究问题的性质和研究条件，选择合适的研究设计类型（真实验/准实验/相关/调查/个案），说明设计的理由和控制措施
• 选择或开发研究工具，包括量表、问卷、测验、观察记录表、访谈提纲等，说明工具的信度（α系数、重测信度）和效度（内容效度、结构效度、效标效度）指标
• 设计研究程序，包括被试选取（抽样方法、样本量确定）、实验处理、数据收集流程等，制定详细的研究执行手册
产出：研究方法与工具设计（含研究设计图、研究工具、研究程序）｜ 质量标准：方法选择恰当，工具可靠有效，程序严谨规范
步骤 3
数据收集与质量控制
本步骤的核心任务是按照研究设计系统收集数据，并实施严格的质量控制。数据是研究的"原材料"，数据质量直接决定研究结论的可靠性。教育心理学研究的数据来源多样（自我报告、行为观察、生理指标、学业成绩等），需要采用不同的质量控制方法。

• 严格按照研究程序收集数据，对主试进行培训，统一指导语和施测流程，确保数据收集的标准化和一致性
• 实施质量控制措施，包括设置操作检验、社会期望控制、重复测查、随机抽查等，及时识别和处理无效数据和异常值
• 建立数据管理规范，对原始数据进行编号、备份、录入和核查，采用双人录入法或逻辑校验法确保数据录入的准确性
产出：研究数据集与质量控制报告（含原始数据、数据清洗记录、质量分析）｜ 质量标准：数据收集规范，质量控制严格，数据真实可靠
步骤 4
数据分析与结果解释
本步骤的核心任务是运用恰当的统计方法分析数据，并对研究结果进行心理学意义上的解释。数据分析不是数字游戏，而是回答研究问题的科学过程。教育心理学常用的统计方法包括描述统计、t检验、方差分析、相关分析、回归分析、结构方程模型等。

• 根据研究设计和数据类型，选择恰当的统计分析方法，使用SPSS、R或Mplus等统计软件进行数据分析，报告统计量和显著性水平
• 对统计结果进行心理学意义的解释，说明结果是否支持研究假设，探讨结果背后的心理机制和教育意义，避免"统计显著=实际显著"的误区
• 进行深入的讨论分析，包括与已有研究的对比、结果的理论贡献、研究的局限性、对教育实践的启示等
产出：数据分析与结果报告（含统计分析表、结果解释、讨论分析）｜ 质量标准：统计方法正确，结果解释深入，讨论有深度
步骤 5
研究报告撰写与实践转化
本步骤的核心任务是撰写规范的教育心理学研究报告，并探讨研究成果向教育实践转化的路径。研究报告是研究成果的集中体现，需要遵循学术规范，清晰、准确、完整地呈现研究过程和结果。教育心理学研究的最终价值在于改进教育实践。

• 按照APA格式或心理学研究报告规范撰写研究报告，包括摘要、引言、方法、结果、讨论、结论、参考文献等部分，确保结构完整、逻辑清晰
• 提炼研究的核心发现和创新点，说明研究的理论贡献和实践价值，客观指出研究的局限性和未来研究方向
• 设计研究成果的实践转化方案，将研究结论转化为具体的教育原则、教学策略、干预方案或政策建议，实现研究的实践价值
产出：教育心理学研究论文/报告（含完整研究报告、实践转化方案）｜ 质量标准：报告规范完整，结论可靠，实践转化有针对性

Steps

Step 1
Research Question Focusing and Variable Definition
The core task of this step is to focus educational psychology research questions, clarify research variables, and provide operational definitions. Educational psychology studies psychological phenomena and laws in the educational process; good research questions should be specific and clear, with theoretical and practical value. Operational definition of variables is a key link in empirical research, directly related to research reproducibility and conclusion reliability.

• Starting from real problems in educational practice, combined with educational psychology theories, extract scientific questions with research value, distinguish research questions from practical problems, and ensure the researchability of questions
• Clarify independent variables, dependent variables, and control variables of the study, provide operational definitions for each core concept, and explain measurement methods and indicator systems of variables
• Propose research hypotheses, including alternative and null hypotheses, explain theoretical basis and logical reasoning process of hypotheses, and ensure hypotheses are testable
Deliverable: Research Design Plan (including problem posing, variable definition, research hypotheses) | Quality standard: Specific and clear questions, clear variable definitions, testable hypotheses
Step 2
Research Design and Tool Selection/Development
The core task of this step is to select appropriate research design types and develop or select standardized research tools. Educational psychology research has diverse methods, including experimental, quasi-experimental, correlational, survey, and case study methods, each with its applicable conditions and limitations. The quality of research tools directly determines the reliability of research data.

• According to the nature of research questions and research conditions, select appropriate research design types (true experiment/quasi-experiment/correlation/survey/case study), explain design rationale and control measures
• Select or develop research tools, including scales, questionnaires, tests, observation record forms, interview outlines, etc., explain reliability (Cronbach's α, test-retest reliability) and validity (content validity, construct validity, criterion validity) indicators of tools
• Design research procedures, including subject selection (sampling method, sample size determination), experimental treatment, data collection process, etc., and develop a detailed research implementation manual
Deliverable: Research Methods and Tool Design (including research design diagram, research tools, research procedures) | Quality standard: Appropriate method selection, reliable and effective tools, rigorous and standardized procedures
Step 3
Data Collection and Quality Control
The core task of this step is to systematically collect data according to research design and implement strict quality control. Data are the "raw materials" of research; data quality directly determines the reliability of research conclusions. Educational psychology research has diverse data sources (self-report, behavioral observation, physiological indicators, academic achievement, etc.), requiring different quality control methods.

• Strictly collect data according to research procedures, train experimenters, standardize instructions and testing processes, and ensure standardization and consistency of data collection
• Implement quality control measures, including manipulation checks, social desirability control, repeated testing, random spot checks, etc., to timely identify and process invalid data and outliers
• Establish data management specifications, number, backup, enter, and verify原始 data, and use double-entry method or logical verification method to ensure accuracy of data entry
Deliverable: Research Dataset and Quality Control Report (including raw data, data cleaning records, quality analysis) | Quality standard: Standardized data collection, strict quality control, authentic and reliable data
Step 4
Data Analysis and Result Interpretation
The core task of this step is to analyze data using appropriate statistical methods and interpret research results in psychological terms. Data analysis is not number games but a scientific process to answer research questions. Commonly used statistical methods in educational psychology include descriptive statistics, t-test, ANOVA, correlation analysis, regression analysis, structural equation modeling, etc.

• According to research design and data types, select appropriate statistical analysis methods, use statistical software such as SPSS, R, or Mplus for data analysis, and report statistics and significance levels
• Interpret statistical results in psychological terms, explain whether results support research hypotheses, explore psychological mechanisms and educational significance behind results, and avoid the误区 of "statistical significance = practical significance"
• Conduct in-depth discussion and analysis, including comparison with existing studies, theoretical contribution of results, research limitations, and implications for educational practice
Deliverable: Data Analysis and Results Report (including statistical analysis tables, result interpretation, discussion analysis) | Quality standard: Correct statistical methods, in-depth result interpretation, profound discussion
Step 5
Research Report Writing and Practice Translation
The core task of this step is to write a standardized educational psychology research report and explore pathways for translating research成果 into educational practice. The research report is the concentrated manifestation of research achievements, requiring compliance with academic norms and clear, accurate, and complete presentation of research process and results. The ultimate value of educational psychology research lies in improving educational practice.

• Write research report according to APA format or psychology research report norms, including abstract, introduction, method, results, discussion, conclusion, references, etc., ensuring complete structure and clear logic
• Extract core findings and innovations of the research, explain theoretical contribution and practical value, and objectively point out research limitations and future research directions
• Design practice translation plan for research achievements, transforming research conclusions into specific educational principles, teaching strategies, intervention programs, or policy recommendations, realizing practical value of research
Deliverable: Educational Psychology Research Paper/Report (including complete research report, practice translation plan) | Quality standard: Standard and complete report, reliable conclusions, targeted practice translation
