教育研究方法

Educational Research Methods

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：教育心理学、教育统计学（可同时修） | 学期：秋季

本课程系统介绍教育科学研究的基本原理与方法。内容包括：研究问题的提出与假设形成；文献检索与综述；研究设计（定量研究、定性研究、混合研究）；数据收集方法（问卷调查、访谈、观察、实验）；数据分析方法（描述统计、推断统计、质性分析）；研究报告撰写与学术规范。课程注重实践操作，培养学生独立开展教育研究的能力。

This course provides systematic training in educational research principles and methods. Topics include research question formulation, literature review, research design (quantitative, qualitative, mixed methods), data collection (surveys, interviews, observations, experiments), data analysis (descriptive and inferential statistics, qualitative analysis), and academic writing. Emphasis is on hands-on practice and developing independent research skills.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：教育实证研究设计与数据分析

自选教育研究问题，完成文献综述、研究假设、研究设计（量化或质性），并使用样本数据进行初步分析，提交完整研究方案。

Choose an educational research question, complete literature review, hypotheses, and research design (quantitative or qualitative), conduct preliminary analysis with sample data, and submit a complete research proposal.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
研究问题确立与文献综述
本步骤的核心任务是确立有价值的研究问题，系统梳理相关文献并撰写文献综述。研究问题是研究的起点，决定着研究的方向和价值。好的研究问题应当具有创新性、可行性和实践意义。文献综述不是文献的简单罗列，而是对已有研究的批判性分析和系统性整合。

• 运用问题树分析方法，从教育实践和理论发展中识别研究问题，评估问题的重要性、创新性和可行性，最终确定具体的研究问题和研究目标
• 系统检索和筛选相关文献，使用CNKI、Web of Science、Google Scholar等数据库，按照纳入排除标准筛选出核心文献，建立文献管理库
• 采用主题分析法和历史分析法对文献进行系统梳理和批判性评述，总结已有研究的主要结论、理论视角、研究方法和不足之处，找出研究空白和本研究的切入点
产出：研究问题与文献综述（含问题陈述、研究目标、文献综述）｜ 质量标准：问题明确有价值，文献全面系统，综述有批判性
步骤 2
研究设计与方法论选择
本步骤的核心任务是选择恰当的研究范式和研究方法，设计完整的研究方案。教育研究存在量化研究、质性研究、混合研究三种主要范式，每种范式有其哲学基础和适用场景。研究设计的核心是确保研究的效度和信度，使研究能够可靠地回答研究问题。

• 根据研究问题的性质选择研究范式（量化/质性/混合），说明研究的范式选择依据和哲学基础（实证主义/建构主义/实用主义）
• 设计具体的研究方案，包括研究对象与抽样方法、研究工具与材料、研究程序与步骤、数据收集与分析方法等核心要素
• 进行研究质量评估，量化研究重点评估内部效度、外部效度、结构效度和结论效度，质性研究重点评估可信度、可转移性、可靠性和可确认性
产出：研究设计方案（含范式选择、研究方案、质量保障）｜ 质量标准：方法选择恰当，设计科学严谨，质量保障有力
步骤 3
研究工具开发与预研究
本步骤的核心任务是开发和完善研究工具，通过预研究检验研究设计的可行性。研究工具是收集数据的手段，其质量直接影响研究结果的可靠性和有效性。预研究是正式研究的"彩排"，可以发现研究设计中存在的问题，及时调整和完善。

• 开发或选择研究工具，如问卷、量表、访谈提纲、观察记录表、测试题等，按照标准化程序进行工具编制和修订
• 进行预研究，选取小样本进行试测，检验研究工具的信度和效度，评估研究程序的可行性，识别可能的问题和困难
• 根据预研究结果修订和完善研究工具和研究设计，调整研究方案，优化数据收集流程，为正式研究做好充分准备
产出：研究工具与预研究报告（含研究工具、预研究结果、修订方案）｜ 质量标准：工具可靠有效，预研究充分，修订有依据
步骤 4
数据收集与分析
本步骤的核心任务是按照研究设计系统收集数据，运用恰当的方法分析数据。数据收集是研究的中心环节，需要严格按照研究方案执行，确保数据的真实性和可靠性。数据分析需要根据数据类型和研究问题选择合适的方法，从数据中提取有意义的信息。

• 严格执行研究方案进行数据收集，遵循伦理规范获得知情同意，采用标准化的施测程序和数据记录方式，确保数据收集的质量
• 根据研究范式选择数据分析方法，量化研究使用描述统计、推论统计、多元统计等方法，质性研究使用编码分析、主题分析、话语分析、叙事分析等方法
• 对分析结果进行解释和讨论，回到研究问题和理论框架，说明研究发现的意义，与已有研究进行对话，探讨研究的理论贡献和实践启示
产出：研究数据分析报告（含数据描述、分析过程、结果讨论）｜ 质量标准：数据真实可靠，分析方法正确，结果解释深入
步骤 5
研究报告撰写与学术规范
本步骤的核心任务是撰写规范的学术研究报告，严格遵守学术道德和学术规范。研究报告是研究成果的最终呈现形式，应当清晰、准确、完整地呈现研究的全过程和主要发现。学术规范是学术研究的底线，包括文献引用规范、数据真实性、知识产权保护等。

• 按照学术规范撰写研究报告/论文，包括摘要、关键词、引言、文献综述、研究方法、研究结果、讨论与分析、结论与建议、参考文献等部分
• 严格遵守学术道德规范，正确引用他人研究成果，杜绝抄袭和剽窃，真实报告研究数据和结果，不篡改、不编造数据
• 进行研究反思，总结研究的创新点和贡献，客观分析研究的局限性，提出未来研究的方向和建议，体现研究者的学术自觉
产出：教育研究学术论文/报告（含完整研究报告、学术申明、反思展望）｜ 质量标准：报告规范完整，学术道德严谨，反思深刻

Steps

Step 1
Research Question Establishment and Literature Review
The core task of this step is to establish valuable research questions, systematically sort out relevant literature, and write a literature review. Research questions are the starting point of research, determining the direction and value of the study. Good research questions should be innovative, feasible, and practically significant. Literature review is not a simple listing of literature but a critical analysis and systematic integration of existing research.

• Use problem tree analysis method to identify research questions from educational practice and theoretical development, evaluate the importance, innovation, and feasibility of questions, and finally determine specific research questions and research objectives
• Systematically retrieve and screen relevant literature, use databases such as CNKI, Web of Science, and Google Scholar, screen core literature according to inclusion and exclusion criteria, and establish a literature management library
• Use thematic analysis and historical analysis methods to systematically sort out and critically review literature, summarize main conclusions, theoretical perspectives, research methods, and deficiencies of existing studies, and identify research gaps and entry points for this study
Deliverable: Research Questions and Literature Review (including problem statement, research objectives, literature review) | Quality standard: Clear and valuable questions, comprehensive and systematic literature, critical review
Step 2
Research Design and Methodology Selection
The core task of this step is to select appropriate research paradigms and methods and design a complete research plan. Educational research has three main paradigms: quantitative research, qualitative research, and mixed research, each with its philosophical foundation and applicable scenarios. The core of research design is to ensure research validity and reliability, enabling the study to reliably answer research questions.

• Select research paradigm (quantitative/qualitative/mixed) according to the nature of research questions, explain paradigm selection rationale and philosophical basis (positivism/constructivism/pragmatism)
• Design specific research plan, including core elements such as research subjects and sampling methods, research tools and materials, research procedures and steps, and data collection and analysis methods
• Conduct research quality assessment: quantitative research focuses on internal validity, external validity, construct validity, and conclusion validity; qualitative research focuses on credibility, transferability, dependability, and confirmability
Deliverable: Research Design Plan (including paradigm selection, research plan, quality assurance) | Quality standard: Appropriate method selection, scientific and rigorous design, strong quality assurance
Step 3
Research Tool Development and Pilot Study
The core task of this step is to develop and improve research tools and test the feasibility of research design through a pilot study. Research tools are means of data collection, and their quality directly affects the reliability and validity of research results. The pilot study is the "rehearsal" of formal research, which can identify problems in research design for timely adjustment and improvement.

• Develop or select research tools, such as questionnaires, scales, interview outlines, observation record forms, test questions, etc., and compile and revise tools according to standardized procedures
• Conduct pilot study, select small sample for pre-testing, test reliability and validity of research tools, evaluate feasibility of research procedures, and identify possible problems and difficulties
• Revise and improve research tools and research design based on pilot study results, adjust research plan, optimize data collection process, and make full preparations for formal research
Deliverable: Research Tools and Pilot Study Report (including research tools, pilot study results, revision plan) | Quality standard: Reliable and effective tools, sufficient pilot study, evidence-based revision
Step 4
Data Collection and Analysis
The core task of this step is to systematically collect data according to research design and analyze data using appropriate methods. Data collection is the central环节 of research, requiring strict implementation according to research plan to ensure data authenticity and reliability. Data analysis requires selecting appropriate methods according to data types and research questions to extract meaningful information from data.

• Strictly implement research plan for data collection, follow ethical norms to obtain informed consent, use standardized testing procedures and data recording methods, and ensure data collection quality
• Select data analysis methods according to research paradigm: quantitative research uses descriptive statistics, inferential statistics, multivariate statistics, etc.; qualitative research uses coding analysis, thematic analysis, discourse analysis, narrative analysis, etc.
• Interpret and discuss analysis results, return to research questions and theoretical framework, explain the significance of research findings, dialogue with existing studies, and explore theoretical contribution and practical implications of the study
Deliverable: Research Data Analysis Report (including data description, analysis process, result discussion) | Quality standard: Authentic and reliable data, correct analysis methods, in-depth result interpretation
Step 5
Research Report Writing and Academic Norms
The core task of this step is to write a standardized academic research report and strictly abide by academic ethics and norms. The research report is the final presentation form of research achievements, and should clearly, accurately, and completely present the entire research process and main findings. Academic norms are the bottom line of academic research, including literature citation norms, data authenticity, intellectual property protection, etc.

• Write research report/paper according to academic norms, including abstract, keywords, introduction, literature review, research methods, research results, discussion and analysis, conclusions and suggestions, references, etc.
• Strictly abide by academic ethics norms, correctly cite other people's research成果,杜绝 plagiarism and plagiarism, truthfully report research data and results, and do not falsify or fabricate data
• Conduct research reflection, summarize innovations and contributions of the study, objectively analyze research limitations, propose future research directions and suggestions, embodying researchers' academic self-awareness
Deliverable: Educational Research Academic Paper/Report (including complete research report, academic statement, reflection and outlook) | Quality standard: Standard and complete report, rigorous academic ethics, profound reflection
