特殊教育导论

Introduction to Special Education

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：秋季

本课程是特殊教育专业的入门课程，系统介绍特殊教育的基本概念、发展历史、法律政策与伦理规范。课程涵盖各类特殊需要儿童的分类与特征，包括学习障碍、智力障碍、自闭症谱系障碍、情绪与行为障碍、感官障碍等。学生将了解个别化教育计划（IEP）的制定流程，以及特殊教育的服务模式与支持体系。通过案例分析和课堂讨论，培养学生对特殊教育的专业认同和基本素养。

This introductory course provides a comprehensive overview of special education, covering foundational concepts, historical development, legal frameworks, and ethical guidelines. Students will explore the classification and characteristics of exceptional children, including learning disabilities, intellectual disabilities, autism spectrum disorder, emotional and behavioral disorders, and sensory impairments. The course also covers the Individualized Education Program (IEP) process and service delivery models, fostering professional identity and basic competencies in special education.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：个别化教育计划（IEP）制定

基于一名模拟特殊需要学生案例，制定一份完整的IEP，包含现有表现水平、年度目标、短期目标、服务安排与评估标准。

Based on a simulated case of a student with special needs, develop a complete IEP including present levels of performance, annual goals, short-term objectives, service arrangements, and evaluation criteria.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
特殊教育需求评估与对象界定
本步骤的核心任务是评估特殊教育需求，科学界定特殊教育对象的类别和程度。特殊教育的前提是准确识别和评估有特殊教育需要的儿童，这涉及到筛查、鉴别、评估等多个环节。全纳教育理念强调每个儿童都有学习的权利，特殊教育的本质是提供适合每个儿童的个性化支持。

• 运用筛查工具和鉴别程序，从儿童群体中筛选出可能有特殊教育需要的对象，采用智力测验、适应行为评定、发展评估等工具进行综合评估
• 依据残疾分类标准（如ICF国际功能、残疾和健康分类），从身体功能、活动参与、环境因素三个维度评估儿童的功能状况和支持需求
• 组织多学科评估团队（特殊教育教师、心理学家、医生、康复师、家长）进行综合研判，形成评估报告和教育安置建议
产出：特殊教育需求评估报告（含评估工具、评估结果、教育安置建议）｜ 质量标准：评估科学规范，多学科参与，建议合理可行
步骤 2
特殊教育理念确立与目标体系设计
本步骤的核心任务是确立以全纳教育为核心理念的特殊教育价值观，设计分层分类的特殊教育目标体系。特殊教育的目标不仅是传授知识技能，更是促进特殊儿童的全面发展和社会融合。零拒绝、零歧视、个别化、最少限制环境是特殊教育的基本原则。

• 深入研究全纳教育、融合教育、个别化教育等核心理念，结合我国特殊教育政策和实际，确立本研究/实践的特殊教育理念和价值取向
• 依据《残疾人教育条例》和《特殊教育提升计划》，设计总目标、分类目标、个别化目标三级目标体系，涵盖身心发展、知识技能、社会适应、生活自理等维度
• 针对不同障碍类型（智力障碍、听力障碍、视力障碍、自闭症、学习障碍等）设计差异化的目标体系，体现分类教育、因材施教的原则
产出：特殊教育理念与目标体系（含理念阐述、目标体系图、分类目标表）｜ 质量标准：理念先进，目标体系完整，分类科学合理
步骤 3
个别化教育计划（IEP）设计
本步骤的核心任务是为特殊儿童设计个别化教育计划（IEP）。个别化教育计划是特殊教育的核心制度和标志性特征，体现了因材施教的教育原则。一个好的IEP应当基于评估、目标明确、内容适切、可测量、可操作。

• 组成IEP团队（包括特殊教育教师、普通教育教师、家长、相关专业人员、学生本人等），建立团队协作机制，明确各成员职责
• 按照"现有水平-年度目标-短期目标-教育服务-支持措施-评价方式"的结构设计IEP，确保每个目标都可观察、可测量、可达成
• 设计个别化的教学内容和方法，包括课程调整、教学适应、辅助技术、支持服务等，确保教育内容和方法匹配儿童的特殊需要
产出：个别化教育计划（IEP）样本（含完整IEP文档、目标分解表、服务清单）｜ 质量标准：IEP要素完整，目标具体可测，支持措施到位
步骤 4
融合教育环境创设与支持体系构建
本步骤的核心任务是创设支持性的融合教育环境，构建全方位的特殊教育支持体系。融合教育不是简单地将特殊儿童放入普通教室，而是需要系统的环境调整和支持保障。最少限制环境（LRE）是特殊教育安置的基本原则。

• 规划融合教育的物理环境和心理环境，包括无障碍设施建设、班级环境调整、积极行为支持、同伴关系建立等，营造包容接纳的班级氛围
• 构建多层次支持体系，包括资源教室、巡回辅导、同伴支持、辅助技术、康复训练、家校协作等，为不同需要的儿童提供适宜的支持
• 建立普通教师与特殊教育教师的协作机制，设计协同教学、团队教学、个别辅导等多种教学组织形式，确保融合教育的质量
产出：融合教育支持体系方案（含环境创设、支持体系、协作机制）｜ 质量标准：环境支持性强，支持体系完善，协作机制有效
步骤 5
特殊教育质量评价与保障机制
本步骤的核心任务是建立特殊教育质量评价体系，构建持续改进的质量保障机制。特殊教育的质量不能只用学业成绩来衡量，更要关注儿童的发展进步、社会适应和生活质量。发展性评价和个别化评价是特殊教育评价的基本原则。

• 构建多元化的特殊教育质量评价体系，包括儿童发展评价、教育教学评价、学校管理评价、支持服务评价等多个维度，体现全面质量观
• 设计个别化的评价工具和方法，采用档案袋评价、表现性评价、生态评估、动态评估等适合特殊儿童的评价方式，真实反映儿童的进步和发展
• 建立特殊教育质量保障机制，包括IEP质量审查、定期评估与调整、家长参与、专业发展、经费保障等，形成持续改进的良性循环
产出：特殊教育质量评价与保障方案（含评价指标、评价工具、保障机制）｜ 质量标准：评价体系科学多元，工具适切有效，保障机制健全

Steps

Step 1
Special Education Needs Assessment and Subject Definition
The core task of this step is to assess special education needs and scientifically define the categories and degrees of special education subjects. The premise of special education is accurate identification and assessment of children with special educational needs, which involves multiple links such as screening, identification, and evaluation. The inclusive education philosophy emphasizes that every child has the right to learn, and the essence of special education is to provide personalized support suitable for each child.

• Use screening tools and identification procedures to screen out children who may have special educational needs from the child population, and conduct comprehensive assessment using intelligence tests, adaptive behavior scales, developmental assessment, and other tools
• Based on disability classification standards (such as ICF International Classification of Functioning, Disability and Health), assess children's functional status and support needs from three dimensions: body function, activity participation, and environmental factors
• Organize a multidisciplinary assessment team (special education teachers, psychologists, doctors, rehabilitation therapists, parents) for comprehensive judgment, forming assessment reports and educational placement recommendations
Deliverable: Special Education Needs Assessment Report (including assessment tools, assessment results, educational placement recommendations) | Quality standard: Scientific and standardized assessment, multidisciplinary participation, reasonable and feasible recommendations
Step 2
Special Education Philosophy Establishment and Goal System Design
The core task of this step is to establish special education values with inclusive education as the core concept and design a layered and classified special education goal system. The goal of special education is not only to impart knowledge and skills but also to promote the comprehensive development and social integration of children with disabilities. Zero rejection, zero discrimination, individualization, and least restrictive environment are the basic principles of special education.

• Deeply study core concepts such as inclusive education, integrated education, and individualized education, and combine China's special education policies and practice to establish the special education philosophy and value orientation of this research/practice
• Based on the "Regulations on Education for Persons with Disabilities" and the "Special Education Promotion Plan", design a three-level goal system: general goals, classified goals, and individualized goals, covering physical and mental development, knowledge and skills, social adaptation, self-care, etc.
• Design differentiated goal systems for different types of disabilities (intellectual disability, hearing impairment, visual impairment, autism, learning disabilities, etc.), embodying the principles of classified education and teaching according to aptitude
Deliverable: Special Education Philosophy and Goal System (including philosophy elaboration, goal system diagram, classified goals table) | Quality standard: Advanced philosophy, complete goal system, scientific and reasonable classification
Step 3
Individualized Education Program (IEP) Design
The core task of this step is to design Individualized Education Programs (IEP) for children with special needs. The IEP is the core system and iconic feature of special education, embodying the educational principle of teaching according to aptitude. A good IEP should be assessment-based, have clear goals, appropriate content, be measurable, and be operable.

• Form an IEP team (including special education teachers, general education teachers, parents, related professionals, students themselves, etc.), establish team collaboration mechanisms, and clarify responsibilities of each member
• Design the IEP according to the structure of "current level - annual goals - short-term goals - educational services - support measures - evaluation methods", ensuring each goal is observable, measurable, and achievable
• Design individualized teaching content and methods, including curriculum adaptation, teaching adaptation, assistive technology, support services, etc., ensuring educational content and methods match children's special needs
Deliverable: Individualized Education Program (IEP) Sample (including complete IEP document, goal breakdown table, service list) | Quality standard: Complete IEP elements, specific and measurable goals, adequate support measures
Step 4
Inclusive Education Environment Creation and Support System Construction
The core task of this step is to create a supportive inclusive education environment and construct a comprehensive special education support system. Inclusive education is not simply placing children with special needs in regular classrooms but requires systematic environmental adjustment and support guarantees. The Least Restrictive Environment (LRE) is the basic principle of special education placement.

• Plan the physical and psychological environment for inclusive education, including barrier-free facility construction, classroom environment adjustment, positive behavior support, peer relationship building, etc., creating an inclusive and accepting classroom atmosphere
• Construct a multi-level support system, including resource rooms, itinerant guidance, peer support, assistive technology, rehabilitation training, home-school collaboration, etc., providing appropriate support for children with different needs
• Establish collaboration mechanisms between general teachers and special education teachers, design various teaching organizational forms such as collaborative teaching, team teaching, and individual tutoring, ensuring the quality of inclusive education
Deliverable: Inclusive Education Support System Plan (including environment creation, support system, collaboration mechanism) | Quality standard: Strong environmental support, complete support system, effective collaboration mechanism
Step 5
Special Education Quality Evaluation and Guarantee Mechanism
The core task of this step is to establish a special education quality evaluation system and construct a quality guarantee mechanism for continuous improvement. The quality of special education cannot be measured solely by academic achievement; more attention should be paid to children's developmental progress, social adaptation, and quality of life. Developmental evaluation and individualized evaluation are the basic principles of special education evaluation.

• Construct a diversified special education quality evaluation system, including multiple dimensions such as child development evaluation, education and teaching evaluation, school management evaluation, and support service evaluation, embodying a comprehensive quality view
• Design individualized evaluation tools and methods, using evaluation methods suitable for special children such as portfolio assessment, performance-based assessment, ecological assessment, and dynamic assessment to truly reflect children's progress and development
• Establish a special education quality guarantee mechanism, including IEP quality review, regular assessment and adjustment, parent participation, professional development, funding guarantee, etc., forming a virtuous cycle of continuous improvement
Deliverable: Special Education Quality Evaluation and Guarantee Plan (including evaluation indicators, evaluation tools, guarantee mechanism) | Quality standard: Scientific and diversified evaluation system, appropriate and effective tools, sound guarantee mechanism
