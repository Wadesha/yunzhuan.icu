特殊教育导论

Introduction to Special Education

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：秋季

本课程是特殊教育专业的入门课程，系统介绍特殊教育的基本概念、发展历史、法律政策与伦理规范。课程涵盖各类特殊需要儿童的分类与特征，包括学习障碍、智力障碍、自闭症谱系障碍、情绪与行为障碍、感官障碍等。学生将了解个别化教育计划（IEP）的制定流程，以及特殊教育的服务模式与支持体系。通过案例分析和课堂讨论，培养学生对特殊教育的专业认同和基本素养。

This introductory course provides a comprehensive overview of special education, covering foundational concepts, historical development, legal frameworks, and ethical guidelines. Students will explore the classification and characteristics of exceptional children, including learning disabilities, intellectual disabilities, autism spectrum disorder, emotional and behavioral disorders, and sensory impairments. The course also covers the Individualized Education Program (IEP) process and service delivery models, fostering professional identity and basic competencies in special education.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：个别化教育计划（IEP）制定

基于一名模拟特殊需要学生案例，制定一份完整的IEP，包含现有表现水平、年度目标、短期目标、服务安排与评估标准。

Based on a simulated case of a student with special needs, develop a complete IEP including present levels of performance, annual goals, short-term objectives, service arrangements, and evaluation criteria.
