特殊教育教学法

Special Education Teaching Methods

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：特殊教育导论 | 学期：春季

本课程聚焦特殊教育的教学策略与方法，培养学生设计和实施有效教学的能力。课程内容包括：个别化教学的设计与实施、任务分析与链式教学、直接教学法、合作学习策略、辅助技术的应用、课堂管理与环境创设、以及差异化教学。学生将学习如何根据学生的个别差异调整课程内容、教学方法和评价方式，掌握各学科领域（语文、数学、社会适应等）的特殊教学策略。

This course focuses on instructional strategies and methods in special education, developing students' ability to design and implement effective instruction. Topics include individualized instructional design, task analysis and chaining, direct instruction, cooperative learning strategies, assistive technology applications, classroom management and environmental design, and differentiated instruction. Students learn to adapt curriculum, methods, and assessment to individual differences, and acquire subject-specific strategies for language arts, mathematics, and social adaptation.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：个别化教学方案设计与微型教学演示

学生需针对一名有特殊教育需要的学生（模拟案例），设计一套完整的个别化教学方案，包括学期教学目标、单元教学计划、详细的课时教案（至少3课时）、教学材料准备、以及教学评价方案。此外，学生需录制一段15-20分钟的微型教学演示视频，展示所选教学方法的实际应用。作业评分将考虑教学目标的适切性、教学策略的针对性、教学活动设计的创新性以及教学演示的效果。

Students will design a comprehensive individualized instructional program for a student with special needs (simulated case), including semester goals, unit plans, detailed lesson plans (at least 3 lessons), instructional materials, and evaluation methods. Additionally, students will record a 15-20 minute microteaching video demonstrating the practical application of selected teaching methods. Evaluation considers goal appropriateness, strategy alignment, activity innovation, and teaching effectiveness.
