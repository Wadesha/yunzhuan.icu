幼儿教育

Early Childhood Education

中文介绍

幼儿教育是一门研究如何为0-8岁儿童提供教育和发展支持的学科。它涵盖了儿童发展、幼儿课程、早期识字、儿童心理、家长教育等多个方向。幼儿教育专业的课程包括儿童发展心理学、幼儿教育课程、早期阅读、游戏理论、教育评估等。毕业生在幼儿园、托儿所、早教中心、儿童发展机构等都有广泛的就业机会。幼儿教育专业培养学生的爱心和教育能力。

English Introduction

Early Childhood Education is the study of providing educational and developmental support to children aged 0-8. It covers child development, early childhood curriculum, early literacy, child psychology, and parent education, and more. Coursework includes child development psychology, early childhood education curriculum, early reading, play theory, and educational assessment. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in kindergartens, nurseries, early education centers, and child development agencies. Early Childhood Education develops students' compassion and educational abilities.
