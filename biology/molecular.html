🧬 分子生物学

Molecular Biology

中文介绍

分子生物学是一门研究生物分子（如DNA、RNA、蛋白质）结构和功能的学科。它涵盖了基因表达、蛋白质合成、细胞信号传导、分子遗传学等多个方向。分子生物学专业的课程包括生物学、化学、生物化学、遗传学、分子生物学实验技术等。毕业生在科研机构、制药公司、生物技术公司、医院等都有广泛的就业机会。随着基因编辑技术的发展，分子生物学专业的需求持续增长。

English Introduction

Molecular Biology is the study of the structure and function of biological molecules such as DNA, RNA, and proteins. It covers gene expression, protein synthesis, cellular signaling, molecular genetics, and more. Coursework includes biology, chemistry, biochemistry, genetics, and molecular biology laboratory techniques. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in research institutions, pharmaceutical companies, biotech companies, and hospitals. With the development of gene editing technology, demand for molecular biology continues to grow.
