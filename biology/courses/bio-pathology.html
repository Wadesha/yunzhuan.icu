病理学

Pathology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：生理学、微生物学 | 学期：第五学期

本课程研究疾病的发生发展规律，分为总论和各论两部分。总论包括细胞和组织的适应与损伤、损伤的修复、局部血液循环障碍、炎症、肿瘤等基本病理过程。各论介绍各系统常见疾病的病因、发病机制、病理变化和临床病理联系。课程通过理论讲授和病理切片观察，培养学生认识和分析疾病的能力。

This course studies the mechanisms and patterns of disease, divided into general and systemic pathology. General pathology covers cellular adaptation and injury, tissue repair, local hemodynamic disorders, inflammation, and neoplasia. Systemic pathology introduces etiology, pathogenesis, pathological changes, and clinicopathological correlations of common diseases in each organ system.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：病理切片观察与疾病诊断分析

观察一组病理切片，识别基本病变和各系统典型疾病病理特征，结合临床资料进行分析诊断。撰写病理诊断报告。

Observe a set of pathological slides, identify basic lesions and typical pathological features of systemic diseases, and analyze diagnoses with clinical data. Write a pathological diagnosis report.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
疾病系统选择与病理切片资料收集
本步骤的核心任务是选择一个重要的疾病系统，收集相关病理切片资料和临床信息。需要从各系统疾病中选择具有典型病理特征的疾病，确保有足够的形态学改变可供观察和分析。病理学是连接基础医学与临床医学的桥梁，病理诊断是疾病诊断的"金标准"。

• 从心血管系统、呼吸系统、消化系统、泌尿系统、生殖系统、内分泌系统、神经系统等中选择一个系统，确定2-3种典型疾病（如高血压心脏病、大叶性肺炎、胃溃疡、肾小球肾炎、乳腺癌等）
• 收集病理资料：收集大体标本照片、HE染色组织切片图像、特殊染色（免疫组化、PAS、Masson三色）图像，以及对应的临床病史资料
• 学习各器官的正常组织学结构作为对照，复习相关疾病的病因、发病机制和临床病理联系
产出：病理资料集（含正常组织对照、病变组织图像、临床资料）、疾病选择依据报告｜ 质量标准：疾病选择典型，图像资料清晰完整，临床信息齐全
步骤 2
基本病理变化观察与识别
本步骤的核心任务是观察并识别各种基本病理变化，包括变性、坏死、凋亡、炎症、修复、肿瘤等基本病变。基本病理变化是各种疾病的共同形态学基础，准确识别基本病变是病理诊断的基本功。需要在显微镜下或数字切片上仔细观察细胞和组织的形态改变。

• 细胞和组织的适应与损伤：观察萎缩、肥大、增生、化生的形态特征；识别各种变性（细胞水肿、脂肪变、玻璃样变、淀粉样变）和坏死（凝固性坏死、液化性坏死、纤维素样坏死、坏疽）的形态特点
• 损伤的修复：观察肉芽组织的组成和形态特点，识别瘢痕组织，理解创伤愈合的基本过程
• 炎症：观察急性炎症的基本病理变化（变质、渗出、增生），识别各种炎症细胞（中性粒细胞、淋巴细胞、巨噬细胞、浆细胞、嗜酸性粒细胞），比较浆液性炎、纤维素性炎、化脓性炎、出血性炎的特点
产出：基本病理变化观察报告（含病变描述、形态特点、鉴别要点、典型图像）｜ 质量标准：病变识别准确，形态描述专业规范，鉴别要点清晰
步骤 3
系统疾病病理形态观察与描述
本步骤的核心任务是系统观察所选疾病的病理形态学改变，进行规范的形态学描述。需要从大体标本和组织学两个层面观察病变，描述病变的部位、数量、大小、形状、颜色、质地等特征，并分析病变与周围组织的关系。规范的病理描述是病理诊断的基础。

• 大体标本观察：描述病变的部位、范围、大小（用具体尺寸如cm×cm×cm）、形状、颜色、质地、边界、与周围组织的关系，绘制大体示意图或拍摄照片
• 组织学观察：在低倍镜下观察病变的整体结构和分布特点，在高倍镜下观察细胞的形态细节（细胞大小、形态、排列、核的特征、核分裂象），记录间质反应和浸润情况
• 病理描述撰写：按照病理学规范撰写病变描述，包括肉眼观察和镜下观察两部分，使用专业术语，客观准确地描述所见病变
产出：系统疾病病理观察报告（含大体描述、镜下描述、病理图像、示意图）｜ 质量标准：观察全面细致，描述规范准确，专业术语使用正确
步骤 4
病理诊断与鉴别诊断分析
本步骤的核心任务是根据病理形态学观察结果，结合临床资料做出病理诊断，并进行鉴别诊断分析。病理诊断需要综合分析形态学特征、免疫表型、分子检测和临床信息，排除相似的疾病。鉴别诊断体现了病理医生的临床思维能力和专业水平。

• 病理诊断：根据病变的形态学特征（组织结构模式、细胞形态特点、浸润方式等）做出初步病理诊断，确定疾病的类型、分级和分期
• 鉴别诊断：列出2-3种需要鉴别的疾病，分析它们之间的形态学异同点，提出进一步的检查方法（特殊染色、免疫组化、分子检测）来明确诊断
• 临床病理联系：讨论病理变化与临床表现的关系，解释患者的症状、体征和实验室检查结果的病理基础，分析疾病的转归和预后
产出：病理诊断分析报告（含病理诊断、诊断依据、鉴别诊断表、临床病理联系）｜ 质量标准：诊断准确有据，鉴别诊断思路清晰，临床病理联系紧密
步骤 5
病理诊断报告撰写与病例讨论
本步骤的核心任务是撰写规范的病理诊断报告，并组织病例讨论。病理诊断报告是病理科最重要的医疗文书，具有法律效力，需要格式规范、诊断准确、描述客观。病例讨论可以培养临床思维能力，提高分析和解决问题的能力。

• 撰写病理诊断报告：按照临床病理报告规范格式撰写，包括患者基本信息、送检标本信息、肉眼检查、镜下检查、病理诊断、备注（免疫组化结果、特殊染色、分子检测）等部分
• 制作病理图文报告：选择典型的大体和镜下图像，标注关键病变特征，制作图文并茂的病理报告
• 病例讨论：组织病例讨论，汇报病例特点、诊断思路、鉴别诊断要点，讨论疾病的病因发病机制、最新研究进展和治疗原则
产出：规范病理诊断报告（PDF格式，含大体描述、镜下描述、病理图像、诊断结论）、病例讨论PPT｜ 质量标准：报告格式规范、诊断准确、图文并茂，病例讨论深入有见解

Steps

Step 1
Disease System Selection and Pathology Slide Collection
The core task of this step is to select an important disease system and collect relevant pathological slide materials and clinical information. Choose diseases with typical pathological features from various systems to ensure sufficient morphological changes for observation and analysis.

• Select one system from cardiovascular, respiratory, digestive, urinary, reproductive, endocrine, nervous systems, etc.; identify 2-3 typical diseases (e.g., hypertensive heart disease, lobar pneumonia, gastric ulcer, glomerulonephritis, breast cancer)
• Collect pathology materials: gather gross specimen photos, H&E stained tissue slide images, special stains (IHC, PAS, Masson's trichrome), and corresponding clinical history data
• Study normal histology of each organ as control; review etiology, pathogenesis, and clinicopathological correlation of related diseases
Deliverable: Pathology material collection (normal tissue control, lesion tissue images, clinical data), disease selection rationale report | Quality standard: Typical disease selection, clear and complete image materials, comprehensive clinical information
Step 2
Basic Pathological Changes Observation and Identification
The core task of this step is to observe and identify various basic pathological changes, including degeneration, necrosis, apoptosis, inflammation, repair, and tumors. Basic pathological changes are the common morphological foundation of all diseases. Accurate identification of basic lesions is fundamental to pathological diagnosis.

• Cell and tissue adaptation and injury: observe morphological features of atrophy, hypertrophy, hyperplasia, metaplasia; identify various degenerations (cellular swelling, fatty change, hyaline change, amyloidosis) and necrosis types (coagulative, liquefactive, fibrinoid, gangrene)
• Injury repair: observe composition and morphological features of granulation tissue; identify scar tissue; understand basic process of wound healing
• Inflammation: observe basic pathological changes of acute inflammation (alteration, exudation, proliferation); identify various inflammatory cells (neutrophils, lymphocytes, macrophages, plasma cells, eosinophils); compare characteristics of serous, fibrinous, purulent, and hemorrhagic inflammation
Deliverable: Basic pathological changes observation report (lesion descriptions, morphological features, differential key points, typical images) | Quality standard: Accurate lesion identification, professional and standard morphology description, clear differential points
Step 3
Systemic Disease Pathomorphology Observation and Description
The core task of this step is to systematically observe pathomorphological changes of selected diseases and perform standardized morphological description. Observe lesions at both gross specimen and histological levels; describe location, number, size, shape, color, texture, and relationship with surrounding tissues.

• Gross specimen observation: describe lesion location, extent, size (with specific dimensions like cm×cm×cm), shape, color, texture, borders, relationship with surrounding tissues; draw gross schematic diagrams or take photographs
• Histological observation: observe overall structure and distribution characteristics at low power; observe cellular morphological details at high power (cell size, shape, arrangement, nuclear features, mitotic figures); record stromal reaction and infiltration
• Pathological description writing: write lesion descriptions following pathology standards, including gross and microscopic sections; use professional terminology; describe observed lesions objectively and accurately
Deliverable: Systemic disease pathology observation report (gross description, microscopic description, pathological images, schematic diagrams) | Quality standard: Comprehensive and careful observation, standard and accurate description, correct professional terminology
Step 4
Pathological Diagnosis and Differential Diagnosis Analysis
The core task of this step is to make pathological diagnosis based on pathomorphological observation results combined with clinical data, and perform differential diagnosis analysis. Pathological diagnosis requires comprehensive analysis of morphological features, immunophenotype, molecular testing, and clinical information.

• Pathological diagnosis: make preliminary pathological diagnosis based on morphological features (tissue architecture pattern, cell morphology, invasion pattern); determine disease type, grade, and stage
• Differential diagnosis: list 2-3 diseases needing differentiation; analyze morphological similarities and differences; propose further examination methods (special stains, immunohistochemistry, molecular testing) for definitive diagnosis
• Clinicopathological correlation: discuss relationship between pathological changes and clinical manifestations; explain pathological basis of patient symptoms, signs, and laboratory results; analyze disease outcome and prognosis
Deliverable: Pathological diagnosis analysis report (pathological diagnosis, diagnostic criteria, differential diagnosis table, clinicopathological correlation) | Quality standard: Accurate and evidence-based diagnosis, clear differential diagnosis thinking, strong clinicopathological correlation
Step 5
Pathology Report Writing and Case Discussion
The core task of this step is to write a standardized pathological diagnosis report and organize case discussion. Pathology report is the most important medical document in pathology department with legal effect, requiring standardized format, accurate diagnosis, and objective description.

• Write pathological diagnosis report: follow clinical pathology report standard format, including patient information, specimen information, gross examination, microscopic examination, pathological diagnosis, remarks (IHC results, special stains, molecular testing)
• Create illustrated pathology report: select typical gross and microscopic images; label key lesion features; produce well-illustrated pathology report with both images and text
• Case discussion: organize case discussion; present case features, diagnostic thinking, differential diagnosis key points; discuss etiology, pathogenesis, latest research progress, and treatment principles
Deliverable: Standard pathological diagnosis report (PDF format with gross description, microscopic description, pathology images, diagnostic conclusion), case discussion PPT | Quality standard: Standard report format, accurate diagnosis, well-illustrated, in-depth and insightful case discussion
