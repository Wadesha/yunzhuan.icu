流行病学

Epidemiology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：生物统计学基础 | 学期：第二学期

本课程是公共卫生专业的核心基础课，系统介绍流行病学的基本概念、原理和方法。内容涵盖疾病分布描述、常用流行病学研究方法（队列研究、病例对照研究、横断面研究、实验流行病学）、偏倚与混杂、因果推断、疾病监测以及突发公共卫生事件调查。通过案例分析和实践练习，培养学生运用流行病学思维解决公共卫生问题的能力。

This core course introduces fundamental concepts, principles, and methods of epidemiology. Topics include disease distribution, common study designs (cohort, case-control, cross-sectional, experimental), bias and confounding, causal inference, disease surveillance, and outbreak investigation. Through case studies and practical exercises, students develop epidemiological thinking and problem-solving skills in public health.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：某地区传染病流行病学调查与数据分析

选择一种传染病，设计流行病学调查方案，收集并分析疾病分布数据，探讨危险因素和传播途径。撰写完整流行病学调查报告。

Select an infectious disease, design an epidemiological investigation plan, collect and analyze disease distribution data, and explore risk factors and transmission routes. Write a complete epidemiological investigation report.
