卫生政策与管理

Health Policy and Management

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：经济学基础 | 学期：第三学期

本课程系统介绍卫生政策与医疗卫生管理的基本理论和实践。内容涵盖卫生政策制定过程、卫生系统组织结构、医疗保障制度、卫生经济学基础、医院管理、公共卫生服务管理、卫生人力资源管理、卫生信息管理、卫生政策分析方法以及卫生改革议题。通过案例分析和小组讨论，培养学生的政策分析能力和卫生管理思维。

This course systematically introduces fundamental theories and practices of health policy and healthcare management. Topics include health policy formulation process, health system organization, medical insurance systems, health economics fundamentals, hospital management, public health service management, health human resource management, health information management, health policy analysis methods, and health reform issues. Through case analysis and group discussions, students develop policy analysis skills and health management thinking.
