卫生政策与管理

Health Policy and Management

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：经济学基础 | 学期：第三学期

本课程系统介绍卫生政策与医疗卫生管理的基本理论和实践。内容涵盖卫生政策制定过程、卫生系统组织结构、医疗保障制度、卫生经济学基础、医院管理、公共卫生服务管理、卫生人力资源管理、卫生信息管理、卫生政策分析方法以及卫生改革议题。通过案例分析和小组讨论，培养学生的政策分析能力和卫生管理思维。

This course systematically introduces fundamental theories and practices of health policy and healthcare management. Topics include health policy formulation process, health system organization, medical insurance systems, health economics fundamentals, hospital management, public health service management, health human resource management, health information management, health policy analysis methods, and health reform issues. Through case analysis and group discussions, students develop policy analysis skills and health management thinking.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：卫生政策分析与改革方案设计

选择一项卫生政策议题（如医保支付、分级诊疗或医院管理），进行政策分析并提出改革建议。撰写政策分析报告。

Select a health policy issue (such as medical insurance payment, tiered diagnosis, or hospital management), conduct policy analysis, and propose reform recommendations. Write a policy analysis report.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

示例：分析一项卫生政策——评估它的实施效果和改进空间。
步骤 1
卫生政策问题识别与政策分析框架设计
本步骤的核心任务是识别重要的卫生政策问题，设计政策分析框架。需要从卫生系统的现实挑战和政策需求中选择研究主题，运用政策科学理论和卫生政策分析方法，构建系统的政策分析框架。准确识别政策问题是政策研究成功的第一步。

• 政策问题识别：从卫生筹资、医疗保障、医疗服务供给、公共卫生体系、药品政策、卫生人力、分级诊疗、医改等领域选择政策问题，分析问题的严重性、紧迫性和政策相关性
• 政策分析框架：选择政策分析模型（政策过程模型、利益相关者分析、政策三角模型、Kingdon多源流模型、Greenhalgh扩散模型），确定分析维度（背景、内容、过程、参与者、效果）
• 研究设计：确定研究方法（文献计量分析、政策文本分析、质性研究、定量评价、案例研究、比较研究），制定资料收集方案和分析时间表
产出：卫生政策研究设计方案（含政策问题分析、分析框架、研究方法、资料收集计划）｜ 质量标准：政策问题识别准确，分析框架科学适用，研究方法恰当可行
步骤 2
政策文本分析与政策过程研究
本步骤的核心任务是系统收集和分析政策文本，研究政策制定和实施过程。需要收集相关的政策文件、法律法规、规划方案等文本资料，运用政策分析方法研究政策议程设置、政策制定、政策实施、政策评估的全过程。政策过程研究有助于理解政策为什么成功或失败。

• 政策文本收集：系统收集相关政策文件——法律、行政法规、部门规章、规范性文件、发展规划、行动计划、标准指南，建立政策文本数据库，记录发文机关、时间、文号、主题
• 政策内容分析：使用内容分析法、文本挖掘法、政策工具分类法（Rothwell & Zegveld分类：供给型、环境型、需求型）分析政策内容，编码政策主题和关键词，绘制政策演进图谱
• 政策过程分析：分析政策议程设置（问题如何进入政策议程）、政策制定过程（决策机制、参与主体、利益博弈）、政策实施路径（执行机构、配套措施、试点推广），识别关键节点和影响因素
产出：政策分析报告（含政策文本数据库、内容分析结果、过程分析、关键发现）｜ 质量标准：政策文本收集全面系统，内容分析深入细致，过程分析逻辑清晰
步骤 3
利益相关者分析与政策实施评估
本步骤的核心任务是分析卫生政策的利益相关者，评估政策实施效果。卫生政策涉及多方利益主体，政策实施效果取决于各方的互动和博弈。需要识别关键利益相关者，分析其立场、影响力和相互关系，评估政策实施的进展、效果和问题。

• 利益相关者分析：识别政策利益相关者（政府部门、医疗机构、医务人员、患者、药企、保险公司、学术机构、社会组织、公众），分析各利益相关方的利益诉求、权力影响力、立场态度，绘制利益相关者矩阵（权力-利益矩阵）
• 实施过程评估：评估政策实施的一致性（是否按计划执行）、充分性（资源是否充足）、适宜性（是否适合当地情况）、公平性（是否惠及目标人群），使用过程追踪法、聚焦评估法（RBF）
• 实施效果评价：评价政策的短期效果（服务利用、可及性）、中期效果（健康结果、费用控制）、长期效果（人群健康水平、卫生系统绩效），使用双重差分（DID）、断点回归（RDD）等准实验方法
产出：利益相关者分析与政策实施评估报告（含利益相关者矩阵、实施进展评估、效果评价结果）｜ 质量标准：利益相关者识别全面，分析深入，实施评估方法科学，结论可靠
步骤 4
卫生经济学评价与政策优化方案
本步骤的核心任务是从卫生经济学角度评价政策，提出政策优化方案。卫生政策不仅要考虑效果，还要考虑效率和公平，需要在资源约束下做出最优选择。卫生经济学评价为政策制定和优化提供量化依据，帮助决策者在多个方案中做出理性选择。

• 卫生经济学评价：对政策方案进行经济学评价——成本最小化分析（CMA）、成本-效果分析（CEA）、成本-效用分析（CUA）、成本-效益分析（CBA），计算增量成本效果比（ICER），判断成本效果阈值（如人均GDP的1-3倍）
• 卫生筹资与支付方式分析：分析政策的筹资机制（税收、社会保险、商业保险、自费）、支付方式（按项目、按病种、按人头、总额预付、DRG/DIP），评估对医疗费用、服务质量、供方行为的影响
• 政策优化方案设计：基于评价结果设计政策优化方案，比较不同政策组合的预期效果，进行敏感性分析和情景分析，提出分阶段、分区域的差异化实施策略，制定配套保障措施
产出：卫生经济学评价与政策优化方案报告（含经济学评价结果、政策比较、优化方案、实施路径）｜ 质量标准：经济学评价方法规范，政策比较全面，优化方案科学可行
步骤 5
政策研究报告撰写与政策建议
本步骤的核心任务是撰写卫生政策研究报告，提出具有可操作性的政策建议。政策研究报告是连接研究与决策的桥梁，需要既科学严谨又通俗易懂，既要呈现研究发现也要提出行动方案。好的政策建议应具体、可行、有优先级、有实施路径。

• 撰写政策研究报告：按照政策研究报告规范撰写——执行摘要、政策背景与问题、分析框架与方法、主要发现、政策建议，面向决策者突出关键信息和政策选项，字数3000-5000字
• 制作政策简报：面向决策者制作1-2页的政策简报（Policy Brief），清晰呈现问题、证据、建议，使用信息图表增强可读性，支持快速决策
• 提出政策建议：提出短期（1年内）、中期（1-3年）、长期（3-5年）政策建议，明确责任主体、资源需求、实施路径、风险与应对，提出监测评估指标
产出：卫生政策研究报告、政策简报、政策建议清单、实施路线图｜ 质量标准：报告结构清晰、证据充分、建议具体可行、决策参考价值高

Steps

Step 1
Health Policy Issue Identification and Policy Analysis Framework Design
The core task of this step is to identify important health policy issues and design a policy analysis framework. Select research topics from real-world health system challenges and policy needs.

• Policy issue identification: select policy issues from health financing, medical insurance, healthcare service delivery, public health system, pharmaceutical policy, health workforce, hierarchical diagnosis, healthcare reform; analyze severity, urgency, and policy relevance
• Policy analysis framework: select policy analysis model (policy process model, stakeholder analysis, policy triangle model, Kingdon's multiple streams, Greenhalgh diffusion model); determine analysis dimensions (context, content, process, actors, outcomes)
• Research design: determine research methods (bibliometric analysis, policy text analysis, qualitative research, quantitative evaluation, case study, comparative research); develop data collection plan and analysis timeline
Deliverable: Health policy research design proposal (policy issue analysis, analytical framework, research methods, data collection plan) | Quality standard: Accurate policy issue identification, scientific and applicable analytical framework, appropriate and feasible research methods
Step 2
Policy Text Analysis and Policy Process Research
The core task of this step is to systematically collect and analyze policy texts and study the policy formulation and implementation process. Collect relevant policy documents, laws, regulations, and plans.

• Policy text collection: systematically collect relevant policy documents—laws, administrative regulations, departmental rules, normative documents, development plans, action plans, standards and guidelines; build policy text database; record issuing agency, time, document number, topic
• Policy content analysis: use content analysis, text mining, policy tool classification (Rothwell & Zegveld: supply-side, environment-side, demand-side) to analyze policy content; code policy themes and keywords; draw policy evolution roadmap
• Policy process analysis: analyze policy agenda setting (how issues enter policy agenda), policy formulation process (decision-making mechanisms, participants, interest dynamics), policy implementation pathways (implementing agencies, supporting measures, pilot scaling); identify key nodes and influencing factors
Deliverable: Policy analysis report (policy text database, content analysis results, process analysis, key findings) | Quality standard: Comprehensive and systematic policy text collection, in-depth content analysis, clear process analysis logic
Step 3
Stakeholder Analysis and Policy Implementation Evaluation
The core task of this step is to analyze stakeholders of health policy and evaluate policy implementation effects. Health policy involves multiple stakeholders; implementation effectiveness depends on interactions among them.

• Stakeholder analysis: identify policy stakeholders (government departments, healthcare institutions, medical staff, patients, pharmaceutical companies, insurance companies, academic institutions, social organizations, public); analyze interests, power/influence, positions; draw stakeholder matrix (power-interest matrix)
• Implementation process assessment: assess policy implementation fidelity (implemented as planned), adequacy (sufficient resources), appropriateness (suitable for local context), equity (reaches target population); use process tracing, RBF
• Implementation effectiveness evaluation: evaluate short-term effects (service utilization, accessibility), medium-term effects (health outcomes, cost control), long-term effects (population health, health system performance); use quasi-experimental methods like DID, RDD
Deliverable: Stakeholder analysis and policy implementation evaluation report (stakeholder matrix, implementation progress assessment, effectiveness evaluation results) | Quality standard: Comprehensive stakeholder identification, in-depth analysis, scientific evaluation methods, reliable conclusions
Step 4
Health Economics Evaluation and Policy Optimization Plan
The core task of this step is to evaluate policy from a health economics perspective and propose policy optimization plans. Health policy must consider not only effectiveness but also efficiency and equity.

• Health economics evaluation: perform economic evaluation of policy options—CMA, CEA, CUA, CBA; calculate ICER; determine cost-effectiveness threshold (e.g., 1-3 times GDP per capita)
• Health financing and payment method analysis: analyze policy financing mechanisms (tax, social insurance, commercial insurance, out-of-pocket); payment methods (fee-for-service, case-based, capitation, global budget, DRG/DIP); assess impacts on medical costs, service quality, provider behavior
• Policy optimization design: design policy optimization plans based on evaluation results; compare expected effects of different policy combinations; perform sensitivity and scenario analysis; propose phased and differentiated implementation strategies by region; develop supporting measures
Deliverable: Health economics evaluation and policy optimization plan report (economics evaluation results, policy comparison, optimization plan, implementation pathway) | Quality standard: Standard economic evaluation methods, comprehensive policy comparison, scientific and feasible optimization plan
Step 5
Policy Research Report Writing and Policy Recommendations
The core task of this step is to write a health policy research report and propose actionable policy recommendations. Policy research reports are the bridge between research and decision-making.

• Write policy research report: follow policy research report standards—executive summary, policy background and issues, analytical framework and methods, main findings, policy recommendations; highlight key information and policy options for decision-makers; 3000-5000 words
• Produce policy brief: create 1-2 page policy brief for decision-makers; clearly present problem, evidence, recommendations; use infographics to enhance readability; support rapid decision-making
• Propose policy recommendations: propose short-term (1 year), medium-term (1-3 years), long-term (3-5 years) policy recommendations; clarify responsible actors, resource needs, implementation pathways, risks and responses; propose monitoring and evaluation indicators
Deliverable: Health policy research report, policy brief, policy recommendation list, implementation roadmap | Quality standard: Clear report structure, sufficient evidence, specific and feasible recommendations, high decision-making reference value
