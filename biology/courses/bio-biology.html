普通生物学

General Biology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第一学期

本课程是生物医学科学专业的入门基础课，系统介绍生命科学的基本概念和原理。内容涵盖细胞结构与功能、生物化学基础、遗传学入门、进化论、生物多样性以及生态学基础。通过理论学习和实验操作，学生将掌握生物学的核心知识体系，培养科学思维能力，为后续专业课程的学习奠定坚实基础。

This course is an introductory foundation for biomedical science majors, systematically presenting basic concepts and principles of life science. Topics include cell structure and function, biochemistry fundamentals, genetics introduction, evolution, biodiversity, and ecology basics. Through theoretical study and laboratory work, students will master the core knowledge framework of biology and develop scientific thinking.
