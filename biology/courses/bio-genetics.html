遗传学

Genetics

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：普通生物学 | 学期：第三学期

本课程系统阐述遗传学的基本原理和方法，内容包括孟德尔遗传定律、染色体遗传、连锁与交换、基因定位、基因突变、染色体畸变、细胞质遗传、数量遗传、群体遗传以及分子遗传学基础。课程结合人类遗传病案例进行讲解，培养学生运用遗传学原理分析和解决生物医学问题的能力。

This course systematically presents fundamental principles and methods of genetics. Topics include Mendelian inheritance, chromosomal genetics, linkage and crossing over, gene mapping, gene mutation, chromosomal aberrations, cytoplasmic inheritance, quantitative genetics, population genetics, and molecular genetics basics. The course uses human genetic disease cases for instruction.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：果蝇遗传杂交实验与基因定位分析

选择果蝇突变品系设计杂交实验，观察子代表型分离比，进行卡方检验和基因定位分析。撰写完整的遗传分析报告。

Select Drosophila mutant strains to design crossing experiments, observe phenotypic segregation ratios in offspring, perform chi-square tests, and conduct gene mapping analysis. Write a complete genetic analysis report.
