妇产科护理学

Maternity Nursing

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：内科护理学 | 学期：第四学期

本课程包括产科护理和妇科护理两大部分。产科护理涵盖女性生殖系统解剖与生理、妊娠生理与诊断、孕期保健与护理、分娩期护理、产褥期护理、正常新生儿护理及高危妊娠护理等；妇科护理涵盖妇科常见疾病护理、计划生育护理、妇女保健等。课程强调整体护理和人文关怀，培养学生对妇女全生命周期健康服务的专业能力。

This course includes both obstetric and gynecologic nursing. Obstetric nursing covers female reproductive anatomy and physiology, pregnancy physiology and diagnosis, prenatal care and nursing, intrapartum care, postpartum care, normal newborn care, and high-risk pregnancy nursing. Gynecologic nursing covers common gynecologic conditions, family planning nursing, and women's health promotion. The course emphasizes holistic care and humanistic compassion, developing students' professional competence in women's health across the lifespan.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：孕产妇围产期护理评估与护理方案制定

选择一个孕产妇典型病例（正常或高危妊娠），进行围产期护理评估并制定各阶段护理方案。提交完整护理计划。

Select a typical maternal case (normal or high-risk pregnancy), conduct perinatal nursing assessment, and develop nursing plans for each stage. Submit a complete care plan.
