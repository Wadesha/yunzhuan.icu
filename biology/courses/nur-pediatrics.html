儿科护理学

Pediatric Nursing

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：内科护理学 | 学期：第四学期

本课程研究从胎儿期到青春期儿童的生长发育规律、健康保健及疾病护理特点。内容包括小儿生长发育评估、儿童营养与喂养、儿科常见疾病护理（如呼吸系统疾病、消化系统疾病、心血管疾病、血液系统疾病、神经系统疾病及传染病等）、儿科危重症护理、儿童心理护理及与患儿家属的沟通技巧，强调以家庭为中心的护理理念。

This course studies growth and development patterns, health promotion, and disease nursing care for children from fetal period through adolescence. Topics include growth and development assessment, pediatric nutrition and feeding, nursing care for common childhood illnesses (respiratory, digestive, cardiovascular, hematologic, neurological, and infectious diseases), pediatric critical care, child psychology, and family communication skills, emphasizing family-centered care.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：儿科常见慢性病患儿家庭护理指导手册编制

学生选取一种儿科常见慢性病（如支气管哮喘、儿童糖尿病、白血病或先天性心脏病），以家庭护理为核心编制一份图文并茂的患儿家庭护理指导手册，内容涵盖疾病知识、用药指导、饮食护理、活动与休息、病情观察、应急处理及心理支持，体现以家庭为中心的护理理念。

Students select a common chronic pediatric condition (e.g., bronchial asthma, childhood diabetes, leukemia, or congenital heart disease), and develop an illustrated family nursing care guidebook centered on home care, covering disease knowledge, medication guidance, dietary care, activity and rest, condition monitoring, emergency management, and psychological support, reflecting family-centered nursing principles.
