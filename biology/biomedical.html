🔬 生物医学科学

Biomedical Sciences

中文介绍

生物医学科学是一门研究生命科学与医学交叉领域的学科。它涵盖了生理学、病理学、药理学、免疫学、分子生物学等多个方向。生物医学科学专业的课程包括生物学、化学、解剖学、生理学、微生物学等。毕业生在制药行业、医疗器械公司、科研机构、医院等都有广泛的就业机会。根据NCES数据，生物与生物医学科学专业的学位授予量排名第四。

English Introduction

Biomedical Sciences is a discipline that studies the intersection of life sciences and medicine. It covers physiology, pathology, pharmacology, immunology, molecular biology, and more. Coursework includes biology, chemistry, anatomy, physiology, and microbiology. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in pharmaceutical industry, medical device companies, research institutions, and hospitals. According to NCES data, degrees in biological and biomedical sciences rank fourth in award volume.
