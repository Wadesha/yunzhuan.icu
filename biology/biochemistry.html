⚗️ 生物化学

Biochemistry

中文介绍

生物化学是一门研究生物体内化学过程和分子结构的学科。它涵盖了蛋白质结构与功能、酶学、代谢途径、生物膜、核酸化学等多个方向。生物化学专业的课程包括普通化学、有机化学、物理化学、生物学、生物化学等。毕业生在制药行业、生物技术公司、科研机构、医院等都有广泛的就业机会。生物化学是生命科学领域的基础学科，具有稳定的需求。

English Introduction

Biochemistry is the study of chemical processes and molecular structures within living organisms. It covers protein structure and function, enzymology, metabolic pathways, biological membranes, nucleic acid chemistry, and more. Coursework includes general chemistry, organic chemistry, physical chemistry, biology, and biochemistry. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in pharmaceutical industry, biotech companies, research institutions, and hospitals. Biochemistry is a foundational discipline in life sciences with stable demand.
