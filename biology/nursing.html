👩‍⚕️ 护理学

Nursing

中文介绍

护理学是一门研究如何为患者提供全面护理和健康服务的学科。它涵盖了临床护理、社区护理、心理健康护理、老年护理等多个方向。护理学专业的课程包括解剖学、生理学、药理学、护理学基础、护理实践等。毕业生在医院、诊所、养老院、社区卫生中心等都有广泛的就业机会。护理专业的需求持续增长，10年岗位增长预计达40%，且薪资较高，平均起薪约126,000美元。

English Introduction

Nursing is the study of providing comprehensive care and health services to patients. It covers clinical nursing, community nursing, mental health nursing, geriatric nursing, and more. Coursework includes anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, nursing fundamentals, and nursing practice. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, and community health centers. Nursing demand continues to grow with 40% projected job growth over 10 years, and offers high salaries with average starting salary around $126,000.
