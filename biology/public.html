公共卫生

Public Health

中文介绍

公共卫生是一门研究如何保护和改善公众健康的学科。它涵盖了流行病学、健康政策、环境健康、健康教育、全球卫生等多个方向。公共卫生专业的课程包括统计学、流行病学、卫生政策、环境健康、健康教育等。毕业生在政府卫生部门、公共卫生机构、非政府组织、医院等都有广泛的就业机会。公共卫生专业的需求随着全球健康挑战的增加而持续增长。

English Introduction

Public Health is the study of protecting and improving public health. It covers epidemiology, health policy, environmental health, health education, global health, and more. Coursework includes statistics, epidemiology, health policy, environmental health, and health education. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in government health departments, public health agencies, NGOs, and hospitals. Public health demand continues to grow with global health challenges.

核心课程 Core Courses

流行病学 生物统计学 环境卫生 卫生政策与管理 社会与行为科学 职业卫生 全球卫生
