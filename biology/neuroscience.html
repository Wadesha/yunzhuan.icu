神经科学

Neuroscience

中文介绍

神经科学是一门研究神经系统结构和功能的学科。它涵盖了神经解剖学、神经生理学、神经药理学、认知神经科学、计算神经科学等多个方向。神经科学专业的课程包括生物学、化学、物理学、解剖学、生理学等。毕业生在科研机构、制药公司、医院、科技公司等都有广泛的就业机会。随着脑科学研究的深入，神经科学专业的需求持续增长。

English Introduction

Neuroscience is the study of the structure and function of the nervous system. It covers neuroanatomy, neurophysiology, neuropharmacology, cognitive neuroscience, computational neuroscience, and more. Coursework includes biology, chemistry, physics, anatomy, and physiology. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in research institutions, pharmaceutical companies, hospitals, and tech companies. With advances in brain science research, demand for neuroscience continues to grow.
