管理学

Management

中文介绍

管理学是一门研究如何有效地组织和管理组织资源的学科。它涵盖了组织行为、战略管理、人力资源管理、运营管理等多个方向。管理学专业的课程包括管理学原理、组织行为学、战略管理、人力资源管理、项目管理等。毕业生在企业、政府部门、非营利组织等都有广泛的就业机会。管理学是商科中最基础的专业之一，培养学生的领导能力和管理能力。

English Introduction

Management is the study of how to effectively organize and manage organizational resources. It covers organizational behavior, strategic management, human resource management, operations management, and more. Coursework includes management principles, organizational behavior, strategic management, human resource management, and project management. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in businesses, government, and non-profit organizations. Management is one of the most fundamental business majors, developing students' leadership and management abilities.
