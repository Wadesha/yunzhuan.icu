营销战略

Marketing Strategy

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：市场营销原理、市场调研、消费者行为 | 学期：大三秋季

本课程从战略高度审视企业营销决策，培养学生的系统性营销思维。课程内容包括：企业战略与营销战略的关系、市场竞争分析（波特五力模型、竞争情报）、竞争定位战略（成本领先、差异化、聚焦）、增长战略（安索夫矩阵）、新市场进入战略、定价战略、渠道战略、营销组织与管控、以及营销战略的制定与实施流程。大量使用哈佛商学院案例进行讨论。

This course examines marketing decisions from a strategic perspective, developing students' systematic marketing thinking. Topics include the relationship between corporate strategy and marketing strategy, competitive market analysis (Porter's Five Forces, competitive intelligence), competitive positioning strategies (cost leadership, differentiation, focus), growth strategies (Ansoff Matrix), new market entry, pricing strategy, channel strategy, marketing organization and control, and strategy implementation process.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：企业营销战略制定与竞争分析

选取一家企业，运用波特五力模型和竞争情报分析市场竞争格局，基于安索夫矩阵设计增长战略，制定定价与渠道战略，形成完整的营销战略方案。

Select an enterprise, use Porter's Five Forces and competitive intelligence to analyze the competitive landscape, design growth strategy using the Ansoff Matrix, formulate pricing and channel strategies, and form a complete marketing strategy.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：为一个真实品牌做营销策划，比如一个新上市的国产咖啡品牌。你需要分析目标用户是谁、竞品在做什么、预算怎么分配，然后设计完整的营销方案——包括线上推广（小红书、抖音）、线下活动、KOL合作，最后预估ROI。
步骤 1
市场调研与消费者洞察
本步骤的核心任务是进行全面的市场调研，深入了解目标市场和消费者。通过定性和定量研究方法，收集市场、竞争、消费者数据，分析消费者行为和需求，为营销策略制定提供依据。

• 市场环境与竞争分析：分析宏观市场环境（PEST）、市场规模与增长趋势、市场细分与格局，进行竞争对手分析（直接竞争对手、间接竞争对手、替代品），绘制竞争态势矩阵
• 消费者行为研究：通过问卷调研、深度访谈、焦点小组、用户观察等方法，研究消费者的购买决策过程、需求偏好、使用习惯、价格敏感度、品牌认知和态度
• 消费者洞察提炼：分析调研数据，进行用户画像和人物角色（Persona）构建，识别消费者痛点、未满足需求和潜在机会，提炼核心洞察，形成消费者洞察报告
产出：市场调研与消费者洞察报告（含市场分析、竞争分析、消费者行为研究、用户画像、核心洞察）｜ 质量标准：调研方法科学，样本具有代表性，消费者洞察深入有价值，数据支撑充分
步骤 2
营销策略制定与定位
本步骤的核心任务是制定营销策略，明确市场定位和品牌价值主张。包括STP战略（市场细分、目标市场选择、市场定位）、营销组合策略（4P/4C/4R）、品牌策略制定。

• STP战略制定：进行市场细分（地理、人口、心理、行为细分），评估各细分市场的吸引力，选择目标市场（无差异、差异化、集中营销策略），进行市场定位，提炼独特价值主张（USP）
• 营销组合策略设计：设计4P营销策略组合——产品策略（产品线、产品生命周期、新产品开发）、价格策略（定价方法、价格调整、价格策略）、渠道策略（渠道类型、渠道管理、渠道冲突）、促销策略（广告、销售促进、公共关系、人员推销）
• 品牌策略规划：制定品牌定位和品牌核心价值，设计品牌识别系统（品牌名称、标志、口号、视觉形象），规划品牌延伸和品牌组合策略，建立品牌资产管理体系
产出：营销策略方案（含STP战略、营销组合4P策略、品牌策略、营销目标与预算）｜ 质量标准：定位精准有差异化，策略系统完整，品牌价值主张清晰，目标明确可衡量
步骤 3
营销活动策划与创意执行
本步骤的核心任务是策划具体的营销活动方案，进行创意开发和执行落地。包括营销活动主题策划、创意构思、媒介计划、活动执行方案、效果预测，确保营销活动有效触达目标受众并达成营销目标。

• 营销活动策划：确定活动目标（品牌认知、用户获取、销售转化、用户留存），策划活动主题和形式（线上活动、线下活动、整合营销、事件营销、内容营销），设计活动流程和用户参与路径
• 创意开发与内容制作：进行创意概念发想（头脑风暴、思维导图），开发核心创意和传播概念，制作营销内容（广告文案、海报、视频、H5、公众号文章），确保创意符合品牌调性
• 媒介计划与投放：选择媒介渠道（传统媒体、社交媒体、搜索引擎、电商平台、KOL/KOC），制定媒介排期和投放计划，预算分配，进行媒介采购和投放执行，监测投放效果
产出：营销活动策划方案（含活动策划、创意方案、内容物料、媒介计划、执行排期、预算分配）｜ 质量标准：创意有吸引力和传播性，媒介选择精准，执行计划详细可落地，预算分配合理
步骤 4
数字营销与数据运营
本步骤的核心任务是开展数字营销活动，建立数据驱动的营销运营体系。包括搜索引擎营销（SEM/SEO）、社交媒体营销、内容营销、邮件营销、营销数据分析与优化，提升营销效果和ROI。

• 数字营销渠道运营：开展SEM搜索引擎营销（关键词广告、出价策略、质量度优化）、SEO搜索引擎优化（关键词研究、网站优化、外链建设）、社交媒体营销（微信、微博、抖音、小红书等平台内容运营和投放）
• 内容营销与用户增长：制定内容营销策略（内容主题、内容形式、内容日历），开展用户增长运营（拉新、促活、留存、转化、推荐），设计用户增长漏斗，优化各环节转化率
• 营销数据分析与优化：建立营销数据监测体系（曝光、点击、转化、ROI等指标），使用Google Analytics、百度统计等工具进行数据分析，进行A/B测试，持续优化营销策略和创意，提升营销效率
产出：数字营销运营方案（含渠道运营计划、内容营销策略、用户增长方案、数据分析体系、优化迭代机制）｜ 质量标准：数字渠道布局合理，内容质量高，数据监测体系完善，优化迭代及时有效
步骤 5
营销效果评估与优化
本步骤的核心任务是全面评估营销活动效果，总结经验并建立持续优化机制。通过建立营销效果评估体系，量化营销ROI，分析成功因素和不足，形成营销知识沉淀，指导后续营销决策。

• 营销效果评估：建立营销效果评估指标体系（品牌指标：知名度、美誉度、偏好度；业务指标：流量、转化率、客单价、复购率、ROI），对比预期目标与实际结果，进行营销ROI计算和归因分析
• 消费者反馈与品牌健康度：收集消费者反馈（评论、投诉、满意度调研），进行品牌健康度追踪（品牌认知、品牌联想、品牌忠诚、品牌感知质量），评估品牌资产变化
• 经验总结与迭代优化：总结营销活动的成功经验和失败教训，提炼可复用的方法论和最佳实践，建立营销知识库，制定下阶段营销优化计划，形成营销闭环
产出：营销效果评估报告（含效果评估结果、ROI分析、消费者反馈、经验总结、优化建议）｜ 质量标准：评估体系科学全面，ROI计算准确，归因分析合理，经验总结有价值，优化建议可行

Steps

Step 1
Market Research & Consumer Insights
The core task of this step is to conduct comprehensive market research and deeply understand target markets and consumers. Through qualitative and quantitative research methods, collect market, competition, and consumer data, analyze consumer behavior and needs.

• Market environment and competitive analysis: analyze macro market environment (PEST), market size and growth trends, market segmentation and landscape, conduct competitor analysis (direct competitors, indirect competitors, substitutes), draw competitive position matrix
• Consumer behavior research: through questionnaire surveys, in-depth interviews, focus groups, user observation and other methods, study consumers' purchase decision process, needs and preferences, usage habits, price sensitivity, brand awareness and attitudes
• Consumer insight extraction: analyze research data, build user personas and characters, identify consumer pain points, unmet needs and potential opportunities, extract core insights, form consumer insight report
Deliverable: Market research and consumer insight report (including market analysis, competitive analysis, consumer behavior research, user personas, core insights) | Quality standard: Scientific research methods, representative samples, in-depth valuable consumer insights, sufficient data support
Step 2
Marketing Strategy & Positioning
The core task of this step is to develop marketing strategy and clarify market positioning and brand value proposition. Includes STP strategy (segmentation, targeting, positioning), marketing mix strategy (4P/4C/4R), brand strategy formulation.

• STP strategy formulation: conduct market segmentation (geographic, demographic, psychographic, behavioral), evaluate attractiveness of each segment, select target market (undifferentiated, differentiated, concentrated marketing strategy), conduct market positioning, extract unique selling proposition (USP)
• Marketing mix strategy design: design 4P marketing mix - product strategy (product line, product lifecycle, new product development), pricing strategy (pricing methods, price adjustment, pricing strategy), channel strategy (channel types, channel management, channel conflict), promotion strategy (advertising, sales promotion, PR, personal selling)
• Brand strategy planning: develop brand positioning and core brand values, design brand identity system (brand name, logo, slogan, visual identity), plan brand extension and brand portfolio strategy, establish brand equity management system
Deliverable: Marketing strategy plan (including STP strategy, 4P marketing mix strategy, brand strategy, marketing objectives and budget) | Quality standard: Accurate differentiated positioning, systematic complete strategy, clear brand value proposition, measurable objectives
Step 3
Marketing Campaign Planning & Creative Execution
The core task of this step is to plan specific marketing campaign plans and carry out creative development and execution. Includes campaign theme planning, creative concept development, media plan, execution plan, effect prediction.

• Marketing campaign planning: define campaign objectives (brand awareness, user acquisition, sales conversion, user retention), plan campaign theme and format (online activities, offline activities, integrated marketing, event marketing, content marketing), design campaign flow and user participation path
• Creative development and content production: conduct creative concept brainstorming (brainstorming, mind mapping), develop core creative and communication concepts, produce marketing content (ad copy, posters, videos, H5, WeChat articles), ensure creativity aligns with brand tone
• Media plan and placement: select media channels (traditional media, social media, search engines, e-commerce platforms, KOL/KOC), develop media schedule and placement plan, budget allocation, conduct media buying and placement execution, monitor placement effects
Deliverable: Marketing campaign plan (including campaign planning, creative方案, content materials, media plan, execution schedule, budget allocation) | Quality standard: Attractive and shareable creativity, precise media selection, detailed executable plan, reasonable budget allocation
Step 4
Digital Marketing & Data Operations
The core task of this step is to carry out digital marketing activities and establish a data-driven marketing operation system. Includes search engine marketing (SEM/SEO), social media marketing, content marketing, email marketing, marketing data analysis and optimization.

• Digital marketing channel operations: conduct SEM search engine marketing (keyword advertising, bidding strategy, quality score optimization), SEO search engine optimization (keyword research, website optimization, link building), social media marketing (WeChat, Weibo, Douyin, Xiaohongshu and other platform content operations and placement)
• Content marketing and user growth: develop content marketing strategy (content topics, content formats, content calendar), conduct user growth operations (acquisition, activation, retention, conversion, referral), design user growth funnel, optimize conversion rates at each stage
• Marketing data analysis and optimization: establish marketing data monitoring system (impressions, clicks, conversions, ROI and other metrics), use tools like Google Analytics for data analysis, conduct A/B testing, continuously optimize marketing strategies and creativity to improve marketing efficiency
Deliverable: Digital marketing operation plan (including channel operation plan, content marketing strategy, user growth plan, data analysis system, optimization iteration mechanism) | Quality standard: Reasonable digital channel layout, high content quality, complete data monitoring system, timely and effective optimization iteration
Step 5
Marketing Effect Evaluation & Optimization
The core task of this step is to comprehensively evaluate marketing campaign effects, summarize experiences and establish continuous optimization mechanism. By establishing marketing effect evaluation system, quantify marketing ROI, analyze success factors and shortcomings.

• Marketing effect evaluation: establish marketing effect evaluation indicator system (brand metrics: awareness, reputation, preference; business metrics: traffic, conversion rate, average order value, repurchase rate, ROI), compare expected targets with actual results, conduct marketing ROI calculation and attribution analysis
• Consumer feedback and brand health: collect consumer feedback (reviews, complaints, satisfaction surveys), conduct brand health tracking (brand awareness, brand association, brand loyalty, perceived quality), evaluate brand equity changes
• Experience summarization and iterative optimization: summarize success experiences and failure lessons of marketing activities, extract reusable methodologies and best practices, establish marketing knowledge base, develop next phase marketing optimization plan, form marketing closed loop
Deliverable: Marketing effect evaluation report (including effect evaluation results, ROI analysis, consumer feedback, experience summarization, optimization suggestions) | Quality standard: Scientific and comprehensive evaluation system, accurate ROI calculation, reasonable attribution analysis, valuable experience summarization, feasible optimization suggestions
