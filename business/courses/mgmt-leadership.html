领导力

Leadership

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：组织行为学 | 学期：大四秋季

本课程深入探讨领导力的理论与实践，帮助学生理解有效领导的本质与核心要素。课程涵盖领导力理论演进（特质理论、行为理论、权变理论、变革型领导、服务型领导、真诚领导等）、领导权力与影响力、领导与团队建设、跨文化领导、领导力与伦理、领导发展等主题。通过领导力评估、案例研讨、角色扮演与反思练习，学生将深入认识自身领导风格，提升领导技能与影响力，为未来走上领导岗位做好准备。

This course explores leadership theory and practice in depth, helping students understand the nature and core elements of effective leadership. Topics include the evolution of leadership theory (trait theory, behavioral theory, contingency theory, transformational leadership, servant leadership, authentic leadership), leadership power and influence, leadership and team building, cross-cultural leadership, leadership and ethics, and leadership development. Through assessments, case studies, role-playing, and reflective exercises, students develop their leadership skills.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：个人领导力发展与团队领导案例分析

通过领导力评估工具认识自身领导风格，选取一位知名领导者或团队领导案例进行深度分析，制定个人领导力发展计划，并结合团队建设情境提出领导改进建议。

Use leadership assessment tools to understand personal leadership style, conduct in-depth analysis of a well-known leader or team leadership case, develop a personal leadership development plan, and propose leadership improvement suggestions.
