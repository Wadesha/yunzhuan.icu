金融学原理

Principles of Finance

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：微观经济学、基础会计 | 学期：大一秋季

本课程是金融学专业的入门基础课，系统介绍金融学的基本概念、理论框架和核心工具。课程内容涵盖货币的时间价值、风险与收益的权衡、金融市场与机构概述、利率决定机制、以及基本的投资决策方法。通过案例分析和计算练习，学生将掌握贴现现金流模型、资产估值原理等金融学核心工具，为后续专业课程学习奠定坚实基础。

This introductory course provides a comprehensive foundation in finance. It covers the time value of money, risk-return tradeoff, financial markets and institutions, interest rate determination, and basic investment decision-making. Through case studies and computational exercises, students master discounted cash flow models and asset valuation principles, building essential skills for advanced finance coursework.
