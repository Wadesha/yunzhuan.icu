审计学

Auditing

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：中级会计 | 学期：大三下

审计学课程介绍审计的基本理论、方法和实务。内容包括审计准则、审计证据、审计工作底稿、风险评估、内部控制测试、实质性程序、审计报告等。学生将学习如何计划和执行审计工作，获取充分适当的审计证据，对财务报表发表审计意见，培养专业判断能力和职业道德。

Auditing introduces basic auditing theories, methods, and practices. Topics include auditing standards, audit evidence, working papers, risk assessment, internal control testing, substantive procedures, and audit reports. Students learn to plan and perform audits, obtain sufficient appropriate audit evidence, express opinions on financial statements, and develop professional judgment and ethical awareness.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：上市公司财务报表审计案例分析

针对一家上市公司的财务报表，制定审计计划，执行风险评估、内部控制测试和实质性程序。识别重大错报风险，获取审计证据，编制审计工作底稿，最终出具审计报告。

For a listed company's financial statements, develop an audit plan, perform risk assessment, internal control testing, and substantive procedures. Identify misstatement risks, obtain audit evidence, prepare working papers, and issue an audit report.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
审计计划制定
了解被审计单位业务并制定总体审计策略
产出：审计计划书
步骤 2
风险评估
识别财务报表层次和认定层次的重大错报风险
产出：风险评估底稿
步骤 3
内控测试
测试关键内部控制的有效性
产出：内控测试底稿
步骤 4
实质性程序
执行细节测试和实质性分析程序
产出：实质性测试底稿
步骤 5
审计报告出具
评价审计结果并出具审计意见
产出：审计报告

Steps

Step 1
Audit Planning
Understand entity and develop audit strategy
Deliverable: Audit Plan
Step 2
Risk Assessment
Identify risks of material misstatement
Deliverable: Risk Working Papers
Step 3
Control Testing
Test effectiveness of key internal controls
Deliverable: Control Test Papers
Step 4
Substantive Procedures
Perform detailed tests and analytical procedures
Deliverable: Substantive Papers
Step 5
Audit Report
Evaluate results and issue audit opinion
Deliverable: Audit Report
