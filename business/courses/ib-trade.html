国际贸易

International Trade

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：国际商务导论、微观经济学 | 学期：大二秋季

本课程系统介绍国际贸易的理论与实践，涵盖古典贸易理论、新古典贸易理论、新贸易理论及当代贸易理论的发展。课程内容包括关税与非关税壁垒、贸易政策工具、区域经济一体化、WTO与多边贸易体制、贸易争端解决机制等。通过模拟贸易谈判和案例分析，学生将掌握国际贸易政策分析方法，理解全球贸易体系的运作机制。

This course systematically introduces the theory and practice of international trade, covering classical trade theory, neoclassical trade theory, new trade theory, and contemporary developments. Topics include tariffs and non-tariff barriers, trade policy instruments, regional economic integration, WTO and the multilateral trading system, and trade dispute settlement mechanisms.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：产品出口方案设计

学生选择一种具有出口潜力的中国产品，设计完整的出口方案。内容包括：目标市场选择与需求分析、出口产品定位与差异化策略、价格制定与贸易术语选择、国际物流与运输方案、支付方式与结算安排、出口报关与单证处理、风险管理与保险规划。要求学生制作商业计划书并模拟出口商与海外买家的谈判。

Students select a Chinese product with export potential and design a complete export plan. The plan includes target market selection and demand analysis, product positioning and differentiation strategy, pricing and trade term selection, international logistics and transportation, payment methods and settlement, customs declaration and documentation, risk management and insurance planning.
