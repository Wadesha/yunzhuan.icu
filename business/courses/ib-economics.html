国际经济学

International Economics

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：微观经济学、宏观经济学 | 学期：大二秋季

本课程运用经济学分析工具研究国际经济关系与全球经济运行，分为国际贸易理论与国际金融理论两大部分。国际贸易部分涵盖李嘉图模型、赫克歇尔-俄林模型、规模经济与不完全竞争贸易理论、贸易政策分析。国际金融部分包括国际收支、汇率决定理论、开放经济宏观经济学、国际宏观经济政策协调。通过理论模型推导与实证数据分析，学生将深入理解全球经济运行规律。

This course applies economic analytical tools to study international economic relations and global economic operations, divided into international trade theory and international finance theory. The trade section covers Ricardian model, Heckscher-Ohlin model, economies of scale and imperfect competition trade theory, trade policy analysis. The finance section includes balance of payments, exchange rate determination, open economy macroeconomics, and international macroeconomic policy coordination.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：中美贸易摩擦的经济影响分析

学生运用国际贸易理论与实证方法，分析中美贸易摩擦对两国及全球经济的影响。内容包括：贸易摩擦的起因与发展历程梳理、关税措施对进出口贸易额与结构的影响测算、对相关产业产出与就业的影响分析、消费者福利变化评估、对全球价值链与投资流向的影响，以及政策建议。要求学生收集真实数据，运用适当的经济学模型进行分析。

Students apply international trade theory and empirical methods to analyze the economic impact of China-US trade frictions on both countries and the global economy. The project includes causes and development of trade frictions, measurement of tariff measures' impact on trade volume and structure, analysis of impact on related industries' output and employment, consumer welfare change assessment, effects on global value chains and investment flows, and policy recommendations.
