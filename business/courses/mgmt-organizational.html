组织行为学

Organizational Behavior

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：管理学原理 | 学期：大二春季

本课程从个体、群体和组织三个层面系统研究组织中人的行为规律。课程涵盖个体行为（人格、知觉、态度、动机）、群体行为（团队动态、沟通、冲突、权力与政治）、组织系统（组织结构、组织文化、工作设计）三大模块。通过经典实验案例与互动讨论，学生将掌握理解、预测和引导组织中人的行为，提升团队管理与人际沟通能力，为有效管理人力资源提供理论指导与实践方法。

This course systematically studies human behavior in organizations at three levels: individual, group, and organizational systems. Topics include individual behavior (personality, perception, attitudes, motivation), group dynamics (team dynamics, communication, conflict, power and politics), and organizational systems (structure, culture, work design). Through case studies and interactive discussions, students develop skills in understanding, predicting, and influencing human behavior.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：组织行为问题诊断与改进方案设计

选取一家存在组织行为问题（如高离职率、团队冲突、低效沟通）的企业，从个体、群体和组织三个层面诊断问题根源，运用组织行为学理论设计改进方案。

Select an enterprise with organizational behavior issues (high turnover, team conflict, inefficient communication), diagnose root causes from individual, group, and organizational levels, and use OB theory to design an improvement plan.
