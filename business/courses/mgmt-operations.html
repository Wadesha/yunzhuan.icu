运营管理

Operations Management

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：管理学原理、统计学 | 学期：大三秋季

本课程研究企业产品与服务的生产交付系统设计、运营与优化。课程涵盖运营战略与竞争力、产品与流程设计、设施选址与布局、质量管理与控制、库存管理与供应链管理、综合计划与主生产计划、物料需求计划、项目管理、精益生产与六西格玛等核心内容。通过定量分析方法与案例研究，学生将掌握运营管理的基本工具与技术，理解如何通过高效运营创造企业价值，培养系统思维与分析解决运营问题的能力。

This course examines the design, operation, and optimization of production and service delivery systems. Topics include operations strategy and competitiveness, product and process design, facility location and layout, quality management and control, inventory and supply chain management, aggregate planning, MRP, project management, lean production, and Six Sigma. Through quantitative analysis and case studies, students develop skills in solving operational problems and creating business value through efficient operations.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：企业运营流程优化与质量管理方案

选取一家制造或服务企业，分析其运营流程，运用精益生产或六西格玛方法识别瓶颈和浪费，设计流程优化方案，并提出质量管理改进建议，量化预期效益。

Select a manufacturing or service enterprise, analyze its operations process, use lean production or six sigma methods to identify bottlenecks and waste, design a process optimization plan, propose quality management improvements, and quantify expected benefits.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

📋 示例：优化一家工厂的生产排程，比如一家电子组装厂。你需要分析瓶颈工序、计算产能利用率，用精益生产方法减少浪费，重新设计作业流程，最后测算产出提升和周期时间缩短的比例。
步骤 1
组织诊断与问题界定
本步骤的核心任务是对企业进行全面的组织诊断，明确管理问题的本质和根源。通过系统的调研和分析，识别组织在战略、结构、流程、人员、文化等方面存在的问题，为后续管理改进奠定基础。

• 进行组织现状调研：通过访谈、问卷、观察、文档分析等方法，收集组织各层面的信息，了解战略目标、组织架构、业务流程、人力资源、企业文化等现状
• 识别管理问题与痛点：分析调研数据，识别组织在效率、效益、协同、创新、人才、文化等方面存在的问题和痛点，区分症状与根源问题
• 进行根因分析：运用5Why分析法、鱼骨图（因果图）、故障树分析等工具，深入挖掘问题的根本原因，建立问题因果关系图，确定优先解决的关键问题
产出：组织诊断报告（含现状调研结果、问题识别与分析、根因分析、关键问题优先级排序）｜ 质量标准：诊断全面系统，问题识别准确，根因分析深入，优先级排序合理
步骤 2
管理方案设计与规划
本步骤的核心任务是设计管理改进方案，制定实施规划。针对诊断出的关键问题，设计具体的解决方案，包括组织架构调整、流程优化、制度建设、人才发展等，制定详细的实施路线图。

• 设计管理改进方案：针对关键问题设计多套备选方案，包括组织设计（部门设置、权责划分、汇报关系）、流程优化（端到端流程再造、审批简化）、制度建设、绩效管理体系设计等
• 方案评估与选择：建立方案评估标准（效果、成本、风险、可行性、接受度），使用决策矩阵或成本效益分析对备选方案进行评估，选择最优方案组合
• 制定实施规划：将方案分解为具体的工作包和任务，制定实施路线图和时间表，明确里程碑、责任人、资源需求，制定沟通计划和变革管理策略
产出：管理改进方案与实施规划（含方案设计、方案评估与选择、实施路线图、资源配置计划）｜ 质量标准：方案设计针对性强，方案评估科学客观，实施规划详细可行
步骤 3
组织变革与实施推动
本步骤的核心任务是推动管理方案的落地实施，进行有效的变革管理。包括组建项目团队、开展变革动员、分阶段实施、过程监控与调整，确保方案顺利落地并达到预期效果。

• 组建变革团队与动员：成立变革领导小组和项目实施小组，明确角色职责，进行变革动员和宣贯，赢得高层支持和员工认同，管理利益相关者期望
• 分阶段实施：按照实施路线图分阶段推进方案落地，包括试点阶段（选择试点单位/部门，验证方案可行性）、推广阶段（全面铺开，分批实施）、深化阶段（持续优化完善）
• 过程监控与调整：建立项目监控机制，定期跟踪进度、质量、成本和效果，识别实施中的问题和阻力，及时调整方案和实施策略，确保变革目标达成
产出：组织变革实施记录（含变革团队组建、试点与推广记录、过程监控报告、问题与调整记录）｜ 质量标准：变革推动有力，试点验证充分，过程监控到位，问题响应及时
步骤 4
绩效管理与人才发展
本步骤的核心任务是建立绩效管理体系和人才发展机制，确保组织能力的持续提升。包括设计绩效考核体系、建立人才发展通道、制定培训发展计划、构建激励机制。

• 设计绩效管理体系：建立KPI关键绩效指标体系（平衡计分卡BSC四个维度），设计绩效考核流程和方法（目标管理MBO、OKR目标与关键成果、360度评估），建立绩效反馈和改进机制
• 构建人才发展体系：进行人才盘点和胜任力建模，建立任职资格体系和职业发展通道（管理通道、专业通道），设计人才梯队建设和继任者计划
• 制定培训与激励方案：设计分层分类的培训发展计划（新员工培训、管理培训、专业技能培训），建立薪酬激励体系（固定薪酬+浮动薪酬+长期激励）和非物质激励机制
产出：绩效与人才发展方案（含绩效管理体系、人才盘点与发展通道、培训发展计划、薪酬激励方案）｜ 质量标准：绩效体系科学合理，人才发展通道清晰，培训激励有针对性
步骤 5
效果评估与持续改进
本步骤的核心任务是评估管理改进的效果，建立持续改进机制。通过定量和定性指标评估方案实施效果，总结经验教训，建立持续改进的闭环管理机制，推动组织能力的螺旋式上升。

• 效果评估：建立评估指标体系（财务指标、运营指标、客户指标、员工指标），对比实施前后的变化，评估目标达成度，进行成本效益分析，识别成功因素和不足
• 经验总结与知识沉淀：总结项目的成功经验和失败教训，提炼可复制的方法论和工具，编写最佳实践案例，建立组织记忆和知识库
• 建立持续改进机制：建立PDCA循环改进机制（计划-执行-检查-处理），设立改进提案制度和精益管理体系，定期进行组织复盘和战略回顾，推动持续学习和创新
产出：效果评估与持续改进报告（含效果评估结果、经验总结、最佳实践、持续改进机制设计）｜ 质量标准：效果评估全面客观，经验总结深入有价值，持续改进机制完善

Steps

Step 1
Organizational Diagnosis & Problem Definition
The core task of this step is to conduct comprehensive organizational diagnosis and clarify the nature and root causes of management problems. Through systematic research and analysis, identify organizational issues in strategy, structure, processes, people, and culture.

• Conduct organizational status research: collect information at all organizational levels through interviews, questionnaires, observation, and document analysis, understand strategic goals, organizational structure, business processes, human resources, and corporate culture status
• Identify management problems and pain points: analyze research data, identify problems and pain points in efficiency, effectiveness, collaboration, innovation, talent, and culture, distinguish symptoms from root cause problems
• Conduct root cause analysis: use tools like 5-Why analysis, fishbone diagram (cause-and-effect diagram), fault tree analysis to deeply explore root causes of problems, establish problem cause-effect relationship map, determine key priority problems to solve
Deliverable: Organizational diagnosis report (including status research results, problem identification and analysis, root cause analysis, key problem priority ranking) | Quality standard: Comprehensive systematic diagnosis, accurate problem identification, in-depth root cause analysis, reasonable priority ranking
Step 2
Management Solution Design & Planning
The core task of this step is to design management improvement solutions and develop implementation plans. Address diagnosed key problems with specific solutions, including organizational structure adjustment, process optimization, system building, talent development, etc.

• Design management improvement solutions: develop multiple alternative solutions for key problems, including organizational design (department setup, authority-responsibility division, reporting relationships), process optimization (end-to-end process reengineering, approval simplification), system building, performance management system design
• Solution evaluation and selection: establish solution evaluation criteria (effectiveness, cost, risk, feasibility, acceptance), evaluate alternative solutions using decision matrix or cost-benefit analysis, select optimal solution combination
• Develop implementation plan: decompose solutions into specific work packages and tasks, develop implementation roadmap and timeline, clarify milestones, responsible persons, resource requirements, develop communication plan and change management strategy
Deliverable: Management improvement solution and implementation plan (including solution design, solution evaluation and selection, implementation roadmap, resource allocation plan) | Quality standard: Highly targeted solution design, scientific and objective solution evaluation, detailed and feasible implementation plan
Step 3
Organizational Change & Implementation
The core task of this step is to drive implementation of management solutions and conduct effective change management. Includes forming project teams, conducting change mobilization, phased implementation, process monitoring and adjustment.

• Form change team and mobilize: establish change leadership group and project implementation team, clarify roles and responsibilities, conduct change mobilization and communication, win senior management support and employee recognition, manage stakeholder expectations
• Phased implementation: advance solution implementation in phases according to roadmap, including pilot phase (select pilot units/departments, verify solution feasibility), rollout phase (full rollout, batch implementation), deepening phase (continuous optimization and improvement)
• Process monitoring and adjustment: establish project monitoring mechanism, regularly track progress, quality, cost and effects, identify implementation problems and resistance, timely adjust solutions and implementation strategies to ensure change goals are achieved
Deliverable: Organizational change implementation records (including change team formation, pilot and rollout records, process monitoring report, problems and adjustment records) | Quality standard: Strong change driving, sufficient pilot verification, effective process monitoring, timely problem response
Step 4
Performance Management & Talent Development
The core task of this step is to establish performance management system and talent development mechanism to ensure continuous improvement of organizational capabilities. Includes designing performance appraisal system, establishing talent development paths, developing training plans.

• Design performance management system: establish KPI system (Balanced Scorecard four dimensions), design performance appraisal processes and methods (MBO, OKR, 360-degree evaluation), establish performance feedback and improvement mechanism
• Build talent development system: conduct talent inventory and competency modeling, establish qualification system and career development paths (management track, professional track), design talent echelon building and succession planning
• Develop training and incentive schemes: design layered categorized training and development plans (new employee training, management training, professional skill training), establish compensation incentive system (fixed + variable + long-term incentives) and non-material incentive mechanisms
Deliverable: Performance and talent development solution (including performance management system, talent inventory and development paths, training development plan, compensation incentive scheme) | Quality standard: Scientific and reasonable performance system, clear talent development paths, targeted training and incentives
Step 5
Effect Evaluation & Continuous Improvement
The core task of this step is to evaluate effects of management improvements and establish continuous improvement mechanism. Evaluate implementation effects through quantitative and qualitative indicators, summarize lessons learned, establish closed-loop continuous improvement mechanism.

• Effect evaluation: establish evaluation indicator system (financial indicators, operational indicators, customer indicators, employee indicators), compare changes before and after implementation, evaluate goal achievement, conduct cost-benefit analysis, identify success factors and shortcomings
• Experience summarization and knowledge management: extract replicable methodologies and tools from success experiences and failure lessons, write best practice cases, establish organizational memory and knowledge base
• Establish continuous improvement mechanism: establish PDCA cycle improvement mechanism (Plan-Do-Check-Act), set up improvement proposal system and lean management system, regularly conduct organizational reviews and strategy retrospectives, drive continuous learning and innovation
Deliverable: Effect evaluation and continuous improvement report (including effect evaluation results, experience summarization, best practices, continuous improvement mechanism design) | Quality standard: Comprehensive and objective effect evaluation, in-depth valuable experience summarization, complete continuous improvement mechanism
