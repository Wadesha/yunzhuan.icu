人力资源管理

Human Resource Management

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：组织行为学 | 学期：大三春季

本课程系统介绍人力资源管理的理论体系与实践方法，探讨如何通过有效的人力资源管理获取并保持企业竞争优势。课程涵盖人力资源规划、工作分析与设计、招聘与甄选、培训与开发、绩效管理、薪酬与福利、员工关系管理、人力资源法律等核心模块。通过真实企业案例分析与模拟演练，学生将掌握人力资源各职能模块的操作方法，理解战略人力资源管理理念，培养人力资源管理的专业能力与战略思维。

This course provides a systematic introduction to the theoretical framework and practical methods of human resource management, exploring how effective HR management creates and sustains competitive advantage. Topics include HR planning, job analysis and design, recruitment and selection, training and development, performance management, compensation and benefits, employee relations, and HR law. Through case studies and simulations, students develop professional HR capabilities and strategic thinking.
