变革管理

Change Management

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：战略管理、组织行为学 | 学期：大四春季

本课程研究组织变革的驱动因素、过程管理与成功实施策略。课程涵盖变革的类型与动因、变革阻力管理、变革管理理论模型（科特八步法、ADKAR模型、行动研究等）、变革领导与沟通、组织文化变革、数字化转型、并购整合中的变革管理、变革能力建设等核心内容。通过真实变革案例深度分析与模拟演练，学生将掌握诊断组织变革需求、设计变革方案、管理变革过程、巩固变革成果的系统方法与实操技能。

This course examines the drivers, process management, and successful implementation strategies of organizational change. Topics include types and drivers of change, resistance management, change management models (Kotter's Eight-Step, ADKAR, action research), change leadership and communication, organizational culture change, digital transformation, change management in mergers and acquisitions, and change capability building. Through case studies and simulations, students develop systematic approaches to managing organizational change.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：企业组织变革方案设计与实施规划

针对一家面临数字化转型或并购整合的企业，诊断变革需求，运用科特八步法或ADKAR模型设计变革方案，分析变革阻力并制定沟通策略，规划变革实施路径。

For an enterprise facing digital transformation or M&A integration, diagnose change needs, use Kotter's 8-step or ADKAR model to design a change plan, analyze resistance and develop communication strategies, and plan the implementation path.
