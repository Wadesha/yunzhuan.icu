国际商务导论

Introduction to International Business

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：大一春季

本课程是国际商务专业的入门基础课，全面介绍国际商务的基本概念、理论框架与实践领域。课程涵盖全球化与国际商务环境、国际贸易与投资理论、跨国公司战略、国际市场进入模式、跨文化管理等核心内容。通过真实案例分析，学生将理解企业在国际市场上面临的机遇与挑战，培养全球视野与跨文化思维能力，为后续专业课程学习奠定基础。

This introductory course provides a comprehensive overview of fundamental concepts, theoretical frameworks, and practical areas of international business. Topics include globalization and the international business environment, international trade and investment theories, multinational corporation strategy, market entry modes, and cross-cultural management. Through real-world case studies, students develop a global perspective and cross-cultural thinking skills.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：跨国公司进入新市场分析报告

学生选择一家知名跨国企业，分析其进入某一新兴市场的战略决策。报告内容包括：目标国家商业环境评估（政治、经济、文化、法律）、行业竞争格局分析、市场进入模式比较与选择、本土化策略建议，以及潜在风险与应对措施。期末进行小组汇报，模拟向公司董事会提交战略建议。

Students select a well-known multinational enterprise and analyze its strategic decision to enter an emerging market. The report includes assessment of the target country's business environment (political, economic, cultural, legal), industry competitive landscape, comparison and selection of market entry modes, localization strategy recommendations, and potential risks with countermeasures.
