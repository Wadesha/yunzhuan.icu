商业分析

Business Analytics

中文介绍

商业分析是一门将数据分析和商业知识相结合的学科，旨在利用数据来支持商业决策。它涵盖了数据挖掘、预测分析、商业智能、决策模型等多个方向。商业分析专业的课程包括统计学、数据分析工具、商业战略、运营管理等。毕业生在科技公司、金融机构、咨询公司等都有广泛的就业机会。商业分析是近年来最热门的商科专业之一，岗位需求持续增长。

English Introduction

Business Analytics combines data analysis with business knowledge to support business decisions using data. It covers data mining, predictive analytics, business intelligence, decision modeling, and more. Coursework includes statistics, data analysis tools, business strategy, and operations management. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in tech companies, financial institutions, and consulting firms. Business Analytics is one of the most popular business majors in recent years, with continued job demand growth.
