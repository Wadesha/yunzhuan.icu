会计学

Accounting

中文介绍

会计学是一门研究经济活动的记录、分类、汇总和报告的学科。它涵盖了财务会计、管理会计、审计、税务等多个方向。会计学专业的课程包括财务报表分析、成本会计、审计学、税法、会计信息系统等。毕业生在会计师事务所、企业财务部门、政府机构等都有广泛的就业机会。会计学是商科中最稳定的专业之一，就业前景良好。

English Introduction

Accounting is the study of recording, classifying, summarizing, and reporting economic activities. It covers financial accounting, management accounting, auditing, taxation, and more. Coursework includes financial statement analysis, cost accounting, auditing, tax law, and accounting information systems. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in accounting firms, corporate finance departments, and government agencies. Accounting is one of the most stable business majors with strong employment prospects.
