供应链管理

Supply Chain Management

中文介绍

供应链管理是一门研究如何优化产品从原材料到最终消费者的流动过程的学科。它涵盖了采购管理、物流管理、库存管理、需求预测等多个方向。供应链管理专业的课程包括运营管理、物流管理、供应链战略、数据分析、风险管理等。毕业生在制造业、零售业、物流企业、电商平台等都有广泛的就业机会。随着全球供应链的复杂化，供应链管理专业的需求日益增加。

English Introduction

Supply Chain Management is the study of how to optimize the flow of products from raw materials to end consumers. It covers procurement management, logistics management, inventory management, demand forecasting, and more. Coursework includes operations management, logistics management, supply chain strategy, data analysis, and risk management. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in manufacturing, retail, logistics companies, and e-commerce platforms. With the complexity of global supply chains, demand for supply chain management continues to increase.
