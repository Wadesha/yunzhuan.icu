💰 金融学

Finance

中文介绍

金融学是一门研究资金的流动和管理的学科。它涵盖了投资分析、风险管理、金融市场、金融机构等多个方向。金融学专业的课程包括会计学、经济学、统计学、投资学、公司金融等。毕业生在银行、证券公司、基金公司、保险公司等金融机构有广泛的就业机会。金融学是最热门的商科专业之一，根据NCES数据，商科专业的学位授予量占所有学位的18.6%。

English Introduction

Finance is the study of the flow and management of funds. It covers investment analysis, risk management, financial markets, financial institutions, and more. Coursework includes accounting, economics, statistics, investments, and corporate finance. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in banks, securities firms, fund companies, insurance companies, and other financial institutions. Finance is one of the most popular business majors, with business degrees accounting for 18.6% of all degrees awarded according to NCES.
