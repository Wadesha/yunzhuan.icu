国际商务

International Business

中文介绍

国际商务是一门研究跨国商业活动和全球市场的学科。它涵盖了国际贸易、国际金融、国际市场营销、跨文化管理等多个方向。国际商务专业的课程包括国际贸易理论、国际商务环境、国际金融、跨文化沟通、全球供应链管理等。毕业生在跨国公司、国际贸易公司、国际组织、政府部门等都有广泛的就业机会。随着全球化的发展，国际商务专业的需求持续增长。

English Introduction

International Business is the study of cross-border business activities and global markets. It covers international trade, international finance, international marketing, cross-cultural management, and more. Coursework includes international trade theory, international business environment, international finance, cross-cultural communication, and global supply chain management. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in multinational corporations, international trade companies, international organizations, and government. With globalization, demand for international business continues to grow.
