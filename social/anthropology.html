人类学

Anthropology

中文介绍

人类学是一门研究人类文化和社会多样性的学科。它涵盖了文化人类学、体质人类学、考古学、语言人类学等多个方向。人类学专业的课程包括人类学导论、文化人类学、考古学方法、人类进化、语言人类学等。毕业生在博物馆、非营利组织、国际组织、研究机构等都有广泛的就业机会。人类学专业培养学生的跨文化理解能力和全球视野。

Anthropology is the study of human cultural and social diversity. It covers cultural anthropology, physical anthropology, archaeology, linguistic anthropology, and more. Coursework includes introduction to anthropology, cultural anthropology, archaeological methods, human evolution, and linguistic anthropology. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in museums, non-profit organizations, international organizations, and research institutions. Anthropology develops students' cross-cultural understanding and global perspective.
