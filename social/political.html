️ 政治学

Political Science

中文介绍

政治学是一门研究政治系统、政治行为和政治理论的学科。它涵盖了比较政治学、国际关系、公共政策、政治哲学、政治经济学等多个方向。政治学专业的课程包括政治学导论、比较政治学、国际关系、政治理论、公共政策等。毕业生在政府部门、国际组织、咨询公司、新闻媒体等都有广泛的就业机会。政治学专业培养学生的分析能力和批判性思维。

English Introduction

Political Science is the study of political systems, political behavior, and political theory. It covers comparative politics, international relations, public policy, political philosophy, political economy, and more. Coursework includes introduction to political science, comparative politics, international relations, political theory, and public policy. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in government, international organizations, consulting firms, and news media. Political science develops students' analytical and critical thinking skills.
