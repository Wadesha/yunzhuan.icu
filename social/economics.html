经济学

Economics

中文介绍

经济学是一门研究资源配置和经济行为的学科。它涵盖了宏观经济学、微观经济学、计量经济学、发展经济学、国际经济学等多个方向。经济学专业的课程包括微观经济学、宏观经济学、统计学、数学、计量经济学等。毕业生在政府部门、金融机构、咨询公司、研究机构等都有广泛的就业机会。经济学是社会科学中最热门的专业之一，具有良好的就业前景。

English Introduction

Economics is the study of resource allocation and economic behavior. It covers macroeconomics, microeconomics, econometrics, development economics, international economics, and more. Coursework includes microeconomics, macroeconomics, statistics, mathematics, and econometrics. Graduates have extensive employment opportunities in government, financial institutions, consulting firms, and research institutions. Economics is one of the most popular social science majors with strong employment prospects.
