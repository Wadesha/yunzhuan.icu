世界近现代史

Modern World History

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：第1-2学期

本课程讲授从文艺复兴到当代世界的历史发展，涵盖政治革命、工业革命、殖民扩张、世界大战、冷战格局及全球化进程。学生将了解主要文明的演进脉络，掌握近现代世界的基本历史线索，理解当今国际格局的历史根源，培养历史思维和全球视野。

This course examines the development of world history from the Renaissance to the present, covering political revolutions, industrialization, colonial expansion, world wars, the Cold War, and globalization. Students will explore the evolution of major civilizations, grasp the key narratives of modern world history, understand the historical roots of contemporary international relations, and develop historical thinking and a global perspective.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：近现代重大历史事件专题研究

学生自选一个近现代世界史上的重大事件或议题（如法国大革命、工业革命的社会影响、非殖民化运动等），利用中英文史料和学术文献进行深入研究，撰写一篇约3000字的研究论文，要求有明确的论点、充分的证据支撑和规范的注释格式。

Students choose a major event or theme in modern world history (such as the French Revolution, social impacts of industrialization, or decolonization movements), conduct in-depth research using primary and secondary sources in both Chinese and English, and write a 3000-word research paper with a clear thesis, supporting evidence, and proper citation format.
