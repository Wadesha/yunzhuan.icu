语言人类学

Linguistic Anthropology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：人类学导论 | 学期：秋季

本课程探讨语言与文化的关系，研究语言如何塑造社会生活和文化认同。内容包括语言人类学的基本理论、语言相对论、语言与身份建构、语言与权力、言语民族志、语言消亡与语言复兴等议题。学生将学习如何运用语言学方法和人类学视角分析日常话语实践，理解语言在文化传承、社会分层和身份政治中的重要作用。

This course explores the relationship between language and culture, examining how language shapes social life and cultural identity. Topics include fundamental theories of linguistic anthropology, linguistic relativity, language and identity construction, language and power, ethnography of speaking, language endangerment and revitalization. Students learn to apply linguistic methods and anthropological perspectives to analyze everyday discourse practices.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：言语社区话语实践民族志研究

选择一个言语社区，通过录音、观察与访谈收集话语实践资料。运用语言人类学理论分析语言使用与身份、权力、文化认同的关系，撰写话语民族志研究报告。

Select a speech community and collect discourse data through recording, observation, and interviews. Apply linguistic anthropology theory to analyze the relationship between language use and identity, power, and cultural belonging, and write a discourse ethnography report.
