全球史

Global History

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：世界近现代史 | 学期：第5学期

本课程从全球视角考察人类历史的发展，突破传统民族国家的叙事框架，关注跨文化交流、贸易网络、移民流动、疾病传播、技术扩散、宗教传播等全球性议题。课程将探讨古代丝绸之路、大航海时代、殖民体系、全球化进程等主题，引导学生理解不同文明之间的相互联系与互动，培养全球性的历史思维。

This course examines human history from a global perspective, moving beyond traditional national narratives to focus on cross-cultural exchanges, trade networks, migration, disease transmission, technological diffusion, and religious spread. Topics include the ancient Silk Road, the Age of Exploration, colonial systems, and globalization processes, guiding students to understand interconnections and interactions among civilizations and develop a global historical perspective.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：跨文明交流与全球联系史研究

选择一个全球史主题（如丝绸之路、大航海时代、殖民贸易或移民流动），考察不同文明间的联系与互动。运用全球史视角呈现跨区域的历史进程，撰写全球史研究论文。

Choose a global history theme (e.g., Silk Road, Age of Discovery, colonial trade, or migration) and examine connections and interactions between civilizations. Apply a global history perspective to present cross-regional historical processes, and write a global history research paper.

实施步骤 Implementation Steps

步骤 1
研究设计与全球史问题凝练
本步骤的核心任务是确定具体的全球联系与交流史研究，并基于文献回顾凝练出有学术价值的研究问题。研究设计是全球史研究的起点，需要明确研究对象、核心问题、理论框架和研究方法。通过系统的初步探索，确保研究问题既有理论意义又有可行性。

• 梳理全球史相关理论与研究现状，通过JSTOR、Google Scholar等数据库检索近十年核心文献20篇以上，识别研究空白与争议点
• 从跨国史、比较史、网络分析、长时段研究等方法中选择适合本研究的方法路径，确定研究对象与分析单位，凝练1个核心研究问题和2-3个子问题
• 撰写1500字的研究设计书，包括研究背景、问题与假设、理论框架、方法选择、伦理考量、时间安排，提交导师审核
产出：研究设计书（含研究问题、理论框架、方法设计、伦理审查表、参考文献20篇）｜ 质量标准：研究问题具体聚焦，理论框架清晰，方法选择合适，伦理考量周全
步骤 2
资料收集与全球史证据获取
本步骤的核心任务是按照研究设计系统地收集研究所需的资料和证据。全球史研究强调证据的多样性和可靠性，需要运用多种方法获取不同来源的资料。资料收集过程需要严格遵循研究规范，确保资料的真实性、完整性和可追溯性。

• 运用跨国史、比较史、网络分析、长时段研究等方法，系统收集一手或二手资料，建立研究资料库，确保资料来源可靠、记录完整
• 使用全球史料数据库、GIS、网络分析工具等工具对资料进行初步整理和分类，建立资料索引系统，便于后续检索和分析
• 进行资料质量评估：核实资料来源、检查完整性、标注可信度等级，剔除不可靠或不相关的资料，确保研究证据的质量
产出：研究资料集（含原始资料、整理后的分类资料、资料索引表、质量评估报告）｜ 质量标准：资料收集系统全面，来源可靠，分类清晰，质量经过严格评估
步骤 3
全球史分析与理论对话
本步骤是研究的核心环节，运用全球史的理论与方法对资料进行深入分析，并与既有理论进行对话。分析过程需要保持理论与经验证据的紧密结合，既要从资料中提炼新的洞见，又要将研究发现置于学术传统中进行定位。通过分析与对话，形成本研究的核心论点。

• 运用全球史的核心概念和分析框架，对资料进行系统编码和深度分析，提炼出10-15个核心分析范畴
• 将分析结果与2-3个经典理论或当代理论进行对话，比较异同，验证或修正既有理论，提出本研究的独特发现
• 构建研究的整体论证逻辑，确定3-4个核心分析章节，撰写每章详细大纲，确保论点有充分的证据支撑
产出：分析报告（含编码体系、理论对话、章节大纲、核心论点提炼）｜ 质量标准：分析深入系统，理论对话有深度，核心论点有充分证据支撑
步骤 4
综合阐释与全球史意义提炼
本步骤的核心任务是将各部分分析进行综合，提炼出研究的整体意义和学术贡献。综合阐释不是分析结果的简单堆砌，而是要在更高层次上进行理论提升，回答"所以呢"（so what）的问题。需要明确指出本研究在理论、方法或实践层面的贡献和价值。

• 从理论层面综合各分析章节的发现，构建整体性的解释框架，回答核心研究问题，阐明本研究的理论贡献
• 从方法论层面反思研究设计与实施的得失，讨论研究方法的优势与局限，为同类研究提供方法参考
• 从实践层面探讨研究发现的现实意义，提出1-2条具有启发性的政策建议或实践启示，拓展研究的应用价值
产出：综合阐释章节（含理论贡献、方法论反思、实践意义、研究局限与未来方向）｜ 质量标准：综合阐释有理论高度，贡献明确，反思深刻，现实意义清晰
步骤 5
学术论文撰写与全球史规范
本步骤的核心任务是将研究成果撰写为一篇符合全球史学术规范的研究论文。论文需要结构完整、逻辑严密、语言精炼、引用规范。从摘要到结论，每个部分都要精心打磨，确保研究成果得到最佳呈现。最终论文应达到可投稿学术期刊的水平。

• 按照标准学术论文结构撰写：摘要（300字）、关键词（3-5个）、引言（1500字）、文献综述（1500字）、研究方法（1000字）、分析章节（4000字）、结论与讨论（1500字）、参考文献，全文不少于10000字
• 统一引用格式（芝加哥注释格式或APA第7版），确保所有引用准确无误，参考文献不少于30篇，格式完全规范
• 进行三轮修改：第一轮调整结构与论证，第二轮润色语言与表达，第三轮校对格式与引文，确保论文达到学术发表水平
产出：完整的全球史研究论文（约10000-15000字，结构完整，引用规范）｜ 质量标准：论文结构完整、逻辑严密、论证充分、引用规范，达到本科优秀课程论文水平

Steps

Step 1
Research Design and Global History Question Formulation
The core task of this step is to determine the specific global connections and exchange history research and formulate academically valuable research questions based on literature review. Research design is the starting point of Global History research, requiring clear research subjects, core questions, theoretical frameworks, and research methods. Systematic preliminary exploration ensures research questions have both theoretical significance and feasibility.

• Review relevant Global History theories and research status, search 20+ core articles from recent decade through databases like JSTOR and Google Scholar, identify research gaps and controversies
• Select appropriate methodological approach from transnational history, comparative history, network analysis, longue durée, determine research subjects and units of analysis, formulate 1 core research question and 2-3 sub-questions
• Write 1500-word research design including background, questions and hypotheses, theoretical framework, methodology, ethical considerations, timeline, submit to advisor for review
Deliverable: Research design (including research questions, theoretical framework, method design, ethics review form, 20 references) | Quality standard: Research questions are specific and focused, theoretical framework is clear, method selection is appropriate, ethical considerations are comprehensive
Step 2
Data Collection and Global History Evidence Gathering
The core task of this step is to systematically collect data and evidence required for the study according to the research design. Global History research emphasizes diversity and reliability of evidence, requiring multiple methods to obtain data from different sources. Data collection must strictly follow research norms to ensure authenticity, completeness, and traceability of data.

• Use methods including transnational history, comparative history, network analysis, longue durée to systematically collect primary or secondary data, build research database, ensuring reliable sources and complete records
• Use tools including global historical databases, GIS, network analysis tools for preliminary organization and classification of data, establish a data indexing system for subsequent retrieval and analysis
• Conduct data quality assessment: verify data sources, check completeness, annotate credibility levels, exclude unreliable or irrelevant data, ensuring quality of research evidence
Deliverable: Research dataset (including raw data, organized classified data, data index table, quality assessment report) | Quality standard: Data collection is systematic and comprehensive, sources are reliable, classification is clear, quality has been rigorously assessed
Step 3
Global History Analysis and Theoretical Dialogue
This step is the core of the research, using Global History theories and methods to conduct in-depth analysis of data and engage in dialogue with existing theories. The analysis process requires close integration of theory and empirical evidence—extracting new insights from data while positioning research findings within academic tradition. Core arguments of the study are formed through analysis and dialogue.

• Apply core concepts and analytical frameworks of Global History for systematic coding and in-depth analysis of data, extracting 10-15 core analytical categories
• Engage analytical results in dialogue with 2-3 classical or contemporary theories, compare similarities and differences, verify or revise existing theories, propose unique findings of this study
• Construct overall argumentative logic of the study, determine 3-4 core analytical chapters, write detailed outlines for each chapter, ensuring arguments are supported by sufficient evidence
Deliverable: Analysis report (including coding system, theoretical dialogue, chapter outlines, core argument extraction) | Quality standard: Analysis is in-depth and systematic, theoretical dialogue has depth, core arguments are supported by sufficient evidence
Step 4
Synthetic Interpretation and Global History Significance Extraction
The core task of this step is to synthesize all analytical components and extract the overall significance and academic contribution of the study. Synthetic interpretation is not simple accumulation of analytical results but theoretical elevation at a higher level, answering the "so what" question. The study's contributions and value at theoretical, methodological, or practical levels must be clearly articulated.

• Synthesize findings from analytical chapters at theoretical level, construct holistic explanatory framework, answer core research questions, clarify theoretical contributions of this study
• Reflect on gains and losses of research design and implementation at methodological level, discuss advantages and limitations of research methods, provide methodological reference for similar studies
• Explore practical significance of research findings at practical level, propose 1-2 inspiring policy recommendations or practical implications, expanding applied value of the study
Deliverable: Synthetic interpretation chapter (including theoretical contributions, methodological reflections, practical significance, research limitations and future directions) | Quality standard: Synthetic interpretation has theoretical height, clear contributions, profound reflection, clear practical significance
Step 5
Academic Paper Writing and Global History Standards
The core task of this step is to write research findings into a research paper conforming to Global History academic standards. The paper must have complete structure, rigorous logic, refined language, and proper citations. From abstract to conclusion, each section must be carefully polished to ensure optimal presentation of research findings. The final paper should reach the level of submission to academic journals.

• Write according to standard academic paper structure: abstract (300 words), keywords (3-5), introduction (1500 words), literature review (1500 words), research methods (1000 words), analysis chapters (4000 words), conclusion and discussion (1500 words), references, total text no less than 10,000 words
• Unify citation format (Chicago Notes or APA 7th edition), ensure all citations are accurate, reference list of no fewer than 30 entries with fully standardized format
• Conduct three rounds of revision: first round adjusts structure and argumentation, second polishes language and expression, third proofreads format and citations, ensuring paper reaches academic publication standard
Deliverable: Complete Global History research paper (approximately 10,000-15,000 words, complete structure, proper citations) | Quality standard: Paper has complete structure, rigorous logic, sufficient argumentation, proper citations, reaching excellent undergraduate course paper level
