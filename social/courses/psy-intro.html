心理学导论

Introduction to Psychology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：大一上

本课程是心理学专业的入门课程，系统介绍心理学的基本概念、理论流派和研究方法。内容涵盖生物学基础、感觉与知觉、学习与记忆、思维与智力、发展心理学、人格理论、心理障碍与治疗、社会心理学等核心领域。通过本课程的学习，学生将对心理学学科有全面的认识，为后续专业课程学习奠定基础。

This introductory course provides a comprehensive overview of psychology as a scientific discipline. Topics include biological foundations, sensation and perception, learning and memory, thinking and intelligence, developmental psychology, personality theories, psychological disorders and therapy, and social psychology. Students will gain a broad understanding of major psychological perspectives and research methods.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：个人心理学应用分析报告

学生选择一个日常生活中的心理学现象（如拖延行为、社交媒体使用、第一印象形成等），运用课程中学到的至少三个心理学理论或概念进行分析。报告需包含现象描述、理论应用、实证研究支持和个人反思，字数约2000字。

Students select a psychological phenomenon in daily life (e.g., procrastination, social media use, first impression formation) and analyze it using at least three psychological theories or concepts learned in the course. The report includes phenomenon description, theoretical application, empirical research support, and personal reflection, approximately 2000 words.
