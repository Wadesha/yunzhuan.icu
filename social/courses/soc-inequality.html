社会不平等

Social Inequality

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：社会学导论 | 学期：春季

本课程深入探讨社会不平等的形式、成因与后果。课程从阶级、阶层、性别、种族、教育等多重维度出发，分析不平等的产生机制与再生产逻辑，考察社会流动的可能性与限制。内容涵盖分层理论、贫困与福利、劳动力市场分割、教育不平等、代际传递等议题，引导学生理解不平等如何塑造个体生活机会与社会结构。

This course examines the forms, causes, and consequences of social inequality. It analyzes mechanisms of inequality and reproduction through multiple dimensions: class, gender, race, and education. Topics include stratification theory, poverty and welfare, labor market segmentation, educational inequality, and intergenerational mobility, exploring how inequality shapes life chances and social structure.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：社会不平等维度实证研究

选择一种社会不平等维度（阶级、性别、种族或教育），运用调查数据或案例进行实证分析。结合分层理论探讨不平等的产生与再生产机制，撰写社会不平等研究论文。

Choose a dimension of social inequality (class, gender, race, or education) and conduct empirical analysis using survey data or cases. Apply stratification theory to explore mechanisms of inequality production and reproduction, and write a social inequality research paper.
