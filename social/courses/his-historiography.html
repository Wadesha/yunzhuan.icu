史学理论与方法

Historiography and Historical Methods

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：世界近现代史 | 学期：第4学期

本课程介绍历史学的学科性质、主要理论流派与研究方法。内容包括历史哲学的基本问题、中国传统史学与西方史学的发展脉络、近现代史学流派（如实证主义史学、年鉴学派、马克思主义史学、新文化史、后现代史学等），以及史料学、史料考证、叙事分析等基本研究方法。学生将培养史学理论素养和批判性思维能力。

This course introduces the nature of history as a discipline, major theoretical schools, and research methods. Topics include fundamental questions of historical philosophy, the development of Chinese traditional historiography and Western historiography, modern historiographical schools (such as positivism, the Annales School, Marxist historiography, new cultural history, and postmodern history), as well as basic research methods including source criticism and narrative analysis.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：史学流派比较与史料考证实践

选择两个史学流派进行比较研究，并对一组具体史料进行考证实践。结合理论反思与方法应用，撰写史学理论与方法研究论文。

Compare two historiographical schools and conduct source criticism on a set of historical sources. Combine theoretical reflection with methodological application, and write a paper on historiographical theory and method.
