政治理论

Political Theory

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：政治学导论 | 学期：春季

本课程系统考察从古希腊到当代的西方政治思想传统，研读柏拉图、亚里士多德、霍布斯、洛克、卢梭、马克思等思想家的经典著作。课程探讨正义、自由、平等、权力、民主等核心政治概念的历史演变，以及不同思想流派之间的争论与对话。学生将通过深度阅读和写作训练，提升文本解读能力和理论思辨水平。

This course systematically examines the Western political thought tradition from ancient Greece to the present. Students read classic works by Plato, Aristotle, Hobbes, Locke, Rousseau, Marx, and other major thinkers. The course explores the historical evolution of core political concepts such as justice, liberty, equality, power, and democracy, as well as debates between different intellectual traditions. Through close reading and writing exercises, students develop textual interpretation skills and theoretical reasoning abilities.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：经典文本诠释与当代议题论文

学生选择一位政治思想家的核心文本，进行深入解读，并将其理论观点与一个当代政治议题（如分配正义、公民不服从、科技伦理等）相结合，撰写一篇研究论文。要求准确把握思想家的核心论点，展现文本细读能力，并对当代议题提出有理论深度的分析。论文字数约2000字。

Students select a core text by one political thinker, conduct an in-depth interpretation, and connect the theoretical perspective to a contemporary political issue (such as distributive justice, civil disobedience, or tech ethics). The paper should accurately capture the thinker's core arguments, demonstrate close reading skills, and offer theoretically informed analysis of the contemporary issue. Approximately 2000 words.
