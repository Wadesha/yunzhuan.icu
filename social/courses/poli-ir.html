国际关系

International Relations

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：政治学导论 | 学期：春季

本课程系统介绍国际关系理论与实践，考察国际体系的演变与当代全球政治的主要议题。课程涵盖现实主义、自由主义、建构主义等主要理论范式，以及战争与和平、国际安全、国际贸易、全球治理、国际法、国际组织、跨国社会运动等核心议题。学生将通过案例分析和模拟演练，理解国际政治的运行逻辑和主要行为体的互动模式。

This course provides a systematic introduction to international relations theory and practice, examining the evolution of the international system and major issues in contemporary global politics. The course covers major theoretical paradigms including realism, liberalism, and constructivism, as well as core topics such as war and peace, international security, international trade, global governance, international law, international organizations, and transnational social movements. Through case studies and simulations, students understand the logic of international politics and interaction patterns among key actors.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：当代国际冲突案例分析与理论应用

选择一个当代国际冲突案例，运用现实主义、自由主义或建构主义等理论进行深入分析。考察冲突的起源、动态与解决路径，撰写国际关系案例分析报告。

Choose a contemporary international conflict case and analyze it using realism, liberalism, or constructivism. Examine the origins, dynamics, and resolution pathways of the conflict, and write an international relations case analysis report.
