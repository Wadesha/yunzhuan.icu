政治学导论

Introduction to Political Science

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：秋季

本课程是政治学专业的入门课程，系统介绍政治学的基本概念、主要分支和研究方法。课程涵盖国家与主权、政治权力与权威、政治意识形态、政治制度、政治行为等核心议题。学生将通过经典文献阅读和案例分析，初步建立政治学的分析框架，为后续专业课程学习奠定基础。课程注重培养学生的批判性思维和政治分析能力。

This introductory course provides a comprehensive overview of political science as an academic discipline. Students will explore fundamental concepts such as state, sovereignty, power, authority, and political ideologies. The course covers major subfields including comparative politics, international relations, political theory, and public policy. Through readings and case studies, students develop basic analytical frameworks and critical thinking skills essential for advanced study in political science.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：当代政治现象分析报告

学生选择一个当代政治现象或热点议题（如选举制度改革、社交媒体与政治参与等），运用课程所学概念和分析框架，撰写一篇分析报告。要求明确研究问题，运用相关理论进行分析，并提出自己的见解。报告字数约1500字，需引用至少三篇学术文献，并正确标注出处。

Students select a contemporary political phenomenon or issue (such as electoral reform or social media and political participation), apply course concepts and analytical frameworks to write an analysis report. The paper should clearly state the research question, apply relevant theories, and present original insights. Approximately 1500 words with at least three academic sources properly cited.
