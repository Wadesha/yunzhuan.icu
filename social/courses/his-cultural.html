文化史

Cultural History

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：世界近现代史 | 学期：第5学期

本课程探讨历史上的文化现象及其与社会的互动关系，内容涵盖思想史、艺术史、宗教史、日常生活史、记忆史等领域。课程将介绍文化史的理论与方法，包括新文化史的理论与实践，分析文本、图像、物质文化、仪式等不同类型的史料，探讨文化如何塑造人们的世界观、价值观和行为方式，以及文化如何与政治、经济相互作用。

This course explores historical cultural phenomena and their interaction with society, covering intellectual history, art history, religious history, everyday life history, and memory studies. The course introduces cultural history theories and methods, including the new cultural history, and analyzes various types of sources such as texts, images, material culture, and rituals, examining how culture shapes worldviews, values, and behaviors, and how culture interacts with politics and economy.
