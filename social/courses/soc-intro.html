社会学导论

Introduction to Sociology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：秋季

本课程是社会学专业的入门课程，旨在引导学生用社会学的视角观察和分析社会现象。课程涵盖社会学的基本概念、主要理论流派和研究方法，包括社会化、社会互动、群体与组织、社会分层、家庭与教育等核心议题。通过案例分析和课堂讨论，培养学生的社会学想象力，学会质疑常识，理解社会结构如何塑造个体行为与观念。

This introductory course provides a foundation for understanding sociology as a discipline. Students learn core concepts, theoretical perspectives, and research methods. Topics include socialization, social interaction, groups and organizations, social stratification, family, and education. Through case studies and discussions, students develop sociological imagination and learn to question common sense assumptions about social life.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：社会现象的社会学想象力分析

选择一个日常社会现象，运用社会学想象力和基本概念进行分析。结合主要理论流派视角，揭示个体经历与社会结构的关联，撰写社会学分析报告。

Choose an everyday social phenomenon and analyze it using sociological imagination and basic concepts. Apply major theoretical perspectives to reveal connections between individual experiences and social structures, and write a sociological analysis report.
