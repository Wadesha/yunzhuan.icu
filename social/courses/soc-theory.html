社会学理论

Sociological Theory

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：社会学导论 | 学期：春季

本课程系统介绍社会学的主要理论传统与当代发展。从经典社会学家马克思、涂尔干、韦伯的思想出发，深入探讨功能主义、冲突理论、符号互动论、社会交换论等理论范式，延伸至结构主义、后现代主义、女性主义等当代理论。课程注重理论与现实的结合，训练学生运用不同理论视角分析社会问题，培养批判性思维与理论建构能力。

This course systematically introduces major sociological theoretical traditions and contemporary developments. Starting with classical theorists Marx, Durkheim, and Weber, it explores functionalism, conflict theory, symbolic interactionism, social exchange theory, and extends to structuralism, postmodernism, and feminism. The course emphasizes applying theoretical perspectives to analyze social issues and develop critical thinking.
