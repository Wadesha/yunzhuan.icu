生物人类学

Biological Anthropology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：4 | 先修课：人类学导论、生物学基础 | 学期：秋季

本课程从生物学视角研究人类的起源、进化与多样性。内容包括灵长类学、古人类学、人类遗传学、人类适应性生物学、法医人类学等。学生将学习人类进化的化石证据、现代人类的生物变异、基因与文化的互动关系等核心议题。课程包含实验环节，通过骨骼标本观察和遗传学实验，培养学生的科学研究方法和批判性思维能力。

This course examines human origins, evolution, and diversity from a biological perspective. Topics include primatology, paleoanthropology, human genetics, human adaptive biology, and forensic anthropology. Students learn about fossil evidence of human evolution, modern human biological variation, and the interaction between genes and culture. The course includes laboratory sessions with skeletal specimen observation and genetics experiments.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：人类骨骼标本观察与生物特征分析

选取一组人类骨骼标本或遗传数据，运用生物人类学方法进行观察、测量与分析。完成骨骼鉴定、性别年龄推断、人群变异分析或遗传特征解读，撰写生物人类学研究报告。

Select a set of human skeletal specimens or genetic data and apply biological anthropology methods for observation, measurement, and analysis. Complete skeletal identification, sex and age estimation, population variation analysis, or genetic trait interpretation, and write a biological anthropology research report.
