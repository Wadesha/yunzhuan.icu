美国政府与政治

American Government and Politics

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：政治学导论 | 学期：秋季

本课程系统考察美国政治制度的结构、运作与变迁。课程内容涵盖宪法原则、三权分立、联邦制、选举制度、政党政治、利益集团、媒体与政治、公民权利与自由、公共政策制定等核心议题。学生将深入理解美国的立法、行政、司法三大分支的权力制衡机制，以及公众舆论、选举政治和社会运动对美国政治进程的影响。

This course systematically examines the structure, operation, and evolution of the American political system. Topics include constitutional principles, separation of powers, federalism, electoral systems, party politics, interest groups, media and politics, civil rights and liberties, and public policy making. Students gain in-depth understanding of the checks and balances among the legislative, executive, and judicial branches, as well as the influence of public opinion, electoral politics, and social movements on the American political process.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：美国政治制度运作案例分析

选择美国政治中的一个具体案例（如最高法院判例、选举过程、立法博弈或政策制定），分析美国政治制度的实际运作。结合制度理论与实证资料，撰写美国政治分析报告。

Choose a specific case in American politics (e.g., Supreme Court ruling, election process, legislative bargaining, or policymaking) and analyze how American political institutions actually operate. Combine institutional theory with empirical data, and write an American politics analysis report.
