微观经济学原理

Principles of Microeconomics

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：无 | 学期：大一上

本课程是经济学专业的入门课程，系统介绍微观经济学的基本概念与原理。主要内容包括：供给与需求理论、消费者行为、生产者行为、市场结构与定价、外部性与公共物品、收入分配等。通过本课程的学习，学生将掌握微观经济学的基本分析框架，能够运用经济学思维理解和分析现实经济问题。

This is an introductory course for economics majors, systematically presenting the basic concepts and principles of microeconomics. Topics include supply and demand theory, consumer behavior, producer behavior, market structure and pricing, externalities and public goods, and income distribution. Students will acquire the basic analytical framework of microeconomics and develop economic thinking to understand real-world economic problems.
