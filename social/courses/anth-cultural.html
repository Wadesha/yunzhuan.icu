文化人类学

Cultural Anthropology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：人类学导论 | 学期：春季

本课程深入探讨文化人类学的核心议题，包括亲属制度、经济人类学、政治人类学、宗教人类学、性别与性等领域。学生将通过阅读经典民族志作品，了解不同文化的社会组织方式、价值体系和生活实践。课程强调文化相对主义视角，培养学生对跨文化差异的敏感度和分析能力，以及运用民族志方法研究当代社会问题的能力。

This course delves into core topics of cultural anthropology including kinship, economic anthropology, political anthropology, religion, gender, and sexuality. Students read classic ethnographies to understand social organization, value systems, and life practices across cultures. The course emphasizes cultural relativism, developing cross-cultural sensitivity and analytical skills for applying ethnographic methods to contemporary social issues.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：社区文化民族志研究

选择一个特定的社区或群体（如移民社区、行业群体、兴趣社群等），进行为期六周的田野调查。通过参与观察和深度访谈收集资料，撰写一份5000字的民族志研究报告。报告需涵盖该社区的社会结构、文化习俗、价值观念等方面，并运用至少一种文化人类学理论进行分析和阐释。

Select a specific community or group (immigrant community, occupational group, interest-based community, etc.) and conduct six weeks of fieldwork. Collect data through participant observation and in-depth interviews, then write a 5000-word ethnographic report covering social structure, cultural customs, and values, analyzed using at least one cultural anthropology theory.
