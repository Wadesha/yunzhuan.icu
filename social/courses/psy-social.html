社会心理学

Social Psychology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：心理学导论 | 学期：大二下

本课程研究社会情境如何影响个体的思想、情感与行为。主要内容包括社会认知与自我概念、社会知觉与归因、态度与态度改变、从众与服从、群体动力、人际吸引与亲密关系、偏见与歧视、亲社会行为与攻击行为等。课程结合经典实验与当代研究，引导学生运用社会心理学视角理解和分析现实社会现象。

This course examines how social situations influence individual thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Topics include social cognition and the self, social perception and attribution, attitudes and persuasion, conformity and obedience, group dynamics, interpersonal attraction and close relationships, prejudice and discrimination, prosocial behavior, and aggression. Classic experiments and contemporary research are integrated to help students understand real-world social phenomena.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：社会态度调查与群体行为分析

选择一个社会态度或群体行为议题，通过问卷调查或实验研究进行实证考察。运用社会心理学理论分析社会情境对个体行为的影响，撰写社会心理学研究报告。

Choose a social attitude or group behavior topic and conduct empirical investigation through surveys or experiments. Apply social psychology theory to analyze how social situations influence individual behavior, and write a social psychology research report.
