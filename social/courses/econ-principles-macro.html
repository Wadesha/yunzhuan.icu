宏观经济学原理

Principles of Macroeconomics

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：微观经济学原理 | 学期：大一下

本课程介绍宏观经济学的基本理论与分析方法，涵盖国民收入核算、总需求与总供给模型、经济增长、失业与通货膨胀、财政政策与货币政策、国际经济等核心内容。学生将学会运用宏观经济指标分析经济运行状况，理解政府政策对经济的影响机制，建立宏观经济思维框架，为后续中级宏观经济学及专业课程学习奠定基础。

This course introduces the basic theories and analytical methods of macroeconomics, covering national income accounting, aggregate demand and aggregate supply models, economic growth, unemployment and inflation, fiscal and monetary policy, and international economics. Students will learn to analyze economic performance using macroeconomic indicators, understand the impact mechanisms of government policies, and build a macroeconomic thinking framework.
