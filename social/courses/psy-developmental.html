发展心理学

Developmental Psychology

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：心理学导论 | 学期：大二下

本课程探讨个体从受精卵到死亡的毕生发展过程，涵盖生理、认知、社会情感三大领域的发展规律。内容包括发展心理学的理论流派（皮亚杰、维果茨基、埃里克森等）、胎儿发育与婴儿期、儿童期的认知与社会性发展、青少年的自我认同与同伴关系、成年期的发展任务、老化与临终心理等。课程强调遗传与环境的交互作用，培养学生以发展视角理解人类行为的能力。

This course explores human development from conception to death, covering physical, cognitive, and social-emotional domains across the lifespan. Topics include major developmental theories (Piaget, Vygotsky, Erikson), prenatal development and infancy, cognitive and social development in childhood, adolescent identity and peer relationships, adult developmental tasks, and aging and end-of-life psychology. The interaction of nature and nurture is emphasized throughout.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：毕生发展议题观察与研究

选择某一发展阶段或发展议题，通过观察、访谈或文献研究进行深入分析。运用发展心理学理论阐释遗传与环境的交互作用，撰写发展心理学研究报告。

Choose a developmental stage or issue and conduct in-depth analysis through observation, interviews, or literature research. Apply developmental psychology theory to explain nature-nurture interactions, and write a developmental psychology research report.
