历史研究方法

Historical Research and Writing

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：史学理论与方法 | 学期：第6学期

本课程是历史学专业的实践训练课程，系统训练学生从事历史研究的实际操作能力。内容包括研究选题的确定、学术史的梳理、史料的搜集与整理、史料的考证与辨析、论文结构与论证、学术规范与引用格式等。通过课堂讲授、写作练习、个别指导等多种形式，帮助学生完成从选题到论文撰写的全过程，为学年论文和毕业论文写作奠定基础。

This is a practical training course for history majors, systematically developing students' hands-on research skills. Topics include formulating research questions, reviewing scholarly literature, collecting and organizing sources, source criticism and analysis, paper structure and argumentation, and academic citation standards. Through lectures, writing exercises, and individual guidance, students complete the full research process from topic selection to paper writing, preparing them for term papers and theses.
