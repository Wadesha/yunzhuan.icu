越轨与社会控制

Deviance and Social Control

课程介绍 Course Introduction

学分：3 | 先修课：社会学导论 | 学期：秋季

本课程从社会学视角探讨越轨行为与社会控制的机制。课程分析越轨的定义如何被社会建构，考察不同理论流派对越轨成因的解释，包括失范理论、差异交往理论、标签理论、冲突理论等。内容涵盖犯罪、青少年越轨、药物滥用、性越轨、精神疾病等多种越轨形式，以及法律、媒体、教育、医疗等社会控制机制的运作逻辑。

This course explores deviant behavior and mechanisms of social control from a sociological perspective. It analyzes how deviance is socially constructed and examines major theoretical explanations including anomie theory, differential association, labeling theory, and conflict theory. Topics include crime, juvenile delinquency, substance abuse, sexual deviance, and mental illness, as well as social control through law, media, education, and medicine.

大作业 Final Project

作业标题：越轨行为案例与社会控制机制分析

选择一种越轨行为（如青少年越轨、药物滥用或网络越轨），运用社会学理论分析其成因与社会回应。考察相关社会控制机制的运作逻辑，撰写越轨社会学研究报告。

Choose a type of deviance (e.g., juvenile delinquency, substance abuse, or online deviance) and apply sociological theory to analyze its causes and social responses. Examine the operation of relevant social control mechanisms, and write a deviance sociology research report.
